## Quest Summary

On the party's last night in Elderholt, Elder Raven seeks out Bazgar and Sabriel in private.

"I need a favor," Raven says without preamble, setting several worn books on the table between them. "The gnolls in the valley. They need belief. A framework for their souls—an afterlife, religion, *something*."

Bazgar and Sabriel exchange glances of confusion and disbelief.

"They have nothing," Raven continues, terse and matter-of-fact. "No spiritual anchor. Elder Ishvar mentioned them some time ago—they were... inconvenient. And now I find that detail irritating. But their soul has no destination, so." She taps one of the books. "I've brought options. Different faiths. You'll teach them one. Any of them."

(pause)

"Once their souls have somewhere to go—once they believe in something beyond themselves—then I can harvest their bodies."

She says it plainly, as though discussing the weather.

"I need them to have belief first. An afterlife anchored somewhere. Once that's settled, what happens to the bodies is irrelevant."

She looks at them, genuinely confused by any objection.
