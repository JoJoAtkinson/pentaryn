## Phase B — Simulation: thrulm-void-scream-aoe

### Scenario Purpose

Full Void Scream triggering sequence vs. all three Compass Edge PCs who have converged on the beholder near the altar. Tests: (1) FRIGHTENED rider application and its downstream effect on Sabriel's ability to close for melee; (2) shrine-proximity disadvantage coverage; (3) whether VS damage ceiling creates one-shot conditions; (4) legendary action economy post-VS.

### Setup

Beholder has descended to within 30 ft of all three PCs (required for VS — "within 30 feet that can hear it"). Void-Feeding active (+1 to all attacks and damage rolls). VS recharged (rolled 6 on d6 at start of this turn).

**Initiative (representative roll):**
- Marwen (Wiz 5, DEX +2): 18
- Beholder (DEX +3): 16 ← acts before Bazgar and Sabriel this round
- Bazgar (Fighter 5, DEX +1): 15
- Sabriel (divine martial 5, DEX +1): 12

Beholder acts before Bazgar and Sabriel; Marwen went before beholder this round.

**Positioning:** Sabriel is closest to the altar (within 10 ft of shrine → disadvantage on VS save). Marwen and Bazgar are within 30 ft but outside 10 ft radius.

### Beholder's Turn — Action: Void Scream

DC 16 Wisdom save. Damage on fail: 6d10 psychic + Void-Feeding +1 = **34 avg**. On save: **17 avg**.
FRIGHTENED on fail: disadvantage on attacks while beholder in LOS; cannot move closer to beholder.

**PC save analysis:**

| PC | WIS mod | Modifier | Roll needed | Fail% | Notes |
|----|---------|----------|-------------|-------|-------|
| Bazgar | +1 (Wis 12 est.) | +1 (no WIS save prof) | 15+ | **70%** | Standard fighter |
| Marwen | +2 (Wis 14 est.) | +4 (INT/WIS save prof) | 12+ | **55%** | Proficient |
| Sabriel | +3 (Wis 16 est.) | +5 (WIS save prof) | 11+ with **disadvantage** | **~62%** | Within 10 ft of shrine |

**Expected outcome this round:**

Statistically: 1–2 PCs fail. Worst case (all 3 fail): Bazgar −34 HP → 15 HP; Marwen −34 HP → **−2 HP (down)**; Sabriel −34 HP → 10 HP. Best case (all 3 save): each takes 17 avg.

**Critical finding (corroborates R322):** Marwen (32 HP) is knocked unconscious on any failed VS save with Void-Feeding active (34 > 32). On a "beholder wins initiative" scenario, this is pre-emptive kill before Marwen acts. Since VS is the beholder's action (not a LA), this outcome requires only normal initiative luck — not an unusual edge case.

*Assessment:* Per design intent (party is meant to lose), this is acceptable. The "unfair rather than lethal" line is not crossed because Marwen is the squishiest target and 32 HP with a 55% fail chance on DC 16 is a known design envelope. However: if the beholder wins initiative AND Void Scream is recharged, Marwen has a ~55% chance of dying before the player takes a turn in round 1. This is worth noting as a first-round pacing concern.

### FRIGHTENED Downstream Effects

PCs who fail VS are frightened (DC 16 WIS save to end at end of each turn):
- **Sabriel (divine martial):** FRIGHTENED prevents closing with beholder for melee. Sabriel's primary counterplay (sanctified weapon, close engagement) is denied while frightened. She must spend turns attempting the save-to-end or attacking with disadvantage at range — her kit offers neither option effectively. This creates a **2–3 round lockout** with ~60% persistence per turn (DC 16 WIS, Sabriel +5 → needs 11+ → 50% break chance per turn end).
- **Bazgar (Fighter Battlemaster):** Disadvantage on attacks vs beholder while frightened. His Action Surge (extra attack) is wasted on disadvantaged rolls. Maneuver dice spent at disadvantage have expected DPR cut approximately in half.
- **Marwen:** If down, irrelevant. If saved: continues casting from range.

**Legendary action economy post-VS:**

Beholder has 3 LA available on other PCs' turns. Post-VS optimal:
- End of Marwen's turn (if conscious): Void Ray (2 LA, DC 16 DEX, 22 force). Marwen at best has 15 HP after a save — Void Ray likely finishes her.
- End of Bazgar's turn: Tentacle (1 LA, +8+1=+9 to hit, 13 bludgeoning + grapple). Bazgar's AC 18; hits on 9+ → 60% hit chance. Grapple sets up Maw on beholder's next turn.
- End of Sabriel's turn: Tentacle (1 LA) or hold for next round.
- Drain Divinity: 3 LA vs Sabriel any time she's within 30 ft. Strips her highest slot (3rd, then 2nd, then 1st over 3 rounds). No counterplay; requires no attack roll.

**Dominant LA pattern:** After VS, the beholder's dominant LA strategy is Tentacle → Maw chain against Bazgar (grapple, then Maw advantage next turn = +8+1 = +9 to hit twice vs grappled target = ~60% each, 21 avg damage per hit). In parallel: Drain Divinity on Sabriel whenever 3 LA accumulate. This matches the known-open Drain Divinity degenerate pattern.

### Feel Issues Identified

**FEEL-VS-03 (NEW LOG — shrineproximity DB gap):** The "creatures within 10 ft of shrine have disadvantage on this save" clause (documented in .md FIX-FC48-A) has no DB representation. The runner cannot auto-apply this disadvantage. The DM must track shrine position manually and call the disadvantage. With the beholder descended to ground level and Sabriel near the altar, this disadvantage is tactically significant (~12% additional fail rate) but invisible to the combat runner. Log for MCP fix when available: add a `save_rider` or `note` field to `void_scream` DB row noting the shrine-proximity condition.

**FEEL-VS-04 (corroborate R322 finding — first-round VS dominance):** If the beholder's round-1 opener is Void Scream (recharged, DEX+3 gives decent initiative), the fight's dramatic arc compresses to: explosive r1, party scrambles r2, collapse r3. Three PCs at expected HPs of ~6/unconscious/17 after r1 VS leaves no room for recovery without PC-side long-rest resources (Second Wind, spell slots). Against a party meant to lose this is valid, but VS-as-round-1-opener feels less like a dramatic loss and more like a math problem. Recommend the beholder open with Tentacle multiattack on round 1 (tactical precedent: beholder is patient, wants grapples to set up Maw advantage, saves VS for when party is clustered and bloodied). Tactical note only — no source edit required.

**FEEL-VS-05 (NEW — unconscious PC edge case, FIX-FC48-A confirmation):** Simulated case: Marwen knocked to 0 HP by VS in round 1. Round 2 VS recharge (6% chance). If it recharges and Marwen is still at 0 HP within 30 ft, FIX-FC48-A ruling says she's excluded. The runner has no mechanism to enforce this automatically. DM must remember. Logging for DM awareness; no source change needed.
