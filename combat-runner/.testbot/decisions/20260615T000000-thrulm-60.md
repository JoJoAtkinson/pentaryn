### Simulation — DR fires R1

**Initiative:**
- Beholder wins easily (DEX +3) vs Marwen (+2) and Sabriel (+0). Likely order: Beholder 18, Marwen 13, Sabriel 9.

**Beholder R1 action: Disintegration Ray vs Marwen**

*Hit rate:* +6 base; +1 Void-Feeding = **+7 vs AC 15**. Needs 8+. P(hit) = **13/20 = 65%**.

*On hit:* 10d8 force = avg **45 damage**. Marwen HP 32. **One-shot guaranteed (45 > 32)**. Target disintegrated: no body, no revivify/LoH/healing word recovery.

*On miss* (35% probability): beholder pivots to **multiattack** (2× TL + Maw). If beholder is in tentacle range of Marwen:
- TL 1 vs Marwen (+7 vs AC 15): 65% hit → avg 13 bludgeoning
- TL 2: 65% hit → avg 13
- Maw (if Marwen grappled): auto-crit, avg 39 piercing
- Even on miss DR, Marwen faces ~13+13 from non-crit tentacles (avg ~17 if both hit); likely down this turn.

**Counterplay options at level 5:**
- *Shield (reaction):* Marwen can use Shield (reaction, L1 slot) if she knows the attack is coming and acts via reaction: +5 AC → AC 20. +7 vs AC 20 needs 13+. P(hit) = **8/20 = 40%**. Marwen needs to have a slot available and to see the beholder's intent.
- *Cover (3/4 cover):* +5 AC → AC 20, same as Shield. Requires positioning before beholder's turn.
- *Higher initiative:* Marwen at +2 vs beholder at +3 — coin flip on tie, beholder usually goes first. If Marwen wins initiative (possible), she can Misty Step out of line of sight.

**Counterplay verdict:** Marwen has meaningful counterplay (Shield, positioning, initiative) but the outcome is binary — she either acts first / hides, or she's disintegrated before her turn. This is a **pre-emptive kill**, not a reactive one.

### FEEL-F60-01 (NEW): Disintegration-before-initiative = campaign deletion, not designed lethal

**Finding:** When DR fires R1 and the beholder wins initiative (the common case), Marwen is disintegrated before she takes a single action. This produces permanent character death with:
1. **No decision made by the player** — she died without acting.
2. **No recovery path** — no True Resurrection or Wish at level 5 → permanent campaign loss.
3. **No dramatic satisfaction** — the beholder's most impactful outcome (disintegration) happens without counterplay.

This is qualitatively different from "the beholder is designed to kill the party." Designed lethal fights should still give players agency. The disintegration outcome removes it entirely for Marwen.

**Mitigation options (log only — Joe decides):**
- **Existing tactic note already handles most cases:** FIX-EV7-32-A says "R1 = Multiattack, R2 = Void Scream." The beholder should NOT fire DR R1 per the written tactics. DR fires when recharged — which is a random event (33% per turn), not the intended opening. DM can enforce the tactic over the dice: "beholder holds DR for R2+ even if recharged R1."
- **Explicit tactic note for DR:** Add a sentence to DR's tactic section: "If DR recharges on R1, hold it — open with multiattack per R1 protocol. Fire DR R2+ once grapple is established and the wizard has had at least one turn." This eliminates the pre-initiative kill without changing any statistics.
- **Recharge 6 instead of 5–6:** Lowers DR recharge from 33% to 17% per turn. More mechanical change; Joe's call.

**Current state:** The tactic note is *implied* (FIX-EV7-32-A restricts R1 to multiattack) but doesn't explicitly say "don't use DR on R1." A new DM running this without full context could fire DR on recharge and permanently kill a PC before they act. Add explicit DR-hold note.

**Severity:** Medium (specific scenario — not every fight, not guaranteed). Design-review for Joe.
