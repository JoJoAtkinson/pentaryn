**FLAG-R261-01 (new this fire):** Shrine-Touched Ancient Resonance DC 14 — with PB+3 (stated explicitly in .md) and best ability mod +2 (DEX 15), the highest derivable DC is 8+3+2=13. The .md shows DC 14, which is 1 over any derivable value. Not in prior open-items lists; first logged here.

This is not auto-fixable (DC corrections are design calls). Joe decides: lower DC to 13 (consistent with PB+3) or raise DEX to 16 (+3, making the derivation work).

Note: this compounds the existing ambiguity about Shrine-Touched's CR. Cheat sheet says CR 3 (PB+2). .md states PB+3 explicitly (implies CR 5-8). With PB+2 (CR 3): max DC = 8+2+2=12, making the .md DC 14 off by 2. Inconsistency depends on which CR/PB is authoritative.

## Phase B — Playtest slice: `disintegration-threat`

*(From rotation.yml — Beholder Round 1 Disintegration Ray threat ceiling vs Compass Edge)*

### Setup

- **Beholder:** HP 110, AC 17, DEX +3, init bonus +3. Void-Feeding active (+1 atk/dmg in chamber). DR recharged (slice assumption).
- **Compass Edge:** Bazgar (Fighter 5, HP 49, AC 18), Marwen (Wizard 5, HP 32, AC 15), Sabriel (Divine Martial 5, HP 44, AC 19).
- **Initiative order assumed (worst-case for party):** Beholder wins initiative (d20+3 vs party d20+0 to +2; roughly 55/45 beholder-wins split).

### Round 1

**Lair action (init 20):** Beholder acts before its own turn. Optimal: Unstable Ground on Bazgar (DC 16 DEX, estimated DEX +0-1 for plate-armored fighter → 75% fail → prone). Sets up multiattack advantage for Round 2.

**Beholder turn:**
- **Disintegration Ray** (recharge assumed): +7 to hit (base +6, Void-Feeding +1) vs Marwen AC 15 → needs 8+ on d20 = **65% hit**.
- **If hit:** 10d8 + 1 (Void-Feeding) force damage. Mean = 45+1 = **46 damage**.
  - Marwen HP 32. P(46 < 32) ≈ 4% (10d8 stdev ≈ 7.25; 32 is 1.79σ below mean).
  - **P(Marwen at 0 HP | hit) ≈ 96%.**
  - If at 0 HP: Marwen is **disintegrated** (turned to ash). Cannot be restored except by True Resurrection (7th level, 1,000gp diamond) or Wish. Neither available at party level 5.
- **Combined P(Marwen permanently dead before acting):** 65% × 96% ≈ **62%**.

**Marwen's turn (if she survived — 38% of timelines):**
- Surviving means either the attack missed (35%) or it hit for < 32 damage (3%). Marwen at worst ~5 HP.
- Options: Misty Step + Shield (reaction), Counterspell... but she doesn't know what's next.
- Likely: Fly/Misty Step to cover + Firebolt from range. Or Hypnotic Pattern (DC 14, beholder immune to charmed — wasted slot).
- **Caveat:** Marwen has limited useful spells vs a beholder (Fireball would damage allies if derro are present; psychic spells resisted by beholder; many control spells fail vs immunities). This is a separate feel concern (not quantified this fire).

**After beholder's action — legendary actions on each PC's turn:**

After Sabriel's turn (assume DEX +2): Void Ray (2 LA), DC 16 DEX → 65% fail.  
E[dmg] = 0.65 × 22 + 0.35 × 11 = 18.3. Sabriel: HP 44 → ~26 HP.

After Bazgar's turn (DEX +0-1, prone from lair action): Tentacle Lash (1 LA), +7+adv vs AC 18 → ~75% hit (advantage vs prone in melee). Hit: 14 dmg + grapple. Bazgar: HP 49 → 35 HP, grappled.

Remaining LA (if Marwen still alive): Tentacle Lash on Marwen (1 LA, +7 vs AC 15 → 65% hit). Hit: 14 dmg. Marwen at ~5 HP → 0 HP, dead (non-disintegration).

### Round 2

**Lair action:** Void Eruption (within 20 ft of shrine, DC 16 DEX). Bazgar grappled — likely near shrine. DEX save fails ~75% → 11 avg dmg. Bazgar: 35 - 8 ≈ 27 HP.

**Beholder turn:** DR not recharged (33% chance; simulate miss). Multiattack.  
Bazgar (prone + grappled): both tentacle attacks with advantage (+7+adv vs AC 18 → 75% hit each).  
- Tentacle 1: hit, 14 dmg. Bazgar 27 → 13 HP.  
- Tentacle 2: hit, 14 dmg. Bazgar 13 → 0 HP. DOWN.  
- Maw: redirects to Sabriel (+7 vs AC 19 → 45% hit; avg 21 dmg). Sabriel: 26 - 9.5 ≈ 17 HP.

**End of Round 2 (worst-case / 62% of timelines):** Marwen disintegrated, Bazgar unconscious, Sabriel at ~17 HP with dwindling slots. TPK within Round 3 is near-certain.

### Quantified threat ceiling

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| P(Marwen dead before acting, Round 1) | ~62% |
| Average DR damage (in chamber) | 46 force |
| Damage needed to kill Marwen | 32 |
| P(one-shot if hit) | ~96% |
| Effective turns before TPK (full-party) | 2–3 |
| Time for Drain Divinity to matter (≥3 rounds) | Unlikely at this level gap |

### Key observations

**FEEL-R261-01 (new — Drain Divinity strictly dominated):**  
Drain Divinity costs the entire LA budget (3 actions) to remove one spell slot. At this CR/level gap (CR 13 vs level 5), the fight ends in 2 rounds via DR + multiattack — Drain Divinity never reaches a state where the removed slots matter. Expected value of Drain Divinity in a 2-round fight: ≤6 THP (removes one 3rd-level slot, gains 6 THP). Expected value of Tentacle + Void Ray (1+2 LA = same 3 LA budget): 14 dmg (Tentacle, 50% hit = 7 EV) + 18 dmg (Void Ray, 65% fail = 11.7 EV) = ~18.7 avg damage against a 44 HP PC. Drain Divinity's 6 THP is a worse use of the same 3 LA. Drain Divinity is **only valuable if**: (a) the fight lasts 4+ rounds, OR (b) the party's divine caster has enough slots that Void Scream or DR won't kill her outright. Neither condition applies vs level 5 Compass Edge. Already flagged as "degenerate vs fast fights" in FEEL-F253-09/FEEL-R259-01 — this simulation provides quantitative confirmation.

**FEEL-003 confirmed:** Disintegration Ray + level 5 party = non-recoverable narrative state on a 62% hit. Marwen's disintegration is permanent; the party cannot revive her. The encounter's "meant to lose" framing creates a design question: should the exit condition be "party retreats" (with all PCs alive) or "beholder kills a PC permanently"? If retreat, then either (a) the beholder should not use DR in the opener (requires explicit DM instruction) or (b) DR should kill but not disintegrate below a threshold (soft-kill variant already proposed in FEEL-003).
