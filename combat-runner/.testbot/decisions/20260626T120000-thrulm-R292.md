### Phase B Findings

---

**AUTOMATION-GAP-R292-01 (NEW): Unstable Form — no DB trigger, no threshold tracking.**

When shrine-touched takes ≥10 damage in one hit, it gains advantage on attack rolls until end
of its next turn. The runner has no mechanism for any of:
- Detecting the ≥10 threshold crossing on a single damage event
- Applying advantage to the Oath-Breaking Retaliation attack (same event, fires as reaction)
- Applying advantage to action attacks on the shrine-touched's next turn
- Auto-clearing the advantage state at end of that turn

**Impact:** Two attack rolls are wrong on every Unstable Form trigger cycle — the reaction
attack AND one of the multiattack attacks on the shrine-touched's next turn. DM must track
entirely manually, and is likely to miss it, especially when the reaction + next-turn attack
all happen under combat pressure.

**Fix path:** Add a `utility` trigger row for `unstable_form` with event `damage`, match
`10+ damage in one hit`, effect `advantage on attack rolls until end of next turn`, and
an auto-expiry hook. This requires runner support for "advantage-state" flagging — a runner
feature, not a DB-only fix. Alternatively, add a bolded checklist note to the shrine-touched
.md start-of-turn checklist. (Prefer .md note as interim; runner flag as eventual fix.)

**Priority: MEDIUM.** Affects every fight where shrine-touched takes a real hit (likely every
fight at this party level). Often invisible because the reaction also fails to roll (BUG-DB-R156-01
means DMs don't expect it to do much anyway).

---

**FEEL-032 (NEW): Oath-Breaking Retaliation fires on any-damage; no minimum threshold.**

The reaction reads: "When the derro takes damage from a source it can see, it can use its
reaction to make one Shrine-Axe attack against the attacker." No minimum damage. A 1-HP graze
from a crossbow bolt triggers a free melee attack (+4 vs AC 19, ~25% hit, ~9 avg damage).

**Feel concern at the table:** This trait punishes any hit, not just meaningful ones. Against
the Compass Edge (all melee or close-range): every single attack on the shrine-touched potentially
returns a reaction swing. Since the DM has to manually track this (see BUG-DB-R156-01), it often
gets forgotten on 1–2 damage hits. When it IS applied consistently, the fight can feel attritional
in an unexpected way — the shrine-touched reacts to everything.

**Contrast with design intent:** Unstable Form (≥10 threshold) is the "you awakened something
dangerous" mechanic. Oath-Breaking Retaliation (no threshold) is "it punishes aggression." These
work together thematically, but having an always-on free attack at +4 vs. an always-gated advantage
buff creates asymmetric activation: the retaliation fires far more often than the advantage.

**Recommendation (log only):** Consider adding a minimum threshold to Oath-Breaking Retaliation
(e.g., "…takes 5 or more damage from a source…") to align the two traits' activation windows. This
would also reduce the manual-tracking burden at the table. Design call for Joe.

beyond CR 3 norms. **Design resolution: accept CR 5-6 or reduce modifiers to PB +2.**
