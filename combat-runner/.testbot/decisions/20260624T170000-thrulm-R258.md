**NEW BUG — BUG-R258-01: Maw missing grapple-escape disadvantage rider in DB.**

The Maw attack in the multiattack attacks array has no rider or effect field noting "if target is
grappled, they have disadvantage on grapple escape ability checks." The .md stat block (FIX-R220-A)
corrected the wording from "saving throw" to "ability check" — that fix is in the .md but not
reflected in the DB. At the table, the runner fires the Maw attack roll and damage, then outputs
nothing about the escape-disadvantage. DM must track manually or the advantage is forgotten.

*Schema note:* This rider is conditional (applies only if the target is already grappled), so a
simple `rider_on_hit` string won't fully capture it. A `conditional_rider` field with
`condition: "target_is_grappled"` and `effect: "disadvantage on STR(Athletics)/DEX(Acrobatics)
grapple-escape checks"` would be needed. Until schema supports conditional riders, a plain
`rider_on_hit` reminder string is the safe minimum. Requires `combat_action_upsert`. Not
auto-fixable.

**NEW FEEL — FEEL-R258-01: Party-wide grapple by end of Round 1.**

Running the scenario forward: beholder multiattack grapples Marwen (down, grappled). Tentacle LA
after Bazgar's turn can grapple Sabriel (if it hits). By Round 2, up to 3 party members may be
grappled (speed = 0, cannot reposition, cannot reach cover). With all 3 PCs grappled, the
beholder can use Shrine-Drift to pass through them without OA risk, positioning for next-round
multiattack with +6 attacks against immobile targets.

This extends FEEL-R257-01 (grapple-snowball on Marwen specifically) to a party-wide immobilization
scenario. The beholder can hold 4 creatures — with only 3 PCs, it can theoretically grapple all of
them by Round 2 and spend legendary actions on Void Ray / Drain Divinity exclusively. Once all 3
PCs are grappled: speed 0, attacks against them are normal (no automatic crit), they escape only on
12+ (Athletics, DC 16) or with Help action — but who's free to Help? Nobody.

Joe should confirm: is party-wide grapple + Maw auto-crit loop (on downed targets within 5 ft)
the intended end state, or should the beholder have a maximum grapple priority rule ("never grapple
more than 1 PC at a time")?

**NEW FEEL — FEEL-R258-02: "tentacle" verb fires multiattack; "lash" fires LA tentacle.**

The verb split (multiattack: "tentacle"; LA: "lash") is correct but counter-intuitive. At the
table mid-round, when the DM wants to spend 1 legendary action on a single tentacle, they may
instinctively type "tentacle" — which fires the full 3-attack multiattack on the beholder's OWN
turn instead. The verb "lash" is less salient. Recommendation: add "legendary tentacle" alias to
the multiattack verb exclusion list, OR add "tentacle" to tentacle_legendary verbs and explicitly
remove it from multiattack verbs (making the multiattack verb "attack" or "multi" the canonical
trigger). Joe/UX decides — not auto-fixable.
