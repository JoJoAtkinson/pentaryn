### Legendary Action Economy Analysis

Across both scenarios, the LA decision tree is identical:
1. **Void Ray (2 LA)** — consistent best use. EV against Marwen: 0.60×23+0.40×11.5=13.8+4.6=**18.4 avg force**. Against Bazgar (DEX +0): 0.75×23+0.25×11.5=17.25+2.875=**20.1 avg**.
2. **Tentacle (1 LA)** — reliable follow-up, sets up grapple.
3. **Move (1 LA)** — used only when repositioning is necessary for next turn.
4. **Drain Divinity (3 LA)** — still **never** the dominant choice this simulation. To use it: beholder must be within 30 ft of a divine caster. In round 2, beholder hovered at 40-60 ft and would need to descend (spend movement or BA Shrine-Drift) to get within 30 ft of Sabriel. That means either sacrificing Shrine-Drift or burning the BA slot. Net: Drain Divinity requires two resource expenditures (position change + the 3 LA) vs Void Ray (2 LA, any range up to 120 ft). Drain Divinity is unambiguously dominated by the Void Ray + Tentacle combination (18+7=25 avg damage vs Drain Divinity's THP return and slot denial). **FEEL-R228-01 confirmed again.**

### Key Findings

**1. (FEEL-DISINT-R245-01 / MEDIUM — NEW) Disintegration Ray removes player agency — attack roll with permanent consequences.**

Every other ability in the beholder's kit that threatens permanent harm is either:
- A save (Void Scream, Void Ray, Drain Divinity, Void Eruption, Unstable Ground) — the player rolls a die and knows they had a chance
- Conditional (death saves after going to 0 HP) — the player makes three rolls before dying

Disintegration Ray is unique: it is an **attack roll with no player-side die** that, on a hit, immediately and permanently removes the character. The 65% hit rate in-chamber means a disintegrated Marwen is the **modal outcome** (>50% per activation). A Wisdom or DEX saving throw instead of an attack roll would maintain the threat while giving Marwen agency. (This mirrors the design of Disintegrate, the 6th-level spell: target makes a DEX save.)

The distinction matters because: the Marwen *player* made no roll. The DM hits or misses on one die. If it hits, the Marwen player's character is gone. This can produce a "it just happened to me" feeling that is qualitatively different from "I failed my death saves" — even if both end in character death.

**Design note (Joe's call, not an auto-fix):** Consider converting Disintegration Ray to a save-based ability (DC 16 DEX save, 45 (10d8) on fail / half on success, disintegrated only if FULL damage reduces to 0 — or simply: disintegrated if damage reduces to 0 AND the target fails the save). This preserves the threat (Marwen at 32 HP is likely gone on fail) while giving the player the roll.

---

**2. (FEEL-DISINT-R245-02 / LOW — NEW) Void Scream (recharge 6) expected to not appear in ~58% of combats at 3-4 round length.**

- P(recharge in any given round) = 1/6 = 16.7%
- P(at least one recharge over rounds 2-4) = 1 - (5/6)³ ≈ 42.1%
- Complement: **~58% of combats never see Void Scream.**

If the beholder acts for only 2 rounds: P(at least one recharge) = 1 - (5/6)² ≈ 30.6%. So ~70% of short combats never see the scream.

This is the encounter's "wow" AoE ability — 33 psychic in a 30-ft radius with frightened rider and shrine proximity disadvantage. It reads as a climactic ability but statistically is missing more often than not. Note: this doesn't mean it's broken (recharge 6 is intentionally rare), but if the encounter typically ends in 3 rounds at this party level, the Void Scream is largely decorative. **Low priority — confirm with Joe whether this is intended drama scarcity (rare = impactful) or an oversight.**

---

**3. (CONFIRMED) Drain Divinity dominated; Void Ray dominant LA choice.**

Both scenarios confirmed FEEL-R224-03 and FEEL-R228-01. Drain Divinity requires 30-ft proximity + 3 LA cost. Void Ray requires no proximity + 2 LA cost and deals 18.4-20.1 avg damage. Drain Divinity's THP return (max +10 THP if Sabriel expends a 5th-level slot she doesn't have) is not competitive. The only scenario where Drain Divinity outperforms is if: (a) the beholder is already within 30 ft, (b) the target has a high-level slot remaining, AND (c) the beholder has no better use of 3 LA. This condition set is unusual. **FEEL-R228-01 age now 6 fires.**
