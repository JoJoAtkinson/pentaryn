### New Findings

#### FEEL-F26-01 (new): Rager Taunt is a no-op against a save-based Wizard

**Severity:** Medium — affects every encounter where Marwen is the Taunt target.

**Finding:** Rager Taunt imposes "disadvantage on attack rolls against targets other than the rager." The Rager tactics note says "target the back-line caster or healer" as highest value — reasoning that it forces them to redirect attacks onto the Rager. But against Marwen (Wizard 5), approximately 0% of her damage spells are attack rolls:
- Fireball (DC 14 DEX save): **not affected** by Taunt.
- Shatter (DC 13 CON save): **not affected**.
- Hold Person (DC 15 WIS save): **not affected**.
- Web, Slow, Grease, etc.: all saves, **not affected**.
- Only Fire Bolt (+5 to hit) would be affected — but Marwen uses cantrips rarely when slots are available.

Taunt on Marwen in practice: zero tactical pressure on her primary actions. She freely Fireballs, Shatters, and casts control spells as if Taunt didn't exist.
