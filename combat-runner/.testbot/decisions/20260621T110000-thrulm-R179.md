### Shrine-proximity disadvantage analysis (NEW this fire)

The beholder's Void-Feeding bonus and the "Creatures within 10 feet of the shrine have disadvantage on this [VS] save" clause interact with the likely party positioning. The beholder floats near the altar to maintain Void-Feeding; PCs closing in to engage are within 10 ft of the shrine.

**Bazgar (WIS +0, within 10 ft shrine → disadvantage on WIS DC 16 save):**
- P(fail with disadvantage) = 1 − P(both dice ≥ 16) = 1 − (5/20)² = 1 − 25/400 = **93.75% fail rate**.
- Result on fail: 33 psychic + FRIGHTENED 1 min (DC 16 WIS save each turn to end). With WIS +0, P(saving each turn) = 25%. Expected frightened duration: 4 rounds. Bazgar has disadvantage on all attacks vs beholder AND cannot willingly move closer — his melee role is entirely disabled.

**Marwen (WIS +2, within 10 ft shrine → disadvantage):**
- P(fail with disadvantage) = 1 − (6/20)² = 1 − 36/400 = **91% fail rate**.
- On fail: takes 33 psychic. Marwen HP 32. **33 > 32 → KO from full HP at average damage.** (Actually, 6d10 average is exactly 33; floor is 6, max is 60. Expected value = exactly 33 → consistent 1-shot if at full HP.)
- FIX-FC48-A applies if Marwen was already unconscious: she cannot hear VS and is excluded.

**Sabriel (divine martial with Paladin WIS proficiency → WIS save ~+5, within 10 ft shrine → disadvantage):**
- P(fail with disadvantage) = 1 − (10/20)² = 1 − 100/400 = **75% fail rate**.
- On fail: 33 psychic. Sabriel HP 44 → 11 HP remaining. FRIGHTENED.

### NEW findings this fire

#### **FEEL-R179-01 (HIGH — NEW): Near-shrine VS save is nearly guaranteed to fail for WIS-dump PCs**

The stat block's "Creatures within 10 feet of the shrine have disadvantage" clause combines with the likely combat positioning (party closing in on the shrine-hovering beholder) to produce near-automatic VS failure for Bazgar (93.75%) and Marwen (91%). This is not reflected anywhere in the cheat sheet or tactics notes.

**Impact:** A player choosing to close into melee on the beholder is unknowingly accepting a ~93% chance of FRIGHTENED + 33 psychic if VS fires that round. There's no in-world warning about the shrine zone. This is "unfair rather than lethal" — the punishment is mechanically foreseeable (shrine zone is marked in the tactics) but players have no cue that the radius is a VS danger zone.

**Recommendation (log-only):** Add to COMBAT-CHEAT-SHEET.md under "When Beholder Acts" section: "*Shrine Zone (within 10 ft of altar): VS saves are taken at disadvantage. Bazgar WIS+0 = 93.75% fail; even Sabriel WIS+5 = 75% fail. DM call: warn players OOC or treat as tactically foreseeable?*" Also update Tactics section in beholder stat block: note that hovering at the shrine creates a VS "kill box" in the 10-ft shrine radius.

---

#### **FEEL-R179-02 (MEDIUM — NEW): Void Scream Recharge 6 creates binary encounter variance**

P(VS fires at least once in 4 rounds) ≈ 52%. When VS fires, it effectively ends the encounter in 1–2 rounds (Marwen perma-risk, FRIGHTENED disabling melee). When VS doesn't fire (48% of encounters), the party grinds an attrition fight of 4–6 rounds instead. The two run-types feel like completely different encounters.

**Impact:** No consistent "thrulm feel" across playthroughs — half the time the beholder ends things with a screech, half the time it's a tentacle-and-ray marathon. This is a design choice, not a bug, but worth Joe reviewing. Suggest either: (a) lower VS to Recharge 5–6 for more consistent presence, (b) raise to "once per encounter, on recharge from 2nd round" to make it a reliable climax moment, or (c) accept the variance as the beholder's unpredictability.
