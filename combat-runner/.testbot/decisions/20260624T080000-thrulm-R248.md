Derro-Shardcaller: CR 2 (per cheat sheet) → PB +2. Highest stat modifier = DEX +2. Maximum save DC with PB +2: 8 + 2 + 2 = **12**. Stated DC 13 is 1 point over. The three values (CR 3, PB +3, DC 14) cannot all be internally correct with any 5e formula. **Human decision needed on which anchor to fix first.**

## Phase B — Generative Playtest: `drain-divinity-vs-sabriel`

*thrulm-rotation.json index 4 of 8.*

**Rotation description (rotation.md slot 1):** "Simulate a round where the beholder spends all 3 legendary actions on Drain Divinity vs Sabriel. Compare temp HP gained vs damage forgone from 1 Tentacle + 1 Void Ray alternative. Flag if the trade is clearly suboptimal."

### Setup

- **Round:** 2. Party engaged, frontliners (Bazgar, Sabriel) within 30 ft of altar. Beholder has used main action (multiattack). 3 LA remaining.
- **Sabriel:** HP 44 → 44 (untouched round 1 — conservative); CHA modifier +3 (Paladin 5 typical); spell slots available: 4×1st, 3×2nd, 2×3rd. Highest slot remaining: 3rd level.
- **Beholder:** Within 30 ft of Sabriel (descended from hover position to use Drain Divinity — required by 30-ft range).

### Drain Divinity (3 LA, all in) — Expected Value

- DC 16 CHA save. Sabriel: +3 CHA → needs 13+. P(success) = 40%, P(fail) = 60%.
- On fail: Sabriel loses 3rd-level slot. Beholder gains 2×3 = **6 THP**.
- Expected THP: 0.60 × 6 = **3.6 avg THP**
- Expected slot denial: 0.60 × 1 slot = 0.6 spell slots
- HP damage dealt: **0**

### Alternative: Tentacle (1 LA) + Void Ray (2 LA) — Expected Value

**Tentacle Lash (1 LA) vs Sabriel (AC 19):**
- +7 to hit (in chamber, +1 Void-Feeding). Needs 12+. P(hit) = 45%.
- Expected damage: 0.45 × 13 = **5.85 avg** + grapple on hit.

**Void Ray (2 LA) vs Sabriel — DC 16 DEX save:**
- Sabriel: estimated DEX +2, no DEX save proficiency. Needs 14+. P(success) = 35%, P(fail) = 65%.
- Expected damage: 0.65 × 23 + 0.35 × 11.5 = **18.975 avg force**

**Total Option B:** 5.85 + 18.975 = **~24.8 avg damage** to Sabriel, plus grapple on Tentacle hit (45% chance).

### Comparison

| Option | Resource | HP Damage | THP Gained | Slot Denial | Positional Cost |
|--------|----------|-----------|------------|-------------|-----------------|
| Drain Divinity (3 LA) | All 3 LA | 0 | 3.6 avg | 0.6 slots | Must be within 30 ft — exposes beholder to melee |
| Tentacle (1 LA) + Void Ray (2 LA) | 3 LA | ~24.8 avg | 0 | 0 | No positional change needed |

**Drain Divinity EV ratio:** ~3.6 THP vs ~24.8 damage equivalent. Option B delivers ~6.9× more game impact.

### Compounding Problem: Melee Exposure

Drain Divinity requires the beholder to be within 30 ft of Sabriel. The beholder's default hover is 40–60 ft above the altar — it must use Shrine-Drift (bonus action, or active repositioning) to close the gap. However:
- The same turn it uses all 3 LA on Drain Divinity, it has **no LA remaining for Move**.
- Bazgar (Fighter) is likely within melee range at this point. After Drain Divinity fires, the beholder is within melee range of Bazgar (Battlemaster 5, Action Surge capable) with no defensive repositioning available.
- If Bazgar has not yet taken his turn and acts next: full attack (2-3 attacks, Battlemaster maneuver) against AC 17, all at normal range.

**Sequence cost:** Drain Divinity not only forgoes ~24.8 HP damage from the alternative, it also grants Bazgar an easier attack turn (beholder in melee range, no Move LA to reposition away).

### Feel Assessment

Drain Divinity is **structurally never optimal** against this party composition. This is a known-open issue (FEEL-R224-03, FEEL-R228-01, now age 20+). This simulation adds new texture: the 30-ft range requirement actively creates a losing positional state that compounding makes the ability even less attractive in practice than the EV comparison alone suggests.

**Confirmed: even against a Paladin (the nominal target of Drain Divinity), the expected value of Tentacle+Void Ray exceeds Drain Divinity by ~7:1, while also avoiding the melee-exposure penalty.**
