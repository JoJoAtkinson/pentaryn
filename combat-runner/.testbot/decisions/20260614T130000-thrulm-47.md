### Full situation simulation (Compass Edge vs 2× Shardcaller)

**Setup:** R2. Bazgar (HP 49, AC 18) has charged SC-1. SC-1 was at 15 ft from Bazgar — within melee threat range. SC-2 is 50 ft from Bazgar at the back of the chamber.

**Initiative:**

**SC-1's Turn (init 16, threatened at 15 ft by Bazgar, Barrage recharged):**

Per checklist item 3: TR fires at the START of own turn when threat is already within 15 ft. SC-1 uses:
- **BA: Tactical Retreat** — moves 30 ft directly away from Bazgar. New position: 45 ft from Bazgar.

TR fires clean. Now SC-1 must choose its main action: Shard-Barrage (recharged) or Multiattack (Shard-Throw)?

**Long-range penalty flag:** After TR, SC-1 is 45 ft from Bazgar. Shard-Throw normal range is 30 ft, long range 60 ft. 45 ft is in the long-range band → **disadvantage on Shard-Throw attack rolls vs Bazgar.**

- Shard-Throw with disadvantage vs Bazgar (AC 18): needs 14+ (35% base). With disadvantage: P(both ≥ 14) = 0.35² ≈ **12% hit rate per throw.** Two throws: expected ~0.25 hits → avg **~1.6 damage** per turn vs Bazgar. Effectively zero.
- Shard-Throw with disadvantage vs Marwen (AC 15, if she's at 40 ft now): needs 11+ (50% base). With disadvantage: 0.50² = **25% hit rate.** Two throws → avg **~6.5 damage.** Marginal.
- Shard-Barrage (recharge): DC 13 DEX **save** — not an attack roll. Long range does NOT apply to saves. Range is 15-ft line; SC-1 needs 3 PCs in the 15-ft line, unlikely after TR repositioning. If only 1-2 PCs in line: worse than the "3+ in line" ideal, but the save still fires at full DC.

**Decision: After TR, if Shard-Barrage is recharged, SC-1 should prefer Barrage over Multiattack when all targets are at 31-60 ft.** Barrage is save-based (unaffected by long range); Multiattack at disadvantage delivers near-zero expected damage vs AC 18.

**SC-1 plays Shard-Barrage:** Targets a 15-ft line through Bazgar (45 ft away) — but wait, Barrage is a 15-ft *line*, not 15-ft *range* to target. The line must originate from SC-1, which means Bazgar must be within 15 ft of the LINE start (SC-1's position). Actually re-reading: "15-ft line" — this is the AoE length, not range. The line starts adjacent to SC-1 or at a point of origin? Likely at SC-1's position, extending 15 ft. If so, Barrage can only hit targets within 15 ft of SC-1 — Bazgar at 45 ft is out of range entirely.

**Critical find:** After TR (SC-1 now 45 ft from Bazgar), Shard-Barrage is also out of range (15-ft line from SC-1). SC-1's only remaining option is Multiattack at long-range disadvantage, or to hold its action.

So the optimal SC-1 play after TR is: **Multiattack at disadvantage vs the closest remaining target within 30 ft normal range.** If no target is within 30 ft (all PCs are 45+ ft), SC-1 has no effective main action this turn except accepting long-range disadvantage.

### Feel analysis

**FEEL-F47-01 (NEW) — TR long-range penalty unspecified in tactics:**

After Tactical Retreat (30 ft away), SC-1 is 45 ft from a target that was originally 15 ft away. Shard-Throw normal range is 30 ft — 45 ft is in the long-range band, imposing disadvantage on attack rolls. Against AC 18 (Bazgar), Shard-Throw drops from 35% to **12% hit rate** — near zero expected damage. Against AC 15 (Marwen), 50% drops to **25%**.

The tactics section says: *"If a melee threat closes within 15 ft: Tactical Retreat (no OA), then continue throwing. Fire in the new position."* This is technically correct but omits the critical caveat: **"if the new position is >30 ft from all targets, every Shard-Throw suffers long-range disadvantage. Against AC 18+, this makes Multiattack effectively useless. See Barrage priority below."**

**Recommended tactics addition:** *"After TR: if new position is 31-60 ft from all targets AND Shard-Barrage is available AND PCs are within 15 ft of your position (within the Barrage line), prefer Barrage — the DC 13 save is unaffected by distance. If Barrage unavailable or no target in Barrage line, use Multiattack at long-range disadvantage vs the lowest-AC target in range (Marwen at AC 15: 25% hit rate is painful but better than 12% vs Bazgar). If all targets are >60 ft, hold action or dash to close."*

**FEEL-F47-02 (NEW) — Barrage range vs TR interaction:**

After TR (SC now 45 ft from Bazgar), Shard-Barrage (15-ft line) cannot reach Bazgar — the line originates at SC-1's position and extends 15 ft, while Bazgar is 45 ft away. The save-based advantage of Barrage evaporates if TR has pushed SC beyond Barrage line coverage. This creates a dead zone: SC has retreated to safety but cannot reach with either Multiattack (at effective range) or Barrage.

**Practical table guidance:** SC should NOT retreat to a position where all PCs are beyond both Shard-Throw range (60 ft) and Barrage line reach (adjacent). The default TR (30 ft back from nearest enemy) often creates a 30-60 ft gap from all targets — acceptable for Barrage if 1+ PC is still within 15 ft of SC's new position. In typical corridor geometry, this means TR is safe only if at least one PC remains within 15 ft of the BACK wall the SC retreats to. This is a map-dependent edge case but worth flagging for Thrulm's chamber layout.

**Scenario pass result:** TR + Shard-Throw fires without conflict on the bonus-action vs main-action split. No crash expected. The long-range penalty and Barrage range interaction are feel/tactics notes, not dispatch bugs.
