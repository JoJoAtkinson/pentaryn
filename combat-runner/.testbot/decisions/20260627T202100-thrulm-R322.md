## Phase B — Simulation: void-scream-clustering

### Scenario Purpose

Test whether Void Scream's area targeting creates a "correct play" dominance once the party clusters near the shrine altar in rounds 2–3. Evaluate: (1) expected damage ceiling vs. party HP budget; (2) FRIGHTENED condition's downstream effect on Bazgar's DPR; (3) whether shrine proximity disadvantage creates meaningful tactical counterplay.

### Encounter Setup

**Round 2 opening.** Party has engaged derro minions near the altar. Beholder has descended from 40-60 ft hover to within 30 ft of all three PCs.
- Bazgar (49 HP max; took 14 avg damage from Tentacle Lash r1 if tentacle hit on 50%/80% chance — assume 35 HP remaining)
- Marwen (32 HP max; took 0 damage r1 if she held back, or up to 35 avg if tentacle targeted her — assume 32 HP for this simulation, worst case)
- Sabriel (44 HP max; no damage r1 — she was healing — assume 44 HP)
- Beholder: Void Scream available (has not been used; Recharge 6 means it starts combat ready)

**Key constraint:** Void Scream requires beholder within 30 ft of targets AND targets can hear it. Cheat sheet confirms: "30 ft radius from beholder — must descend from default hover." The beholder must descend to engage, which reduces its distance advantage. ✓

### Save Analysis

DC 16 Wisdom saving throw:

| PC | Wis mod | Roll needed | Fail % | Save % |
|----|---------|------------|--------|--------|
| Bazgar (Fighter 5) | +1 (Wis 12 assumed) | 15+ on d20 | **70%** | 30% |
| Marwen (Wizard 5) | +2 (Wis 14 assumed) | 14+ on d20 | **65%** | 35% |
| Sabriel (divine martial 5) | +3 (Wis 16 assumed) | 13+ on d20 | **60%** | 40% |

### Damage Expected Values

On fail: 6d10 psychic (avg 33) + Void-Feeding +1 = **34 avg**
On save: half = **17 avg**

| PC | Expected damage | HP after |
|----|----------------|----------|
| Bazgar | 0.70×34 + 0.30×17 = **28.9** | 35 - 28.9 = **6.1** |
| Marwen | 0.65×34 + 0.35×17 = **28.1** | 32 - 28.1 = **3.9** |
| Sabriel | 0.60×34 + 0.40×17 = **27.2** | 44 - 27.2 = **16.8** |
| **Total** | **84.2 avg** | Party at ~27 HP total after this one action |

Probability Marwen drops to 0 HP in this one action (fresh at 32 HP):
- Fail (65%): need 10d10 roll ≥ 33 for roll = 32+ (well within range; minimum 10, average 33; P(fail AND ≥ 33 damage) ≈ 0.65 × 0.52 = ~34%)
- Pass (35%): 17 damage leaves Marwen at 15 HP — not down
- **Overall: ~34% chance Void Scream alone drops Marwen to 0 on round 2 from full HP.** Higher if she took ANY prior damage.

### FRIGHTENED Condition Analysis

On failed save: frightened of beholder for 1 minute, DC 16 Wis save at END of each turn to end.
- Bazgar fails frightened: 70% chance. Clearance per turn: 30%. Expected duration: ~3.3 rounds.
- Under FRIGHTENED: disadvantage on all attack rolls while beholder in LoS; cannot willingly move toward beholder.

**Bazgar DPR comparison (vs AC 17 beholder, 2 attacks/round, assume +7 to hit, 1d8+4 avg damage):**

| Condition | Hit rate per attack | DPR (2 attacks) |
|-----------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Normal | 55% (needs 10+) | 2 × 0.55 × 8.5 = **9.35** |
| FRIGHTENED (disadvantage) | ~30% (P(max of 2 ≥ 10) = 0.55² ≈ 0.30) | 2 × 0.30 × 8.5 = **5.1** |
| FRIGHTENED + Antireality on first | first: ~17%; second: ~30%; avg per attack ~23.5% | **4.0** |

Bazgar with non-magical weapons also faces beholder nonmagical B/P/S resistance (half damage). Effective DPR against beholder:
- Normal: **4.7** (halved)
- Frightened: **2.0–2.55** (halved)

Bazgar is effectively removed from meaningful damage output for 2-3 rounds after a Void Scream that lands FRIGHTENED. At 4.7 base DPR halved, even at full power he needs ~23 rounds to kill the beholder alone.

### Finding: VOID-SCREAM-CLUSTER-R322-01

**Void Scream is the dominant action whenever all three PCs are within 30 ft of the beholder.** In round 2 with party clustered near the altar (which derro engagement encourages), expected output is 84 avg damage across all three PCs — exhausting ~27 HP total remaining across the party after round 2 action. Combined effects:
1. ~34% one-shot of Marwen (from full HP) — higher if she took any damage in round 1
2. ~70% FRIGHTENED on Bazgar for ~3 rounds — collapses his already-halved DPR by another 45%
3. Party at ~27 total HP remaining after this one ability, facing full beholder HP and ongoing legendary actions

**No counterplay path at this table position.** The only avoidance strategy is spreading out beyond 30 ft of the beholder — but derro engagement (especially Rager Taunt and Shardcaller positioning) tends to pull the party toward the altar zone, not away from it.

### Finding: VOID-SCREAM-CLUSTER-R322-02 — shrine proximity rule rarely creates decision texture

The "within 10 ft of shrine: disadvantage on save" modifier (cheat sheet confirmed) would stack with an already-low save chance. But in practice, if PCs are within 10 ft of the shrine (which the beholder hovers over), they are within reach of Tentacle Lash. The party should never WANT to be within 10 ft of the shrine. This creates a position rule that rarely fires because it triggers precisely when players are already making a catastrophic positioning error. The rule doesn't create interesting decisions — it only deepens punishment for avoidable mistakes. **Flag for DM: consider removing or changing the 10 ft threshold to 30 ft (= edge of beholder's "sphere of control"), so players who engage the beholder directly pay a meaningful save penalty rather than only players who walk under it.**

### LA Economy Note (reconfirmed from R321)

After Void Scream, beholder has expended its action. Legendary actions this round:
- After Marwen's turn: Tentacle Lash LA (1 LA) → +9 vs AC 15 → 80% hit → avg 14 bludgeoning + grapple
- After Bazgar's turn: Void Ray LA (2 LA) → DC 16 Dex vs Bazgar (70% fail) → avg 23 force (Void-Feeding +1 on 4d10)
- After Sabriel's turn: 0 LA remaining

Expected LA damage this round alongside Void Scream:
- Tentacle on Marwen (if she's alive/conscious): 0.80 × 14 = 11.2 avg
- Void Ray on Bazgar: 0.70 × 23 + 0.30 × 11.5 = 16.1 + 3.45 = 19.6 avg

Combined output this round: Void Scream (84 avg total) + Tentacle (11.2) + Void Ray (19.6) = **~115 avg** across the party. Party total HP entering round 2: 35 + 32 + 44 = 111.

**The party can be statistically expected to have near-zero total HP after one beholder round in round 2 if the party is clustered.** This is consistent with the "meant to lose" design, but the speed (round 2) may preclude meaningful engagement.
