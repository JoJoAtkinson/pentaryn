### Beholder context: Compel Thrall BA interaction

Beholder's **Compel Thrall (1/Turn)** BA: targets one charmed creature within 60 ft;
DC 16 CHA save or moves 30 ft toward beholder's target.
- Thrall CHA 7 (-2), no proficiency (saves: none): needs 18+ to succeed. Fail rate 85%.

Thrall's own **Compelled Movement** BA: no save, always moves 30 ft toward beholder's
target.

These appear to be the same effect from two mechanical perspectives. Both fire in the
same turn = 60 ft of forced movement (30 ft from thrall's BA + 30 ft from beholder's
BA), which is unintended. See FEEL-R280-01 below.

### FEEL-R280-01 (NEW — LOG ONLY)

**Compel Thrall / Compelled Movement redundancy.**
Beholder's Compel Thrall (BA, DC 16 CHA save, 85% forced movement) vs. Thrall's own
Compelled Movement (BA, no save, always moves 30 ft) are two representations of the
same mechanical effect. If the DM runs both in the same turn, the thrall moves 60 ft
(unintended). Clarification needed: does one replace the other, or can both fire?
Suggested resolution: Compelled Movement (thrall BA) fires autonomously when beholder
CANNOT use Compel Thrall BA (beholder stunned, out of range, etc.) — otherwise
Compel Thrall is the active driver and the thrall's BA is suppressed that turn. DM note.

### FEEL-R280-02 (NEW — LOG ONLY)

**hand_axe_weak vs dagger_desperate: no DB priority.**
Both attacks are accessible by verb ("axe" / "attack" / "stab" etc.) and the DB has no
indication of which the thrall prefers. The Dagger is strictly better at every Compass
Edge AC level (+3 vs +1 to hit; 1d4+1 vs 1d4). In practice DMs will always use the
dagger. The hand axe will never fire except accidentally via "attack" (which hits
hand_axe_weak before dagger_desperate alphabetically). Consider making dagger the
primary attack verb if the flavor intent is "thrall grabs whatever is at hand" (in which
case both should have equal odds) — or remove hand_axe_weak from DB if it's only a stat
block formality.

### BUG-THRALL-DAGGER-DB-R280-01 (NEW — LOG ONLY)

See Phase A above. DB `damage_modifier: 2` should be 1. Requires `combat_action_upsert`
in an interactive session.
