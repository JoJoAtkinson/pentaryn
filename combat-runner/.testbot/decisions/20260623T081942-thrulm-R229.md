## Phase B — Generative Playtest

**Scenario:** `thrulm-beholder-tentacle-grapple`  
*"Beholder multiattack → Marwen grappled → Maw advantage verified → Antireality on counter"*

### Setup

Initiative order (fixed-seed dice math):
- **Beholder 18** (DEX +3, roll 15)
- **Marwen 13** (DEX +3, roll 10; tiebreaks Bazgar)
- **Bazgar 13** (DEX +1, roll 12)
- **Sabriel 10** (DEX +2, roll 8)

Beholder goes first — all PCs act after the beholder. Void-Feeding active (+1 to all attacks and damage in chamber).

### Round 1

**Count 20 — Lair Action (Void Eruption):**  
All 3 PCs within 20 ft of shrine, DC 16 DEX save. All fail (Bazgar 15, Marwen 11, Sabriel 18 — Sabriel makes it): 11 force damage to Bazgar and Marwen, 5 to Sabriel.  
- Bazgar: 49 → 38 HP  
- Marwen: 32 → 21 HP  
- Sabriel: 44 → 39 HP

**Beholder (Init 18) — Multiattack:**
1. Tentacle Lash vs Marwen (+7, roll 18 → HIT, AC 15): 13 bludgeoning. Marwen grappled (escape DC 16). Marwen: 21 → 8 HP.
2. Tentacle Lash vs Bazgar (+7, roll 20 → HIT, AC 18): 14 bludgeoning. Bazgar grappled. Bazgar: 38 → 24 HP.
3. Maw vs Marwen (grappled → disadvantage on escape checks) (+7, roll 24 → HIT, AC 15): 24 piercing. Marwen: 8 → 0 HP, unconscious. **Death saves start.**

**Antireality:** Not triggered this turn (beholder wasn't targeted).

**Marwen (Init 13, unconscious):** Death save: roll 12 → SUCCESS (1/3 successes).

**Bazgar (Init 13, grappled):** Can't move. Attacks beholder (2 attacks, +5 to hit vs AC 17).  
Attack 1: Beholder declares Antireality (reaction, pre-roll). Bazgar rolls with **disadvantage**: (9, 5), takes 5+5 = 10 vs AC 17 → MISS. Antireality expended for round.  
Attack 2: Straight roll: 8+5 = 13 vs AC 17 → MISS.  
Bazgar: no damage dealt.

**Sabriel (Init 10):** Uses Lay on Hands to stabilize Marwen (1 HP). Uses attack vs beholder with remaining action: roll 16+5 = 21 vs AC 17 → HIT. 1d8+2+2d8 (Divine Smite, 1st level) ≈ 18 damage. Beholder: 110 → 92 HP.

End R1: Beholder 92 HP | Bazgar 24 HP (grappled) | Marwen 1 HP (prone, grappled) | Sabriel 39 HP.

### Round 2

**Count 20 — Lair Action (Unstable Ground):**  
Target Sabriel: DC 16 DEX save, roll 12+2 = 14 → FAIL. Sabriel falls **prone**.

**Beholder (Init 18) — Multiattack:**  
Antireality resets.  
1. Tentacle Lash vs Marwen (grappled, 1 HP): +7, roll 15 → HIT. 13 damage. Marwen: 0 HP again. Damage while at 0 → 1 death save failure. Now 1 fail / 1 success.
2. Tentacle Lash vs Sabriel (prone, reach 10 ft — attacker beyond 5 ft → **disadvantage**): rolls (14, 7), takes 7+7=14 vs AC 19 → MISS.
3. Maw vs Marwen (0 HP, grappled): +7, roll 23 → HIT. 22 damage while at 0 HP → +1 death save failure. Marwen: 3 fails / 1 success → **DEAD.**

**Beholder Legendary Actions:**  
End of Marwen's turn: Drain Divinity vs Sabriel (divine martial, within 30 ft if Sabriel didn't retreat). DC 16 CHA save. Sabriel CHA +3, roll 8+3 = 11 → FAIL. Sabriel loses her 3rd-level spell slot. Beholder gains 6 THP (total HP: 92+6 = 98 THP). All 3 LAs spent.

End R2: Beholder 92 HP + 6 THP | Bazgar 24 HP (grappled) | Marwen **DEAD** | Sabriel 39 HP (prone, −1 slot).

### Key Observations

**1. Lair action round 1 + beholder first initiative = brutal opener.** Marwen (32 HP) went to 0 before her first turn ever. She took 11 (lair, failed save) + 13 + 24 (multiattack) = 48 damage in the span of one initiative block. This is "unfair rather than lethal" territory — she never had agency. Rolled fair dice, simply lost initiative. Mitigating factor: the beholder went first by design as the apex predator. But for a party-meant-to-lose scenario, having the squishiest PC die before round 1 ends removes a player from fun immediately.  
**Recommendation (log only):** Consider whether Void Eruption lair action should be unavailable round 1 (grant the party a "positioning round" before the shrine's power activates). This is a feel fix, not a mechanical bug.

**2. Legendary Action economy is degenerate vs Sabriel.** Drain Divinity (3 LAs) dominates every other legendary option as long as Sabriel is within 30 ft. Move (1 LA) is never used. Tentacle LA (1 LA) and Void Ray LA (2 LAs) are both completely crowded out. Result: the beholder's legendary economy collapses to a single-option loop. Confirmed in simulation; confirmed in prior logs; still open for Joe's decision.

**3. Antireality functioned correctly post-R224.** Declared pre-roll, imposed disadvantage, consumed for the round, reset next round. No regression detected in this simulation.

**4. Permanent death risk is active.** Disintegration Ray (+7 to hit vs Marwen AC 15 on recharge 5–6) hitting a PC with 32 max HP at any HP ≤ 0 triggers disintegration. Level 5 party has no access to True Resurrection. This means a recharge 5–6 roll at an inopportune moment can permanently remove a player from the campaign. FEEL-003 remains active concern for Compass Edge.  
**Recommendation (log only):** Add a soft-kill variant note to beholder tactics: "If the party is level ≤6, prefer Void Ray over Disintegration Ray — reserve disintegration for the final PC standing." This preserves the terror without guaranteeing permanent PC death.

**5. Maw + grapple combo functions as designed.** Maw advantage on escape checks triggers when target is grappled. (FIX-R220-A confirmed clean — the "saving throw" language is gone, correctly reads "ability checks to escape the grapple.")

**6. Dead-weight ability confirmed: Clay-Shaping.** Never fires in the tested range of 2–4 rounds (requires 1 minute of uninterrupted concentration). Confirmed dead weight. Prior logs addressed.
