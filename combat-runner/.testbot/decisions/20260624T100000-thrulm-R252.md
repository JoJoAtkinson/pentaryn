### Feel Issues

**FEEL-ALPHA-01: Marwen zero-turn kill (first appearance this fire).**
Marwen (Init 10) never acts before going unconscious (Init 19 beholder). Tentacle + Maw avg 36 vs 32 HP. Hit probability with Void-Feeding: Tentacle at +7 vs AC 15 = 65% hit; Maw at +7 vs AC 15 = 65%. Combined Tentacle-then-Maw sequence hitting the same target is contingent, but happens more than half the time. A wizard dying before her first turn is a bad player experience even if the encounter is meant to be deadly. Consider: (a) beholder AI avoids focusing both attacks on the same target R1 unless grapple sets it up, (b) beholder splits Tentacles between Bazgar and Marwen (Maw then goes to grappled Bazgar), reducing Marwen's R1 damage to ~14.

**FEEL-ALPHA-02: Drain Divinity LA economy confirmed lopsided.**
Costs all 3 LA for 1 slot. After Sabriel drops, no valid targets remain (Bazgar is Fighter, Marwen is dead). The beholder's optimal play then becomes Tentacle + Void Ray (3 LA) or Tentacle × 3. Drain Divinity becomes wasted design space once the divine caster is down. This confirms the known design flag. Additionally: the 6 THP gained is irrelevant at 110 HP — the beholder is not surviving on THP alone.

**FEEL-ALPHA-03: Marwen permanent-death risk on Disintegration Ray.**
Marwen max HP 32. Disintegration Ray avg 45 force. Any hit while Marwen is at ≥0 HP from Ray = 45-32=13 excess > 0; but 13 < 32 (max HP), so NOT instant death. However: if Marwen starts at lower HP (R2 she's at 5 after Healing Word), a Ray hit at 5 HP would deal 45-5=40 excess > 32 max HP → **instant death with no death saves, body destroyed.** This is not a theoretical edge case — it's the expected outcome if the beholder fires its Ray at a previously-hurt Marwen. Permanent death (requires Wish or True Resurrection) on a "meant to lose" encounter may be unintended. Flag FEEL-003 (as in scenario notes).

**FEEL-ALPHA-04: Void-Feeding is invisible to the runner.**
All beholder rolls use +6 (DB base); in-chamber correct value is +7. DM must manually add +1 to every beholder attack and damage roll. The cheat sheet already documents this ("runner shows +6; correct in-chamber value is +7"), but it's a persistent tracking gap with real mechanical impact (1 damage per hit, 5% better accuracy). Could be resolved by a separate DB row with a Void-Feeding modifier, or by baking the +1 into the DB values with a note. Either option requires a design choice from Joe.
