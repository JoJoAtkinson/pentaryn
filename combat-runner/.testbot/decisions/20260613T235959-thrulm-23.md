## New findings this fire

### FEEL-F23-01 (new): UG→TL same-target disadvantage trap

**Severity:** Medium — affects every R1 opening where beholder wins initiative and lair fires at init 20.

**Finding:** Using Unstable Ground (lair action, prone on failed DC 16 DEX save) on the same target the beholder plans to Tentacle Lash this turn imposes **disadvantage** on the TL attack, not advantage, if the beholder is hovering at 6–10 ft (Tentacle Lash reach). PHB: prone target at >5 ft = disadvantage on attack rolls. This cuts TL hit rate from 45% to 20% (at standard hover vs AC 18). FIX-EV7-34-A documents this for ranged spell attacks (DR, VR) but not for melee-reach weapons (TL).

**Suggested tactics note (log only):**
> *UG→TL same-target rule (FEEL-F23-01):* Do NOT use Unstable Ground on the same creature you plan to Tentacle Lash this round unless you can reposition to within 5 ft (advantage instead of disadvantage). At 10 ft hover (normal Tentacle Lash range), UG on the same target cuts TL hit rate from 45% to 20%. Either (a) UG a different target from your TL targets, or (b) descend to within 5 ft before TL (spend movement, accept melee-range OA exposure). Optimal R1 play: UG Sabriel (disrupts her approach, different target from Bazgar TL focus), then TL Bazgar at normal 45% hit rate.
