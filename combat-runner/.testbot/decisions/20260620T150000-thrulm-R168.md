### Beholder's Turn (Init 19) — Decision Analysis

**Option A — Disintegration Ray vs Marwen (recharge assumed available round 1):**
- +7 vs AC 15. Needs 8+ = **65% hit**.
- Average damage: 10d8 = 45 force.
- Marwen HP 32 < 45 average: on a hit, Marwen is dropped to 0 **100% of the time** (even minimum 10 damage doesn't drop her, but avg/typical 40+ always does; P(DR < 32) = P(10d8 ≤ 32) ≈ 3%).
- So: **P(Marwen permanently dead round 1) ≈ 0.65 × 0.97 ≈ 63%**.
- On a hit: cannot be raised except true resurrection or wish. Party loses wizard permanently before she acts.
- On a miss (35%): DR on 6-recharge, likely unavailable round 2 (~83% chance it doesn't recharge).

**Option B — Multiattack (TL1 + TL2 + Maw):**

Optimal targeting: both tentacle lashes to set up Maw, with one tentacle on Marwen.

*Tentacle Lash 1 vs Marwen (AC 15):*
- +7. Needs 8+. **Hit rate: 65%**.
- Average damage on hit: 3d6+4 (Void-Feeding +1) = 10.5+4 = 14.5 → **14 bludgeoning**.
- On hit: Marwen grappled (speed 0, escape DC 16; Wizard STR likely -1 to +0, fails ~85% of checks).
- Marwen: 32 → 18 HP (expected avg).

*Tentacle Lash 2 vs Sabriel (AC 19):*
- +7. Needs 12+. **Hit rate: 50%**.
- Average damage: **14 bludgeoning**.
- Sabriel grappled on hit (STR +3 for divine martial, escape DC 16: needs 13+ = 50%).
- Sabriel: 44 → 30 HP (expected avg).

*Maw vs Marwen (grappled, AC 15):*
- +7. Needs 8+. **Hit rate: 65%**.
- Average damage: 4d8+4 = 18+4 = **22 piercing**. (Orphaned "disadvantage on saving throw" clause — STAT-R153-01 — irrelevant; Maw has no save.)
- Marwen: 18 → -4 HP. **DOWN.**

Expected multiattack damage summary:
- Marwen: 0.65×14 + 0.65×22 = 9.1 + 14.3 = **23.4 expected damage** (vs 32 HP)
- Sabriel: 0.50×14 = **7 expected damage** (vs 44 HP)
- Total expected: 30.4 damage / round from multiattack

P(Marwen dropped in one multiattack turn):
- Need TL1 and Maw both to hit AND total damage ≥ 32.
- P(both hit) = 0.65² = **42.25%**.
- When both hit, average = 36.5; P(total < 32 | both hit) ≈ 20% (dice variance below mean).
- **P(Marwen dropped from multiattack) ≈ 42% × 80% ≈ 34%.**

### Decision tree conclusion

| Option | P(Marwen drops R1) | Permanent death? | Grapples? |
|--------|-------------------|-----------------|-----------|
| Disintegration Ray | ~63% | Yes (if dropped) | No |
| Multiattack (TL+TL+Maw) | ~34% | No | Yes (TL1+TL2) |
| Multiattack + DR (impossible; DR is the action, not bonus) | — | — | — |

**Optimal play for an INT 17 beholder:**
- If DR is available and the beholder has no other concerns: DR vs Marwen = 2× higher chance to eliminate the primary caster, and ensures permanent death.
- If the beholder wants grapples for sustained control (dragging PCs into difficult terrain per tactics): multiattack preserves the DR for a follow-up kill shot round 2.
- Either way: Marwen has a 34–63% chance of being incapacitated before she gets a single turn.

The stat block's tactics section says both "Prioritizes disintegration of spellcasters" AND "Grapples with tentacles and drags into difficult terrain." These two strategies are mutually exclusive round 1 (DR uses the action; multiattack uses the action). **The tactics section is ambiguous about which to prefer round 1.** This is a NEW feel issue.

### NEW feel findings — Phase B

**FEEL-R168-01 (NEW):** Round-1 beholder tactics section doesn't specify whether to use DR or multiattack first. Both are optimal in different contexts; the current text points at both simultaneously. Without clearer DM guidance, the beholder will either (a) use DR and permanently kill Marwen round 1 (~63%), or (b) use multiattack and still potentially drop Marwen (~34%). Player-facing consent: Marwen's player should be warned pre-session that their character faces permanent death odds before round 2. This is by design, but the tactics section's ambiguity means the DM may not realize they're making a decision, not following a script. Recommend adding a note in the tactics section: "Round 1 default: multiattack (establishes grapples, saves DR as kill-shot for round 2). If DR is available and Marwen is below 35 HP, switch to DR."
