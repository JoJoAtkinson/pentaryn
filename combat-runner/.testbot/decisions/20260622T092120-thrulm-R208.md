  - **BUG-R156-01 confirmed this fire (sub-scenario A: non-lethal blow).**
  - Self-damage: shrine-touched should take 1d4 psychic damage (avg 2). **Not in DB row.** Runner does not output self-damage. DM must apply manually.
  - Shrine-Touched: 17 → ~15 HP (after avg 2 self-damage). Reaction expended.

**Step 2 — Shrine-Touched's own turn (before being killed).**

With Unstable Form advantage active: multiattack 2× Shrine-Axe vs Sabriel (AC 19, grappled or adjacent).
- Attack with advantage (+4): P(hit AC 19) ≈ 30% without adv. → with advantage: P ≈ 51%.
- Expected hits: ~1.02/2. Expected damage: 1.02 × (4.5+1+4.5) = 1.02 × 10 = **~10.2 necrotic+slashing on Sabriel.**
- Sabriel: 44 → ~34 HP.

Shrine-Touched self-damages 2 psychic on retaliation → effectively 15 HP after its own turn.

**Step 3 — Bazgar or Sabriel attacks for 29 damage.**

Shrine-Touched: 15 HP → **15 - 29 = -14 → 0 HP. Dead.**

Reaction window: "When the derro takes damage from a source it can see." Reaction refreshed at start of shrine-touched's turn (it took its turn in Step 2). Reaction is available.

**Ruling on reaction vs killing blow:**
Per 5e PHB (p. 190): Reactions occur "in response to a trigger." The trigger is TAKING damage, which is the same instant as the HP reduction. The creature can declare its reaction before the HP drop renders it unconscious (this is the same ruling applied to Uncanny Dodge, Shield of Faith, Hellish Rebuke — all can be declared in response to the killing blow). Ruling: **reaction fires on the lethal blow.**

Runner behavior (actual):
- Prompt surfaces again: `oath_breaking_retaliation` trigger fires on damage event.
- Narration: "Pain becomes prophecy — the shrine screams through its vessel." No dice.
- **BUG-R156-01 confirmed (sub-scenario B: lethal blow).**
- Same missing fields as sub-scenario A.

### Phase B findings

**1. BUG-R156-01 — EXPLICITLY CONFIRMED on both sub-scenarios (HIGH, 50+ fires)**

`oath_breaking_retaliation` DB row confirmed missing:
- `to_hit_bonus` (+4 → STR+1+PB+3)
- `damage_dice` (1d8 slashing, 1d8 necrotic — two dice expressions)
- `damage_modifier` (+1 to slashing component)
- `damage_type` (slashing + necrotic dual-type)
- Self-damage rider: 1d4 psychic on self regardless of hit/miss

This scenario was designed as the regression gate for this bug. It **fails** as expected: prompt fires, no roll output.

Fix path: `combat_action_upsert` for `shrine-touched-derro / oath_breaking_retaliation` needs to embed a Shrine-Axe attack spec. However the `reaction` type in the DB schema may not support an `attacks` array — if so, this requires a schema-level fix before the upsert can work. **Needs interactive MCP session.** (Joe decision.)

**2. NEW — BUG-R208-01: Oath-Breaking Retaliation self-damage not in DB (MEDIUM)**

The stat block specifies "Regardless of whether the attack hits, the derro takes 2 (1d4) psychic damage." This self-damage has no DB representation. It is a separate sub-bug from the missing attack dice (BUG-R156-01). Even once BUG-R156-01 is fixed with embedded attack fields, the self-damage rider will still be absent unless a `self_damage` field is added. Distinct enough to track separately.

**3. FEEL-R208-01: Unstable Form advantage never fires when shrine-touched is two-shot (MEDIUM)**

In this trace: shrine-touched triggered Unstable Form on step 1 (28 damage ≥ 10 threshold), took its turn with advantage in step 2, then was killed in step 3 before it could use the empowered turn again. The advantage fired exactly once (its own turn). Against a level-5 party dealing 20–30 damage per PC, the shrine-touched's typical HP window (45 HP, often reaching 0 in 2 PC attacks) means Unstable Form fires on one attack sequence only, then the creature is dead. The trait has high narrative flavor but limited mechanical payoff at this party level because the shrine-touched rarely survives to a second Unstable Form turn. This is a distinct feel note from CS-DRIFT-15 (absence from checklist) — CS-DRIFT-15 is about documentation; this is about whether the trait does work in practice.

**4. FEEL-R208-02: Lethal-blow reaction timing ambiguous at table (LOW)**

The ruling "reaction fires on the blow that kills the creature" is RAW-supportable but requires a DM decision. The runner fires the prompt regardless (because the trigger fires on the damage event, before HP state is checked). This creates a table-awkward moment: the creature that just "died" is making an attack. Currently no timing note exists in the stat block or cheat sheet. Low priority (common ruling), but a one-liner in the stat block's reaction section would eliminate the table question.

**5. Carry-forward confirmed — BUG-R159-01: Ancient Resonance 1d4 psychic rider absent from DB**

Directly related to the psychic self-damage issue in BUG-R208-01. The `ancient_resonance` DB row also has no `extra_damage` field for the on-fail 1d4 psychic rider. Both `ancient_resonance` and `oath_breaking_retaliation` have un-automated psychic components. Thematically consistent: the shrine-touched leaks psychic damage both outward (resonance) and inward (retaliation self-damage) — worth fixing together in one MCP session.

### Beholder LA economy check (secondary observation)

In this encounter slice (shrine-touched focus), beholder had 3 LA at end of Rager's turn. Given Sabriel is revealed as divine martial landing 28-damage smites, optimal LA use would be Drain Divinity (3 LA vs Sabriel) to prevent the killing blow on shrine-touched. This is the first time the simulation suggests **DD as tactically correct** (not just thematically dominant) — the beholder is trying to preserve a shrine-touched thrall by stripping Sabriel's smite slots. DD EV in this context: prevent ~28 damage to shrine-touched (save its life) vs gain 3rd-level THP (6 THP). DD wins here by a wide margin. This partially offsets FEEL-R188-03 (which found DD suboptimal in most other contexts). Add to the tactics note: DD is high-value when shrine-touched allies are threatened by divine smite builds.
