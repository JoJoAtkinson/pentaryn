### FEEL-F61-01 (NEW): `legendary-action-loop` slice prescribed impossible 4-LA sequence

**Finding:** The rotation.yml slice (as written before this fire) said "Move → Tentacle → Void Ray" = 1+1+2 = 4 LAs. The beholder has only 3 per round. Any previous fire that ran this slice was simulating an illegal sequence. **Auto-fixed:** FIX-F61-A changed the slice to Tentacle (1) + Void Ray (2) = 3 LAs.

**Impact:** Prior sims that ran this slice would have over-estimated LA output by 1 "free" repositioning. Damage totals are unaffected (Move deals 0 damage), but any positioning assumption from "free Move LA" is invalid — the beholder must choose between moving *or* using an offensive LA, not both.

### FEEL-F61-02 (NEW): Void Ray / Disintegration Ray naming confusion risk

**Finding:** Both "Void Ray" (LA, DC 16 DEX save, 4d10 force) and "Disintegration Ray" (main action, attack roll +7, 10d8 force + perma-death) are ranged force attacks with "Ray" in the name. In live play, a DM switching between them mid-round may:
- Apply the wrong resolution mechanic (save vs attack roll)
- Confuse damage dice (4d10 = avg 22 vs 10d8 = avg 45)

The distinction is significant: DR at avg 45 force is 2× VR damage and can permadeath; VR at avg 22 is a pressure tool. Mixing them up in either direction is a table error with major consequence.

**Recommendation (log only — Joe decides):** Either add an explicit disambiguation tag to the LA description ("Void Ray is a SAVE, not an attack roll") or rename one of them. Example: "Void Lance (LA)" or "Disintegration Beam (action)." Naming fix would also update the cheat sheet.

**Severity:** Low (requires DM error to manifest). Design review for Joe.

### FEEL-F57-01 (carry): Move LA confirmed dead weight, fire #61

**Observation:** The `legendary-action-loop` slice was specifically designed to include Move LA to evaluate its usefulness. Removing it from the budget (arithmetic fix) didn't change the damage output at all. Move LA repositions up to 30 ft — the same distance as Shrine-Drift (bonus action). Replacing Move LA with either a Tentacle (1 LA) or absorbing it into a Void Ray (2 LA) is always superior. Move LA provides zero damage, no grapple, no condition. The beholder's normal fly speed (30 ft, hover) covers the same ground on its own turn without costing an LA.

**Conclusion:** Move LA has now been confirmed dead weight across fires #57–#61 in every simulated round. The LA slot it occupies is always better spent on Tentacle or banked for Void Ray. Joe's decision: remove Move LA or give it a secondary effect (e.g., Move LA repositions AND ends one condition on the beholder, or allows passing through a creature without provocation beyond Shrine-Drift's cost).
