### Key interactions observed

**Frightened cascade on Bazgar:**

Bazgar (Battlemaster Fighter) is the party's primary damage dealer. Frightened imposes:
- Disadvantage on all attack rolls while beholder is in line of sight
- Cannot willingly move closer to the beholder

The beholder has truesight 120 ft and is in line of sight from any position in the chamber. Bazgar cannot break line of sight (the chamber is a vault, not a maze). Consequence: Bazgar attacks with disadvantage for the entire duration of Frightened (up to 1 minute), reducing his hit probability from 50% to 25% vs AC 17. His expected DPR with Battlemaster (2 GS attacks +4, 1d10+2 avg 7.5 each) drops from ~7.5 damage/round to ~3.75.

To end Frightened: Bazgar saves at end of each of his turns (DC 16 WIS, +0 = needs 16, P = 25%). Expected duration: 4 turns = 4 rounds. In a 2–3 round fight, Bazgar is functionally removed from combat for the entire encounter.

**Shrine proximity as geometric trap:**

The beholder hovers at altitude above the altar, using Shrine-Drift (BA) to reposition within the shrine chamber. To attack it with melee (Sabriel, Bazgar), PCs must close to within 5–10 ft of the altar. The altar is the center of the shrine proximity zone. This forces the melee-capable PCs into the highest disadvantage zone for VS exactly when VS fires. The shrine proximity rule interacts adversarially with the "close to fight beholder" imperative: approach to engage = worsen your save against VS.

Marwen (ranged, spells) is the only PC who can maintain distance (30+ ft) and avoid shrine proximity — but she has only 32 HP and the beholder targets casters preferentially.

**Void Scream timing:**

VS can fire on round 1 if the beholder wins initiative (17 vs party range 8–14). P(beholder goes first) ≈ 55%. In a beholder-first scenario, VS fires before the party has taken any action. In a party-first scenario (P ≈ 45%), Marwen can cast Fireball or other AoE — but she likely needs cover, limiting her own clustering behavior.

**Recharge and second use:**

VS recharges on a 6 (1/6 = 17% per round). Expected rounds until recharge: 6. This fight ends in 2–3 rounds; VS fires at most once with negligible P(second use). The recharge-6 label is correctly calibrated for this power level.

**LA interaction during VS round:**

When beholder uses VS (action), it retains:
- Bonus action: Shrine-Drift (reposition freely)
- Legendary actions: 3 available for end-of-PC-turn use

Post-VS optimal LA: Tentacle×3 on surviving PCs. After VS drops Sabriel and brings Bazgar to 1 HP, the beholder has 3 LAs to confirm kills (Tentacle on 1-HP Bazgar = guaranteed kill) and damage Marwen further. Round 1 ends with 2 PCs down and Marwen at 9 HP (or worse under shrine proximity).

## Phase B findings summary

**FEEL-VS-R271-01 (NEW): VS + shrine proximity + beholder initiative = 94 expected damage in round 1 before party acts. Structural TPK before first PC turn.**

When the beholder wins initiative (P ≈ 55%) and all 3 PCs approach the altar, VS + shrine proximity disadvantage delivers 94 expected total damage against a 125 HP party. With lair action preceding (16 expected force damage), the party enters the VS round pre-weakened. Sabriel (44 HP) is expected to drop on failed save. Bazgar (49 HP) takes ~32 expected (leaves him critically wounded or dead depending on rolls). Marwen (32 HP) takes ~31 expected (likely drops or nears 0).

This isn't feel-unfair in the "no counterplay" sense — the party CHOSE to approach. But the counterplay is invisible: "don't cluster near the shrine" is not communicated to players who are trying to engage the beholder hovering above it. The shrine proximity disadvantage is listed in the stat block (the "within 10 feet of the shrine have disadvantage" line on VS) but is not in the cheat sheet's Round-by-Round section, making it easy to miss until VS fires.

*Joe's options:* (1) Add shrine-proximity VS reminder to cheat sheet Round-by-Round (this is CS-OMIT-R263-01, already tracked — mark as HIGH priority given this fire's quantification). (2) Give party a warning tell before VS fires (e.g., beholder hums/charges for one round when VS is ready, allowing retreat). (3) Keep as-is — the encounter is designed for loss; instant TPK is an acceptable outcome.

**FEEL-VS-R271-02 (NEW): Frightened rider from VS removes Bazgar from melee for the entire combat duration.**

Bazgar's optimal role is melee DPR (2× 1d10+2 attacks + Battlemaster maneuvers). Frightened + cannot-close-in removes him from that role entirely. P(remove Frightened per turn) = 25%. Expected duration: 4 rounds. In a 2–3 round fight, Frightened is effectively permanent. Bazgar becomes a crossbow user with disadvantage on attacks, dealing ~2 expected damage per round. This is worse than useless — it costs Sabriel a Lay on Hands action to try to stabilize him before the condition even matters.

*This synergizes destructively with FEEL-LA-R270-01 (Move LA dead weight) and FEEL-R263-02 (hover = no approach) — the three combine to create a full-party control lockout with no L5 counterplay.*

*Joe's options:* (1) Reduce VS FRIGHTENED duration to 1 round (no repeated save mechanic). (2) Allow Bazgar an Athletics check to "grit through" and advance despite Frightened (once per encounter). (3) Keep as-is — per design, party is meant to lose.

**FEEL-VS-R271-03 (NEW): CS-OMIT-R263-01 should be upgraded to HIGH priority.**

The omission of shrine proximity disadvantage from the Round-by-Round checklist is most likely to cause mid-combat errors because it's the factor that tips VS from "survivable AoE" (33 avg damage / PC) to "round-1 wipe" (31 avg with disadvantage). Without the checklist reminder, a DM running this encounter at speed may forget the shrine proximity rule and apply flat saves — making VS deal 67% of expected damage and giving the party a survivable first round. The cheat sheet's current description is:

> "Is the Beholder using Clay-Shaping?" and other checks but VS proximity is absent.

This creates a critical table-error window. Recommend Joe adds: `[CHECK] Are any PCs within 10 ft of shrine? → Apply DISADVANTAGE to their Void Scream WIS save.`
