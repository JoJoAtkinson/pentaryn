### Findings

**FEEL-R302-01: Drain Divinity vs Tentacle+Void Ray is a real DM choice (not degenerate).**

Against Sabriel (divine martial): Drain Divinity (3 LA) strips highest slot per fail (DC 16 CHA, Sabriel +2 → 65% fail rate). Round 1 expected: 0.65 of Sabriel's 3rd-level slot gone + 12 beholder THP gained (if 3rd slot = 3×2 = 6 THP). That trades ~19.4 expected Void Ray damage for stripping healing resources + THP.

Against divine casters specifically, Drain Divinity trades higher immediate party damage for faster resource drain of the primary healer. Neither is strictly dominant — it depends on whether Sabriel smites or heals. For parties where the divine martial is the only healer (as in Compass Edge), Drain Divinity becomes increasingly dominant by round 2–3 when Bazgar and Marwen have taken damage.

**Confirmed carry-forward:** The 3-LA all-or-nothing Drain Divinity cost remains a design issue. Joe's decision on 2-LA vs 3-LA cost still pending (R285, R301).

---

**FEEL-R302-02: Void Ray naming clarity — marginally ambiguous, low priority.**

Void Ray (LA, DC 16 DEX save, 4d10+1 force) and Disintegration Ray (action, +9 attack roll, 10d8 force) share "Ray" in name. In practice, the LA table in the cheat sheet and the initiative-order context make these distinguishable at the table. However, the SAVE DCs section of the cheat sheet labels Disintegration Ray as "+9 to hit" (attack roll) without explicitly marking it as NOT a save. A single-line clarifier in the SAVE DCs table — e.g., "Beholder | Disintegration Ray | **attack roll** +9 | force (no save; 10d8, disintegrates on kill)" — would prevent new-DM confusion.

Logged as feel note; not auto-fixable (cheat sheet edit, not a stat-block arithmetic error).

**FEEL-R302-03: Marwen grapple doom loop — Misty Step counterplay not surfaced for DMs.**

Marwen grappled by first tentacle at 75% probability per multiattack. Escape via action: Acrobatics +2 vs DC 16 → 35% success. Likely cannot escape. However, Misty Step (BA, 2nd-level slot) immediately breaks the grapple and repositions 60 ft. This is the intended counterplay but the cheat sheet says only "Cleric? → Beholder wants to Drain Divinity from you. Position defensively, heal from range." Marwen (the target, not a Cleric) gets no guidance.

Recommend adding one line to cheat sheet IF-YOUR-PC-ASKS section: "Wizard? → You will be grappled. Misty Step (bonus action, 2nd slot) breaks any grapple immediately and repositions you 60 ft."

Logged as feel note; not auto-fixable without cheat sheet edit (Phase C log only).

**FEEL-R302-04: Bazgar's Battlemaster maneuvers — all offensive maneuvers blocked by beholder immunities.**

- Trip Attack → beholder immune to prone ✗
- Menacing Attack → beholder immune to frightened ✗
- Disarming Attack → beholder has no weapons ✗

- Goading Attack → useful (disadvantage on beholder vs non-Bazgar targets)
