**Feel issues (log only):**

1. **DW output is negligible vs L5 party.** DW Hand Axe +2 vs AC 18 = 25% hit × 3.5 avg = 0.88 expected per swing. Two DW dealing ~3.5 combined expected damage per round. The party takes ~30× more expected damage from the Shardcaller than from both DW combined. The DW are functioning as intended (speedbump), but the fight feels like a "kill the guys blocking the door" exercise rather than a meaningful encounter — 500 XP for a L5 party of 3 is trivial (L5 threshold ~6,750 XP for Hard).

   **Joe to decide:** Is the patrol encounter (E2) intentionally trivial as a resource gate before the beholder? If so, no change needed. If it's meant to feel like a real fight: add 1–2 Ragers or replace one DW with a Shrine-Touched to spike the threat level.

The patrol currently runs with a Shardcaller in the sergeant role (CR 2) which over-powers the patrol slightly. Joe to decide: author a CR 1/2 Derro Sergeant or leave Shardcaller as the patrol leader.
