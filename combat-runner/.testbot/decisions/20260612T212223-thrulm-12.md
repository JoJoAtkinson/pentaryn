## Phase B — Manual playtest: drain-divinity-vs-sabriel

*Rotation.yml slice 4. Tests: DD LA economy, slot attrition over 3 rounds, whether 3-LA cost is mechanically sound.*

---

### Encounter setup

- Beholder HP 110, AC 17, Void-Feeding active (+1 atk/dmg → +7 total attack)
- Bazgar HP 49, AC 18, STR +4, DEX −1 — Orc Fighter (Battlemaster) 5
- Marwen HP 32, AC 15, INT +4 — Wizard 5 (assumed: 4×L1, 3×L2, 2×L3 arcane slots)
- Sabriel HP 44, AC 19 — divine martial 5 (roster: "heavy armor + shield")
  - Assumed spell slots (rotation description): 4×L1, 3×L2, 2×L3
  - CHA +2 assumed (rotation description)

---

### Rotation slice factual error: slot count vs. stated loss expectation

The rotation description reads:
> "If all fail: Sabriel loses **three 3rd-level slots**; beholder gains **18 temp HP**."

But the stated slot table in the same description says Sabriel has **two** 3rd-level slots. After 2 successful DD failures, the third DD targets the highest **remaining** slot, which is L2 (not L3). Correct expectations:

| DD round | Target slot | P(fail) | Beholder THP on success |
|----------|-------------|---------|------------------------|
| Round 1  | L3 (of 2)   | 65%     | 6                      |
| Round 2  | L3 (last)   | 65%     | 6                      |
| Round 3  | L2 (of 3)   | 65%     | 4                      |

**Corrected expected THP:** 0.65×6 + 0.65×6 + 0.65×4 = **10.4 expected THP** (not 18).
**Corrected max THP (all 3 fail):** 6+6+4 = **16** (not 18).

The "18 THP" figure in the rotation assumes 3×L3 slot losses, which requires a 3rd-level slot the character doesn't have. The rotation needs to be corrected.

---

### Save probability distribution — 3 rounds of DD

DC 16 CHA save at +2: fail on d20 ≤ 13, P(fail) = 0.65.

| # successful strips | P | Expected slots stripped |
|----|---------|---|
| 0 of 3 | 0.35³ = 4.3% | 0 |
| 1 of 3 | 3 × 0.65 × 0.35² = 23.9% | 1 |
| 2 of 3 | 3 × 0.65² × 0.35 = 44.2% | 2 |
| 3 of 3 | 0.65³ = 27.5% | 3 |

**E(strips) = 1.95 slots** over 3 rounds. Sabriel's L3 smite pool (2 slots) is ~90% depleted after 3 rounds.

---

### LA opportunity cost — DD vs Void Ray

Each DD round burns all 3 LA. Alternative: 3 rounds × 3 LA → Void Ray (2 LA) = 4.5 effective Void Rays.

**Void Ray vs Sabriel (DC 16 DEX, Sabriel DEX ≈ +0 → 75% fail rate):**

| Per-use avg damage | × 4 Void Rays (9 LA equiv) |
|----|---|
| 22 × 0.75 + 11 × 0.25 = 19.25 | 77 dmg → Sabriel 44 HP → downed |

**DD over 3 rounds:** expected 1.95 slots stripped, 10 THP gained, Sabriel still standing.

**Verdict:** VR does more work per LA against this party. DD is correct only when the fight is expected to last 4+ rounds and slot attrition (preventing R3+ smites) matters more than raw kill speed. Against a 3-PC level-5 party with design intent to lose by R3, DD is the thematically correct choice but mechanically slower than pure damage pressure. Log only — the design note "use DD early (R1–R2) while the target's slot pool is full" in the tactics is sound; this simulation confirms it.

### NEW FINDING: DD targets arcane spell slots — Marwen is a valid target

The DD text: **"one creature within 30 feet that has spell slots, divine favor, or clerical powers."** This includes Marwen (Wizard 5 with arcane spell slots). The tactics section exclusively frames DD as anti-divine ("It hates sanctified weapons… focuses on Drain Divinity to remove their healing and buffs. Against Clerics/Paladins:"). This framing is incomplete.

**DD vs Marwen (arcane) comparison:**

| Target | Highest slot | THP on success | Slot impact |
|--------|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| Sabriel (divine) | L3 (smite) | 6 | Prevents +2d8 smite rider (~9 dmg) |
| Marwen (arcane) | L3 (Fireball) | 6 | Prevents 8d6 AoE (~28 dmg to beholder) |

Stripping Marwen's L3 Fireball slot preempts 28 avg fire damage (unhresisted by the beholder) vs preventing one smite rider. **DD vs Marwen before she casts Fireball is higher value than DD vs Sabriel after she smites.** The DD protocol should include a branch: *if Marwen has L3 slots remaining and has not yet cast Fireball this combat, DD Marwen first.*

CHA save for Marwen: Wizard CHA is typically +0 to −1. At +0, P(fail vs DC 16) = 75%. Even higher strip rate than Sabriel.

---

### R1 DD + hover-height conflict (reinforces A12-06)

The "hold all 3 LA → DD post-Sabriel" protocol assumes Sabriel is within 30 ft of the beholder when DD fires (end of Sabriel's R1 turn). Standard beholder position: hovering 40–60 ft above altar. Sabriel in melee range of the beholder (10 ft) would be 40–60 ft up — above the altar floor. Ground-based Sabriel (altar floor) is 40–60 ft below the beholder → DD range exceeded.

**Result:** The flagship LA protocol (R1 DD post-Sabriel) is range-blocked at default beholder positioning. This cannot execute as written without the beholder descending to ≤30 ft altitude before Sabriel's turn. This changes the beholder's R1 positioning decision: either start at 30 ft or lower (multiattack range is 10 ft, tentacles reach 10 ft → to melee, beholder must be within 10 ft of a target → ≤10 ft altitude over the ground PC). From that position DD is in range. So: if the beholder makes its R1 multiattack from melee range (≤10 ft from the ground PCs), DD is in range (10 ft < 30 ft). The DD protocol is implicitly conditioned on the beholder descending to melee range for the multiattack. This is physically consistent but not spelled out. The tactics text should add: *"For DD to reach Sabriel after her turn, the beholder must be hovering at ≤30 ft altitude (i.e., within tentacle/maw reach of at least one ground PC). Multiattack and DD are compatible only from this lower hover position."*

## Feel issues this fire

### FEEL-F12-01: rotation.yml slice "drain-divinity-vs-sabriel" factual error — "three 3rd-level slots"

Sabriel's stated slot table (in the same slice) has 2 L3 slots, not 3. The expected THP figure (18) is derived from 3×L3 losses; corrected to 16 max (2×L3 + 1×L2 if all 3 fail). Slice description needs correction. **Suggest:**
- Change "two 3rd-level" to "two 3rd-level" in the description (already correct in count, wrong in the "If all fail" outcome)
- Change "Sabriel loses three 3rd-level slots; beholder gains 18 temp HP" → "Sabriel loses both 3rd-level slots and one 2nd-level slot; beholder gains 16 temp HP at most"

### FEEL-F12-02: DD vs arcane slots not in tactics text

Mechanics cover all spellcasters but the tactics exclusively frame DD as anti-divine. Marwen's L3 Fireball (unhresisted by beholder, ~28 avg damage to full party) is worth stripping before it fires. Suggested tactics addendum:
> *"DD is valid against ANY spellcaster (arcane or divine). If Marwen has a L3 slot intact and has not yet cast Fireball this combat, prioritize DD vs Marwen over DD vs Sabriel — preventing 28-damage area fire is higher value than preventing one smite rider."*

### FEEL-F12-03: DD 30-ft range physically blocked at beholder's default hover height (reinforces A12-06)

Confirmed through simulation that the canonical R1 DD protocol requires the beholder to descend to melee hover range (≤10 ft above ground PCs). This is consistent with the R1 multiattack protocol (tentacles are 10-ft reach) but is undocumented. A DM running the beholder at 40 ft hover (flavored as "floating above the altar") cannot fire DD at all.
