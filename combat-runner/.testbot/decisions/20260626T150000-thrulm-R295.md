### BUG-ANTIREALITY-REACTION-KIND-R295-01 (NEW)

`antireality` DB row has `reaction_kind: "buff"`. This is the same incorrect classification as
`oath_breaking_retaliation` (open item #8 / BUG-OBR-REACTION-KIND-R293-01). Antireality imposes

(e.g., during the beholder's own turn rather than on incoming attacks).

runner's enum supports before fixing. Fix path: `combat_action_upsert`. Log only (requires MCP).

**NOTE: DB uses `save_ability: "str"` for the grapple escape.** The stat block's "(escape DC 16)"
is homebrew flat-DC (non-standard 5e). Standard 5e grapple escape uses Athletics (STR) OR
Acrobatics (DEX) — not a pure STR saving throw. The `save_ability: "str"` field means the runner
will offer only "STR save" not "Athletics or Acrobatics skill check." This is logged as
AUTOMATION-GAP-GRAPPLE-ESCAPE-R295-01 below.

**Dice math — multiattack targeting Marwen (nearest caster, AC 15):**

DB attack bonuses are +6 (pre-Void-Feeding). In chamber, +1 applies → effective +7. Assuming the
DM adds +1 manually per the cheat sheet reminder.

| Attack | To-Hit | Hit% | Avg Dmg | EV |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Tentacle Lash 1 vs Marwen (AC 15) | 8+ | 65% | 14.5 | 9.4 |
| Tentacle Lash 2 vs Bazgar (AC 18) | 11+ | 50% | 14.5 | 7.25 |
| Maw vs Marwen (AC 15, grappled) | 8+ | 65% | 22 | 14.3 |

*Assumes Tentacle 1 targets Marwen (grapples her), Tentacle 2 targets Bazgar, Maw follows up on
grappled Marwen. Maw's grapple-escape-disadvantage clause is live but doesn't affect the hit roll.*

**One-round KO probability for Marwen:**
P(Tentacle 1 hits AND Maw hits) = 0.65 × 0.65 = **42%**. If both land: 14.5 + 22 = 36.5 > 32 HP
→ Marwen to death saves. This is a first-action KO of a party member with no preceding conditions.

Sabriel's 25-pt Lay on Hands pool becomes critical immediately: one stabilize (1 HP) or a 25-point
heal. Either way, Sabriel's primary resource is committed to Marwen by round 1.

**Grapple success probability:**
Marwen STR modifier est. -1, no proficiency. DC 16 STR "save": needs 17+ → 20% success.
**80% chance Marwen is grappled after Tentacle 1 hits.** P(grappled after Tentacle 1) = 0.65 × 0.80 = 52%.

**Maw grapple-escape-disadvantage analysis:**
If Marwen survives (tentacle hit but Maw missed, or low damage rolls), her round 2 best escape
option is Acrobatics with DEX: DEX +3 (Wizard est.), disadvantage applied by Maw.
P(escape via Acrobatics with disadvantage against DC 16) = P(lower die ≥ 13) = 1 - P(both dice < 13)²
= 1 - (12/20)² = 1 - 0.36 = ... actually P(succeed with disadvantage) = P(max(d1,d2) ≥ 13) where
we take the LOWER — so P(both ≥ 13) = (8/20)² = 0.16. Only **16% escape chance per round.**

Marwen escaping the grapple costs her action (instead of a spell) AND she's still at low HP.
The practical effect: Marwen is removed from combat offense for 2-4 rounds minimum, or until dead.

**Maw mechanic feel note:** The disadvantage on grapple escape is largely irrelevant against the
squishiest party member (Marwen 32 HP). If Tentacle + Maw both connect, Marwen is at 0 HP and
doesn't attempt escape. The mechanic matters most against durable targets (Bazgar 49 HP, Sabriel 44
HP) where the creature might survive both hits. Against Marwen, the Maw's special is cosmetic.
This design tension is noted at FEEL-GRAPPLE-MAW-MARWEN-R295-01 below.

### Step 4 — `[type: "-15"]` → beholder takes 15 HP

HP: 110 - 15 = 95.

**Antireality trigger analysis (regression gate for BUG-F7-02):**
Antireality trigger: `event: "action_executed"`, scope: "self". This should fire when an incoming
attack against the beholder is declared. BUT: `-15` is a raw damage shorthand — it is NOT an attack
declaration. It is direct HP subtraction. 

Whether Antireality fires depends on runner implementation:
- If the runner fires `action_executed` on ALL incoming damage (including raw `-N` inputs), Antireality prompts. ✓
- If the runner only fires `action_executed` when a PC uses an attack action through their tab, Antireality is bypassed by the `-15` shorthand. ✗

In the scenario script, the `-15` represents "PC's attack landed for 15 damage" — but the runner
sees this as a tab-typed damage input, not a structured attack declaration from a PC's tab. The
Antireality `action_executed` trigger was designed to fire when the beholder detects an incoming
attack (pre-roll), giving the DM the chance to declare disadvantage. A raw damage input has no
"pre-roll" moment — the damage is already resolved.

**Regression gate status:** This scenario WILL FAIL to verify the BUG-F7-02 fix properly, because
the test input (`-15`) bypasses the attack-declaration event entirely. The scenario should be
rewritten to use the PC's tab for the attack (e.g., `switch_tab: bazgar`, then `type: "sword"` or
equivalent) so the runner can fire the `action_executed` event before resolving damage. As written,
the scenario can only verify that HP subtracts correctly — not that Antireality fires.

This is a new scenario design gap: SCENARIO-BUG-R295-01 (see below).

- 1 LA tentacle (1 LA spent, 2 remaining)

Remaining LA optimal spend:

or void ray. This confirms the ongoing open item #4 (Drain Divinity 3-LA all-in):

Given the optimal opener: if the beholder uses its best round-1 strategy (LA tentacle + LA void ray),
**Drain Divinity is incompatible**. The DM faces a forced choice every round:
- Drain Divinity (3 LA, removes a divine ability, gains THP) — OR —
- Tentacle (1 LA) + Void Ray (2 LA) — higher damage EV against non-divine targets

**New feel note:** Drain Divinity vs Tentacle+VoidRay is a skill-testing decision for the DM, but
the math strongly favors Tentacle+VoidRay (~21 EV damage) over Drain Divinity (~THP 4-10 + slot
drain). The tactical dominance is not total (Drain Divinity removes healing, which can win wars of
attrition), but the EV case for Drain Divinity is weak unless Sabriel is casting Cure Wounds every
round. This extends open item #4.

**AUTOMATION-GAP-GRAPPLE-ESCAPE-R295-01 (NEW):**

Beholder multiattack DB rows use `save_ability: "str"` for grapple escape. The runner will prompt
"DC 16 STR save" to escape. Standard 5e grapple escape is an Athletics (STR) OR Acrobatics (DEX)
**skill check** vs grappler's Athletics (or in this case the homebrew flat DC 16). The runner only
offering a STR path prevents Marwen (DEX-based Wizard) from using her stronger Acrobatics.

Marwen DEX +3 (est.), Acrobatics proficiency possible → Acrobatics might be +3 or +6. Even without
proficiency, DEX+3 (vs DC 16, needs 13+, 40% success) is better than STR-1 (needs 17+, 20% success).
The runner's `"str"` field halves Marwen's already-slim escape chance.

Fix path: change `save_ability` from `"str"` to a choice prompt (e.g., `"str_or_dex_skill"`) if
runner supports it, or document in stat block that escape uses Athletics OR Acrobatics. Requires
`combat_action_upsert`. Log only.

**SCENARIO-BUG-R295-01 (NEW): Scenario `thrulm-beholder-tentacle-grapple` cannot verify BUG-F7-02 fix.**

Step 4 uses `-15` (raw damage input) instead of a PC attack declaration. The Antireality trigger
(`event: "action_executed"`) requires the beholder to see an incoming attack before the roll.
A raw `-15` has no pre-roll moment — it is resolved damage, not a declared attack.

**Result:** The scenario cannot distinguish between (a) Antireality correctly fired and the DM
accepted disadvantage but the PC still hit, or (b) Antireality never fired at all and the beholder
simply took 15 damage uncontested.

**Proposed fix for the scenario script:** Insert a PC attack sequence before the damage step:
```yaml
- {kind: switch_tab, slug: bazgar}           # or PC stub
- {kind: type, tab: bazgar, text: "attack"}  # declare attack → triggers action_executed
- {kind: switch_tab, slug: beholder-thrulm}  # back to beholder
- {kind: type, tab: beholder-thrulm, text: "-15"}  # record damage result
```
Or, add an `expect_watch` for `antireality` before the `-15` step to make the test assertion explicit.

Until fixed, the scenario can confirm HP subtraction (pass condition 4 from notes) but cannot
verify Antireality timing. Scenario is **partial pass** at best.

Priority: MEDIUM — this is a regression gate for a fixed bug; the gap weakens confidence in the fix.

**FEEL-GRAPPLE-MAW-MARWEN-R295-01 (NEW): Maw's grapple-escape-disadvantage is cosmetic against
the party's squishiest member.**

Against Marwen (32 HP), if both Tentacle Lash 1 and Maw hit (42% probability), she drops to 0 HP
and never attempts a grapple escape — the disadvantage is never applied. Against Bazgar (49 HP)
or Sabriel (44 HP), a similar hit sequence leaves them at 12-20 HP remaining, and the disadvantage
is meaningful (8% → 16% escape chance with DEX/Acrobatics vs DC 16).

**The Maw mechanic penalizes the wrong targets at this party level.** It fires most meaningfully
against durable targets, but the beholder's tactical priority (kill the caster first) means the
Maw always lands on Marwen — the one target where the mechanic is irrelevant.

Design recommendation (log for Joe): Consider adding a secondary Maw trigger that fires on
grappled targets regardless of HP — e.g., "the beholder treats grappled targets as restrained for
purpose of the Maw attack" (advantage on attack roll vs grappled target). This would replace the
escape-disadvantage rider with something that fires every time the Maw targets a grappled creature,
making the mechanic visible and impactful on every use.

Priority: LOW (feel/design).

---

**FEEL-LA-DRAINANOMALY-R295-01 (NEW): Drain Divinity 3-LA cost incompatible with Tentacle+VoidRay
in all standard combat rounds.**

Drain Divinity costs all 3 LA. Optimal round-1 LA spend is tentacle (1 LA) + void ray (2 LA) for
~21 EV damage. Drain Divinity (~THP 4-10 + spell slot drain) only exceeds this when the party has a
dedicated healer burning 3rd-level slots per round — which requires Sabriel to forgo all offense.

In practice, Drain Divinity is only worthwhile:
- Round 1, before any LA tentacle is spent (requires beholder not to use tentacle LA round 1)
- OR when the party has a healer healing allies rather than dealing damage

This confirms and extends open item #4. The 3-LA all-in cost doesn't just feel bad — it is
mechanically dominated by Tentacle+VoidRay in ~80% of realistic combat states. Recommend: reduce
Drain Divinity to 2 LA (competes with Void Ray as an option) or add a cooldown (1/round) so DM
can split the budget without losing Tentacle LA access.

Priority: MEDIUM (systemic feel issue; affects most combats with Sabriel or divine casters).
