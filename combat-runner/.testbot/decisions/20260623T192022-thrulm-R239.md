### Key Findings

**1. Scenario gap — BUG-R218-02 not regression-gated (FEEL-R239-03):** `thrulm-void-scream-aoe` does not set `max_llm_fallback: 0`. The LLM can paper over the missing frightened rider in the DB. Tests (2) and (3) are not reliable regression gates until this is added. This is the same structural problem as FEEL-R238-01 for the disintegration scenario.

**2. Round-1 wipe confirmed (FEEL-R239-01 — deepens FEEL-R227-01):** In the exact scenario described (all 3 PCs converging), the lair action (init 20) + Void Scream (init 17) fires before any PC. Combined avg output puts Marwen at 0 HP and Bazgar/Sabriel critically low, both Frightened. Zero-counterplay window is structurally guaranteed by initiative ordering: lair fires at init 20, beholder fires at init 17, PCs fire at 13/11/9. This is worse than FEEL-R227-01's framing ("same round") because the exact sequencing is initiative-structural, not just "possible."

**3. Shrine-proximity disadvantage runner gap (FEEL-R239-02):** If PCs are within 10 ft of shrine, VS WIS save is at disadvantage. The beholder hovers near the altar; PCs converging means near-shrine is the natural encounter geometry. The runner cannot infer PC positioning and will not apply disadvantage automatically. DM must pre-check each PC's position at the moment VS fires. In an overwhelming R1 sequence, this is a second table-state judgment call under pressure. The cheat sheet VS entry documents this correctly but the runner cannot assist.

**4. Frightened duration + catch-22 (FEEL-R239-04):** Frightened prevents approach. PCs shake on DC 16 Wis per turn. With Bazgar WIS +0 (~4 turn duration) and Sabriel WIS +2 (~3 turn duration), the party is effectively locked out of melee for the remainder of a fight they are likely to lose in 2 rounds. The condition is not "feel bad" — it's mechanically irrelevant by the time it expires because the party will be at 0 HP. However, it does prevent the "tactical retreat" scenario: Frightened PCs can't move closer, so they also can't pick up their fallen ally or reach the exit near the altar. Retreat is geometrically blocked until the condition breaks.

**5. Legendary action economy after VS (confirmed):** Drain Divinity is not optimal on any turn after VS has already fired. Tentacle + Void Ray (1+2 LA) consistently outperforms DD (3 LA) for all party compositions. Confirms FEEL-R228-01 and FEEL-R238-02 at this scenario angle.
