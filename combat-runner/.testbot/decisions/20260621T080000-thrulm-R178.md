### NEW finding: LA strategy shifts when Marwen dies round 1

R177 established: **P(Marwen dead before her turn, permanently) ≈ 63%** from Disintegration Ray on round 1 (FEEL-R177-01 / FEEL-R176-01). This is the most likely round-1 outcome. The `legendary-action-loop` slice has not previously analyzed what happens to LA economy once Marwen is gone.

**When Marwen is dead (63% of encounters):**

Marwen's initiative slot (12) is empty. LA options after PC turns:

- Tentacle (1 LA) on Sabriel (AC 19): +7 vs AC 19. Needs **12+. 45% hit rate.**
  Expected damage: 0.45 × 14 = **6.3 avg** (vs 9.1 when targeting Marwen — **−31%**)
- Void Ray (2 LA) on Bazgar (DEX +0): unchanged — **20.0 avg**.

LA total with Marwen dead: 6.3 + 20.0 = **26.3 avg** (vs 29.1 with Marwen alive — **−10%**).

**Drain Divinity comparison when Marwen is dead (all 3 LA):**
- Target: Sabriel (divine martial, 3rd-level slots remaining). DC 16 CHA save.
  Sabriel CHA estimated +2–3: needs 13–14+ to save. **60–65% fail rate.**
- On fail: Sabriel loses highest remaining spell slot. Beholder gains 2 × slot_level THP.
  For a 3rd-level slot: 6 THP to beholder; Sabriel loses her highest healing/smite slot.

**LA break-even analysis:**
- Tentacle+VoidRay: 26.3 avg direct damage per round.
- Drain Divinity: ~0 direct damage + 6 THP + 1 spell slot removed from Sabriel.

At Bazgar HP 49 and Sabriel HP 44 (both near full), 26.3 direct damage per round is far more efficient at reducing PC HP to zero. Drain Divinity wins only if Sabriel's spell slots are doing more than 26.3 damage/healing equivalent per round — possible if she's upcast smiting or spending 3rd-level slots on Aura of Vitality.

**Conclusion:** Post-Marwen-death, Tentacle+VoidRay is still dominant against raw HP. But the efficiency gap closes: 26.3 vs 29.1 means DD (0 direct damage) looks worse, not better. The one scenario where DD wins: Sabriel is the only PC still alive AND using high-level spell slots for healing Bazgar. In that narrow window, stripping her slots ends the encounter faster than grinding HP.
