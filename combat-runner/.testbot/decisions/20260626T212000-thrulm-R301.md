## Phase B — Scenario: disintegration-threat (rotation index 1)

*Beholder Disintegration Ray: threat ceiling, one-shot probability, and permanent-death feel vs Compass Edge.*

### Setup

Party (Compass Edge, Level 5):
- Bazgar: Fighter 5, HP 49, AC 18
- Marwen: Wizard 5, HP 32, AC 15
- Sabriel: divine martial 5, HP 44, AC 19

Beholder (in chamber, Void-Feeding active):
- Disintegration Ray: +9 to hit (DB +8 + Void-Feeding +1), 10d8 force, starts available
- Beholder wins initiative (INT 17, tactics: target caster first)

### Round 1 — Beholder turn (Disintegration Ray available)

DR targets Marwen (AC 15, lowest HP, highest spell-threat):

| | Value |
|---|---|
| To-hit bonus | +9 (base +8 + Void-Feeding +1) |
| Hit threshold | d20 ≥ 6 |
| Hit probability | **75%** |
| Average damage | 10d8 avg 45 + 1 (VF) = **46 force** |
| Marwen HP | 32 |
| Kill on hit? | Yes (46 >> 32) |
| Disintegration trigger | Yes — target at 0 HP = disintegrated |
| P(Marwen disintegrated R1) | **~75%** |
| Party counterplay available | None — Marwen has not acted |

**With the old stale +7 values** (used in R296–R300): P(hit) = 65%, P(disintegration R1) ≈ 65%. The actual risk is 10% higher than previous simulations computed.

### Findings

1. **FEEL-003 confirmed, threat ceiling higher than logged.** Prior sims used +7 in-chamber (65% hit vs Marwen). Correct value is +9 (75%). The turn-1 disintegration risk is 75%, not 65%.

2. **Permanent death before first PC action.** DR starts available. Beholder wins initiative with reasonable probability (+3 DEX vs party average ~+1). On the common case (beholder first), Marwen faces 75% disintegration before her turn 1.

3. **FEEL-003 soft-kill recommendation (carry-forward from R278-01).** Four options remain under consideration:
   - A: Narrative soft-kill — "target reaches 0 HP, collapses unconscious, no body ash"; revivify works post-fight.
   - B: CON save escape — DC 16 CON save when DR would disintegrate; on success, target at 0 HP with death saves.
   - C: DR disintegrates only on kill damage ≥ twice max HP (catastrophic overkill threshold).
   - D: As-written, but tactics note: beholder holds DR until R2 (prefers grapple → drain divinity on R1).
   **Recommendation for "meant-to-lose" encounter: Option D costs nothing mechanically and fixes the feel issue. Add to tactics: "Beholder holds Disintegration Ray until round 2 or later unless Marwen has already cast a concentration spell; priority is Drain Divinity on Sabriel in R1."**

4. **Tactics/flavor gap (FEEL-R301-03, new).** Tactics section says "Against Casters: Prioritizes disintegration of spellcasters." But beholder's design motivation is feeding on *absence of divinity* — Marwen (arcane wizard) is not a divine target. The beholder's highest-priority action thematically should be Drain Divinity on Sabriel, not immediate destruction of Marwen. Tactical inconsistency between the mechanical optimization (kill Marwen first = remove highest damage) and the encounter's thematic intent (beholder craves Sabriel's divine spark). Joe may want to swap the tactic priority.

5. **Drain Divinity range constraint (carry-forward flag).** Beholder hovers 40–60 ft above altar by default. Drain Divinity is 30 ft range — beholder must descend to use it. This constraint is correctly noted in the cheat sheet ("Drain Divinity (leg. 3): 30 ft range — beholder must descend") but not explicit in the stat block tactics section. If beholder descends to use DD, it becomes targetable by melee PCs. Design tension worth preserving.

### Lair Action — Void Eruption risk (FEEL-R301-04, new)

Void Eruption (lair action, init 20) deals 11 (2d10) force to all creatures within 20 ft of shrine, including charmed thralls. A thrall within 20 ft of shrine (typical) fails DC 16 DEX on 95% of rolls (DEX +1). Expected Void Eruption → 11 damage → one-shots a Thrall at 22 HP if they've taken any prior damage, or forces a WIS save... wait, thralls have 22 HP; Void Eruption alone does 11 (avg), survivable. But combined with any prior damage, thralls inside 20 ft die. No text warns DMs to avoid Void Eruption when thralls are near shrine. Manifest Thralls gives 1 THP + a reaction — then Void Eruption in the same round kills that thrall. Add note to cheat sheet or lair action block.
