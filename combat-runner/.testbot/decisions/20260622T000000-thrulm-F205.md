### Phase A bugs — log only (no auto-fix applied)

**A205-01 — Thrall missing CR line in stat block**  
`thrall-derro.md` has no `Challenge` row. The cheat sheet lists it as CR 1/4, but this can't be verified against the stat block. The thrall may be intentionally CR-less (not an XP target; purely a beholder puppet), but the cheat sheet reference creates a drift. Design call: add Challenge row or remove CR from cheat sheet.

**A205-02 — Thrall saving-throw contradiction**  
`thrall-derro.md` header: `Saving Throws | None; dominated mind has no saves`. Contradicts Fractured Will trait body: "makes a DC 14 Wisdom saving throw." The header says no saves; the trait text invokes one. Either the header should read `None (except Fractured Will: DC 14 Wisdom)` or Fractured Will should use a flat DC check. Design call.

**A205-03 — Rager Taunt DC 12, undocumented derivation**  
With CHA −1 and PB +2 (CR 2), formula gives DC 9. With STR +2 and PB +2, formula gives DC 12 — which matches. But the stat block doesn't say "Strength-based." The DM has to guess that Taunt is STR-derived (fear through physical intimidation), not CHA-derived (verbal). Should label: "The rager targets one creature… DC 12 Strength-based Charisma saving throw" — or simplify to "DC 12 Charisma save (Strength-based)." Design call.

**A205-04 — Shrine-Touched Ancient Resonance DC 14, 1 off**  
Explicitly-listed PB +3. Highest stat mods: DEX +2, CON +2. Formula: 8+3+2=13. Stated DC: 14. Off by 1. No stat in the block supports DC 14 at PB +3. If designed as a flat round number, should be noted as intentional in the stat block. Design call: lower to 13 or bump one stat to +3.

**A205-05 — Shardcaller Shard-Barrage DC 13, 1 off**  
CR 2 → PB +2. Highest mods: WIS +2, DEX +2. Formula: 8+2+2=12. Stated: 13. Off by 1. Same pattern as shrine-touched. Design call.

**A205-06 — Cheat sheet: Thrall listed as CR 1/4 without stat block source**  
Cross-reference of cheat sheet vs stat block. Cheat sheet DERRO TYPES table lists "Thrall (CR 1/4)" but `thrall-derro.md` has no Challenge row. Minor drift — if CR 1/4 is correct, add it to the stat block. See A205-01.

### Phase B findings — log only

**B205-01 — `compelled_movement` utility has no bonus-action flag**  
DB row type "utility" does not distinguish action vs bonus action. The runner narrates correctly (no attack roll), but the output doesn't label it as a bonus action. DM must track manually. Automation gap — not auto-fixable without a schema change. Log for future `action_kind: bonus_action` field consideration.

**B205-02 — Thrall hand_axe_weak near-useless vs Compass Edge party**  
Hit rates: +1 vs Bazgar AC 18 = 20%; vs Sabriel AC 19 = 15%; vs Marwen AC 15 = 35%. At 20% with avg 2 damage, expected damage per attack ≈ 0.4. The thrall's only non-trivial contribution is serving as a vehicle for Compel Thrall's positioning. Design-intentional (dominated creature). Flag as feel-note: consider whether a once-per-encounter "lunge" (d6 bonus, no modifier) could make the thrall briefly scary rather than filler.

**B205-03 — Beholder alpha-strike: Marwen is one-round-killable before meaningful contribution**  
Full multiattack (Lash×2 + Maw, all +7) vs AC 15 averages 50 damage. Marwen's 32 HP doesn't survive one full action sequence. She acts once (Fire Bolt: 11 damage) then dies. Lethal by design — party is meant to lose. But the specific feeling is "one turn, then dead." Existing counterplay (Shield reaction, 40 ft standoff, Misty Step out of grapple) requires player foreknowledge. Note for DM: explicitly warn Marwen's player to stay 40+ ft from the altar at combat start so the beholder must spend a round descending.

**B205-04 — Drain Divinity degenerate: re-confirmed (fire #205)**  
Against Sabriel (divine martial, 5 spell slots), beholder prioritizes Drain Divinity (3 LA) every round. Move and Tentacle legendary actions went unused across both rounds. No new finding beyond prior fires; re-confirming the pattern persists. Existing open flag for human decision.
