## Issues Found This Run (Log Only — No Auto-Fix Applied)

### CS-DRIFT-R303-01 — Shrine-Touched CR 3 asserted in cheat sheet, PB +3 in stat block
**File:** `COMBAT-CHEAT-SHEET.md` (line 13 in derro types table)
**Stat block:** `shrine-touched-derro.md` has `Proficiency Bonus +3` explicitly but no CR field.
**Problem:** CR 3 normally gives PB +2. If PB is truly +3, the creature is CR 5–8.
Cheat sheet asserts "CR 3" as fact but the stat block contradicts it via explicit PB +3.
The saves (Dex +5 = DEX+2+PB+3, Con +5 = CON+2+PB+3) and attack bonus (+4 = STR+1+PB+3)
are internally consistent with PB +3. CR label is the inconsistent element.
**Decision needed:** Lower PB to +2 (matching CR 3 → Dex +4, Con +4, attack +3, DC 12)
or raise CR to 5+ (or leave as fiat design with PB +3 at "CR 3").

### DB-SOFT-R303-01 — Ancient Resonance DC 14 doesn't fit standard formula
**Shrine-Touched PB +3, DEX +2:** 8+3+2 = DC 13, not 14.
DC 14 would require PB +4 (CR 9+) or a non-DEX ability. Not an auto-fix candidate;
likely a fiat design DC. Flag for consistency pass if CR/PB is corrected.

### DB-BUG-R156-01 (pre-existing, still open) — oath_breaking_retaliation has no dice
The DB reaction row for shrine-touched-derro has `narration_preview` only.

### KNOWN-ISSUE-CORRECTION-R303 — Beholder PB inconsistency description is backwards
The open issue in the briefing says "attack/save bonuses appear to use PB +3 while
stated CR 13 calls for PB +5." This is inverted:
- Saves (Dex +8 = 3+5, Wis +7 = 2+5) and attacks (+8 = 3+5 with DEX) ARE consistent
  with PB +5, which is correct for CR 13.
- SKILL BONUSES are the inconsistent ones: Arcana +6 = INT(+3)+PB(+3) and
  Perception +5 = WIS(+2)+PB(+3) — both suggest PB +3, not +5.
So the beholder CR 13 is arguably sound for its saves/attacks; the skill proficiencies
were authored with PB +3. Correction needed in briefing KNOWN STATE section only
(factual error, not a stat block bug). At-table impact: beholder's Arcana/Perception
checks would roll higher than listed if PB +5 were applied. Minor.

## Phase B — Generative Playtest Slice: `void-scream-clustering`

**Scenario:** Round 2. Beholder waited for Void Scream recharge (DC 6 on recharge roll).
Bazgar and Sabriel have advanced into melee range (20-30 ft from shrine altar).
Marwen is at the 30 ft boundary. Beholder uses Void Scream from hover above altar.

**Void Scream stats:** 6d10 psychic (avg 33), DC 16 WIS, half on success.
Frightened on failed save (1 min; DC 16 WIS save at end of each turn to end).
Creatures within 10 ft of shrine: disadvantage on this save.

### Save simulations

**Bazgar** (WIS mod assumed +1; within 10 ft of shrine = disadvantage):
- Success threshold: d20+1 ≥ 16 → need 15+ → base 30% success.
- With disadvantage: P(success) = 0.30² ≈ 9%.
- On failure (91%): 33 psychic damage + Frightened. Bazgar at 49 HP → 16 HP remaining.
- Frightened: disadvantage on attacks while beholder in LoS; cannot willingly move closer.
  Bazgar is in melee range — this means he can't choose to close further but can attack
  in place with disadvantage. He's still useful, just reduced.

**Sabriel** (divine martial, WIS mod +3 assumed; 20-25 ft from shrine, standard save):
- Success threshold: d20+3 ≥ 16 → need 13+ → 40% success.
- On failure (60%): 33 psychic damage + Frightened. Sabriel at 44 HP → **11 HP remaining**.
- CRITICAL: Sabriel is the designated anti-beholder PC (sanctified weapons, paladin analog).
  Frightened prevents willingly approaching the beholder. Beholder hovers 40-60 ft above.
  A frightened Sabriel cannot move toward the beholder for the duration, must make DC 16
  WIS saves each round to end effect (~40% chance per turn). Expected lockout: 2-3 turns.
  During this time, the party's main damage source against the beholder is neutralized.
  This is not "lethal-but-fair" — it's a shutdown with no counterplay options for the PC.

**Marwen** (WIS mod +1 assumed; at 30 ft boundary of VS, standard save):
- Success threshold: d20+1 ≥ 16 → need 15+ → 30% success.
- On failure (70%): **33 psychic damage. Marwen max HP = 32. This is an instant kill.**
- On success: 16-17 damage. Marwen survives at 15-16 HP, then targeted by Void Ray LA.

### Key observations

**FEEL-R303-01 — VOID SCREAM ONE-SHOTS MARWEN ON AVERAGE (log only)**
Void Scream average damage (33) exceeds Marwen's max HP (32) by 1. On a failed save
(~70% probability), this is instant death from full HP with a single beholder ability.
This is not conditional on prior damage — it happens from full HP on any round the
beholder uses VS and Marwen is within 30 ft. The party is meant to lose, but this
removes a PC before she can take any meaningful action if she's positioned in range.
Recommendation for Joe: reduce VS damage to 5d10 (avg 27.5) so Marwen survives the
average case (32-27=5 HP, low but alive), or add tactical escape for the Wizard PC
(Misty Step, Counterspell opportunity). A hard one-shot with no action is removal,
not challenge.

**FEEL-R303-02 — FRIGHTENED FULLY NEUTRALIZES SABRIEL VS BEHOLDER (log only)**
The intended anti-beholder PC is the divine martial with sanctified weapons. The
beholder hovers 40-60 ft above the shrine altar. Frightened condition prevents
Sabriel from willingly moving closer. Combined:
- Can't approach the beholder while frightened (movement blocked)
- Can't make melee attacks she can't reach
- Has disadvantage on attacks (against beholder in LoS) if she COULD reach it
This creates a complete tactical shutdown of the party's designated threat. No
counterplay: no way to Counterspell VS (no slot, already fired), no cover
from "behind a pillar" (VS is spherical area, not LoS-gated). Expected lockout:
2-3 rounds × (1 - 0.40) success rate ≈ 1.5 additional successful saves needed
before Sabriel re-engages. During this time, beholder farms remaining LA budget.
Recommendation: give Sabriel's archetype a Fear immunity or resistance at level 5,
OR add the Frightened condition to the cheat sheet's "for divine martials" callout.

**FEEL-R303-03 — SHRINE PROXIMITY DISADVANTAGE IS A PREP DEPENDENCY (log only)**
The "within 10 ft of shrine = disadvantage on VS save" rule is mentioned only once
in the SAVE DCs table in the cheat sheet. It does not appear in the "Start of Round"
checklist or in the beholder's start-of-turn checklist in the .md file. At a busy
table, this will be missed. Recommend adding to the beholder's start-of-turn
checklist as: "Void Scream: creatures within 10 ft of the shrine save with
DISADVANTAGE" or to the cheat sheet Round-by-Round checklist.

**LA economy observation — round 2 with Void Scream:**
After VS (beholder's action), 3 LA are available. If Marwen is dead, there's no
natural LA Tentacle target on her initiative slot. Beholder optimally: Tentacle Lash
(1 LA) after Bazgar's turn → Void Ray (2 LA) after Sabriel's turn. Expected Void Ray
output: 0.70 × 23 + 0.30 × 11 = 19.4 avg force damage on Sabriel (11 HP remaining
after VS) → second down in the same round. Bazgar (16 HP) then faces the beholder
alone. This is consistent with design intent but the speed is 2 PCs downed in round 2.
No round was "unfair rather than lethal" in isolation — VS is a legal beholder ability.
The feel issue is the clustering of these two events (Marwen dies, Sabriel locked out)
into a single beholder turn with no prior counterplay window.
