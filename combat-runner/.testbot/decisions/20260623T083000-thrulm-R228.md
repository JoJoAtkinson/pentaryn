### New Phase A finding: BUG-R228-01 — Thrall-Derro "Saving Throws: None" contradicts Fractured Will

**Location:** `thrall-derro.md` stat block table, `Saving Throws` row.

**Stat block says:** `| **Saving Throws** | None; dominated mind has no saves |`

**Fractured Will trait says:** "When the derro is forced to act against its original oath, it makes a **DC 14 Wisdom saving throw**. On a success, it resists for one round…"

**Contradiction:** "No saves" in the stat block block conventionally means no saving throw proficiencies (the creature uses raw ability scores). But the parenthetical "dominated mind has no saves" implies the creature cannot make saving throws at all — which directly contradicts the Fractured Will mechanic.

**At-table risk:** A DM reading the stat block header first (common for fast reference) will believe the thrall auto-fails all saves. When Fractured Will triggers, the DM may skip the roll. Fractured Will is the ONLY mechanism by which a thrall can resist the beholder, so suppressing it has a feel impact (thralls feel even more like furniture than designed).

**Not auto-fixed:** Text change required; phrasing depends on design intent. Suggested replacement: `| **Saving Throws** | None proficient (uses base modifiers; see Fractured Will for WIS checks) |`

→ **BUG-R228-01 (LOW — thrall-derro "no saves" text suppresses Fractured Will WIS save at table)**

## Phase B — Generative Playtest: `drain-divinity-vs-sabriel`

**Scenario:** 3 consecutive rounds in which the beholder spends its entire 3-LA budget on Drain Divinity targeting Sabriel. Per rotation.yml definition: DC 16 CHA save, assume Sabriel CHA +2 (no proficiency in this scenario assumption). Simulate 3 individual saves; track slot strip and beholder THP.

**Geometric prerequisite (BUG-R226-02 live):** Drain Divinity range is 30 ft. Beholder hovers 40-60 ft above altar at default. Must use Shrine-Drift BA each round to descend to ≤30 ft. BA consumed = Compel Thrall unavailable. Confirmed: geometry works IF beholder descends on its own turn.

---

### State entering simulation

- Beholder: 100 HP, Void-Feeding active, 3 LA each round, Antireality available each round.
- Sabriel: 44 HP, AC 19. Spell slots: 4×1st, 3×2nd, 2×3rd. CHA +2 (rotation.yml assumption). CHA save +2.

---

### Round 1

**Beholder main turn:**
- **Shrine-Drift (BA):** Descends to 25 ft elevation. Now within 30 ft of Sabriel. BA consumed.
- **Multiattack (Action):** 2× Tentacle Lash + Maw (separate from LA budget).
  - Tentacle 1 vs Sabriel (AC 19): +7 to hit, needs 12+. P(hit) = 45%. Avg 14 bludg + grapple (DC 16 STR save per BUG-R225-02). **Expected: 6.3 bludgeoning avg.**
  - Tentacle 2 vs Bazgar (AC 18): +7 to hit, needs 11+. P(hit) = 50%. **Expected: 7 avg.**
  - Maw vs Sabriel (AC 19, if grappled — disadvantage on escape): +7, needs 12+. P(hit) = 45%. Avg 22 piercing. **Expected: 9.9 avg.**
  - Sabriel expected HP after multiattack: 44 − 6.3 − 9.9 = **27.8 avg.**

**After Sabriel's turn → Drain Divinity LA (costs 3):**
- Range: Beholder at 25 ft, Sabriel below. ✓ within 30 ft.
- **DC 16 CHA save, Sabriel +2:** Needs 14+ → P(succeed) = 35%, **P(fail) = 65%**.
- **Result if fail:** Sabriel loses one 3rd-level slot. Beholder gains 6 THP. LA = 0.

| LA action | Cost | Remaining LA |
|---|---|---|
| Drain Divinity | 3 | 0 |

---

### Round 2

Assumed state: Round 1 Drain Divinity succeeded (65% likely). Sabriel now has 1×3rd-level slot.

- **Shrine-Drift (BA):** Maintains position within 30 ft.
- **Multiattack (Action):** Same as round 1 vs available targets.
- **After Sabriel's turn → Drain Divinity (3 LA):**
  - Sabriel highest slot: 3rd level (last one).
  - DC 16 CHA save +2. **P(fail) = 65%.**
  - If fail: last 3rd-level slot stripped. Beholder total THP = 12. Sabriel has no 3rd-level slots — cannot Divine Smite at 3rd level.

---

### Round 3

Assumed state: Both rounds succeeded (65%² = 42% joint probability both drained). Sabriel now highest slot = 2nd level.

- **Drain Divinity (3 LA):**
  - Highest slot: 2nd level. DC 16 CHA save +2. **P(fail) = 65%.**
  - If fail: one 2nd-level slot stripped. Beholder gains 4 THP. Total THP = 16.

---

### Statistical summary (3-round Drain Divinity focus)

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| P(save fails each round) | 65% per roll |
| P(all 3 fail — strips both 3rd + one 2nd) | 0.65³ = **27.4%** |
| P(at least 2 fail — strips both 3rd-level slots) | P(2) + P(3) = 3×0.65²×0.35 + 0.274 = 0.443 + 0.274 = **71.7%** |
| Expected THP gain over 3 rounds | 0.65×6 + 0.65²×6 + 0.65³×4 = 3.9 + 2.5 + 1.1 = **7.5 avg** |
| Sabriel's expected Divine Smite capacity at round 4 | Two 3rd-level smites stripped with 71.7% probability; 2×(2d8+1d8 avg = ~13.5) = 27 smite damage denied over multiple attacks |

---

### EV comparison: Drain Divinity vs Tentacle + Void Ray

| LA spend (3 total) | Expected combat output |
|---|---|
| **Drain Divinity (3 LA)** | 0.65×smite_denial (~8.8 avg dmg prevented) + 7.5 THP = ~16 "equivalent value" per round |
| **Tentacle (1 LA) + Void Ray (2 LA)** | 0.45×14 (vs AC 19) + 0.70×22+0.30×11 = 6.3 + 18.7 = **25 avg direct damage** per round |

The Tentacle+Void Ray combination delivers **~56% more combat value per round** in direct damage, and direct damage cannot be saved against via CHA proficiency.

Over 3 rounds:
- Drain Divinity: ~48 "equivalent" (16×3 rounds)
- Tentacle+Void Ray: **~75 direct damage** (25×3 rounds)

---

### Scenario-specific finding: Drain Divinity geometry + LA cost creates a self-defeating loop

When the beholder descends to use Drain Divinity, it:
1. Exposes itself to full melee — Bazgar expected 2 attacks per round (+7 vs AC 17, 50% hit, 9.5 avg = 9.5 per attack). Over 3 rounds: ~2 hits/round × 9.5 = 19 avg damage per round, **57 avg damage in 3 rounds**.
2. Sabriel may also land melee strikes if un-grappled.
3. Antireality mitigates ONE attack per round (-50% hit → ~25% hit with disadvantage). Net Bazgar DPR with Antireality on first attack: 0.25×9.5 + 0.50×9.5 = 7.1 avg/round = 21 over 3 rounds.

**Net: beholder takes ~21 damage from Bazgar alone during 3-round Drain Divinity focus, while generating ~48 EV.**
**Net: beholder takes ~21 damage from Bazgar during 3-round Tentacle+Void Ray focus, while generating ~75 EV.**

The geometry (descending within melee range) is the same cost either way. Drain Divinity simply generates less output for that exposure.

---

### Feel Assessment

**Is spending all 3 LA on one effect mechanically sound?** Technically yes — Drain Divinity costs 3 LA and that IS all of them. The mechanic is coherent. But the outcome is:
- ~72% chance to strip both 3rd-level slots from Sabriel over 3 rounds
- ~7.5 expected THP (negligible on a 110 HP creature)
- Sabriel's 2nd-level slots remain intact, allowing continued 2nd-level smites (2d8 = 9 avg bonus)

**Is the slot-stripping narratively satisfying?** YES — watching the beholder drain Sabriel's holy power is thematically perfect for the encounter. But the mechanical outcome is disappointing: 9-10 avg smite damage denied per Drain Divinity doesn't feel proportional to spending the entire LA budget. A paladin at level 5 still has 3×2nd and 4×1st slots; stripping 2nd-level slots gives the illusion of resource denial without fundamentally changing Sabriel's ability to function.

**Dominant counterplay gap:** Sabriel at +2 CHA (no proficiency) is the *most favorable* target for Drain Divinity. If Sabriel had CHA save proficiency (which paladin class grants at level 1), her save would be CHA +2 + PB +3 = +5 → P(fail) drops to 50%, making the EV comparison even worse.
