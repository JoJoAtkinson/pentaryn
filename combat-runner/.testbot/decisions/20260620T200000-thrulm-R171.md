### Void Scream simulation

**Beholder action — Void Scream (Recharge 6, beholder burns its action):**

Range: 30 ft centered on beholder. Beholder hovers near altar — Bazgar and Marwen within 30 ft (both grappled, prone/suppressed). Sabriel at 25 ft also within range.

**Shrine proximity disadvantage (within 10 ft of shrine):** Bazgar, grappled at the shrine base, is within 10 ft. Disadvantage on this save.

**Save results (DC 16 WIS):**

**Bazgar (WIS +1, disadvantage from shrine proximity):**
- Normal pass rate: d20 + 1 ≥ 16 → needs 15+ → P(pass) = 6/20 = 30%
- With disadvantage: P(pass) = P(both dice ≥ 15) = (6/20)² = **9% pass, 91% fail**
- Result: FAILS. Takes 33 psychic damage + FRIGHTENED (1 min, DC 16 WIS at end of each turn to end).
- Bazgar HP: 15 − 33 = **−18 HP → unconscious, failed 1 death save**
- *Bazgar is down at the shrine base. Two death saves already ticking.*

**Marwen (WIS +2, no proficiency — Wizard saves CON/INT, not WIS; grappled at ~30 ft — no shrine proximity):**
- Pass rate: d20 + 2 ≥ 16 → needs 14+ → P(pass) = 7/20 = **35% pass, 65% fail**
- With 18 HP remaining (vs full 32 HP): fails → 33 psychic.
- Marwen HP: 18 − 33 = **−15 HP → unconscious**
- *Even if Marwen succeeds (35%), she takes 16 psychic and drops to 2 HP — effectively the next stray attack or legendary tentacle finishes her.*

**Sabriel (WIS +3, proficient in WIS saves — Paladin-class, PB +3 → WIS save +6):**
- Pass rate: d20 + 6 ≥ 16 → needs 10+ → P(pass) = 11/20 = **55% pass, 45% fail**
- Result (roll d20 = 12): 12 + 6 = 18 → SUCCESS. Takes half: 16 psychic.
- Sabriel HP: 44 − 16 = **28 HP**
- *Sabriel is the last PC standing with meaningful HP.*

### Feel findings

**FEEL-R171-01 (NEW — HIGH):** Void Scream is a near-guaranteed wipe of Marwen from any HP below full. Average damage (33) exceeds Marwen's max HP (32), so ANY failed save from full HP one-shots her. Failure rate from full HP is 65% (no shrine proximity). By round 2 (after R1 multiattack damage), Marwen is virtually guaranteed to go to 0 HP on any VS use she fails. This crosses from "lethal but fair" into "wizard has a 65% chance of dying before Sabriel gets a second turn." The specific concern: the party has no resurrection capability at level 5 and no Dispel Magic, so if DR disintegrates (permadeath) and VS one-shots the Wizard, two party members are effectively eliminated in the same round. **Consider: cap VS to 5d10 instead of 6d10, or exclude grappled targets (they can't hear as effectively while being crushed). Or reduce DR/VS priority so both don't fire on the same round.**

**FEEL-R171-02 (NEW — MEDIUM):** Shrine proximity disadvantage (10 ft) is not recoverable in melee. Bazgar cannot move away from the shrine while grappled by the beholder (beholder hovers at 40–60 ft altitude; tentacle reach is 10 ft, so the grappled Bazgar hangs near the shrine). This means shrine proximity disadvantage on Void Scream is effectively **permanent for Bazgar once grappled** — he cannot escape the shrine's influence without breaking the grapple first. The rule is thematically correct (shrine hates melee combatants) but mechanically locks the fighter into guaranteed VS failure. Needs a DM note in the stat block or cheat sheet.

**FEEL-R171-03 (NEW — MEDIUM):** Sabriel is the only PC with a meaningful WIS save bonus (55% pass rate). If Sabriel is the divine martial with WIS proficiency, the encounter has one PC who can sometimes survive VS and two who almost cannot. This party-shape dependency (one PC soaks the save, two auto-fail) makes VS outcomes predictable after character creation. The same beholder fight with a WIS-heavy party (Druid + Cleric + Paladin) would play completely differently — VS becomes a manageable hazard. With Compass Edge, it's a decisive round-ender. This is a design note for Joe about party fit, not a bug.

### "Can hear" clause check — FIX-FC48-A (confirmed still relevant)

Per stat block annotation FIX-FC48-A: unconscious creatures cannot hear VS and are excluded. After this slice, BOTH Bazgar and Marwen are unconscious. If the beholder recharges VS (rolls a 6 on round 3+), only Sabriel is the valid target. VS becomes a 30-ft single-target psychic attack against the one remaining standing PC. This is still devastating (DC 16 WIS save vs +6 = 45% fail → 33 damage from ~28 HP) but no longer a party-wipe in one action.

**CS-DRIFT-16 (carry-forward confirmed urgent):** FIX-FC48-A is only in the stat block wall of text. A rushed DM WILL apply VS to downed PCs. This needs a bold reminder in COMBAT-CHEAT-SHEET.md "When Beholder Acts" section. Adding this to CS-DRIFT-16 priority comment below.
