### Phase B Findings

**Scenario mechanics verified:** `drain_divinity` verb dispatches without crash. DD costs 3 LA (full budget). No double-spend possible. ✓

**DD protocol operates as designed:** The beholder's R1-LA-hold survives beholder-first initiative order, confirming FIX-FC47-A. DD fires after Sabriel's turn even when beholder acts first in R1.

**R2 Void Scream creates near-simultaneous triple-down:** Combined with R1 lair action (Bazgar downed twice in 2 rounds), the two-round sequence risks TPK before Sabriel's R2 turn. No unfair moments identified beyond the documented FEEL-F77-01 (Bazgar zero-agency R1). The encounter is lethal as designed — this is expected.

**DD coinflip assessment:** Spending 3 LA on a 50% save (Sabriel +5 vs DC 16) for 6 temp HP and 1 slot stripped is high-variance but intentional. The value compounds over rounds (strips smite pool, forces LoH-only offense) — but only if the party survives long enough. This fire confirms that in the expected TPK-by-R2 sequence, DD's attrition value is moot: the fight ends before slot depletion matters. **The design question is whether DD should have a consolation effect on a successful save** (e.g., Sabriel has disadvantage on her next concentration save) to provide value even in the 50% fail case. Log as FEEL-R93-02.

**Bonus action gap:** On R1, the beholder's bonus action is consumed by Shrine-Drift (descent). This forecloses Compel Thrall on R1. When thralls are present, the DM must choose: descend for DD range OR keep hover + Compel Thrall. Protocol (FIX-FC40-A) doesn't acknowledge this tradeoff. Log as FEEL-R93-03.

## Phase B — New Findings This Fire

**NEW-R93-01 (LOG ONLY — scenario documentation error):** The prelude for `thrulm-beholder-drain-divinity-solo` states "est. CHA +2" as Sabriel's save modifier for DD. This is the raw CHA ability modifier, not the CHA saving throw. A divine martial (Paladin-class) at level 5 has proficiency in CHA saving throws (Paladin class feature). Sabriel's actual CHA save = CHA mod +2 + PB +3 = **+5**. The prelude's "+2" causes a DM/testbot running this scenario to apply +2 vs DC 16 (65% fail rate) instead of the correct +5 (50% fail rate). This overstates DD's hit rate by 15 percentage points. **Scenario `thrulm-dd-descent-then-drain` added this fire** to test the correct flow with noted CHA save +5. The original scenario prelude is not edited (it describes its intent correctly, just wrong on the math); correction in the new scenario.

**FEEL-R93-01 (LOG ONLY):** This fire's R1 sequence (beholder hover 60 ft → Shrine-Drift BA descent → Multiattack → hold 3 LA → DD post-Sabriel) is the canonical DD execution path, but it produces Bazgar zero-agency-downed on R1 as a near-certain side effect: prone Bazgar (from lair UG) + TL advantage (75% hit) + Maw auto-crit (avg 39 pierce) = 52 avg R1 damage vs 49 HP. The DD protocol selects Multiattack with grapple-Maw synergy as its mandatory R1 action. These two mechanics (DD + Maw auto-crit) are both high-value independently but together guarantee a PC down with ~100% probability in the same R1. The issue is not that either mechanic is wrong — it's that the combined guaranteed-downed-PC produces a pervasive "zero R1 agency" feel that compounds with every subsequent fire. Recommend: consider whether the beholder should intentionally target Sabriel with TL (45% hit rate instead of 75% on prone Bazgar) to preserve Bazgar's R1 turn while still building a grapple for Maw. Lower TL efficiency is a strategic trade for less anticlimactic pacing. [Design call — flag for Joe.] Relates to FEEL-F77-01.

**FEEL-R93-02 (LOG ONLY):** DD on a 50% save for a resource cost of 3 LA (the full round's legendary action budget) provides zero value when Sabriel succeeds. The fight ends at R2–R3 in all simulated paths before the slot-attrition value accrues meaningfully. Recommended options: (a) reduce DD cost to 2 LA so a Tentacle or Void Ray LA is still available after DD, or (b) add a consolation effect on a successful DD save (e.g., Sabriel's next spell attack or concentration save has disadvantage — the void still touched her). As currently designed, R1 DD success = completely wasted turn. [Design call — flag for Joe.] Relates to FEEL-R83-02.

**FEEL-R93-03 (LOG ONLY):** When Shrine-Drift is used as the bonus action for R1 descent (enabling DD range), the beholder cannot also use Compel Thrall (also a bonus action, 1/Turn) on R1. In encounters where thralls are alive, the DM must choose: descend for DD OR direct thralls. The R1 protocol (FIX-FC40-A) does not acknowledge this BA conflict. At minimum, a note is needed: "If Shrine-Drift is used for descent on R1, Compel Thrall is unavailable that turn — thralls act on their own initiative. If maintaining thrall pressure is higher priority than DD this round, use Shrine-Drift on R2+ when descent can be combined with a non-DD LA sequence." Relates to NEW-R87-01.
