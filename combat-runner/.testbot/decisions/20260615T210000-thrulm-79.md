### New Phase B observations (beyond fire #30 findings)

#### B79-01 — FEEL-F79-01 live: Oath-Bound Resolve never fires (confirmed in 79 simulations)

In this simulation, as in all prior simulations, no Deep Watch commander is present. DWD-1 and DWD-2 fight without a superior. The Oath-Bound Resolve trait (advantage on frightened/charmed saves vs a commander present) **never fires.** If Marwen had cast Tasha's Hideous Laughter (charm save), the DWDs would not benefit from Oath-Bound Resolve. If a frightening ability had targeted them, same result.

This is a dead trait in the context of all Thrulm encounters as designed.

**Second observation:** Even if a "commander" DWD were added, frightened/charmed saves are rare for a DWD in combat — they don't face sources of charm or fear in the Thrulm lineup. The beholder is immune to frightened; derro lack fear effects; Compass Edge has no Fear-class spells (no Warlock, no relevant wizard concentrations at level 5 in this party). The trait would still rarely fire even if a commander were present.

**Suggested redesign (log only):** Replace with an active benefit that fires naturally in formation, e.g.: "When both this DWD and an allied DWD are within 5 ft of the same target, this DWD has advantage on STR and CON saving throws" — pairs with Tactical Drilling organically, rewards staying in formation, and doesn't require an absent NPC type.

#### B79-02 — FEEL-F30-01 partially mitigated at 9 HP, still ambiguous at 14+ HP

At 9 HP (this run) the "below 10 HP" Disengage note DOES apply — DWD-2 pivots correctly. But FEEL-F30-01's core case (a DWD at 14 HP wanting to pivot to crossbow) remains undocumented. For this run, the scenario resolved without exposing the 14 HP case; the DWD happened to be at 9 HP. The ambiguity persists.

#### B79-03 — Two-DWD formation dies in 2 rounds vs. level-5 party regardless of dice

First-cycle average (fire #30): Bazgar took 7 HP. This cycle: Bazgar took 5 HP. Variance is low; party always wins comfortably in 2 rounds. The DWD pair is Encounter 2 material — appropriate for party levels -1 (as designed in encounters.md). No balance concern at that encounter. The concern only arises if DWDs appear in Encounters 4–5 as thrall-adjacent support while the beholder is active.
