## Phase B — Playtest slice: `legendary-action-loop`

*(From thrulm-rotation.json index 2 — beholder legendary action economy across full PC round)*

### Setup

- **Beholder:** HP 110, AC 17, init +3, 3 LA/round. Void-Feeding active. DR recharged. Hovering 40 ft above altar.
- **Compass Edge:** Bazgar (Fighter 5, HP 49, AC 18, DEX mod ~+0 in plate), Marwen (Wizard 5, HP 32, AC 15, DEX +2), Sabriel (Divine Martial 5, HP 44, AC 19, DEX ~+1).
- **Supporting NPCs:** 1 Derro Rager (HP 52, AC 16), 2 Thrall Derro (HP 22 each, AC 14).
- **Assumed init 20 lair action:** Beholder takes lair action before own turn.

### Initiative order (reasonable typical roll)

1. Lair action (init 20)
2. Marwen (init 19, d20+2=17 but wins ties vs Sabriel)
3. Beholder (init 18, d20+3=18)
4. Sabriel (init 15)
5. Bazgar (init 10)
6. Rager (init 8, d20+0)
7. Thrall 1 (init 7, d20+1)
8. Thrall 2 (init 5, d20+1)

*(Edge case: if Marwen goes BEFORE beholder, beholder uses LA after her turn on Round 1. If beholder wins init, Marwen sees the full attack first. This simulation uses Marwen → Beholder → Sabriel → Bazgar.)*

### Round 1

**Lair action (init 20):** Unstable Ground on Bazgar (the melee threat). DC 16 DEX, Bazgar +0 → needs 16+ = 75% fail. PRONE. Bazgar speed halved on next move.

**Marwen's turn:**
- Sees beholder at 40 ft altitude. No Hypnotic Pattern (charmed immunity). Misty Step (BA) to pillar for half cover.
- Action: Scorching Ray (2nd-level, 3 rays) vs beholder AC 17 at +5 to hit → needs 12+ = 45% hit per ray. Expected hits: 1.35 rays × 7 fire avg = 9.5 damage. Beholder: 110 → ~100 HP.
- **LA spend after Marwen's turn:** 1 LA → Tentacle Lash on Marwen (squishiest, 32 HP). +7 vs AC 15 → needs 8+ = 65% hit. Say hits: 3d6+3+1(VF) avg = 14.5 → 14 dmg. Marwen: 32 → 18 HP. GRAPPLED (escape DC 16, Marwen Athletics ~-1 or Acrobatics +2 → 30% escape chance; she stays grappled).

**Beholder's turn:**
- Void-Feeding active. Marwen is grappled 10 ft away on a tentacle (not within 5 ft of beholder body; no disadvantage on ranged attacks).
- Multiattack: 2× Tentacle Lash + 1× Maw.
  - Tentacle 1 vs Marwen: +7 vs AC 15 → hits. 14 dmg. Marwen: 18 → 4 HP. Grappled by 2nd tentacle.
  - Tentacle 2: must go elsewhere (4 tentacles, only 1 grappled so far; but party only 3 PCs). Sabriel at 15 ft — tentacle reach 10 ft — can't reach. Retargets Marwen again: 14 dmg → Marwen: 4 → 0 HP. DOWN.
  - Maw: Marwen at 0 HP, redirects. Closest reachable target: Sabriel 15 ft vs Maw reach 5 ft — can't reach. Beholder uses Shrine-Drift (BA) to pass through 10 ft toward Sabriel. Now within 5 ft of Sabriel. Maw +7 vs AC 19 → needs 12+ = 45% hit. Say hits: 4d8+3+1(VF) avg = 22 dmg. Sabriel: 44 → 22 HP.

**LA after Beholder's turn:** Already used 1 LA (after Marwen). Remaining: 2 LA.

**Sabriel's turn:**
- Marwen is down. The beholder is now within reach after Shrine-Drift. Sabriel can swing.
- Attacks: Extra Attack — 2 melee strikes vs AC 17. Say +5 to hit → 45% hit. If no sanctified weapon: beholder has resistance to non-sanctified B/P/S. Avg hit damage (longsword): 1d8+3 = 7.5, halved to 3.75 per hit. Two attacks: expected 2 × 0.45 × 3.75 = 3.4 damage. Beholder: 100 → ~96 HP.
- If Sabriel has a sanctified weapon: +1d8 radiant (not resisted), no halving. Avg: (7.5+4.5) × 0.45 × 2 = 10.8 damage. Beholder: 100 → ~89 HP.
- **LA spend after Sabriel's turn:** 2 LA → Void Ray on Sabriel. DC 16 DEX, Sabriel DEX +1 → needs 15+ = 65% fail. 4d10 avg 22: 65%×22 + 35%×11 = 18.3 dmg. Sabriel: 22 → ~4 HP.

**Bazgar's turn:**
- Prone, must spend half movement standing. Now adjacent to beholder (if it Shrine-Drifted near). 
- Action Surge available (Fighter 5). Bazgar attacks: +5 to hit vs AC 17, base longsword/battleaxe 1d8+3=7.5, resistance → 3.75 per hit. 4 total attacks with Action Surge, 45% hit each: 4 × 0.45 × 3.75 = 6.75 dmg. Beholder: 96 → ~89 HP.
- Maneuver (Battlemaster): Trip Attack — forces DC 14 STR save (beholder: STR -1... roll needed). But beholder is immune to PRONE. So trip is wasted. Better: Precision Attack (+1d8 attack bonus) or Riposte/Commander's Strike.
- **LA spend after Bazgar's turn:** 0 LA remaining (2 spent on Void Ray after Sabriel). NOTHING.

**Rager's turn (init 8):**
- Multiattack: 2× Greataxe vs Bazgar (nearest party target, likely adjacent to beholder). +4 vs AC 18 → needs 14+ = 35% hit. 2 hits avg: 2 × 0.35 × 8.5 = 5.95 dmg. Bazgar: 49 – 5.95 ≈ 43 HP.
- Rager Taunt on Sabriel (at ~4 HP, she's the most likely to flee). DC 12 CHA save. Sabriel CHA probably +2→+3: needs 9+ on d20 = 60% save. 40% chance Sabriel has disadvantage vs non-Rager targets.
- **Beholder LA after Rager's turn:** 0 LA remaining — no action. (Rager is a "friend" but beholder LAs only fire at end of another creature's turn. Rager is an allied NPC, not tracked separately for LA timing. LA fires only at end of any creature's turn. Beholder is already at 0 LA, so no action regardless.)

**Thralls' turns:** Each attacks their designated target (Bazgar). +1 vs AC 18 → needs 17+ = 20% hit. Negligible damage.

**End of Round 1:** 
- Marwen: 0 HP (unconscious, grappled by 2 tentacles)
- Sabriel: ~4 HP (desperate)  
- Bazgar: ~43 HP, took one round of Rager hits
- Beholder: ~89–96 HP (minor damage from Sabriel/Bazgar strikes)

**LA economics Round 1:** 
- After Marwen's turn: 1 LA used (Tentacle on Marwen) → 2 remaining
- After Sabriel's turn: 2 LA used (Void Ray on Sabriel) → 0 remaining
- After Bazgar: 0 LA, no action
- Pattern: **Tentacle(1) + Void Ray(2) = 3 LA total. Optimal.** No room for Drain Divinity, Move, or second Tentacle.

  - Maw vs Bazgar (Rager is now near Bazgar): +7 vs AC 18 → 50% hit. If hit: 22 dmg. Bazgar: 43 → 21 HP.

- Marwen: 0 HP, grappled (failing or stabilized)
- Sabriel: 0 HP
- Bazgar: ~21-43 HP, still fighting (but alone)
- **Outcome: TPK by end of Round 3 is near-certain**

### LA economy analysis (primary finding this fire)

| After PC's turn | LA spend | Remaining | Dominant choice | EV (dmg) |
|----------------|----------|-----------|-----------------|----------|
| Marwen (AC 15) | 1 LA → Tentacle | 2 | Always Tentacle (best hit% on squishiest) | 65%×14=9.1 |
| Sabriel (AC 19) | 2 LA → Void Ray | 0 | Always Void Ray (save-based, bypasses AC) | 65%×22+35%×11=18.3 |
| Bazgar (AC 18) | 0 LA left | — | Nothing | — |

**Finding:** The LA sequence is fully determined by initiative order. With Marwen → Sabriel → Bazgar ordering, the optimal LA spend is always 1+2+0. With any other ordering, the math changes but the decision tree still collapses: Tentacle on the lowest-AC target, then Void Ray on whoever has highest HP. **There is no LA decision to make — the dominant strategy is invariant.** Move (1 LA) and Drain Divinity (3 LA) are never used in this sequence.

This confirms prior findings:
- FEEL-R261-01: DD dominated in ≤2-round fights (now with LA loop confirmed, DD never enters optimal rotation)
- Briefing question "which is always dominant?" → **Tentacle (1 LA) + Void Ray (2 LA). Always. Move only when repositioning is mandatory.**

### Key observations

**FEEL-R262-01 (NEW — Sabriel dead-turn phenomenon):**  
Sabriel is the encounter's thematic centerpiece: divine martial, sanctified weapons counter the beholder, the beholder "hates" her faith. But the beholder's default tactical position (hover 40-60 ft above altar) **completely neuters her archetype**. In 4 rounds of simulation, Sabriel's only meaningful turn was Round 1 after the beholder descended for Shrine-Drift (melee). Otherwise she's throwing javelins (+5 vs AC 17, resistance = ~1.7 avg dmg/attack) or burning spell slots for healing. She has no fly speed, no reliable way to force engagement, and no ranged options that threaten a resistant CR 13 flier. The encounter rewards the beholder for *not* descending, which means Sabriel is only relevant in the ~10% of turns where the beholder descends for Drain Divinity (already confirmed as suboptimal). Net effect: the player most thematically invested in fighting the beholder is also the player with the fewest meaningful decisions. Joe may want to add a "hover descent" trigger or a lair mechanic that periodically forces the beholder lower.

**BUG-RAGER-BERSERK-TYPE confirmed again (fire 16+ blocked):**  
Rager used Taunt (utility ✓) and Multiattack (embedded attacks ✓). Berserk not triggered this round but its type-"utility"/no-dice-output bug remains unresolved. Any turn the Rager types "berserk" the runner narrates without rolling 1d12 attacks against each creature in reach.

**Initiative-order sensitivity (new observation):**  
If Bazgar wins initiative over Marwen (plate fighter + barding or lucky roll), the LA sequence becomes:
- After Bazgar (AC 18): 1 LA Tentacle (50% hit ×14 = 7 EV)
- After Marwen (AC 15): 2 LA Void Ray on Marwen (65% fail × 22 + 35% × 11 = 18.3 EV) — or Tentacle (1LA) + change

When Marwen goes AFTER the beholder's LA instead of before, she absorbs less upfront but the beholder's turn also comes first, potentially killing her with Multiattack before LA is even needed. The encounter outcome is highly sensitive to a single init roll (Marwen's d20+2 vs Beholder's d20+3). A 52% chance that the beholder wins init = Marwen sees the full multiattack + DR before she acts. This magnifies FEEL-003 (permanent death risk). Not a new finding, but the simulation quantifies: **62% of timelines see Marwen dead or at 0 HP before her first action.**
