## Feel issues this fire

### FEEL-F9-01: DR turn-1 kill denies player agency entirely

DR on turn 1 (beholder wins initiative + DR recharged) is a ~43% event per run when DR starts recharged. In that scenario, Marwen acts 0 times before dying permanently. The disintegration clause (no body) prevents even the delayed recovery arc (downed → stabilized → revived by Sabriel).

This is mechanically valid — it's a CR 13 beholder vs a level-5 party. But it's the specific combination of:
1. Beholder has higher initiative than a +2-DEX caster ~65% of the time
2. DR is an attack roll (no partial mitigation via save)
3. Disintegration leaves no body (blocks Revivify, a L3 slot Sabriel would realistically have)

…that makes the outcome feel more like a random instant-kill than a lethal-but-playable encounter.

**Suggested DM mitigation (log only — do not edit source):** In the pre-combat description, establish that Marwen can start behind a collapsed shrine pillar (half cover, AC 17). This cuts DR's effective hit rate to 50% and gives Marwen a ~12% chance of surviving a hit (P(10d8 ≤ 15) after 2 damage is taken... actually no: half cover doesn't split damage, it just raises AC). With AC 17, P(10d8 ≥ 33) is still ~96% if the DR hits — the cover matters for avoiding the hit, not surviving it. Cover reduces the hit chance; it does not help once hit.

**Actual mitigation path:** Give Marwen a reaction option to avoid the DR. Nothing in current stat blocks provides this. This is a design note for Joe — possibly a party-side house rule (Shield spell? Beholder's Void-Feeding explicitly says divine healing spells hurt it, but doesn't say force-resist exists for the party).

### FEEL-F9-02: Void-Feeding hit rate inconsistency in tactics note (carry)

As noted in A9-05: the tactics note at MQ-FC51-B uses Void-Feeding (+7) for Marwen's 65% but base attack (+6) for Bazgar/Sabriel — and the Bazgar/Sabriel values are also computed with an off-by-one formula. Correct rates with Void-Feeding active (should always apply inside the shrine chamber):

| Target | AC | +7 to hit | Written | Error |
|--------|----|-----------|---------|-------|
| Marwen | 15 | 65% | 65% | ✓ |
| Bazgar | 18 | 50% | 40% | −10% |
| Sabriel | 19 | 45% | 35% | −10% |

DM reading the cheat sheet may deprioritize DR vs Bazgar ("only 40%") when the real threat rate is 50%. Not a combat-breaking error but a DM decision-support issue.
