## Feel issues this fire

### FEEL-A7-01: Permadowned Fighter Loop (new observation)

Bazgar was KO'd R1 by grapple+Maw-autocrit (39 pierce, avg ~2× his max HP worth), revived by Sabriel LoH at 10 HP, then KO'd again R2 by a non-auto-crit Maw hit (21 pierce to 20 HP). Two consecutive full KOs with no offensive contribution.

The loop is partially the design: Sabriel's LoH costs her whole action while the beholder recovers the opportunity cost with a single Maw swing. Net result: one LoH charge consumed, Bazgar incapacitated again, Sabriel is now one action behind on her own positioning.

**Specific counterplay gap**: Bazgar's Battlemaster features (Parry, Riposte, Precision Attack) are all reaction- or bonus-action-based. When he is grappled + prone, he cannot stand up (grappled = speed 0), cannot escape + attack in the same action, and has no Battlemaster reaction that triggers on being hit by a melee attack. His class features are structurally invisible in this fight.

**Not necessarily a bug** — the encounter is designed as a loss. But if the player brings a Battlemaster Fighter, they should *feel* their class features exist. Possible DM mitigation: allow Parry to trigger against the Maw's auto-crit (it fires after the attack roll succeeds, before damage is rolled — valid timing).

**Status**: Log only. Design decision.

### FEEL-A7-02: Void Scream recharge starvation (carry from EV7-B, fire #1+)

VS did not recharge in 3 rounds. P(no VS in 3R) = (5/6)³ ≈ 58%. In solo-beholder scenarios (EV7 slice), VS fires less than half the time over 5 rounds. The beholder's "most dramatic" ability is absent in the majority of fights.

**Status**: Log only. Carry from DD-EV7-B. Recommend Recharge 5–6.

### FEEL-A7-03: Drain Divinity — confirmed working but ~35% whiff rate at DC 16

DD fired correctly this run (Sabriel failed CHA save). From FC-cycle 52: DD missed twice in a row (two CHA passes at 16+). Against Sabriel CHA +3, the pass rate is 40%+ — meaning 60%+ of the time DD consumes all 3 LA and fails.

**Specific math**: DC 16 vs CHA +3 → need d20 ≥ 13 to save → (8/20) = 40% pass. DD whiffs 40% per cast. Over 3 attempts (R1, R2, R3): P(all whiff) = 0.4³ ≈ 6%; P(at least one whiff) = ~78%. The single-use-per-round DD means one whiff is a round of 0 legendary action value.

**Status**: Log only. Carry from FI-FC52-C. Recommend DC 14 or reduce LA cost to 2.
