## Phase C — Feel Issues and Open Bugs

### FEEL-R296-01 — Void Scream + Lair Action is pre-emptively lethal on Round 1
**Severity: feel/design. Log only.**

In the "all three PCs converge" setup, the beholder wins before the party acts. The lair action fires at init 20, then the beholder uses Void Scream as its first action. Marwen (32 HP) cannot survive the combination under any plausible roll scenario. Sabriel goes down 75% of the time before she acts.

The encounter is "meant to lose" by design, but the design intent seems to be *a hard fight with clear danger*, not *the party is incapacitated before making a decision*. One-round kills before any PC turn cross the "unfair rather than lethal" line the playtest is watching for.

**Possible mitigations (for Joe to decide):**
- Beholder holds Void Scream for round 2 (tactical: grapple first, then scream when party is clustered and grappled).
- Cap Void Scream damage at half Marwen's max HP (16) — makes it a near-death hit rather than a guaranteed down.
- Reduce VS from 6d10 to 5d10 (avg 27.5) — Marwen at 32 HP survives a failed save by 4 HP.
- Lair action: use Unstable Ground (single target prone) instead of Void Eruption on round 1, so the party has a softer entry.

### FEEL-R296-02 — Drain Divinity legendary economy (reaffirm, still open)
**Status: previously logged, still needs Joe's decision.**

Drain Divinity costs 3 LA = full legendary budget every round. Against Sabriel (divine martial), the beholder would use it each round if possible, completely suppressing any divine resource regen. Against Marwen it's equally effective (strips 3rd-level spell slots rapidly). No cooldown or minimum-cost floor exists. The degenerate play is: beholder spends 3 LA on Drain Divinity every round vs the divine martial → Sabriel cannot cast or channel → fight devolves to beholder vs two martial/arcane PCs who lose without divine support.

No change needed if Joe intends "drain the paladin and watch the rest collapse." Flag if this isn't the intent.
