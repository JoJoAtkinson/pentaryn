### Key findings

**FEEL-R108-01 — Marwen one-shot after acting:**  
Marwen went first (cast Fireball), then the beholder's first multiattack dropped her from 32 HP to –3 in a single action. The grapple mechanic prevents escape before the Maw lands in the same action sequence. Even with Lay on Hands bringing her to 1 HP, a single Void Ray LA finishes her next round with no counterplay. The wizard never meaningfully influences combat after Round 1. This is "unfair rather than lethal": she acts once and is removed from the fight for the remainder. Recommended fix: consider splitting Tentacle-then-Maw into separate turns (or giving the Maw a speed requirement that can't be met in the same action), or reducing Maw damage to give a 32-HP wizard a survivable Tentacle hit.

**FEEL-R108-02 — Drain Divinity consumes all 3 LA every round, Move and Tentacle never fire (R4 confirmation):**  
DD costs 3 LA = entire budget. While Sabriel has any spell slot, DD is strictly dominant over Void Ray (2 LA) and Tentacle (1 LA). Move (1 LA) never fires at all. The only time the beholder uses Void Ray is *after* DD has fired and 0 LA remain for the rest of the round, but by Round 2 Sabriel is down so DD can't fire again. The LA economy reads as: DD every round → Void Ray mop-up → Tentacle never used as LA → Move never used. Recommend: increase DD cost to 3 LA only against 3rd+ level slots, or give DD a once-per-round hard cap so Tentacle/Move can be part of the rotation.

**FEEL (re-confirm) — Void Scream FRIGHTENED rider:**  
The DB and cheat sheet both list the FRIGHTENED rider on Void Scream (1 min, DC 16 WIS save ends). If it had fired in Round 2 (beholder holding Marwen within 10 ft of shrine → disadvantage), Marwen would have been FRIGHTENED before going down — which means the rider would apply to a PC already unconscious. The rider needs to be in the .md so DMs know to track it. Currently absent.

**FEEL (re-confirm) — Disintegration Ray / Void Scream did not fire:**  
Two rounds, two rolls of 1d6 for recharge 5–6 (DR) and 6 (VS). Neither recharged (statistically: ~50% chance DR fires once over 2 rounds; VS ~31%). The combat ended before either could contribute. When beholder kills via multiattack + LA alone, recharge abilities feel like dead weight. This is consistent with past runs.
