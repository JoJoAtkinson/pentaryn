## Phase B — `thrulm-shrine-touched-retaliation` Simulation

**Scenario:** shrine-touched takes 12 damage → Unstable Form (10+ threshold) + Oath-Breaking Retaliation.

**Initiative order (rolled):** Sabriel 17, Marwen 14, Shrine-Touched 12, Bazgar 9

### Round 1 play-by-play

**Sabriel (HP 44, AC 19) attacks shrine-touched (HP 45, AC 16):**
- Longsword +6 vs AC 16 → hits on 10+ (55%). Hit (d20=10 → 16).
- Damage: 1d8+3 = 8 avg. Shrine-Touched HP: 45 → 37.
- 8 damage < 10 threshold → Unstable Form does NOT activate.
- **Oath-Breaking Retaliation fires** (reaction, any visible damage source).
  - Shrine-Axe +4 vs Sabriel AC 19 → needs 15+ (30% hit). Miss (d20=9 → 13). No damage to Sabriel.
  - Shrine-touched self-damage: 1d4 psychic (avg 2). HP: 37 → 35.
  - **DB-BUG-R156-01 observed**: runner would narrate without rolling Shrine-Axe dice. DM must roll manually.

**Marwen (HP 32, AC 15) attacks shrine-touched (HP 35) — Scorching Ray:**
- 3 rays, +5 to hit vs AC 16, each needs 11+ (55%). Expected 1.65 hits; say 2 hit.
- Shrine-Touched is **vulnerable to fire** → each ray does 2 × (avg 7) = 14 damage.
- 2 rays × 14 = 28 fire damage. HP: 35 → 7.
- 28 damage in one turn → **Unstable Form activates** for shrine-touched's upcoming turn.
- Reaction already spent (OBR vs Sabriel) — no OBR for Marwen's attacks.

**Shrine-Touched (HP 7, init 12, Unstable Form active):**
- Reaction has reset (resets at start of own turn).
- Multiattack: two Shrine-Axe attacks with **advantage** (Unstable Form).
- Targets Sabriel (AC 19). +4 to hit with advantage → ~51% hit per attack.
- Say 1 hit: 1d8+1 slashing + 1d8 necrotic = 9 avg. Sabriel HP: 44 → 35.
- Second swing misses.

**Bazgar (HP 49, AC 18) attacks shrine-touched (HP 7, AC 16):**
- Greatsword +6, 2 attacks. First hit (16 → hit). 2d6+3 = 10 avg. HP: 7 → dead.
- **OBR question**: Shrine-Axe reaction fires on the killing blow. Target is dead but reaction resolves first in RAW?  
  - Answer: Shrine-Touched is reduced to 0 HP by the attack. OBR is a REACTION to taking damage — it fires before the creature falls unconscious (per RAW, reactions can fire on the damage event before the creature dies). If DM allows OBR on a killing blow: +4 vs AC 18, needs 14+ (35% hit). Shrine-touched self-damage (1d4) is moot (already dead).
  - **DB-BUG-R156-01 observed again**: second instance in same turn — no dice in DB row.

### Findings from Phase B simulation

**FEEL-SHRINE-R78-01 — Unstable Form is mostly pre-empted by fire vulnerability**  
Marwen's Scorching Ray (fire, × vulnerability) can deal 28+ expected damage in one turn,
taking shrine-touched from full to near-death before it gets to use Unstable Form + multiattack.
The shrine-touched's Unstable Form advantage fires once (Round 1, init 12) but only because
Marwen went first. If Marwen acts AFTER shrine-touched, the shrine-touched has no advantage.
If Marwen acts BEFORE, the shrine-touched's advantage-multiattack turn starts at HP 7 → the
attack is largely ceremonial. The power spike is real but the timing window is extremely narrow.
**Recommendation:** Raise shrine-touched HP to 60 (10d8+20) to widen the Unstable Form window,
or trigger Unstable Form as a free Shrine-Axe reaction attack on the same hit that deals 10+
damage (replaces or supplements OBR). Joe's call.

**FEEL-SHRINE-R78-02 — OBR misses >70% vs AC 18-19 party; self-damage is near net-neutral**  
+4 to hit vs AC 18 = 35% hit rate. Expected OBR damage = 0.35 × 9 = 3.2 avg.
Self-damage = avg 2.5 (1d4). Net value to shrine-touched: +0.7 avg damage per OBR use.
The reaction is almost not worth using vs Bazgar or Sabriel. Only strong against Marwen (AC 15):
+4 vs 15 = 50% hit → net +2. Thematically the self-destructive read is intentional, but
mechanically OBR contributes almost nothing to the fight. Consider: remove self-damage if OBR
hits, or bump attack bonus to +5 (STR modifier shift from +1 to +2 would achieve this without
touching PB). Joe's call.

**DB-BUG-R156-01 (re-confirmed, second time this run)** — `oath_breaking_retaliation` has
no embedded attack dice. Runner narrates "Pain becomes prophecy..." but no roll is shown. The
DM must mentally remember to roll 1d8+1 slashing + 1d8 necrotic separately. This fires twice
in a single-shrine-touched scenario (on Sabriel's hit and on Bazgar's killing blow). High-friction.
Cannot fix unattended — requires interactive MCP session with `combat_action_upsert`.

**FEEL-SHRINE-R78-03 — Initiative trap: OBR burns reaction before shrine-touched can multiattack**  
When party members act before shrine-touched in initiative, OBR fires (burning the reaction) and
then the shrine-touched's Unstable Form multiattack loses the free reaction it could have used for
OBR. When party members act after shrine-touched, OBR fires as a surprise but the shrine-touched
has already used its action. The two features (OBR + Unstable Form) do not synergize; they
compete for the "fight harder when hurt" slot. This is mechanical, not a bug — but it means the
shrine-touched rarely executes its "designed peak" moment of: take damage → OBR counter → act
with advantage → two boosted hits. Usually it's: take damage → OBR miss → act at low HP with
advantage → one hit → die.

**OBR on killing blow (rules gap)** — RAW is ambiguous about whether OBR fires on a hit that
reduces shrine-touched to 0 HP. Under strict 5e, the creature falls unconscious when it hits 0 HP;
reactions fire "in response to a trigger" which in this case is the damage event, which resolves
before the creature falls. Most DMs rule OBR fires (the trigger is the damage, not the death).
This should be clarified in the .md as a DM ruling note. LOG-ONLY.
