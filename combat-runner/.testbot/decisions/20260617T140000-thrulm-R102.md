## Phase B — Generative Playtest: `thrulm-dd-hover-range-descent`

### Scenario

Beholder at default hover altitude (50 ft above the shrine floor). Party enters at the far end of the chamber
(60 ft from altar). Beholder attempts Drain Divinity (3 LA, 30-ft range). Core question: is DD reachable from
hover without explicit descent?

### Geometry setup

- Beholder starting position: directly above the altar, 50 ft altitude.
- Party entry point: 60 ft horizontal from altar (threshold end of the main vault).
- Distance from beholder to party: √(60² + 50²) ≈ **78 ft**.
- Beholder combined movement per turn: fly 30 ft + Shrine-Drift BA 30 ft = **60 ft maximum**.
- Conclusion: **the beholder CANNOT close to melee range on R1 from default hover vs. a party at threshold**.
  60 ft of movement < 78 ft distance. It needs ~70 ft to get within TL reach (10 ft); it has 60 ft available.
  This is a significant gap in the R1 multiattack protocol.

### Initiative (simulated)

| Creature | Roll + mod | Init |
|----------|-----------|------|
| Lair action | — | 20 |
| Beholder | 14 + 3 | 17 |
| Marwen | 15 + 2 | 17 (goes after beholder on tie) |
| Sabriel | 11 + 1 | 12 |
| Bazgar | 8 − 1 | 7 |

### Round 1

**Lair (init 20):** Unstable Ground → Bazgar (DC 16 DEX, Bazgar DEX −1 → needs 17+ → 20% success).
Bazgar fails → **prone**. (Good lair action use regardless of beholder movement constraints.)

**Beholder (init 17):** At 78 ft from the party, options:

- **Option A (descend + melee):** Shrine-Drift BA (30 ft descent) + fly 30 ft → total 60 ft movement.
  Reaches 18 ft from Bazgar. TL reach = 10 ft. **Still can't melee by 8 ft.** Full movement is
  insufficient. The beholder is stuck at 18 ft range — tentacles can't reach.

- **Option B (stay at altitude, Disintegration Ray):** +7 vs Marwen AC 15 = 65% hit. All three PCs are
  within 120 ft range. Say roll 13 → 20 → **hit**. 45 (10d8) force damage. Marwen 32 HP → **0 HP,
  disintegrated**. Beholder holds all 3 LA.

- **Option B is the only viable R1 choice.** The beholder cannot reach melee this round. Ranged attack on
  the lowest-AC target (Marwen, AC 15) is correct.

**Hold 3 LA:** After Marwen's death, Bazgar (init 7) hasn't acted yet. Sabriel (init 12) acts next.

**Marwen:** Disintegrated. No action.

**Sabriel (init 12):** Marwen is dead and gone (no body — LoH can't target ash). Sabriel's options:
- Attack the beholder at 50 ft altitude. She has ranged capability? A divine martial (Paladin) has:
  - Longsword (melee, 5 ft) — can't reach.
  - No ranged weapons assumed.
  - Divine Smite requires a melee hit.
  - Likely can't deal damage this turn; she might use Sacred Flame (DC 13 DEX vs beholder DEX +3 = 65%
    success rate for beholder → negligible threat), or move toward the beholder.
  - Sabriel moves 30 ft closer. Now 48 ft from beholder. Still out of melee.

**After Sabriel's turn: DD fires (3 LA).**
- Beholder is at 50 ft altitude; Sabriel has moved to a point 48 ft away (horizontal 30 ft + vertical 50 ft).
  DD range = 30 ft. Distance to Sabriel = √(30² + 50²) ≈ 58 ft. **DD is out of range.**
- Even if Sabriel charged at full 30 ft movement, she's still 48 ft from the beholder. DD cannot fire.
- **3 LA budget held all round — WASTED.** There is no legal DD target in R1.

**Bazgar (init 7):** Prone. Can stand up (half movement = 15 ft used to stand). Then moves toward beholder.
Throws a handaxe if Battlemaster has it? Most likely has no ranged option → frustrated.

**End of Round 1:** Marwen dead (disintegrated), 3 LA wasted, beholder untouched, party closing.

### Round 2

**Lair (init 20):** Beholder has descended 30 ft via fly movement at end of R1 (start of R2 it refreshes LA
and has descended to 20 ft altitude as part of movement). Unstable Ground → Sabriel (nearest conscious PC,
Bazgar is standing after prone). DC 16 DEX, Sabriel DEX +0 → 20% success rate. Sabriel fails → **prone**.

**Beholder (init 17):** Now at 20 ft altitude. Party has closed. Bazgar at ~18 ft from beholder (he moved
after standing). Sabriel is ~15 ft away (she moved 30 ft R1 + her R2 movement).

Now within fly+SD reach of the party. Options:
- **Shrine-Drift (BA, 30 ft descent)** to floor → then Multiattack (TL×2 + Maw).
  - Distance to Bazgar: ~18 ft - 20 ft of SD descent movement still leaves them at ~15 ft range.
    Actually: if beholder descends 20 ft (to floor) and has 30 ft SD = 30 ft total movement toward Bazgar.
    Bazgar is 18 ft from beholder's current position at 20 ft altitude → within reach after SD.
  - TL1 vs Bazgar (prone, advantage): +6 adv vs AC 18 → 75% hit → say hit for **13 bludg, grappled**.
  - TL2 vs Bazgar (grappled, prone, advantage): +6 adv → say hit for **13 bludg**. Maw auto-crit.
  - Maw vs grappled Bazgar: 8d8+3 avg **39 pierce**.
  - Total R2 damage on Bazgar: 13 + 13 + 39 = **65 dmg**. Bazgar HP 49 → **0 HP, downed**.
- Hold 3 LA for DD post-Sabriel. But SD used → no Compel Thrall this turn.

**After Sabriel's turn (init 12):** DD fires. Beholder is now at floor level, within 30 ft of Sabriel?
Sabriel moved 30 ft R1 + some R2 movement. If she's near the altar (~10-15 ft from altar = 10-15 ft from
beholder on the floor), DD is in range. DC 16 CHA save, Sabriel +5 (PB +3 + CHA +2, Paladin proficiency).
**50% success rate.** On fail: loses highest spell slot (L3 smite), beholder gains 6 temp HP.
On success: 3 LA wasted, nothing.

**Bazgar (init 7):** Downed.

**End of R2:** Marwen dead (R1), Bazgar downed (R2). Sabriel alone, likely also in serious trouble.
The encounter is a TPK trajectory — as intended for CR 13 vs level 5 party.

### Feel Analysis

**FEEL-R102-01 (NEW): Default hover altitude makes R1 melee impossible from threshold entry.**

At 50 ft hover above the altar with party at the threshold (60 ft from altar), beholder-to-party distance
is ~78 ft. Total available movement (fly 30 ft + Shrine-Drift BA 30 ft) = 60 ft. The beholder is short by
~18 ft of reaching melee. R1 multiattack is physically impossible without a closer starting position.

The existing R1 protocol (FIX-FC40-A) and all Phase B simulations in prior fires implicitly assume the party
is already within melee range at the start of combat. This assumption holds ONLY if the party has advanced
to within ~30 ft of the altar before initiative rolls (distance: √(30² + 50²) ≈ 58 ft; 60 ft movement
covers 58 ft). If the party opens at the threshold, the beholder's R1 is forced to ranged-only (DR or VS).

**Recommendation:** Add a DM note to the beholder's tactics section: "If the party opens at the vault
threshold (≥60 ft from altar), the beholder CANNOT reach melee on R1. Use Disintegration Ray on Marwen
(+7 vs AC 15, 65% hit, avg 45 force) and hold fly movement to begin descending. Multiattack becomes
available R2 once the party has closed or the beholder has descended."

**FEEL-R102-02 (NEW): Shrine-Drift (BA) used for descent forecloses Compel Thrall — undocumented.**

When the beholder uses Shrine-Drift (BA) on its turn to descend toward melee range, Compel Thrall (BA)
is unavailable that turn. Both are bonus actions. The tactics note (FEEL-R93-03, prior fire) flags this
conflict for the R1 descent-then-DD sequence, but it is NOT documented in the beholder's Actions section
or in the Shrine-Drift note. A DM reading the .md sees two bonus actions with no mutual-exclusion flag
between them.

**Recommendation:** Add to Shrine-Drift note: "⚠ Bonus action conflict: using Shrine-Drift this turn
prevents Compel Thrall (both are bonus actions). Choose one or the other each turn — if descending to
melee range, CT is unavailable until next turn."

**FEEL-R102-03 (NEW): 3 LA held for DD are wasted if party stays at threshold.**

If the beholder can't reach melee on R1, its R1 action is DR (ranged). The R1-LA-reserve protocol
(FIX-FC40-A) says to hold all 3 LA for DD post-Sabriel. But Sabriel at the threshold is 58+ ft from
the beholder at altitude — DD range = 30 ft. She is out of range. The 3 LA are held, then wasted,
because DD can never legally fire against a threshold-entry Sabriel without R1 descent.

The correct R1 play when party is at threshold:
- R1: DR on Marwen (ranged, no descent). Hold 3 LA → but DD is out of range after Sabriel's turn.
  → After Sabriel acts (without moving toward beholder), DD can't fire → wasted.
- OR: R1: Use fly 30 ft to descend to 20 ft altitude. DD range from 20 ft altitude to floor Sabriel
  (30 ft horizontal) = √(30² + 20²) ≈ 36 ft. Still > 30 ft DD range.
  → Only DR at range is viable R1 if party stays back.

**Implication:** Against a cautious party that stays at the threshold, the DD protocol cannot fire in R1
regardless of beholder hover altitude. The beholder must wait until the party closes, OR fire DD on R2
after using a full turn's movement to descend. This effectively delays DD by one full round.

**Recommendation:** Add to R1 LA reserve protocol (FIX-FC40-A): "RANGE PRE-CHECK — before holding
3 LA for DD post-Sabriel, verify Sabriel is within 30 ft. If party is at threshold (≥60 ft from altar),
DD range is not achievable from default hover. In this case: spend R1 LAs on Tentacle+Void Ray
(1+2 LA) after available PCs act; reload full LA budget R2 once descended."

**FEEL-R102-04 (CONFIRM BUG-A12-06): Scenario confirms DD hover-range gap is a real table error.**

The scenario `thrulm-dd-hover-range-descent` was added specifically to test DM awareness of the range gap
(BUG-A12-06, 66+ fires open). This simulation confirms it: the beholder's DB effect text says "within 30
ft" but does NOT enforce range. A DM running the encounter for the first time will attempt DD from altitude
and produce an illegal action. The runner will not prevent it.

BUG-A12-06 remains open pending Joe's decision on whether to: (a) add a range-enforcement note to the
DB entry, (b) add a DM prompt in the beholder .md tactics, or (c) add a range pre-check to the runner.

**Legendary action economy:**

R1 example showed: beholder holds 3 LA (correct per protocol) but DD is out of range (wrong outcome).
The only time R1 DD fires cleanly is when: (1) the beholder descends before Sabriel's turn, AND (2)
Sabriel has advanced to within 30 ft of the beholder's post-descent position. Both conditions require
the party to be aggressive rather than cautious. Against a cautious Sabriel who stays at range, DD
never fires in R1.

Void Ray (2 LA) remains a better R1 LA spend when DD is out of range: 120 ft range, DC 16 DEX, 22 avg
force. Against Bazgar (DEX −1, 80% failure): 0.8 × 22 = 17.6 expected HP per LA-2 spend.
