### Supplementary Phase B — Beholder LA Economy: R1–R4 degradation pattern

*Context: Phase B asks to evaluate "which LA is always dominant?" The fire #56 simulation covered DD hover/range. This supplement covers the LA economy collapse pattern in R3–R4 once Sabriel's slots are exhausted.*

**Representative combat state (post-R2):**

| PC | HP (post-R2) | Key resource |
|----|-------------|-------------|
| Bazgar | ~25 HP (revived R1 by Sabriel LoH, hit again R2) | Second Wind spent |
| Marwen | ~18 HP (Shardcaller Shard-Barrage R1, Tentacle-LA R2) | L3 slot spent (Fireball R1) |
| Sabriel | ~32 HP (took TL damage R2, healed Bazgar R1) | L2 slots: 0 remaining (DD stripped R1, Sabriel smited once R2) |

**Beholder post-R2 state:**
- HP: ~73 (Fireball 19 avg R1, Sabriel smite 14 avg R2)
- Void Scream: recharged (not yet fired — R1-R2 reserved for grapple/DD per protocol)
- LA pool: fresh 3 LA (R3 start)

---

**R3 LA decision tree:**

With Sabriel at 0 L2+ slots, Drain Divinity has no legal target (no slots to strip). The beholder must pivot to VR + Tentacle rotation:

| Option | LA cost | Expected value vs current party |
|--------|---------|--------------------------------|
| Void Ray at Marwen (AC 15, DEX +2 save) | 2 LA | DC 16 DEX: P(fail) ≈ 40%. Avg damage: 0.6×11 + 0.4×22 = 6.6+8.8 = **15.4 HP**. Marwen at 18 HP → 40% chance of instant KO |
| Tentacle Lash at Bazgar or Sabriel | 1 LA | +7 vs AC 18 (50%) or AC 19 (45%). Grapple sets up Maw autocrit on beholder's next full turn |
| Move | 1 LA | Shrine-Drift (BA) is strictly better; Move is dead weight |
| Drain Divinity at Sabriel | 3 LA | **Illegal** — Sabriel has no L2+ slots |

**R3 optimal sequence:** VR at Marwen (2 LA) + Tentacle at Bazgar (1 LA). This matches a mechanical rote — no strategic decision space after R2.

---

**LA Economy Degradation — new feel flag (FEEL-F57-01):**

The beholder LA toolkit has a hard phase transition at "Sabriel loses her last L2+ slot":
- **Before:** DD is the mandatory optimal (3 LA, strips smite, anti-divine)
- **After:** VR + Tentacle is the mandatory optimal (2+1 LA, pure damage)

This transition is abrupt and one-directional — there is no escalation, no creative use of Move, no situation where the beholder's decision feels interesting after R2. The LA pool in R3+ functions as "fire VR at the squishiest target, fire Tentacle for setup." This is mechanically valid but narratively flat for a CR 13 boss encounter.

**Specific issue:** Move LA is dead weight throughout the entire encounter. The beholder has Shrine-Drift (30 ft bonus action, phase-through objects, no OA) which is strictly superior to Move LA (fly speed only, no phase). Shrine-Drift makes Move LA redundant in all scenarios. In 57 playtest fires, Move LA appears in 0 optimal lines.

**Recommendation (log only — Joe decides):** Replace Move LA with something that introduces a third decision axis. Candidates:
- (a) **Siphon Void (1 LA):** The beholder targets one grappled creature — that creature loses one use of a class feature (Rage, Ki, Superiority Die, Channel Divinity, etc.). Creates different DD-parallel pressure without stacking with DD.
- (b) **Void Pulse (1 LA):** All creatures within 10 ft of the beholder must make a DC 14 CON save or lose concentration. Rewards beholder staying near a caster.
- (c) Keep Move but add a rider: "Move and force each creature the beholder moves through to make DC 14 DEX save or take 1d6 force damage (beholder is immune to the Shrine-Drift self-damage on LA movement)."

None of these should be implemented unilaterally — Joe designs the mechanic. This is a feel flag only.
