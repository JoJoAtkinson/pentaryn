**Lair action at init 20:** Unstable Ground → Bazgar (Dex -1 vs DC 16 → ~85% fail) → Bazgar prone.

**R1 — Beholder:** Uses Shrine-Drift (BA, 30 ft down) + full fly move (30 ft down) to
descend from 50-ft hover to floor level. Multiattack vs prone Bazgar:
- Tentacle 1 (advantage, +7 vs AC 18, 75% hit): hit → 14 bludgeoning (13 base +1 Void-Feeding); Bazgar grappled.
- Tentacle 2 (advantage, +7 vs AC 18, 75% hit): hit → 14 bludgeoning; Bazgar grappled (×2).

### Feel assessment

**Working as designed** — the party cannot win this encounter. Every damage sequence is
lethal and follows established synergies (UG→prone→advantage→grapple→Maw autocrit).

**No unfair-before-first-turn moments:** Bazgar acts (R1 init 9) AFTER the beholder attacks
(init 20). Bazgar never gets a turn before being downed. This is _lethal but not unfair_ —
the party had low initiative, and the beholder acting first is a known characteristic (Dex +3 +
Void-Feeding awareness). A high-Dex party member would change the dynamics.

**Maw autocrit is the kill:** Average multiattack damage = 14 (T1) + 14 (T2) + 39 (Maw crit) = 67.
Bazgar's 49 HP is 73% of that — he's reliably one-shot by a full multiattack if both tentacles hit.
This is the design intent but worth noting: Bazgar's HP threshold (49) is below the "avg for 2 hits
before Maw" threshold (28), meaning both tentacles could kill Bazgar outright before Maw fires,
wasting the autocrit. DMs should be aware: if the party has a fighter below 29 HP, direct Maw at
a different grappled target.

**FEEL-F78-01 (NEW): R1 multiattack from default hover requires Shrine-Drift BA for descent**  
The beholder's stat block notes it "hovers near the shrine altar (40-60 ft up)" per cheat sheet.
At 50 ft altitude, Tentacle Lash (reach 10 ft) cannot hit floor targets. Closing to melee in R1
requires full fly movement (30 ft) + Shrine-Drift BA (30 ft) = 60 ft combined — just enough to
reach floor from 50-60 ft hover. **Consequence:** Compel Thrall (BA) is unavailable in R1 because
Shrine-Drift consumed the bonus action for descent. The tactics section (FIX-EV7-32-A: "R1 =
Multiattack") does NOT document this descent constraint or its cost. DMs who don't burn Shrine-Drift
for descent will find themselves out of Tentacle Lash range in R1 and defaulting to Disintegration
Ray or Void Ray — changing the encounter opening dramatically.
- **Suggested fix:** Add a note to the Tactics "Environmental Use" section:  
  *"R1 descent — if starting at 40-60 ft altitude: use Shrine-Drift (BA, 30 ft) + full fly
  movement (30 ft) to close to floor level. This costs the Shrine-Drift bonus action; Compel
  Thrall is unavailable on the descent turn. Do NOT open with DR or VS from altitude when
  the multiattack grapple plan is live — descend first, then multiattack."*
- This is log-only (tactics note update); does NOT require auto-fix to the stat block.
