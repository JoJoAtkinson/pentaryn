**1. BUG-R197-01 (CONFIRMED — HIGH):** `drain_divinity` type "utility" → CHA save not rolled. Runner narrates but DM must manually adjudicate save. Scenario step `"drain"` fires narration only.

**2. BUG-R184-01 (CONFIRMED):** Maw "disadvantage on the saving throw" rider — no save exists. Still orphaned at 15 fires.

**4. FEEL-R197-03 (CONFIRMED — HIGH):** Drain Divinity fires at end of Bazgar's turn (init 18) before Sabriel's first action (init 14). Sabriel loses her 3rd-level slot before acting, then her 2nd-level slot in Round 2 before acting. By Round 3 she has only 1st-level slots. The "pre-action tax" pattern is severe: both Drain Divinity uses happen before the divine caster can spend those slots.

**5. FEEL-R188-03 (CONFIRMED):** On both LA opportunities, beholder used Drain Divinity (3 LA) instead of Void Ray (2 LA) + Tentacle (1 LA). The EV of Drain Divinity vs Sabriel (lose one spell slot, beholder gains THP) is low: losing a 3rd-level slot blocks ~7 HP of future healing (Cure Wounds level 3 avg 17.5 HP — but Sabriel as divine martial likely uses slots for Divine Smite, not healing). Void Ray (22 force, Bazgar DEX +1 → 75% fail → 16.5 expected) + Tentacle (65% hit → 9.1 expected) = 25.6 expected damage from same 3 LA budget, far higher EV. This is FEEL-R188-03 (EV ≈7 vs ≈28.8) confirmed again. Drain Divinity dominates for thematic/mechanical feel reasons but is quantifiably suboptimal.

**6. Void Ray → Marwen (0 HP) at end of Round 3:** Beholder switched to Void Ray once Sabriel had no high slots remaining (Drain Divinity value collapses when only 1st-level slots are left). This is the natural transition the beholder should make: after 2 rounds of slot-drain, revert to raw damage. The tactics text doesn't describe this transition point explicitly. Worth noting as tactical documentation gap.

**7. Marwen threat timeline confirmed:** Marwen (32 HP) is brought to 0 HP in Round 1 via Tentacle (15) + Maw (22) = 37 total damage in one multiattack. ≤2 rounds confirmed. Scenario note question met.
