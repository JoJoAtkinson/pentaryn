### New findings this fire

**FEEL-R274-01 — Unstable Form threshold effectively always triggers at party level 5**

Unstable Form activates when shrine-touched takes 10+ damage in a single turn. At party level 5:
- Any Paladin Divine Smite hit: 1d8+3 slashing + 2d8 radiant avg = 16.5 ≥ 10. Always triggers.
- Any Fireball: minimum on success (half, and shrine-touched is fire-vulnerable): see above — 28+ to shrine-touched. Always triggers.
- Bazgar BM multiattack: first hit avg 7.5 slashing. Only one hit needed to trigger if second hit also lands: 7.5+7.5=15 ≥ 10. Or if he uses a Superiority Die (Precision/Trip): avg 9.5 single hit is below 10, but two attacks totaling 15 is above.

**In practice:** Unstable Form activates after the very first time the party focuses the shrine-touched (Round 1). It is essentially permanent for all subsequent turns. The threshold (10+) may be too low for level 5 opponents — consider raising to 15+ or adding a cap ("once per combat") to preserve the design intention as a dramatic escalation rather than a passive always-on.

**This is a new feel finding — not in prior open items.**

**CS-OMIT-R274-01 — Oath-Breaking Retaliation absent from COMBAT-CHEAT-SHEET**

The reaction is mechanically significant (free attack on any damage hit, plus self-damage) but does not appear anywhere in COMBAT-CHEAT-SHEET.md:

- DERRO TYPES table: shows "Key Action" as "Ancient Resonance" only. No reaction listed.
- Round-by-round checklist "Shrine-Touched?" → mentions recharge and Ancient Resonance only.
- ONE-LINER TACTICS "Shrine-Touched": no mention of reaction.

A DM running this encounter without the stat block open will not know to prompt the reaction after every damage hit. At the table, this is easy to miss — the shrine-touched silently loses a significant mechanic.

**Recommended addition to COMBAT-CHEAT-SHEET:**
- DERRO TYPES "Key Action" column: append "(also: **Oath-Breaking Retaliation** — reaction on any hit, free Shrine-Axe back, 1d4 psychic self-damage)"
- Round-by-round checklist "Shrine-Touched?" entry: add "REACTION: Oath-Breaking Retaliation fires on every damage hit (once/round) — don't forget to prompt."

**Not auto-fixable (content addition). Log only.**

**CS-OMIT-R274-02 — Unstable Form tracking missing from round-by-round checklist**

The checklist entry for Shrine-Touched ("Ancient Resonance (Recharge 5–6) on groups, melee on solo targets; track recharge each turn") has no mention of tracking Unstable Form. DMs who don't have the stat block open will miss the advantage trigger.

**Recommended addition:** "Also track: if shrine-touched took 10+ damage in ONE turn → **advantage on attacks** until end of its next turn (Unstable Form)."
