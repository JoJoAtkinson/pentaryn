## Phase B — Generative Playtest: `solo-rager-rush`

**Encounter config:** 3 Derro Ragers (HP 52 each, AC 16, half-plate) vs full party. No beholder, no support units. This is a formation stress test, not a balanced encounter — designed to probe rager sub-system mechanics.

**XP check:** 3 × 450 = 1350 XP × 2 (3-monster multiplier for 3 PCs) = **2700 effective XP — Deadly** for a L5 party (threshold ~1700 XP). Paper threat is real; Fireball changes the math drastically.

### Initiative (representative rolls)

| Init | Actor |

### Round 1

**Init 17 (Marwen):** Fireball (3rd level, 8d6 avg 28 fire, DC 15) centered on the rager cluster advancing in formation. All 3 ragers within 20-ft radius burst.

- Rager-A/B/C: DEX+0 vs DC 15. Needs 15+. 30% save rate (70% fail). Each rager expected: 0.70×28 + 0.30×14 = **~24 fire damage**.
- Ragers: HP 52 → ~**28 HP** each.

**Init 15 (Rager-C):** Closes to Bazgar (30 ft advance). One PC in reach — Berserk wasteful (needs 2+ targets). **Multiattack** × 2 vs Bazgar: +4 hit vs AC 18. Needs 14+. 35% each. Madness Endurance active (+1 to-hit from Fireball damage): +5 hit, needs 13+, 40%.
- Expected: 2 × 0.40 × 8 = **6 damage**. Bazgar: 49 → **43 HP**.

**Init 14 (Bazgar):** Attacks Rager-C (highest threat, in melee). +7 to hit vs AC 16. Needs 9+. 60%.
- Multiattack × 2 expected: 2 × 0.60 × 11 = **13 damage**. Rager-C: 28 → **15 HP**.
- *Action Surge* (once per short rest): 2 more attacks → +13 more. Rager-C: 15 → **2 HP** (barely alive; ~90% probability kill with full 4-attack spread of 26 expected).
  - Assume Rager-C at **0 HP** (dead) given typical variance in 4 attacks.

**Init 12 (Rager-A):** Charges Bazgar. Now 2 PCs potentially in reach (Sabriel approaching from behind). If only 1 in reach: Multiattack (no Berserk). +5 (ME active) vs AC 18: 40% each.
- Expected: **6 damage**. Bazgar: 43 → **37 HP**.

**Init 7 (Rager-B):** Same situation. +5 vs AC 18.
- Expected: **6 damage**. Bazgar: 37 → **31 HP**.
- Taunt (BA) on Marwen (highest-value non-rager). DC 12 CHA vs Marwen CHA~+0: 55% fail. Marwen has disadvantage vs non-rager targets on a fail. (Rager Taunt fires as designed on Marwen — but see FEEL-R81-01 below re: same-type-formation Taunt.)

**Init 5 (Sabriel):** Attacks Rager-A (closest). +6 to hit vs AC 16. 55%. Divine Smite L2 on hit (+3d8=13.5 avg radiant).
- Attack 1 with smite: 0.55 × (11+13.5) = **13.5 expected**. Rager-A: 28 → **~15 HP**.
- Attack 2 (no smite): 0.55 × 11 = **6 expected**. Rager-A: 15 → **~9 HP**.

**End Round 1:**
- Rager-C: dead
- Rager-A: ~9 HP
- Rager-B: 28 HP (untouched by melee; only Fireball hit)
- Bazgar: **31 HP** (took ~18 across 3 multiattacks)
- Marwen: **32 HP** (untouched — ragers went for melee range)
- Sabriel: **44 HP** (untouched)

Bazgar used Action Surge. Sabriel used one L2 smite slot.

## Feel Assessment: `solo-rager-rush`

### FEEL-R81-01 (NEW — LOG ONLY): Solo-rager-rush is trivially weak under Fireball; formation lacks pressure at L5

**Finding:** Three Ragers vs a L5 party with Fireball is not a meaningful encounter. Fireball (avg 28 fire, no save-spell resistance on ragers) front-loads ~24 damage to each rager before they close, leaving all three at ~28 HP against a party that deals 11+ per attack at 60% hit rate. The "Deadly" XP rating does not account for L5 AoE uptime.

**Root cause:** The solo-rager-rush slice has no description in `thrulm-rotation.json` (only id strings). The formation presumably exists to test rager mechanics in isolation, not to challenge the party. The key findings from the simulation are:

1. **Taunt is dead in same-type formation** (reconfirms FI-SR5-46-A, FI-SR5-51-A): Rager-B taunted Marwen, but with only ragers on the field, "disadvantage on attacks against non-rager targets" has nothing to constrain. Marwen casts Fireball (no attack roll) and Magic Missile (no attack roll) — Taunt never interacts. The Taunt BA is wasted but fires mechanically correctly. No crash.

2. **Madness Endurance under AoE works correctly:** Fireball hits Rager-C at init 15 (after Marwen's init 17 turn). When Rager-C acts at init 15, ME is active (+1 to-hit). ✓ PHB timing: "takes damage" → ME active "until end of its next turn" — this round's turn qualifies. Correct behavior.

3. **Berserk stagger rule correct:** Rager-C entered melee alone with only one PC adjacent. Berserk requires 2+ targets in reach; Rager-C correctly used Multiattack instead. The DB shows 3 attack lines for Berserk — DM would cap at actual count. This is documented behavior (not a bug) but a recurring DM ergonomics issue. If the encounter had 3 PCs clustered within 5 ft in R1, stagger holds (only 1 rager fires Berserk per round); the other two Multiattack.

4. **No new mechanical bugs found in rager sub-system** this run.

**Recommendation (LOG ONLY):** Add a brief description to the "solo-rager-rush" slice in `thrulm-rotation.json` clarifying it is a mechanics test, not a challenge test. Consider scaling to 5 ragers or adding a shardcaller for meaningful L5 encounter pressure if the intent is a real fight test. Design call for Joe.
