## Phase B — Generative Playtest: drain-divinity-vs-sabriel

**Rotation.yml index 4.** Tests: DD legendary action economy, save math, slot attrition vs Sabriel (divine martial 5), feel of spending all 3 LA on one effect.

---

### Setup

Simulation: R1, post-Sabriel's turn. Per R1-LA-hold protocol (FIX-FC40-A), beholder reserved all 3 LA through Bazgar and Marwen's turns. Now firing DD immediately after Sabriel ends her turn.

**Sabriel stat assumptions (combat-roster.yml + slice spec):**
- L5 divine martial (Paladin-like chassis): spell slots L3×2, L2×3, L1×4
- CHA save modifier: +2 (from slice spec; note: actual Paladin 5 proficiency would add PB+3 → total +5, but using slice's +2 for fidelity)
- AC 19, HP 44 at full

**DD parameters:**
- Cost: 3 legendary actions (consumes entire round budget)
- Range: 30 ft (target must be within 30 ft of beholder — see BUG-A12-06)
- Effect: DC 16 CHA save; on fail: lose 1 highest-remaining slot; beholder gains 2 × slot-level temp HP

---

### Save simulation

**Sabriel CHA save:**
- Modifier: +2 (per slice spec)
- P(save) = P(d20+2 ≥ 16) = P(d20 ≥ 14) = 7/20 = **35%**
- P(fail) = **65%**

Simulated roll: d20 = 9 → 9 + 2 = **11 vs DC 16 → FAIL**

**Result:** Sabriel loses 1 L3 slot (highest remaining; assumed unspent in R1 if DD fires before her attack). Beholder gains 2 × 3 = **6 temp HP**.

---

### Mechanical soundness

Mechanically correct as written. One concern:

**ROT-20-01 (new): Slice description says "simulate 3 saves" — DD only fires once per round**

The slice spec says: *"Beholder spends all 3 legendary actions on Drain Divinity targeting Sabriel … Simulate 3 saves. If all fail: Sabriel loses three 3rd-level slots; beholder gains 18 temp HP."*

This is wrong. DD costs **all 3 legendary actions per use** — you can use it exactly **once per round**. Three uses would require 9 LA; the beholder only generates 3 per round. The slice's "3 saves" framing cannot occur in a single round and is mechanically impossible. One correct simulation = 1 save.

If the slice intended "3 saves across 3 consecutive rounds (1 DD per round)," the framing needs rewriting to be explicit about multi-round scope. **Joe edits rotation.yml.**

---

### Slot attrition projection — 3-round chain

Assuming beholder holds all 3 LA every round for DD (no reactive LA pressure on Bazgar/Marwen):

| Round | Sabriel's remaining slots | DD target slot | P(fail) | Beholder temp HP gained (if lands) |
|-------|---------------------------|-----------------|---------|-------------------------------------|
| R1 | L3×2, L2×3, L1×4 | L3 (2/sp-lvl) | 65% | +6 |
| R2 | L3×1 (if R1 landed), L2×3, L1×4 | L3 (last) | 65% | +6 |
| R3 | L2×3, L1×4 (L3 exhausted) | L2 | 65% | +4 |

Expected temp HP over 3 rounds: 0.65×6 + 0.65×6 + 0.65×4 = 3.9 + 3.9 + 2.6 = **10.4 temp HP expected**

vs beholder max HP 110: **9.5% of max HP** over 3 full rounds of DD — negligible as a sustain tool.

**Real value is slot denial:** At 65% per round, expected L3 strips = 1.3 over 2 attempts. Sabriel's L3 pool is exhausted in ~2 rounds on expectation. After that, DD targets L2 slots (3 available) — another 4–5 rounds to strip those. Total slot-denial timeline: ~6–7 rounds to exhaust all slots above L1.

The party is unlikely to survive 6–7 rounds, making full slot exhaustion a theoretical maximum rather than a practical outcome.

---

### Feel assessment

**FEEL-F20-01 (new): 3-LA cost creates binary round economy**

When DD fires, the beholder spends ALL 3 LA on a single Sabriel-targeted strip. For the rest of that round, it has:
- Zero Tentacle LA (no reactive grapple pressure on Bazgar)
- Zero Void Ray LA (no force-damage pressure on Marwen)
- Zero Move LA (no repositioning)

Bazgar and Marwen each take their turns with zero LA threat from the beholder. The beholder becomes passive for those two PCs. A CR 13 creature surrendering all reactive pressure to deny a single spell slot feels disproportionate — the DD economics reward the beholder far less than Tentacle + Void Ray would (which would apply pressure to two different PCs at lower LA cost).

Concrete comparison: DD (3 LA) = 1 slot denied (65% landing) vs Tentacle+VR (1+2 LA) = grapple attempt (Bazgar, ~55% hit at +6 vs AC 18) + 22 expected force damage (16 avg × 65% fail). The Tentacle+VR combination applies real pressure and damage where DD applies no pressure and marginal attrition.

**Suggested fix (log only — Joe decides):** Reduce DD to cost **2 LA** (same as Void Ray). This lets beholder use DD + Tentacle in the same round. The slot denial is preserved; the reactive-pressure gap closes. Alternatively, add a per-day limit (e.g. 1/Day) to make DD a deliberate nova rather than a standing-rotation ability. Either change resolves DD-DD.

---

**FEEL-F20-02 (new): DD cannot touch Lay on Hands — Sabriel's healing floor is immune**

DD strips spell slots. It cannot strip LoH charges (a pool, not a slot). Sabriel L5 LoH = 25 HP pool. Even after full L3+L2 slot exhaustion (6–7 rounds), Sabriel retains:
- LoH: 25 HP of healing (5 uses at 5 HP each, or any split)
- L1 slots: 4 remaining (smites, healing word equivalent)

DD never reaches L1 slots in a realistic fight timeline (parties rarely survive 6+ rounds against CR 13). The practical cap: DD strips L3+L2 = 5 slots, then the beholder dies or the party dies. L1 slots and LoH remain intact.

**Implication:** DD's stated purpose (deny Sabriel's healing) is incomplete. It slows smite damage (good) and slows L2+ spell healing (good) but never cuts LoH. The beholder cannot prevent Sabriel from reviving a downed ally once per short rest. If the fight hangs on whether Bazgar gets revived at 1 HP (via LoH), DD cannot stop it.

**Suggested DM note (log only):** After DD fires and Sabriel's high slots are stripped, shift beholder priority: Void Ray at Sabriel immediately after she uses LoH on a downed ally (don't let the revived ally act — LA Void Ray on Sabriel at her next HP state). This is already partially implied by the LoH override note in the .md tactics section but is not explicitly sequenced.

### BUG-A12-06 interaction — DD range vs hover height

Confirmed again this fire: the R1-LA-hold protocol (FIX-FC40-A) requires the beholder to hold all 3 LA through Bazgar and Marwen's turns, then fire DD post-Sabriel. But DD range is 30 ft. Beholder default hover = 40–60 ft from altar floor.

For DD to land, the beholder must descend to within 30 ft of Sabriel. Sabriel starts at the chamber entrance (~40–50 ft from altar). To reach her, the beholder must either:
1. Shrine-Drift (BA) toward her before firing DD (main action) — but Shrine-Drift is 30 ft, so beholder can close to ~10–30 ft from Sabriel. This works if beholder starts within 60 ft of Sabriel's position. However, Shrine-Drift is also the retreat tool — using it to approach for DD means it's unavailable this turn for escape.
2. Use Move LA (0 LA cost per carry-forward FEEL-F10-02 — falls to global dash) before DD. But Move LA fires during another creature's turn (between Bazgar and Marwen's turns), spending 1 LA on repositioning. This breaks the R1-LA-hold protocol (which requires holding ALL 3 LA until post-Sabriel).

**Conclusion:** DD and the R1-LA-hold protocol are **mutually incompatible with the hover-height constraint.** To execute DD correctly you need to: descend (using Shrine-Drift BA or Move LA) AND hold 3 LA AND be within 30 ft of Sabriel. These three requirements cannot all be satisfied simultaneously within the stated protocol. This is the sharpest expression of BUG-A12-06 to date. **Joe must decide:** lower hover height to 30 ft (closing the gap), or change DD range to 60 ft, or make descent explicit in the R1-hold protocol.
