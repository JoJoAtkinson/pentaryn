### Scenario Description

> Beholder waits until Bazgar and Sabriel are both within 30 ft of the shrine (likely round 2). Void Scream (recharge 6), DC 16 Wis save. Bazgar: Wis save (assume +1 Wis, d20+1 vs 16). Sabriel: Wis save (divine martial, assume +3 Wis, d20+3 vs 16). Marwen: likely within 30 ft too. Simulate all 3 saves. Note shrine proximity disadvantage rule and whether it's clear enough to run without prep.

### Round-by-Round Simulation

**Initiative order (nominal):**
- Beholder +3 → 18
- Marwen +2 → 14
- Bazgar +1 → 12
- Sabriel +1 → 11
- Thrall derro +1 → 8

Beholder acts first. Per FEEL-R239-01 (open, age 4): round-1 multiattack likely drops Marwen before she acts. This simulation assumes that outcome and focuses on round-2 Void Scream positioning.

**End of Round 1 (assumed state):**
- Marwen: 0 HP (unconscious; death saves begin)
- Bazgar: engaged in melee near altar — moved to close with beholder (which would have to be close for Drain Divinity); HP 49 (undamaged R1 if beholder targeted Marwen)
- Sabriel: likewise near altar; HP 44 (or slightly reduced from lair Void Eruption on init 20)
- Beholder: used multiattack, has 3 LA refreshed for round 2

**Round 2 setup — beholder using Void Scream:**

Void Scream radius: 30 ft from beholder.
Beholder default hover: 40–60 ft above floor (per tactics).

**3D geometry analysis (NEW finding this fire — BUG-R243-01):**
Beholder hovering at height h. PC standing at horizontal distance d from altar base.
Distance to PC = sqrt(h² + d²).

For VS to reach a PC at d=0 (standing at altar):
- h=30 ft: distance = 30 ft. Barely in range ✓
- h=40 ft: distance = 40 ft. Out of range ✗

For VS to catch Bazgar at d=10 ft from altar:
- h=20 ft: sqrt(400+100) = 22.4 ft ✓
- h=26 ft: sqrt(676+100) = 27.9 ft ✓
- h=28 ft: sqrt(784+100) = 29.7 ft ✓

**Conclusion: Beholder must descend to ~25–28 ft altitude to land VS on PCs 10 ft from the altar.** At default 40–60 ft hover, VS cannot reach most of the party. Tactics section says hover 40–60 ft; VS range is 30 ft. DM using defaults will attempt VS and frequently find the party out of range. This is undocumented — see BUG-R243-01.

**Beholder descends to 25 ft altitude to use VS.** This puts it within Bazgar's melee reach if he takes his action to close (dash or readied action). This is genuine counterplay — logged as FEEL-R243-01.

**Void Scream Saves (round 2, assumed all conscious PCs in range):**

Marwen at 0 HP: unconscious → cannot hear → excluded per FIX-FC48-A ruling. ✓

Bazgar (WIS save +1, slice assumption):
- DC 16. Need 15+ on d20. P(fail) = 14/20 = **70%**.
- Shrine proximity: if Bazgar is within 10 ft of altar (likely if he closed for melee) → disadvantage on this save. P(fail with disadvantage) = 1 − 0.30² = **91%**.
- Expected damage (no proximity): 0.70×33 + 0.30×16 = 23.1+4.8 = **27.9 expected psychic**
- Expected damage (proximity): 0.91×33 + 0.09×16 = 30.0+1.4 = **31.4 expected psychic**
- Bazgar after VS: ~21 HP (no prox) or ~18 HP (with prox). Also FRIGHTENED ~70–91% of cases.

Sabriel (WIS save +3, slice assumption):
- DC 16. Need 13+ on d20. P(fail) = 12/20 = **60%**.
- Shrine proximity: Sabriel (divine martial) likely near altar. If within 10 ft: P(fail with disadvantage) = 1 − 0.40² = **84%**.
- Expected damage (no proximity): 0.60×33 + 0.40×16 = 19.8+6.4 = **26.2 expected psychic**
- Expected damage (proximity): 0.84×33 + 0.16×16 = 27.7+2.6 = **30.3 expected psychic**
- Sabriel after VS: ~18 HP (no prox) or ~14 HP (with prox). Also FRIGHTENED ~60–84% of cases.

**Combined round-2 HP totals after VS:**
- Bazgar: 49 − 28 ≈ 21 HP remaining
- Sabriel: 44 − 26 ≈ 18 HP remaining
- Marwen: 0 HP (unconscious, death saves)

**Frightened condition implications:**

Frightened prevents the party from moving toward the beholder. Once VS lands:
1. Beholder re-ascends (Move LA after next PC's turn) to 40+ ft.
2. Frightened PCs cannot close to melee.
3. Remaining attacks: Bazgar ranged throws (no ranged weapon for a Battlemaster in standard build), Sabriel ranged smites (if she has a ranged option — non-standard for divine martial).
4. Effectively: frightened PCs are in a ranged disadvantage situation vs a flying target while beholder uses Void Ray + lair actions from safety.
5. Compass Edge has no calm emotions spell (not in Wizard/Fighter/divine martial spell lists at level 5). No natural counter to FRIGHTENED.

DC 16 WIS save at end of each turn to end frightened:
- Bazgar (+1): P(end per turn) = 5/20 = 25%. Expected duration: 4 turns.
- Sabriel (+3): P(end per turn) = 7/20 = 35%. Expected duration: 2.9 turns.

**Fight expected to end within 3–4 more rounds** given HP levels. Frightened lasts the full endgame for Bazgar (~4 turns > 3 remaining rounds). This is FEEL-R243-02.

### Shrine Proximity Disadvantage — Table Clarity Assessment

The rule "Creatures within 10 feet of the shrine have disadvantage" requires:
1. DM knowing at cast time which PCs are ≤10 ft from the shrine object
2. Physical measurement or battle-map zone marker

**Ambiguity: what counts as "the shrine"?** The stat block says "within 10 feet of the shrine." The encounter references "the sealed shrine altar." A DM unfamiliar with the location could interpret "shrine" as:
a) The altar object only (likely 5×5 or 10×10 ft — clear zone)
b) The shrine chamber broadly (entire room — would disadvantage everyone)

The correct reading is (a) — the altar object — but this is not made explicit. A pre-encounter note like "the shrine altar occupies a 10×10 ft area; the disadvantage zone is a 20×20 ft area around it" would remove ambiguity. Carry-forward FEEL-R239-02 (shrine proximity unresolvable by runner, age 4).

### Phase B Scenario Structural Review — `condition-duration-tick`

*scenarios.yml index 3 of 20. Counter 243 → 244.*

```yaml
- id: condition-duration-tick
  title: Apply paralyzed for 3 rounds; advance 3 rounds; condition auto-removed
  encounter: mountin-pass
  turns:
    - {kind: switch_tab, slug: glacier-stalker}
    - {kind: type, tab: glacier-stalker, text: "@paralyzed 3"}
    - {kind: assert_condition, slug: glacier-stalker, condition: paralyzed, remaining: 3}
    - {kind: click_round}
    - {kind: click_round}
    - {kind: click_round}
    - {kind: assert_no_condition, slug: glacier-stalker, condition: paralyzed}
  targets:
    max_click_count: 8
```

**What this gates:** Condition tick-down over round advances; auto-expiry.

**Potential issue (SCEN-R243-01 — NEW, LOW):** `assert_no_condition` with `condition: paralyzed` after 3 `click_round` calls assumes rounds advance exactly once per `click_round` event. If a `click_round` click also triggers a recharge roll pop-up (e.g. Disintegration Ray or Pounce) that requires DM acknowledgment before the round counter increments, the click_count may be correct but the round may not have advanced. `max_click_count: 8` allows 5 clicks beyond 3 switches — that's headroom for pop-ups, but if **two** pop-ups appear per round, the scenario needs up to 9 clicks. Recommend bumping to `max_click_count: 11` or adding `max_click_count: null` (unlimited, watch-mode). Log only — no thrulm impact.
