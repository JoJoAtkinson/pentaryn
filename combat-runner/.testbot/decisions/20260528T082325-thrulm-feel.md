## Feel issues (not auto-fixed — require designer decision)

### FEEL-001: Disintegration Ray — permakill probability is high against level 5
- ~22% chance of permakilling Marwen in round 1 alone (recharge × hit probability).
- If it doesn't fire round 1: cumulative probability over 4 rounds approaches 65%.
- Marwen has no HP buffer to survive a hit (32 HP vs 45 avg).
- **The "no revivification" clause is the emotional sting.** Players lose the character entirely.
- **Recommendation:** Minimum-HP insta-kill threshold: disintegration only if target has ≤ half their max HP when hit. At level 5 that means ≤ 16 HP for Marwen — still very achievable and telegraphed.
- **Alternative:** Change to Recharge 6 (same as Void Scream) so both big guns compete for the same recharge slot.

### FEEL-002: Legendary Action Void Ray + Tentacle chain kills the wizard in one round
- Standard sequence: Multiattack (Tentacle on Marwen = 16 damage, grappled), then after her turn LA Void Ray (22 avg force if she fails DC 16) → 38 total = dead.
- She cannot flee (grappled = speed 0), cannot use Misty Step without a bonus action spell slot, and DC 16 DEX save is hard for a level 5 wizard (typical +4 DEX save = 50% fail).
- **Recommendation:** LA Void Ray should target non-grappled creatures as a "reach out" option, not as a kill-chain finisher on an already-softened grappled target. Add flavor constraint: beholder prefers un-grappled targets for Void Ray.

### FEEL-003: Antireality reaction has retroactive AC timing
- "After seeing the roll" is not standard D&D 5e reaction language. Mechanically this means the beholder can choose to activate it only when it would turn a hit into a miss (i.e., only against rolls of 18 or 19, given base AC 17).
- **This gives the beholder perfect information about which attacks to spend the reaction on.** Against AC 17+2=19, a level-5 fighter with +5 to hit needs a 14+ (35% hit chance vs stated AC 17's 40%). The difference is small but feels unfair because the creature is never "tricked" by a high roll.
- **Recommendation:** Change to "declare before attack roll resolves" (like the Parry maneuver) OR change to Shield-style: "+2 AC for the rest of this round" (passive, no information gaming).

### FEEL-004: Clay-Shaping listed in Combat Stats but is not a combat action
- 1-minute ritual in the Combat Stats section creates false expectations.
- **Recommendation:** Move to Lore/Background section.

### FEEL-005: Drain Divinity (3 LA) gives weak return
- Cost: ALL 3 legendary actions (no other LA use that round).
- Return: 6 temp HP (for a 3rd-level stolen slot, which is maximum level 5 PCs have).
- The temp HP are minor relative to the beholder's 110 HP and will be gone in one hit.
- **Recommendation:** 5 × slot level (15 temp HP for a 3rd-level slot) OR reduce to 2 LA.

### FEEL-006: Void Scream shrine-proximity disadvantage has no spatial reference
- "Within 10 feet of the shrine" is not anchored to any landmark in the combat environment.
- DMs running the encounter need to know where the shrine is relative to the altar where the beholder starts.
- **Recommendation:** Add a note to `_overview.md` terrain section: "The sealed shrine ruin is in the northeast corner, 30 ft from the altar. Mark it on the encounter map."
