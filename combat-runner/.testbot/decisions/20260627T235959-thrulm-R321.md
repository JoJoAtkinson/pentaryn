### Encounter Setup

Prelude: Marwen is down (0 HP, death saves). Round 2 opening. Beholder at 110 HP, Disintegration Ray available (not yet used this encounter). Sabriel (44 HP, AC 19) healing Marwen last round. Bazgar (49 HP, AC 18) engaging the rager.

Order this round: Rager (init 7) → Beholder (init 18, has acted) — we pick up on Beholder's action.

### Simulation: "disintegrate" command

**Beholder selects Bazgar (49 HP, AC 18) as target for Disintegration Ray:**
- Attack bonus: +8 base + 1 Void-Feeding (in chamber) = **+9 to hit** vs AC 18.
- Needs d20 ≥ 9 → **55% hit rate**.
- Simulated roll: d20 = 15 → total 24 vs AC 18 → **HIT**.
- Damage: 10d8 force + 1 (Void-Feeding) → avg **46 force damage**.
- Bazgar HP: 49 − 46 = **3 HP remaining.** NOT dropped to 0; no disintegration this roll.

**Variance analysis — permanent-death probability:**
- P(instant Bazgar death from full HP, hit already confirmed): P(10d8 ≥ 49) = P(10d8 ≥ 48).
  - Mean 45, std ≈ 5.2. P(Z ≥ 0.58) ≈ **28%**.
- Combined with hit probability (55%): **~15% chance of outright permanent PC death per ray usage.**
- Against Marwen at reduced HP (stabilized at 1–4 HP): P(instant death) ≈ 100% on a hit (46 avg >> 32 max HP; excess from 0 HP would be ~42, which exceeds max HP 32 → instant death).
- This confirms FEEL-OPEN-04 is live: in a "meant to lose" encounter, permanent disintegration of a PC is not rare.

### NEW BUG — NARRATION-GAP-R321-01

The `disintegration_ray` narration_preview contains no mention of:
- The disintegration effect ("turned to ash")
- The permanent-death restriction ("cannot be restored to life except by true resurrection or wish")

This is safety-critical information for the DM. A kill-confirming Disintegration Ray resolves live in the runner with ~3 seconds of reaction time. If the permanent-death rider is absent from the output, the DM may not flag it until after the player has already assumed normal resurrection applies. In a campaign where PC permadeath is rare or unexpected, this omission can cause post-session disputes.

**Stat block text (authoritative):** "If this damage reduces the target to 0 hit points, the target is disintegrated (turned to ash). A creature reduced to 0 HP by this attack cannot be restored to life except by true resurrection or wish."
