### Condition Immunity / Trait Redundancy

**NEW: Beholder Void-Feeding — redundant turn-resistance clause (AUTO-FIXED)**

Void-Feeding contained a second bullet: "Advantage on checks to resist being turned by divine magic." The beholder is explicitly immune to the frightened condition. Any Turn mechanic in 5e applies the Turned/frightened condition; with frightened immunity the beholder automatically succeeds on any such attempt and never makes a saving throw. The advantage clause is therefore unreachable — the save never occurs. This is the same redundancy pattern as Rager Madness Endurance (frightened-save advantage removed in an earlier fire).

### Shrine Proximity Check

Who is within 10 ft of the shrine (disadvantage on Void Scream save)?
- Bazgar (20 ft from shrine): **no disadvantage** — outside 10-ft radius
- Sabriel (25 ft from shrine): **no disadvantage** — outside 10-ft radius

### Key Findings

**1. (NEW, FEEL-VS-R247-01, MEDIUM) Void Scream all-fail → near-total party shutdown in same round as ability use.**

When Void Scream fires and all 3 PCs fail (23.1% chance), the combined state after Void Scream + LA play is: 2 PCs unconscious, 1 PC frightened and grappled at 2 HP. This collapses in round 3. The party gets no counterplay after the Void Scream fires — the frightened condition eliminates melee pressure (can't move closer), and the wizard's 32 HP is below the average Void Scream damage (33 avg), making unconsciousness the modal outcome on a Marwen fail.

This is qualitatively different from FEEL-R227-01 (Void Eruption + Void Scream chain = 44 avg, no counterplay) — this is Void Scream alone producing near-TPK in the "all 3 fail" case. The 23.1% all-fail probability is not trivial; roughly 1 in 4 Void Scream uses collapses the party in the same round.

**Flag for Joe:** Is 23.1% same-round TPK probability on Void Scream acceptable given the encounter is "meant to be lost"? If yes, no change needed. If a more gradual loss is preferred, reduce Void Scream damage (e.g., 4d10 = 22 avg) or remove the frightened rider.

**2. (NEW, FEEL-VS-R247-02, LOW) Marwen: even a successful save on Void Scream leaves her one hit from unconscious.**

Half damage on Void Scream success = 16 (floor of 16.5). Marwen at 32 HP: 32 − 16 = **16 HP.** Any subsequent attack that deals ≥16 damage drops her. Tentacle Lash averages 13-14 (without Void-Feeding) or 14-15 (in chamber) — one more tentacle hit finishes her. Effectively, Marwen has no "safe" outcome from Void Scream: fail = unconscious immediately, save = unconscious next round. This isn't a bug (party is meant to lose), but DM should know Marwen is on a 1-2 round clock regardless of how Void Scream resolves.

**3. (NEW, FEEL-VS-R247-03, LOW) Shrine proximity disadvantage rule (within 10 ft) is correctly in cheat sheet but likely missed without encounter map.**

The cheat sheet SAVE DCs table correctly notes: "within 10 ft of shrine: **disadvantage** on this save." However:
- The _overview.md and encounter description don't include a labeled encounter map with the shrine marked
- When the beholder uses Shrine-Drift to reposition, the beholder and the shrine are two distinct points in space; a DM running at speed might apply "within 30 ft of beholder" as the radius and forget the shrine is the actual reference point for the disadvantage condition
- In the scenario where the beholder moves off-altar (e.g., using Shrine-Drift toward the party), the 10-ft shrine proximity zone becomes relevant but spatially ambiguous

The rule functions correctly at a prepared table with a map; it's a usability friction point without one. Low priority — add a reminder note to the cheat sheet's round-by-round checklist? Log only, Joe's call.

**4. (CONFIRMED, FEEL-DISINT-R245-02) Void Scream absent in ~58% of short combats, but its impact when it fires is disproportionate.**

Recharge 6 probability: 16.7% per round. In a 3-round fight, ~42% chance Void Scream fires at all. But when it does fire with clustering (all 3 PCs within range), expected total party damage is 27.9 + 26.2 + 25.35 = **~79.5 avg psychic across all 3 PCs** in a single action. That's 63.6% of total party HP (49+32+44=125). The ability is appropriately rare and appropriately lethal — the problem is that 23.1% of the time it fires, it produces near-instant collapse with no drama window.

**5. (CONFIRMED, FEEL-R228-01) Drain Divinity not optimal this slice — beholder uses Void Scream (main action), leaving no room for Drain Divinity.**

Void Scream as main action + 3 LA is strictly better than Drain Divinity (3 LA only). Expected EV comparison:
- Void Scream + Tentacle (1 LA) + Void Ray (2 LA): 79.5 avg party damage + 7.25 avg LA damage = 86.75 EV plus frightened + grapple
- Drain Divinity (3 LA only) on Sabriel: 3.9 expected THP + slot denial (one 3rd-level slot)
Void Scream + LA economy produces ~22× more damage than Drain Divinity alone. FEEL-R228-01 age: 19 fires.
