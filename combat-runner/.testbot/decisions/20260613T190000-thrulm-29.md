### New Findings

#### FEEL-F29-01 (new): Shard-Barrage 15-ft line has no range qualifier — unusable at standard SC positioning under RAW

**Severity:** High — affects every encounter where SCs fight at standard back-line positioning.

**Finding:** The DB `shard_barrage` action has `"area": "15-ft line"` with no range qualifier. In 5e, a line area effect begins at the caster and extends in one direction. Under this interpretation, the SC (at 40–60 ft from the PCs) cannot hit PCs with a 15-ft line — the line covers only the 15 ft immediately adjacent to the SC, not the PCs at range.

The only way Shard-Barrage functions at range is if the line can be placed at an origin point within some stated range (e.g., "a 15-ft line starting at any point within 30 ft"). No such range is stated in the DB or in the .md's compact format.

**Practical impact by positioning:**
- If line starts from SC at 50 ft away: Barrage covers only the 15-ft zone adjacent to the SC (empty space in normal combat). **Barrage never hits a PC.**
- If line is placed like a ranged AoE (origin within 30 ft): Barrage hits 2+ PCs in normal formation. This is how this simulation ran it.

**Three possible resolutions (log only):**
1. **Range qualifier (intended):** Add explicit range to shard_barrage DB entry: `"area": "15-ft line; origin within 30 ft"`. This matches the 30-ft Shard-Throw range and makes Barrage functionally a 2-target version of the throw. Highest-probability fix.
2. **Starting-from-caster (RAW):** Accept that Barrage requires the SC to advance to within 15 ft of targets. This fundamentally changes SC positioning (closer = more dangerous) and may be intentional for higher-difficulty scenarios. Unlikely given the back-line design intent.
3. **Redesign:** Replace 15-ft line with a longer range (e.g., "30-ft line starting from the SC") so the ability reaches standard engagement range.

**Needs Joe + MCP fix** to update DB with the range qualifier. The .md should also be updated to clarify the ability's range when the rule is decided.

#### FEEL-F29-02 (new): Barrage's R1 window is blocked in 45% of combats by Rager initiative

**Severity:** Low-Medium.

**Finding:** Shard-Barrage's stagger rule allows only one Barrage per round per SC formation. If the highest-initiative SC (SC-A, init +2) fires R1 Barrage, SC-B cannot fire until R2. But SC-A's stagger responsibility depends on its initiative vs the Rager:

- SC acts BEFORE Rager in initiative (~55% chance at init +2 vs +0): SC-A can fire Barrage R1 BEFORE the Rager charges. Line is clear of the Rager (hasn't reached melee yet). **Barrage lands cleanly.**
- Rager acts BEFORE SC-A (~45% chance): Rager charges to melee and stands adjacent to a PC. SC-A turn arrives after the charge. The front-liners (PCs) are now grouped with the Rager — any line that hits 2 PCs likely also passes through the Rager (friendly fire). **Barrage is unsafe; SC-A Multiattacks.**
