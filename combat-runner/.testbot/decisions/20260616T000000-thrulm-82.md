## Phase B — Scenario Simulation

**Scenario:** `thrulm-beholder-legendary-rotation` (index 25 in 49-entry scenarios.yml)

**Finding: LA budget overflow — SCENARIO BUG (FIXED this fire)**

The scenario turns list was: tentacle lash (1 LA) → void ray (2 LA) → drain divinity (3 LA) = **6 LA total**. The beholder's legendary action budget is **3 per round**. This scenario would:
- Correctly resolve tentacle lash (1 LA spent, 2 remaining)
- Correctly resolve void ray (2 LA spent, 0 remaining)  
- Attempt drain divinity with **0 LA remaining** (3 LA cost)

If the combat runner enforces LA budget, drain divinity is rejected silently or with an error. If it does NOT enforce LA budget (likely, since LA tracking is DM-side), drain divinity fires in the narrative even though it's mechanically illegal. Either outcome is wrong for a regression test.

**Fix applied this fire:**
- Removed drain divinity from `thrulm-beholder-legendary-rotation`; title updated to "Beholder spends all 3 legendary actions — tentacle (1 LA) + void ray (2 LA)"
- Added new scenario `thrulm-beholder-drain-divinity-solo`: Drain Divinity alone (3 LA, full budget), targeting Sabriel after her turn

**Manual simulation of correct LA sequence (for log):**

*Context:* End of round. PCs have all taken their turns. Beholder has 3 LA.

- **Tentacle Lash LA (1 LA)** after Marwen's turn: +7 vs AC 15 (65% hit). Avg 13 (3d6+3) bludgeoning + grappled on hit. Expected damage: 8.5. LA remaining: 2.
- **Void Ray LA (2 LA)** after Bazgar's turn: DC 16 DEX save vs Bazgar (Fighter, DEX +0 est., 80% fail). Avg 22 (4d10) force on fail, 11 on save. Expected damage: 19.6. LA remaining: 0.
- **Total LA expected damage:** ~28 avg. Matches rotation.yml target ("~28 avg total LA damage"). ✓

*Alternative: Drain Divinity alone (3 LA) targeting Sabriel:*
- Sabriel CHA save (divine martial, est. +2 CHA): DC 16, fails on 14+ ≈ 70%. On fail: loses highest remaining slot (3rd level at L5). Beholder gains 6 temp HP. 
- DM note (BUG-A12-06): Drain Divinity range is 30 ft; beholder default hover is 40–60 ft above shrine floor. At standard hover altitude, DD CANNOT reach Sabriel. Beholder must descend (Shrine-Drift BA or move action) before using DD. This descent trade-off is not documented in the tactics note.
