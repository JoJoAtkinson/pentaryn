### Lair action options evaluated:

**Option 1: Unstable Ground → Bazgar**
- DC 16 DEX, Bazgar DEX +1 → needs 15+ → 70% fail.
- Effect: Bazgar prone. Melee attacks from adjacent creatures gain advantage.
- Value: High if Rager or thralls are adjacent. Bazgar must spend half movement (15 ft) to stand.
- Expected value: conditional on adjacency of melee allies. Moderate–high.

**Option 2: Void Eruption (all within 20 ft of shrine)**
- Bazgar in zone. Thralls may or may not be in 20-ft radius. DC 16 DEX, 2d10 (avg 11) force, half on save.
- Against Bazgar (DEX +1): 70% fail → expected 0.70×11 + 0.30×5.5 = 7.7+1.65 = **9.35 avg**.
- Friendly fire risk: if thralls wander into 20-ft zone, they take the same damage (no ally exclusion in stat block).
- Expected value: ~9–19 avg depending on how many creatures in zone. Best when PCs cluster near shrine.

**Option 3: Manifest Thralls (both thralls)**
- Each thrall gains 1 THP (CHA modifier +1). Each can use reaction to move or make a weapon attack.
- Reaction attack (hand axe): +1 to hit vs AC 18 Bazgar → needs 17+ → **15% hit chance**.
- Expected damage per thrall: 0.15 × avg(1d4) = 0.15 × 2.5 = **0.375 avg**. Both thralls: **0.75 avg total**.
- 1 THP provides negligible buffer (dies on any hit).
- **Assessment: Almost always the worst choice.** Confirmed again this fire.

### NEW Feel Issue: Beholder Hover Height vs Tentacle Reach

**Critical finding:** At standard hover height (40–60 ft above floor per cheat sheet positioning map),
beholder's Tentacle Lash (reach 10 ft) and Maw (reach 5 ft) **cannot reach floor-level targets.**
The beholder must descend to roughly 10 ft or lower for Multiattack to connect.

This creates an undocumented tactical tension:
- **Hovering high (40–60 ft):** Safe from Bazgar's melee (Bazgar cannot reach without climbing/leap),
  good for Disintegration Ray and Void Scream. But Multiattack is impossible.
- **Descending to 10 ft:** Enables Multiattack (2 tentacles + maw), but puts beholder within
  Bazgar's melee reach (Bazgar has no bonus to jump). Risk: Opportunity Attack from Bazgar on
  descent, Antireality reaction may be consumed by melee targeting.

This is **not documented in the tactics section or cheat sheet.** The tactics say "grapples with
tentacles and drags into difficult terrain" without clarifying that the beholder must descend.

**Impact at table:** DM may attempt Multiattack from 40 ft hover and get confused why it's out of
range, or may assume Tentacle Lash is a ranged attack. Should be called out in cheat sheet
"When Beholder Acts" section.

**Status: LOG ONLY — tactics clarification, not a stat block bug.**

### Lair action feel assessment:

The three-way decision is genuinely interesting at the table — two real options (Unstable Ground
vs Void Eruption) depending on PC positioning. The dead option (Manifest Thralls) degrades the

recommendation: redesign Manifest Thralls as a readied-attack trigger (when a PC moves into a
thrall's threatened space, the thrall attacks with the beholder's bonus) to give it real teeth.
