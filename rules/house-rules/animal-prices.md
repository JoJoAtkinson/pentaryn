## Bulk Purchasing

- **Flock of 10 Sheep:** 650 gp (70 gp × 10, 10% discount)
- **Herd of 5 Cows:** 700 gp (150 gp × 5, ~7% discount)
- **Pair of Oxen:** 180 gp (100 gp × 2, 10% discount)

## Replacement Costs (Quests & Raids)

When livestock are killed or stolen, use these costs for compensation:

| Loss Type | Compensation | Notes |
|-----------|--------------|-------|
| **Single sheep** | 70 gp | Full replacement cost |
| **Prize ram/ewe** | 100-150 gp | Breeding stock premium |
| **Dairy cow** | 150 gp | Includes lost production value |
| **Draft animal** | 80-150 gp | Includes training time loss |

### Example Quest Rewards

- **5 sheep killed by goblins:** 350 gp compensation (5 × 70 gp)
- **Herd scattered (no deaths):** 50 gp for recovery effort
- **Prize breeding ram stolen:** 120 gp + 30 gp finder's fee

## Monthly Costs for Farmers

Average monthly feed costs per animal (30 days):

- **Sheep:** 6 sp/month
- **Cow:** 15 sp/month
- **Horse:** 15 sp/month
- **Ox:** 12 sp/month
- **Dog:** 6 sp/month

*Note: These prices reflect Ardenhaven region market rates. Adjust ±20% for other regions based on local availability.*
