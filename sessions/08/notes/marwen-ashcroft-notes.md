# Marwen & the Ashcroft Bloodline — DM Notes

Quick reference for the Ashcroft family threads and Marwen's story hooks.

---

## The Ashcroft Line

```
Marrith the Ashen Measure (Elder, great-grandmother)
  └─ Elwen (daughter, deceased — executed by Marrith)
       └─ Breann (granddaughter, Votary at time of Elwen's death)
            ├─ Clair Ashcroft (born 4304, ward-witch Tollbearer, left Elderholt)
            └─ Marwen Ashcroft (born 4307, threadling, left Elderholt age 20)
```

---

## Marwen — Key Facts

- Born male, reborn female at age 6 through a fire started by her own connection to the Voice
- Ishvar opposed her initiation (boys are never tithed); the Axiom accepted her anyway
- Naelith spoke her name once in dark prophecy (great ruin and destruction) — refuses to elaborate
- Brannith taught her without asking permission ("If you wait to be welcomed, you will rot in the doorway")
- Clair earned ward-markings and became a Tollbearer in 4324; Marwen watched with pride
- Left Elderholt at 20 (4327) heading toward ruins near Ardenhaven

## Elder Advice Given to Marwen Before Departure

Each elder gave specific counsel. Use these if Marwen (or a PC connected to her) needs to remember guidance:

- **Brannith:** Gave "road rules" — don't bargain when hungry/lonely/proud; name your price before your help; keep one ward you can cast half-asleep
- **Ishvar:** Don't reveal where you're from unless it benefits you. Spend truth like coin.
- **Taramet:** "Don't lie. Shape it. Give them a handle that fits their grip."
- **Raven:** "I love visitors." (walks off)
- **Marrith:** Run from broken witches. Find a traveling party. Don't go alone out of pride. If cornered, lie — promise you can heal it, buy time for allies.
