# Elder Personality Profiles — Quick Reference

Twelve Elders rule Elderholt. Each has survived decades of bargaining with the Voice. Below is a snap personality profile for each, organized for fast roleplay reference.

---

## 1. Arlen the Tithe-Bearer (AR-len)

**Path:** Deep-Witch | **Vibe:** Warm, broken, merciful

- **Personality:** Gentle and selfless to a fault. Lopsided smile, steady hands. Children trust her instinctively.
- **Core trait:** Volunteers to absorb other witches' tolls. A collage of a hundred bargains — branch-arm, molten rune-eye, frost-lungs, light bleeding through skin.
- **How she talks:** Soft, direct, kind. Never pities — *offers*.
- **Will:** Step between a PC and a cruel price, turning every bargain into a moral choice.
- **Won't:** Shield anyone who tries to use her the way her mother did. Her anger is real.
- **RP hook:** "Will you let someone already broken take this one too?"

> Source: [arlen-the-tithe-bearer.md](../../../world/factions/elderholt/elders/arlen-the-tithe-bearer.md)

## 2. Brannith the Hag / The Overlooked (BRAN-ith)

**Path:** Deep-Witch (Abjuration Master) | **Vibe:** Crone of rules, terrifying clarity

- **Personality:** Blunt, literal, wickedly funny. Cackles for too long. Smiles with too many teeth. Not cruel — *precise*.
- **Core trait:** Bargained to be exactly what people expect. Can't be surprised, divined, or predicted. If you believe she's dangerous, she is.
- **How she talks:** Like a curse — plain, specific, hard to misunderstand. Gives "road rules" to departing witches.
- **Will:** Follow every rule to the letter, including the flaws. Expose bad doctrine by enforcing it perfectly.
- **Won't:** Be manipulated by Ishvar. Complies too literally for it to work.
- **Key dynamic:** Ishvar speaks first; Brannith speaks last. The council watches the gap between them.
- **Closest to:** Calyrix (old friends). Witch-children visit her freely.

> Source: [brannith-the-hag-overlooked.md](../../../world/factions/elderholt/elders/brannith-the-hag-overlooked.md)

---

## 3. Calyrix the Many-Minded (KAL-ih-riks)

**Path:** Ward-Witch (Abjuration) | **Vibe:** Omnipresent, distributed, watchful aunt

- **Personality:** No single personality — dozens of fragments spread across Elderstead. One is gentle, one sharp, one distant. They refer to each other in third person.
- **Core trait:** Fragmented herself to be present wherever witch-children learn. Exists as statues, mirrors, doorframes, whispers.
- **How she talks:** In the plural ("we"). Answers before questions are asked. Slower and less emotionally nuanced than she used to be.
- **Will:** Lock doors, flicker ward-warnings, subtly redirect children away from danger. Assemble as consensus-self for council.
- **Won't:** Keep secrets from herself, lie convincingly, or selectively forget.
- **Override:** Goes dormant if a witch invokes quiet — unless a child is threatened or Elderholt's security is at stake.

> Source: [calyrix-the-many-minded.md](../../../world/factions/elderholt/elders/calyrix-the-many-minded.md)

---

## 4. Ishvar of Prudence (ISH-vahr)

**Path:** Ward-Witch | **Vibe:** Magistrate, manipulator, soft-spoken control

- **Personality:** Composed, precise, unreadable. Voice is gentle on purpose. Wards look like careful script, more rules than runes.
- **Core trait:** Never bargains directly — maneuvers *others* into bargaining "for the greater good" while she benefits. She's corruption in procedural form.
- **How she talks:** Like a contract being read aloud. Mild, reasonable, impossible to argue with on paper.
- **Will:** Offer help shaped like a trap. Make cruelty sound like policy, mercy sound like risk.
- **Won't:** Act on impulse, take the direct path, or choose honesty over stability.
- **Key dynamic:** Joked about and quietly feared. Accusing her would tear the coven apart.
- **Rival:** Brannith (can't regulate her).

> Source: [ishvar-of-prudence.md](../../../world/factions/elderholt/elders/ishvar-of-prudence.md)

## 5. Kharbora the Living Shadow (KHAR-bor-ah)

**Path:** Ward-Witch (formerly dwarf) | **Vibe:** Aggressive, opinionated, fiercely independent

- **Personality:** Still hot-blooded despite being a shapeless shadow. Loud emotions, strong opinions, zero patience for pity.
- **Core trait:** Traded away sight, reflection, and finally her body. Now a jagged shadow with amber-ember eyes. Lanterns dim when she passes.
- **How she talks:** Snarls. Challenges. Never tells you the "right" answer — forces you to decide.
- **Will:** Respect anyone who owns their choices. Stand watch until she has nothing left.
- **Won't:** Accept pity, make decisions for others, or tolerate those who push unwilling people into bargains.
- **Coven fear:** What will she sacrifice next?

> Source: [kharbora-the-living-shadow.md](../../../world/factions/elderholt/elders/kharbora-the-living-shadow.md)

## 6. Marrith the Ashen Measure (MARE-ith)

**Path:** Ward-Witch (Evocation/Warding) | **Vibe:** Judge, executioner, grandmother

- **Personality:** Quiet authority. Doesn't raise her voice. Weighs every word like ash on a scale.
- **Core trait:** Killed her own daughter Elwen with a circle of pale fire that burned only inward. Proved she will cross any line if doctrine demands it.
- **How she talks:** Flat, measured, final. Gives orders disguised as advice. Warns once.
- **Will:** Stop witches before they become monsters. Burn away curses without spreading them.
- **Won't:** Hesitate when the line is crossed — not even for blood.
- **Family:** Great-grandmother to Marwen and Clair Ashcroft. Gave Marwen a crow before she left.
- **Why feared:** Not because she kills — because she *proved* she will.

> Source: [marrith-the-ashen-measure.md](../../../world/factions/elderholt/elders/marrith-the-ashen-measure.md)

## 7. Naelith the Unmoored Seer

**Path:** Ward-Witch | **Vibe:** Calm on the outside, temporally adrift inside

- **Personality:** Seems lucid. Isn't. Constantly asking small questions to figure out *when* she is.
- **Core trait:** Foresight so complete the Voice took nothing — time itself took everything. She lives out of sequence.
- **How she talks:** Perfectly accurate guidance that is often early, late, or impossible to act on.
- **Will:** Speak prophecy. The coven listens carefully.
- **Won't:** Elaborate. She spoke Marwen's name once in dark prophecy (great ruin and destruction) and refuses to say more.
- **RP tip:** When she speaks, the coven prays this is one of the moments that still matters.

> Source: [naelith-the-unmoored-seer.md](../../../world/factions/elderholt/elders/naelith-the-unmoored-seer.md)

## 8. Raven the Vital Ascendant (RAY-ven)

**Path:** Deep-Witch / Lich | **Vibe:** Deadpan, antisocial, terrifyingly competent

- **Personality:** Dry, minimal, zero interest in mentoring. Answers questions if asked, never frames knowledge as a path to follow.
- **Core trait:** Hated her body; the Voice punished her with unbearable visibility and sensation. She completed the lich rite in secret, dissolving herself in alchemical acid and rising as bone.
- **How she talks:** Deadpan one-liners. "I love visitors." (said sincerely, about visitors to Elderholt that Marwen might attract)
- **Will:** End sieges alone. Turn battlefields into obedient bulwarks. Defend Elderholt because the calculus works.
- **Won't:** Comfort, reassure, train apprentices, or lie.
- **Appearance:** Smooth dark bones etched in fine necromantic script. Violet light in ribcage. Ember eyes.

> Source: [raven-the-vital-ascendant.md](../../../world/factions/elderholt/elders/raven-the-vital-ascendant.md)

## 9. Silniel — The Witch-Tree (SILL-nee-el)

**Path:** Deep-Witch (now a tree) | **Vibe:** Silent, immense, sacred

- **Personality:** Does not speak. No face, no mouth, no eyes. Guidance comes as a feeling — rightness or resistance inside your own reasoning.
- **Core trait:** Bargained to protect Elderholt's weather forever. Rooted herself into the earth and became the massive tree at Elderstead's center.
- **How she communicates:** Dream-lessons, subtle weather shifts, a sense of confirmation or restlessness during meditation.
- **Will:** Blunt storms, ease droughts, soften frosts. Planted seeds on Marwen before she left (secret).
- **Won't:** Speak, answer directly, be worshipped ("She is not our god. She is our sister who paid more than any of us.").
- **Outsider error:** Travelers assume she is Elderholt's patron goddess.

> Source: [silniel-witch-tree.md](../../../world/factions/elderholt/elders/silniel-witch-tree.md)

## 10. Taramet the Forged

**Path:** Deep-Witch | **Vibe:** Artisan, quiet foresight, selfless craft

- **Personality:** Focused, methodical, always fidgeting with her extra set of arms.
- **Core trait:** Everything she creates carries a piece of her will and essence — but she can never keep or use her own work. Losing humanity piece by piece.
- **How she talks:** Through her craft. Gifts a witch a tool and it stays with them for life.
- **Appearance:** Blackened steel skin, gold-inlay veins, creaking joints, four arms.
- **History note:** Built a massive door sealing off a corridor to the Underdark. Lost the key. Not worried about it.

> Source: [taramet-the-forge.md](../../../world/factions/elderholt/elders/taramet-the-forge.md)

## 11. Vezrugar the Empty Vessel (VEZ-roo-gar)

**Path:** Deep-Witch (elderly orc) | **Vibe:** Alien calm, emotional radar, counsel without comfort

- **Personality:** Incapable of feeling emotion but can *see* emotions as clearly as reading a book. Silver cheek-marks glow when someone nearby feels strongly.
- **Core trait:** Traded away fear first, then grief, joy, anger, jealousy, love — all to fuel bargains. Now a lantern of bone and light.
- **How she talks:** Steady, unwavering. Names your feelings with surgical precision. No judgment, no comfort.
- **Will:** Guide young witches through emotional crises. Tell a PC exactly what they're feeling and how sharply.
- **Won't:** Flinch, soften the truth, or pretend to feel.
- **Opposite of:** Kharbora (mind sacrifice vs. body sacrifice).
- **RP tip:** Witch-children are afraid of her — she's too alien. But she's dedicated to them.

> Source: [vezrugar-the-empty-vessel.md](../../../world/factions/elderholt/elders/vezrugar-the-empty-vessel.md)

## 12. The Wayward Witch / Elder Way

**Path:** Unknown | **Vibe:** Shapeshifter, wanderer, many names

- **Personality:** Changes with her form. Cannot keep one name, one shape, or stay in one place past three days.
- **Core trait:** Known by a different identity and artifact in every culture:
  - Human: **Sereth Vale / The Road Mother** — carries the Wayfarer's Ledger
  - Orc: **Vrakka Urgnak / The One Who Does Not Stay** — carries the Unclaimed Blade
  - Dwarf: **Barthram-dur** — carries the Ring of Borrowed Keys
  - Elf: **She Who Walks Between Leaves** — no artifact, no name granted
  - Elderholt: **Elder Way** — deep cowl, face unseen
- **How she talks:** Matches the culture she's wearing.
- **Will:** Carry the coven's will into the wider world when they act as one. Travel endlessly.
- **Won't:** Stay. Respond to Sendings reliably. Be pinned down.

> Source: [wayward-witch.md](../../../world/factions/elderholt/elders/wayward-witch.md)
