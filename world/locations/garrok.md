# Garrok

> Mootgrounds and longhouse courts.

- Culture: Orc (Garrok Confederation)
- Type: Capital gathering grounds
- Faction: [Garrok-Confederation](../factions/garrok-confederation.md)

## Description
Garrok is a sprawling mootground where clans erect longhouses during conclaves. Authority is appointed in crisis and dissolved when the threat passes.

## Points of Interest
- High Moot Circle — stone ring for choosing a War-Leader
- Oathfire Pits — where recompense is judged and settled
- Farbanner Row — market for raids’ spoils and barter oaths

## Adventure Hooks
- A single betrayal threatens to unify all clans in vengeance.
- A disputed recompense risks igniting a generational feud.
- Scouts report a threat requiring an immediate war moot.
