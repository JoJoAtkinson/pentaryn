# Merrowgate

> The city itself is the capital; everything is for sale.

- Culture: Human (City of Bargains)
- Type: Capital city
- Faction: [Merrowgate](../factions/merrowgate.md)

## Description
Canals and counting houses define Merrowgate. Prosperity hums under the Silent Patron’s unseen influence, guiding contracts and civic choices.

## Points of Interest
- Ledger Court — grand forum for auctions and contracts
- Maskers’ Quarter — guild of faces, whispers, and forgery
- Silent Vault — rumored shrine to a higher patronage

## Adventure Hooks
- A citywide charter subtly rewrites itself overnight.
- A masked councilor’s true patron is not of this world.
- A dragon-tithe rumor spikes insurance rates across wards.
