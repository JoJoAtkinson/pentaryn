# Aeralithil (AIR-ah-LITH-il)

> Sacred heart-city beneath the Veiled Crown.

- Culture: Elven (Araethilion)
- Type: Capital city
- Faction: [Araethilion](../factions/araethilion.md)

## Description
Aeralithil is a radiant forest capital where living temples and songwoven halls encircle the Veiled Crown’s sanctum. Beauty and abundance mask the city’s hidden rites.

## Points of Interest
- Veiled Crown Sanctum — seat of the "living god" and high priestly court
- Lifelume Arbors — festival groves blessed with uncanny harvests
- Whisper Galleries — dissident circles trading visions and truths

## Adventure Hooks
- A seer seeks proof of the life-siphon rite.
- A harvest festival hides a high ritual with dire costs.
- A dissident choir requests protection to reveal a prophecy.
