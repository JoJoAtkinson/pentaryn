# Elderstead

> High Coven seat and tithe archives.

- Culture: Human (Elderholt — Coven Realm)
- Type: Capital city
- Faction: [Elderholt](../factions/elderholt.md)

## Description
Elderstead’s ritual circles and tithe archives dominate its skyline. Protective wards ring the city; the cost of peace is written in names.

## Points of Interest
- High Coven Spire — seat of the Witch-Queen
- Tithe Archives — ledgers of taken children and returned protectors
- Warding Stones — ancient anchors for the realm’s protections

## Adventure Hooks
- A ledger discrepancy reveals a hidden class of “living wards.”
- A village petitions to end the tithe; the wards falter.
- A coven’s rival hints at redirected threats.
