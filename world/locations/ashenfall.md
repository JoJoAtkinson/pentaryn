# Ashenfall

> Soot-wreathed imperial capital and throne district.

- Culture: Human (Calderon Imperium — The Ashen Empire)
- Type: Capital city
- Faction: [Calderon Imperium](../factions/calderon-imperium.md)

## Description
A vast metropolis of basalt courts and smokestack manufactories, Ashenfall’s throne district overlooks regimented avenues patrolled by secret police and imperial legions.

## Points of Interest
- Black Throne Court — seat of the Emperor/Empress
- Census Basilica — bureaucratic nerve center recording every soul
- Veilwatch — headquarters of the Empire’s clandestine enforcers

## Adventure Hooks
- A missing ledger ties a noble to infernal pacts.
- Undead troop movements are masked as “sanitation brigades.”
- A factory fire exposes a forbidden research wing.
