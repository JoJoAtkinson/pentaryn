# Ardenford

> River-crossing capital and council seat.

- Culture: Human (Ardenhaven)
- Type: Capital city
- Faction: [Ardenhaven](../factions/ardenhaven.md)

## Description
Set astride a broad ford and stone bridgeworks, Ardenford houses the crown’s palace and the Council Hall where lords, guilds, and temples convene.

## Points of Interest
- Council Hall — debating chamber for the constitutional monarchy
- Fordspine Bridge — the city’s lifeline and trade artery
- Crownward Keep — modest palace with open petitions days

## Adventure Hooks
- A vote risks deadlock on rebuilding border defenses.
- Smugglers exploit a war-scarred district’s weak oversight.
- Guild intrigue threatens fair grain prices ahead of winter.
