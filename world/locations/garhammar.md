# Garhammar

> Guildhall metropolis and charter vaults.

- Culture: Dwarven (Garhammar Trade League)
- Type: Capital city
- Faction: [Garhammar-Trade-League](../factions/garhammar-trade-league.md)

## Description
Garhammar thrives on ledgers and engines. Guild charters, arbitration halls, and contract foundries power a weaponized neutrality.

## Points of Interest
- Charter Vault — sealed repository of guild compacts
- Arbitration Hall — resolves cross-border disputes for a fee
- Gearworks — industrial quarter exporting machines and arms

## Adventure Hooks
- A guild compact quietly sells ancestral mines to foreign powers.
- Arbitration threatens to arm both sides of a looming war.
- A whistleblower exposes a ledger cooked by infernal counsel.
