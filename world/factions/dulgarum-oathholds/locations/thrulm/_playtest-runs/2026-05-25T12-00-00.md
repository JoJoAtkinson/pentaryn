Until DD-18 is resolved (terrain constraint, HP buff, or Fireball restriction), this slot of findings will repeat. **Log for human; do NOT auto-fix.**

**FI-66 (NEW): PTV disadv triggered once but never consumed — 5th-cycle pattern confirmed.**

SC-C's R1 Throw 2 hit Marwen → PTV set (disadv on Marwen's next save). Marwen's R2 action: Scorching Ray (attack roll, not a save). PTV expired unused. The PTV→save-disadv chain requires: (1) SC multiattack hit, (2) the surviving SC still has a save-based ability lined up (Barrage is not save-based for the *caster*, but it IS Dex-save based for the *targets*), and (3) the fight continues long enough for the chain to complete. With only SC-C surviving at 6 HP into R2, the chain is structurally impossible. Positive direction: in cycles where 2+ SCs survive to R2, PTV demonstrated correctly (cycle 4: FI-33). The mechanic works when given time. **Log for human; do NOT auto-fix.**

Sabriel's low initiative (4) means she is consistently the last PC to act. In R1 she attacked SC-C (1 miss + 1 hit with smite for 12 dmg).
