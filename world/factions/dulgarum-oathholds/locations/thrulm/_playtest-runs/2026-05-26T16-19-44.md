- **FI-154 (NEW PATTERN): DD-39 / FI-74 8th consecutive cycle confirm — DW1 (low init) killed before acting.** Bazgar init 19 beat SC init 18 by 1. SC CW'd dead DW1 (−9 HP). The DW's +2 Dex init is too low to avoid pre-emptive death vs a Fighter with AS. No design fix needed (patrol fragility is intended), but the CW-waste is a feel problem: the SC should scan the battlefield before spending CW. The sim's CW logic doesn't check target alive state — this is a sim harness bug, not an encounter bug. **Log for human awareness: 8/8 TP cycles have produced a dead-before-acting DW. Consider whether this is a desired encounter feel.**
