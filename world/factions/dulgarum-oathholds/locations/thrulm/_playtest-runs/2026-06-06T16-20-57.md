### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-TP0-A: Threshold Patrol is a non-encounter for a L5 party (balance)**
The formation (2× CR⅛ + 1× CR2) has roughly 87 combined HP and no resistance to spells. A single Fireball wiped the entire patrol before SC or DW2 acted. The fight lasted 1 round and consumed negligible resources (1 spell slot, 1 Action Surge). If this is meant as the "alarm fight" before the real encounter, it is appropriate as a surprise ambush. But as a standalone encounter it creates zero tension. Recommend either: (a) add 2× derro-rager to create melee pressure before the caster can fire, or (b) use a room-layout that blocks Fireball LoS until the party has already been hit.

**FI-TP0-B: SC initiative puts it behind Marwen in average-roll scenarios (feel)**
Marwen and SC both have +2 Dex init modifier. In 50% of cases Marwen acts before SC; when she does, Fireball preemptively wipes SC's field of play. SC's Shard-Barrage and Call Weakness never fired in this run. SC is the tactically interesting unit in this formation — its toolkit (CW + stagger + barrage) deserves at least 1 round of spotlight. Consider scripting a surprise-round for the NPCs (they were mid-conversation, the party initiated) or giving SC a +4 Dex modifier (+2 → +4) to ensure it reliably acts before casters. *Note: changing SC Dex is a balance change — flag for human decision.*

**FI-TP0-C: Shard-Barrage never fired in any of the Threshold Patrol slices (rotation 0)**
The corridor setup is specifically designed to test Barrage in the narrow-hall scenario (per the MQ-41/FI-75 note in the SC tactics). But Fireball wiped the field before init 8. The barrage's corridor heuristic ("fire R1 in a ≤30ft corridor") is correct but assumes SC acts before or concurrent with party casters — which doesn't hold when SC rolls low. Flag for DM: explicitly set SC init at table in threshold encounters to guarantee at least one Barrage.

**FI-TP0-D: CW use-case guide missing for sub-2-round encounters (DM guidance)**
Call Weakness (3/day) is useless in a 1-round fight. The SC tactics docs have extensive guidance for multi-round CW usage but no guidance for "fight might end before CW pays off." Add a note: in fast-collapsing fights (≤3 total NPC HP pools), fire Barrage first; if combat will clearly last only 1 round, CW is wasted — save charges for the next encounter. This is a docs fix, not a spec bug.

### Mechanical questions
- **Tactical Drilling re-roll methodology:** My playtest re-rolled both DW1 attacks with advantage when Tactical Drilling was active. The spec says "advantage on melee attack roll against the target." The DB output pre-rolls without TD (flat +2 to-hit). At the table, DM should apply advantage on top of the DB roll — re-roll the d20 portion manually. This is expected behavior; note added for DM reference.
- **Fireball vs SC survivor at 2 HP:** SC survived Fireball at 2/33 (31 fire, FAIL save). This is correct — SC has 33 HP; 31 damage leaves 2 HP. Bazgar Action Surge finished it. No spec issue.
