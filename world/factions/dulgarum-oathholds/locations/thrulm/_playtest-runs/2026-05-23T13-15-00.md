### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

#### DD-33 (NEW): Shrine-touched consistently outpace party in initiative — final confrontation is one-sided before R1 party turn
- **Both shrine-touched rolled init 23 (20+3 Dex).** Bazgar rolled 15, Sabriel rolled 11. Both PCs acted *after* both shrine-touched and after lair action. Result: Sabriel was reduced from 44 HP to 27 HP before she could act once in R1. In R2, both shrine-touched fired Ancient Resonance simultaneously before any PC turn, dropping Bazgar to 17 HP and Marwen to 6 HP (then prone from lair action) before any PC action. This is the second consecutive final-confrontation run where shrine-touched initiative (consistently 18–23) dominates R1–R2.
- **Root cause:** Shrine-touched Dex modifier is +3 (Speed 30 ft +10 near altar, AC 16 hardened skin implies high Dex). This consistently rolls 17–23 initiative — above the party's average of 13–16.
- **Recommendation (do NOT auto-fix):** Either (a) give shrine-touched lower Dex mod (+1 or +0) for initiative purposes only (physical agility is suppressed by the shrine's weight on them), OR (b) in the final confrontation, have shrine-touched start 120 ft away (opposite end of chamber) so they close over R1 rather than acting first. Option (b) preserves the stat block and fits the lore (shrine-touched would orbit the altar, not rush the entrance). Human review required.

#### DD-34 (NEW): Drain Divinity succeeded but had zero impact — fight ends too fast for legendary compounding
- **First Drain Divinity success** across all runs: Sabriel Cha save 15 vs DC 16 (R2). Effect: stripped 1 L2 smite slot, beholder gained 4 temp HP. By the time it fired, Sabriel had 20 HP and was going to die in R3 regardless. The stripped slot was never used. The 4 temp HP on a 110-HP creature is set-dressing.
- **Pattern:** Drain Divinity is only useful if it fires in R1 (when slot resources still affect the fight's arc) and the party survives long enough to attempt healing/buffing. In the final confrontation, the fight ends R3 and the party never attempts a heal. The ability's counter-heal design goal requires ≥4 rounds to manifest.
- **Recommendation (log only):** No change. This is structural: the final confrontation is designed to be a TPK. Drain Divinity's design is correctly aimed at longer engagements (beholder-escorts-limited, empty-void). The only way to demonstrate it in final confrontation is if the party somehow survives to R4+ — which may happen with different seeds or a better-positioned party start.

#### DD-35 (NEW): Fireball killed 2 thralls in R1, but the 2 survivors harassed Bazgar for 2 rounds without consequence — thrall HP floor creates noise not threat
- Thrall-1 and Thrall-3 each survived Fireball at 10 HP (saved for half, 25/2=12 → with 22 HP each). They continued attacking Bazgar for R1–R3 (dealt 4 piercing damage total across all attacks — one hit in R2). Their 3-round harassment contributed 4 damage and zero meaningful threat while their removal would have freed Bazgar from one condition check per round.
- The thrall-derro HP (22 = 4d8+4) is calibrated for a CR 1/4 creature. At L5, a Fireball half-damage save still leaves them at 10 HP — just enough to continue attacking for 1–2 more rounds before the party can clean them up. This creates "cleanup tax" without threat.
- **Recommendation (log only):** No structural change. At table, a DM would use the beholder's `compel_thrall` bonus action to direct the surviving thralls toward more useful positioning (blocking Marwen's LoS, moving to engage Sabriel). The simulation's dumb-targeting underestimates thrall utility. Not a DB bug.

#### FI-4 (ONGOING): Healing-reversal mechanic still never tested
- In this run: Sabriel had LoH available (25 HP) and L1 heals available, but was never in a position to use them — shrine-touched went before her in every round where she was at risky HP. The healing-backfire mechanic (spells deal necrotic, LoH unaffected) has never been tested in any final-confrontation run because the party dies too fast to attempt heals. This is not a bug, but the DM table-warning ("this ruling WILL come up — make it before the first heal attempt") has never been validated in play.
- **Recommendation:** Add a note to the encounter briefing: "If this fight runs longer than 3 rounds (higher-level party, extra terrain features), prepare to rule on healing backfire before combat. Sabriel's player WILL reach for Cure Wounds." Not a change to current stats.

#### Confirmation of DD-6 (2nd cycle): Double Ancient Resonance structural — not seed-dependent
- DD-6 (2026-05-23 shrine-wedge run) first noted: both shrine-touched firing Resonance R1 simultaneously depletes the mechanic before recharge tension emerges. This run confirms the same pattern with both shrine-touched firing Resonance R2 (both available, both met trigger conditions). The "will Resonance recharge?" dynamic never appears in any shrine-heavy slice.
- **Status of DD-6 recommendation:** Still pending human review. Auto-stagger tactic added to `shrine-touched-derro.md` for the shrine-wedge slice, but the final-confrontation slice has no such restriction.

#### MQ-7: Prone from Void Eruption + Beholder tentacle — advantage not applied (same as DD-31)
