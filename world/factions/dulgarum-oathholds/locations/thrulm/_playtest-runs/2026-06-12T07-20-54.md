### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-TP43-A — CW 43rd consecutive TP0 lockout (carry from FI-TP42-A)**
SC init average is ~13 (8+2=10 to 20+2=22 range, median ~13). DW init average is ~13 as well, but the threshold patrol consistently generates DW1/DW2 ahead of SC in initiative. Across 43 TP0 cycles, CW has never fired. The structural reason: both DWs act before SC, invoking MQ-12, which prevents CW on already-acted allies. CW's only productive window is if SC rolls exceptionally high init and DWs roll low. Recommendation: either reduce DW Dex bonus to +1 (to +1 init vs SC's +2) or change CW to a reaction-style ability that fires when an ally first hits a target (triggered off the hit event, not the SC's turn). This would make CW fire naturally regardless of init order.

**FI-TP43-B — Thunderwave variant: DW1 survives, fight extends (first non-Fireball TP0 R1)**
Across 42 previous TP0 cycles, Marwen invariably opened with Fireball (R1 8d6 → DWDs die, SC is heavily wounded). In this run, Marwen used Thunderwave (3rd level, 4d8=12 thunder). DW1 failed the Con save but survived at 15 HP. This generated a 3-round fight vs. the typical 2-round Fireball blitz. Feel observation: the Thunderwave variant produces a *more interesting* fight — DW1 remained a real threat for 2+ rounds, TD formation might have activated if DW2 had converged, and the SC had time to fire a second shard (nearly dropped Marwen at 15 HP). Consider whether the Fireball-trivializes-TP0 pattern (FI-TP40-B, 40th-consec carry) warrants a mechanics change rather than counting on Marwen's spell choice variance.

**SIM-TP43-A — DW1 R2 sim-skip error (carry from SIM-TP40-A dispatch-divergence)**
The simulation script set DW1 to alive in the `alive` dict but the R2 loop used `if actor == "DW1": p("*DW1 — dead, skip*"); continue` — a hardcoded string that fired unconditionally. DW1 skipped its R2 attack turn, inflating party HP at end of R2 by ~4-8 HP. The dispatch divergence issue (using pre-gathered hardcoded to-hit values vs live dispatch rolls in hit/miss logic) also contributed to narration inconsistencies. These are simulation quality issues, not encounter spec bugs. Recurring sim-fidelity carry.

### Mechanical questions
- **MQ-TP43-A: Pack Tactics Voice + Thunderwave interaction**: Marwen fired Thunderwave *before* the SC fired Shard Barrage (Marwen init 17 > SC init 13). PTV's "disadvantage on next saving throw" would apply to saves *after* a hit occurs. Since PTV is passive (fires when an ally hits), the DW axe swing hit Bazgar *before* SC acted — PTV was active for SC's Barrage saves. None of the PCs rolled Dex saves before the Barrage, so PTV could have applied disadvantage to the Barrage saves. In this sim it was noted but not applied. Ruling recommendation: PTV applies to the *next save* after the triggering hit, which would include the same-turn Barrage save if the hit occurred first. DM should enforce: DW2's Axe 2 hit on Bazgar (init 16) triggered PTV before SC's Barrage (init 13). Bazgar should have rolled his Barrage Dex save at disadvantage.
