### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-SW1-41-A** (carry): **UF advantage structurally inaccessible below 20 HP — 41st consecutive SW1**
Both STDs had Unstable Form active entering R2 (STD-A 31 dmg, STD-B 16 dmg — both well above the 10-dmg threshold). Neither expressed UF advantage on attack rolls. STD-A was in low-HP mode (< 20 HP) → forced to Driven Escape + reckless AR, bypassing attack rolls entirely. STD-B was killed before its turn.

The pattern is structural: the damage threshold that activates UF (≥10 damage in one round) is low enough that it triggers even on moderate hits. But by the time two STDs have both taken ≥10 damage in a round, at least one is likely also below 20 HP (since their max is 45). The low-HP mode unconditionally overrides to AR + Driven Escape, which makes UF advantage unreachable for that STD in that round.

Recommendation (from previous cycles): Consider raising the low-HP threshold to 15 HP instead of 20 HP to create a window where UF advantage on attacks can express before low-HP mode kicks in. Or: explicitly allow UF to override the "prefer AR when 2+ in cone" rule and force multiattack when UF is active even in the cone, using the fact that adv-on-attacks is directly lost by using save-based AR.

**FI-SW1-41-B** (carry): **VICTORY R2 — 3rd R2 result today, 41st consecutive SW1 party victory**
The shrine-wedge slice has produced a party victory every single run. R2 fights (today: 3/3) indicate Fireball + smite burst is consistently resolving the slice faster than the STDs' mechanics can develop. UF, stagger-hold arc (fire R1, hold R2, fire R2), and OBR counter-attack chain all require surviving into rounds 3-4 to express meaningfully. With Fireball available R1, that doesn't happen.

**SIM-SW1-41-A**: Marwen cast Fireball R2 with Bazgar at 5 HP in the blast zone → Bazgar downed by friendly fire. A real DM would cast Magic Missile or another targeted spell. Simulation gap; does not affect NPC spec.

**FI-SW1-41-C** (altar zone): Altar zone fire suppression confirmed decisive for 41st consecutive SW1. Without suppression:
- R1 Fireball 32 fire: both STDs fail save → 64 fire each (doubled vuln) → both dead R1
- With suppression: both take 16 (saved) → both survive to R2 with 29 HP
Zone adds at least one full round to every fire-heavy fight. Mechanic is working as designed.
