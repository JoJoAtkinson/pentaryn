### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-TW2-A — Taunt+CW pairing never executed (core mechanic untested)**
The slice title is "tank-wall" specifically to test Taunt + Call Weakness working in concert. Neither mechanic reached resolution: the Rager rolled nat 1 initiative (init = 1, last in order), was killed in R1 by Sabriel + Bazgar before it ever acted. Taunt was never used. CW was fired by the SC on the Rager (correct per tactics: Rager had a later turn slot when SC acted), but the Rager was then killed before collecting the advantage. This is not a bug — the tactics logic was correct — but the core playtest goal was missed entirely. **Recommendation:** In the tank-wall slice, consider giving the Rager a fixed initiative bonus (e.g. +3 initiative like "Berserker's Fury" trait) so it reliably acts in the top half of the order. A melee tank that always goes last never absorbs any hits.

**FI-TW2-B — CW on doomed Rager (predictable waste at very low init)**
MQ-12 (already-acted-ally guard) prevents CW from being wasted on allies who have *already taken their turn*. But there is no guard for "ally will be killed before their turn by multiple PCs." When the Rager has init 1 (last) and the Rager is at 35 HP against Sabriel+Bazgar who both act before it, the CW advantage will very often be wasted. The SC tactics could add: "If the Rager's initiative is significantly lower than the heaviest-hitting PCs, hold CW until R2 when the Rager will likely have survived." This is a edge-case refinement, not critical — but worth noting since it fired in this run.

**FI-TW2-C — Fireball-decisive pattern: 4th+ consecutive occurrence**
In every slice so far where Marwen has initiative before the SC, she opens with Fireball and the encounter resolves in 1–2 rounds. Slices 0, 1, 2 (and prior runs) all show this. The SC Barrage is mechanically sound but it fires AFTER Fireball has already halved both NPCs. The issue is not the SC design — it is that a L5 Wizard with initiative advantage trivially wins any 2-NPC fight with a single L3 slot. This is **expected** (this encounter is a sub-encounter; full thrulm with beholder is where the real threat lives), but worth noting: if the DM wants to stress-test NPC mechanics, the playtest should seed initiative to ensure the SC acts first at least 50% of the time. Recommend adding a `force_initiative_order` key to the rotation slice spec.

### Mechanical questions

**MQ-TW2-A — Rager DEX +0: tank role requires reliable early initiative**
A melee tank's whole design is "absorbs hits, taunts, forces redirects." If the Rager consistently goes last (DEX+0 vs a party with Marwen DEX+2 and high rolls), it never absorbs any hits in R1 — it just dies. Should the Rager have an initiative bonus trait like "Battle Fury" (+3 initiative)? Or should the Rager's stats be bumped to DEX 12 (+1)? This is a balance design question — logging for human review, not auto-fixing.

**MQ-TW2-B — SC Barrage hit Marwen for 12 damage (dropped to 20 HP) — is this intended for a 2-NPC slice?**
Marwen at 20 HP is not immediately threatened, but a second Barrage R2 (which recharged on a 6) plus two Shard Throw hits would have likely downed her. Given that the Rager never acted, the SC did reasonable damage single-handedly. The 2-NPC tank-wall slice is probably appropriately lethal if Fireball is not available. No action needed — this is the intended feel for a CR4 equivalent pair.
