### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-NEW-FC4-A — 2-round TPK, 63% beholder HP remaining:** Party dealt only 40/110 HP. TPK before any PC's turn 2+ except Bazgar (1 attack) and Sabriel (1 LoH use each round). The death feels fast and may feel unearned at the table — PCs had almost no window to show what they could do. The intended design is that this encounter is not survivable, but a 2-round sweep before any defining moments may leave players flat. **Recommendation for human review**: consider whether a 4-5 round TPK with the party denting the beholder to 50-60 HP would feel better, or whether the R2 speed is correct for the "overwhelming abyss" vibe. No auto-fix — this is a balance/feel call.

- **FI-NEW-FC4-B — VS overkill makes fear rider irrelevant (FI-149 3rd-confirm in FC context):** Bazgar was at 9 HP when VS fired; took 42 psychic, died instantly. Fear rider (frightened) applied but never used — he was unconscious before his next turn. The fear rider value described in FI-149 requires the target to survive VS. When the fighter is softened by lair actions/vs to sub-20 HP before VS, overkill is nearly guaranteed. Pattern: lair void_eruption softened Bazgar from 21 → 9 HP, then VS one-shotted him. This is mechanically correct but means VS's signature ongoing effect (halved Bazgar melee for 2-3 rounds) never triggers in the FC context where he's already low. Logged; no fix needed.

- **FI-NEW-FC4-C — Killing blow was CR 1/4 thrall dagger, not a beholder ability:** Sabriel at 6 HP was finished by three thrall daggers (5+6+6=17 damage over 3 consecutive turns). While narratively coherent (the swarm overwhelms the last defender while the beholder watches), at the table a player may feel their paladin died to a minor summon rather than the void boss. No fix needed — the design intent is "death by attrition" — but the human should be aware this is the typical FC kill pattern when Sabriel survives VS.

- **MQ-NEW-FC4-D — DR spec is to-hit (+6), not save; Phase A dispatch rolled to-hit 12 (misses Marwen AC15):** The DB defines disintegration_ray as a `single_attack` with `to_hit_bonus: 6`. The simulation incorrectly applied a Dex save (DC16, half on save) instead. The Phase A dispatch rolled to-hit 12, which would MISS Marwen AC15 — meaning actual R1 DR might miss entirely. If DR misses R1, Marwen survives R1 at full HP and Fireball + Scorching Ray output in R1-R2 would be higher, potentially extending the fight 1-2 rounds. This is a **simulation accuracy issue**, not an encounter bug. Recommendation: future sim dispatches should use the DB to-hit output directly rather than re-simulating saves. Also worth noting at the table: DMs accustomed to standard beholder lore expect DR to be a Con save, not a to-hit. Consider adding a note to the .md tactical section.

- **Phase A infrastructure note (21st consecutive cycle):** quantumnumbers API and random.org both network-blocked in this sandbox. Patched with local pseudo-random seeded fallback. All 28 actions pass. No spec regression.
