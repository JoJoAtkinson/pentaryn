## Pre-Combat: Negotiation Attempt

The party descends the shaft into the main vault. Torchlight bends wrong.
The Hunger Below hovers over the altar, a hollow sphere of coagulated shadow,
four root-tentacles trailing across the stone. No eyes. Complete presence.

**Marwen steps forward.** She speaks in Common — no response. She tries fragments
of Dwarven she picked up at Dulgarum: 'We are not here to restore the shrine.'

Cold pressure floods three minds simultaneously. Incomplete dwarven, or something
older wearing its shape: *'shrine is fed / absence is fed / you carry light / light is hunger.*'

**Sabriel**, unknowingly, has her holy symbol visible. The beholder perceives divine
potential radiating off her — the antithesis of the void it feeds on. Its tentacles
tighten against the altar stone.

Marwen: 'We only want passage to the deeper shaft.' (Persuasion)
[Marwen Persuasion: d20(14)+3=17 → beholder pauses]

The beholder pauses. A flicker of something — not reason, but attention.
*'What do you offer? What is below is also mine. You cannot have it freely.'*

**Sabriel attempts to broker terms.** She offers to seal the upper gate — keeping
others out, preserving the void's feed-state.
[Sabriel Persuasion: d20(1)+4=5 — nat-1 body language, holy symbol blazes]

*'You carry the old faith's warmth. You would not keep the gate. You would heal this.'*
The beholder lunges. **Combat begins.**

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-EV7-40-A [NEW] Negotiation DC undefined — no written Persuasion DC for beholder cooperation:**
The beholder's Interaction Notes describe *when* negotiation can work (party not restoring shrine, offers something divine-adjacent, etc.) but there is no explicit DC anywhere in the `.md`. In this run, Sabriel's nat-1 (total 5) triggered combat; if she had rolled 15+ the outcome might have been "negotiated passage." At level 5 with +4 Cha, a DC 15 = 50% success, DC 20 = 25%. Without a written DC, each cron run's negotiation scene is arbitrary. **DESIGN DECISION needed:** Set explicit Persuasion DC (recommend DC 18 base for passage; DC 14 if party demonstrates concrete hostility to shrine restoration; advantage if party carries an artifact connected to Khôr-Bhog-Khôr). Add to Interaction Notes section of `beholder-thrulm.md`.

**FI-EV7-40-B [CARRY] LR 3/3 idle in all EV7 runs:**
As in cycles 37–39, the beholder never spent a single Legendary Resistance in a 3-round fight. LR is a "tank big save" mechanic, but this party doesn't land the kind of multi-hit save-or-suck spells (Hold Person, Hypnotic Pattern, Banishment) that force LR burn. The beholder's damage output resolves the fight before any LR pressure is needed. This is probably fine (LR is calibrated for parties harder than level 5), but across 40 EV7 cycles LR has fired ~3 times total (all against Hold Person). Consider noting explicitly in the `.md` that LR against Dex saves from Fireball is **not** recommended — reserve LR for Wis/Cha control spells. (The tactics section already says this implicitly via the DD-reserve protocol; a clearer "LR spending priority" note would help live DMs.)

**FI-EV7-40-C [CARRY] DR never fires in short fights:**
Disintegration Ray has recharged exactly 0 times in the last 4+ EV7 cycles (2 attempts per fight at 5-6, ≈11% per fight). In a 3-round fight the recharge window is narrow. The tactical notes in the `.md` about DR targeting (FIX-EV7-30-A, MQ-FC45-A) are solid, but DR is never getting a turn in this format. For a "player is meant to know they are outmatched" encounter, DR firing at least once per full encounter would reinforce that dread. **DESIGN DECISION:** Consider recharge 4-6 or 3-6 for DR to increase its expected fire rate against a level-5 party. Current rate: ~33% per attempt, ~11% per 3-round fight.

**FI-EV7-40-D [NEW POSITIVE] Void Ray + Void Scream R2 combo is a clean two-step kill:**
Sabriel passed the VS save (rolled 20) but still died because she was at 9hp (reduced by VR) and took 18 psychic (half VS). The sequence — VR strips HP, VS finishes even on a save — feels earned and avoids the "lucky save saves the day" narrative. The combo is a natural product of the LA economy (2 LA for VR, then VS action) and doesn't feel cheesy. This is a positive feel note: the beholder rewarding good action sequencing.

**FI-EV7-40-E [NEW] DD fired correctly with correct slot target:**
In this run, DD stripped Sabriel's L1 slot (lowest remaining highest-level = L1, since L2 had 2 slots remaining). **Wait — this is a rules question.** DD text says "loses one spell slot of the **highest level** it has remaining." Sabriel had L2 (2 slots) and L1 (4 slots) at DD time. Highest level = L2. The sim stripped L1 instead. This is a **simulation error** — the correct result is L2 stripped, not L1. The THP gain would have been 4 (not 2). Impact is minor (2 THP vs 4 THP) but the slot stripped matters: losing an L2 is more painful than L1 for smite economy. **SIM-EV7-40-A:** DD should always strip the highest available slot level, not the lowest.

### Mechanical questions

**MQ-EV7-40-A [NEW] VR + VS combo: does VR grapple-plus-VS constitute double dipping?**
In this run Sabriel was not grappled when VS fired (grapple would have been from R1 multiattack, cleared between rounds). But in rounds where a tentacle grapples a PC and then VS fires in the same turn — does frightened (from VS) + grappled (from tentacle) stack meaningfully? Yes: frightened imposes disadvantage on attacks while beholder in LoS; grappled imposes speed=0. Both apply. Neither interferes with the other. No confusion here, but worth noting for the live DM who might miss the double condition.

**MQ-EV7-40-B [CARRY from 40+ cycles] Void-Feeding (+1 attack/damage near shrine) not reflected in DB spec:**
The beholder's `.md` specifies +1 to attack rolls and damage rolls while within the shrine chamber (Void-Feeding trait). None of the DB action specs include this modifier — all attack rolls are hardcoded at +6 (not +7 in the shrine). The dispatch output is therefore slightly under-reporting the beholder's to-hit and damage. In 40+ EV7 cycles, this gap has never changed a meaningful outcome (the +1 is small), but a DB update would close the gap between `.md` and `actions.jsonl`. DESIGN DECISION: add +1 to_hit_bonus and damage_modifier in all beholder attack actions, or add a conditional note in the `.md` for DMs to manually adjust.

**MQ-EV7-40-C [CARRY] Negotiation path unexplored — no cron test for full negotiation success:**
All 40 EV7 runs have ended in combat. The negotiation branch (party successfully passes, fight averted) has never been playtested. This is a structural gap: the "empty void" slice description says "party tries to negotiate" but the test never reaches the negotiation-success branch. With a defined DC (see FI-EV7-40-A) the cron could test both paths. Consider a separate EV7 sub-variant with a modified party (high-Cha bard or +6 Persuasion PC) to test the de-escalation path.
