### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-1 (DD-14): Solo-rager-rush resolves in 2 rounds — signature mechanics untested**

The slice is supposed to test "Berserk recharge, aggro-mark riders, taunt-induced movement" (briefing). Marwen's two Fireballs dealt 60–80 fire damage to each rager across 2 rounds, doing more damage than all 6 multiattacks combined. The fight ended before:
- Any Berserk recharge roll was needed (Berserk fired but the same-round Fireball killed the berserker)
- Any taunt-forced movement happened (Taunt only forces disadvantage on attacks, not movement — briefing's "taunt-induced movement" may be a mismatch with the DB spec)
- Madness Endurance ever produced a visible hit

**Recommendation:** Either (a) bump rager HP to ~65 to survive a Fireball hit and force a 4+ round fight, or (b) add terrain/position rules that limit Fireball AOE overlap (natural columns in the vault could restrict 3-in-area). The slice as designed is solved by the wizard in 2 turns.

---

**FI-2: Berserk always shows 3 attack lines regardless of in-reach count**

The DB `attacks` array for `berserk` has 3 hardcoded entries: "Greataxe (target 1)", "(target 2)", "(target 3)". When Rager#2 berserked in Round 2 with only 2 living PCs in range, the output still printed a third attack line with a resolved roll. The prerequisite text says "fewer attacks if fewer in reach" but the roller cannot enforce this — DM must skip the extra line manually.

**Recommendation:** Document in `derro-rager.md` tactics: "Berserk output always shows 3 attacks — mentally cap at number of creatures actually in reach (DM prunes)." OR restructure the DB spec so the DM knows to hand-resolve variable-target Berserk using only the first N attacks for N creatures.
*(Not auto-fixed — DB spec design, not a broken spec.)*

---

**FI-3: Taunt targets squishy PC but ragers already focus squishy PC — redundant pressure**

Ragers naturally converge on Marwen (lowest HP/AC). Taunt is designed to force disadvantage on attacking anyone *but* the rager — but since the rager is already pursuing Marwen, Taunt's effect is wasted on the same axis. The mechanic only has value when ragers want to draw fire *off* a weaker ally or protect a Shardcaller. In the solo-rager slice with no support NPCs, Taunt degrades into a flavor flourish with no mechanical upside.

**Recommendation:** Update Taunt tactics to: "Do NOT taunt the PC you are already targeting. Taunt the PC most likely to switch targets this turn (the one NOT currently engaged with another rager)." Low-risk authoring clarification.

---

**FI-4: Berserk hits all 3 PCs — terrain not constraining Berserk's AOE**

The Thrulm vault has natural stone columns (from `_overview.md`). A spinning greataxe arc should plausibly NOT hit every PC if some are behind cover or at column-split positions. The sim assumed all PCs in reach, which inflated Berserk's value. A DM note in `_overview.md` or the rager tactics suggesting "Berserk is credibly AOE only if 2+ PCs are within 10 ft in an open corridor — use column positions to limit this" would improve encounter ecology.

### Mechanical questions

**MQ-1: "Taunt-induced movement" in briefing doesn't match DB spec**

Briefing says the solo-rager-rush "tests... taunt-induced movement." But the Taunt spec only imposes disadvantage on attacks (DC 12 Cha save), not forced movement. There is no push/pull/redirect in the Taunt DB entry. Either (a) the briefing is wrong and should say "taunt-induced attack disadvantage," or (b) there's an intended mechanic where a failed taunt forces the target to move toward the rager that hasn't been authored yet.

**Recommendation:** Clarify in `thrulm-cron-prompt.md` or add a Taunt note in `derro-rager.md`. If forced movement is intended, it should be in the DB `effect` field.

---

**MQ-2: Madness Endurance (+1 to hit after taking damage) is untracked by roller**

The `.md` start-of-turn checklist item 2 says rager gets +1 to attack rolls if it took damage last turn. This is never reflected in `roll_combat_action` output — the DB multiattack spec doesn't carry a conditional `to_hit_bonus` modifier. Every DM must apply +1 manually after cross-checking HP vs last-turn HP.

**Recommendation:** Same as DD-7 / DD-10 recommendation family: out of cron blast radius. Human to decide: either add a `conditional_bonus` field to the spec schema, or explicitly document in tactics that Madness Endurance is DM-manual-only (and strip it from the start-of-turn checklist to avoid confusion).
