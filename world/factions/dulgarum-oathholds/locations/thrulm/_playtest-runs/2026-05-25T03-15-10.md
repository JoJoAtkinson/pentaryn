### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

#### FI-32 (NEW): Tactical Retreat structural 0/4 cycles — SCs die before PCs close to melee
- **Observation:** TR never fires in any shardcaller-team cycle (0/4). The DD-47 authoring fix clarified TR is a BONUS ACTION (not a main-action trade). But the structural reason TR doesn't fire is that PCs focus-fire and eliminate SCs at range before the party ever closes to 15 ft. SC-A died before acting at 55+ ft range. SC-B died at range in R2. SC-C (sole survivor at 15 HP) would need to survive long enough for PCs to close — it did not.
- **Root cause:** SCs have 33 HP. Sabriel's L2 smite (avg 21 + weapon 7 = ~28) plus one Bazgar hit wipes a SC in one PC turn. PCs never need to advance into melee because the fight resolves from range.
- **Recommendation (no auto-fix):** Two options: (a) Raise SC HP to ~45 (10d8+10) so a smite doesn't one-shot — more rounds = PCs close = TR fires; (b) Change TR trigger from "if melee threat within 15 ft" to "when 1+ ally drops — pre-emptive retreat to maintain 40 ft range." Option (b) is an authoring-only change, low risk. DD-47 remains open.

#### FI-33 (NEW — POSITIVE): PTV fired for first time across 4 shardcaller-team cycles
- **Observation:** SC-C's double multiattack hit Marwen twice in R2 (throws 22 and 23 vs AC 15). Both triggered Pack Tactics Voice — Marwen had disadvantage on her next saving throw. PTV toggled (correctly: second hit refreshed rather than stacked). However, PTV disadv was never consumed — SC-C had no Barrage (not recharged) and no subsequent SC turn to enforce it. The mechanic works correctly at the authoring level; its payoff requires a Barrage to follow within the same round or PTV target takes a save-based action.
- **Positive signal:** PTV demonstrates in multiattack turns where 2+ hits land on the same target. CW advantage (from SC-B's R1 bonus action) was also active on SC-C's R2 throws — first time CW+multiattack synergy demonstrated in shardcaller-team.
- **Recommendation (no change):** PTV mechanic is correct. The payoff chain (SC hits → PTV active → Barrage follows → disadv saves) requires a longer fight with recharged Barrage available. Confirm at table: DM must track PTV flag between turns.

#### FI-34 (NEW): Fireball NOT used — first shardcaller-team cycle where Marwen never Fireballs
- **Observation:** SC-A died before acting, leaving only SC-B+SC-C by Marwen's R1 turn. Scorching Ray (3 bolts, more precise) was correct R1 choice. By R2, only 1 SC remained — Fireball wasteful on 1 target. DD-18 (Fireball trivializes) was not triggered this cycle. Instead, the fight resolved via focused melee+smite eliminating SCs one per turn.
- **Signal:** The outcome (VICTORY R3, party lightly damaged) is the same as cycles where Fireball fires (DD-18 pattern), but the dynamic was more interesting — SC-C's double hit on Marwen (17 piercing, 5/32 HP) was the first genuinely dangerous moment in any shardcaller-team cycle. Marwen survived by LoH.
- **Recommendation (no change):** This cycle suggests the slice CAN produce interesting moments without Fireball dominance IF the initiative order puts SCs first (forcing PCs to respond). The variance in SC initiative (SC-A at 18 in this run = effectively late due to PC tiebreak) creates wildly different threat levels. SC-B going last (init 3) is the main culprit — a SC acting last does nothing relevant.

#### MQ-23 (NEW): Harness doesn't apply CW advantage to SC multiattack rolls
- **Observation:** SC-B CW'd SC-C in R1 bonus action. SC-C's R2 multiattack should have rolled with advantage (max of 2d20 per throw). The sim loop used single d20 rolls — the high natural rolls (18, 19) happened to hit anyway, so the harness gap had no outcome impact this cycle. But CW advantage could be decisive on lower rolls.
- **Note:** Pre-existing infra gap (similar to DD-22 and DD-37 prior cycles). Fix: track `cw_active[target]` and apply `max(d20a, d20b)` in the NPC multiattack branch. Out of blast radius.

#### MQ-24 (NEW): SC-C is sole survivor with 15 HP and recharged Barrage — should it kite?
- **Observation:** R3, SC-C at 15 HP (below half HP = 16.5 threshold). Tactics say "Below 12 HP: retreat behind a pillar." SC-C was at 15/33 = 45% HP. In a shardcaller-only formation (no Rager), the 12 HP threshold is for retreating behind cover. SC-C wasn't at 12 HP, so per-tactics it should fight. It died to Sabriel's first swing.
- **Question:** Should the below-half-HP threshold for retreat be raised to ≤16 HP (half of 33) in a shardcaller-only formation with sole-NPC status? At 15/33 HP with party at full-range focus-fire, survival probability is ~0 regardless. At table, the DM might reasonably have SC-C sprint toward the deeper shaft (tactical retreat + Barrage if it recharges). The "below 12 HP" threshold seems too conservative for sole-survivor scenarios.
- **Recommendation (no auto-fix):** Consider adding "sole-survivor rule: if only 1 SC remains below half HP (≤16), Tactical Retreat and Barrage if recharged — maximize damage before falling." Requires human sign-off.
