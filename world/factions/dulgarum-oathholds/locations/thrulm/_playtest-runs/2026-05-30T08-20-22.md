### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**MQ-SCT-16-A (mechanical question — stagger/CW spec tension):**
SC-C called CW on SC-B (the barrage slot) after SC-B had already fired barrage at init 22. At the moment of CW (SC-C acts init 9), SC-B was at 33 HP (>20 HP guard threshold). MQ-12 literally allows CW on an already-acted ally at >20 HP. However, the stagger guidance says "CW on non-barrage multiattacking SCs" — SC-B is the barrage-slot SC. Outcome: SC-B died in R2 before collecting the CW advantage. The CW charge was wasted.

Resolution gap: when SC-C acts after the barrage-slot SC (who already fired and is at >20 HP), should MQ-12 permit CW on the barrage-slot SC, or should the stagger guidance exclude the barrage-slot SC from CW targeting entirely (even post-barrage, when the advantage would carry to next round)?

**Recommendation (log for human):** Add a line to the stagger CW guidance: "When no non-barrage-slot SC above 20 HP is available as a CW target, hold the charge rather than CW'ing the barrage-slot SC — the barrage-slot SC is the highest-priority kill target and unlikely to survive to collect the advantage." Not a spec change to mechanics, just a priority clarification.

---

**FI-SCT-16-B (feel — PTV 0-fire, 16th SCT cycle confirm):**
SC-C missed both shard-throws vs Marwen (AC15) in R1. PTV did not trigger. Pack Tactics Voice has now failed to generate any saving-throw disadvantage in 15 of 16 shardcaller-team cycles. The one exception (cycle 5) had low impact. PTV is structurally nearly vestigial in this formation: (a) SC multiattack hit rate vs AC15 is ~56% per throw; (b) two misses in R1 = 20% probability, common enough to deny PTV frequently; (c) when SCs die R1-R2 due to Fireball, there's rarely a "next saving throw" to penalize. This is an ongoing design concern (previously logged as FI-SCT-C).

---

**FI-SCT-16-C — NEW DESIGN DECISION (shardcaller-team with stagger enforced: encounter feel):**
With stagger correctly enforced (1 barrage R1 = 6 piercing to each saved PC), the 3× shardcaller encounter is not threatening a level-5 party. All PCs ended at 87-100% HP. The encounter resolved in 2 rounds with only a Fireball and two smites. The "dangerous mode" requires either: (a) stagger enforcement failure (triple-barrage R1 nearly TPK'd in cycle 15), or (b) all PCs failing their Dex saves (statistically ~7% for a DC13 save with +2 Dex, all three = 0.5%). With stagger working as designed, the shardcaller-team encounter is reliably a 2-3 round VICTORY.

**For human review:** Is the 3× shardcaller-team encounter intended to be a warming-up encounter (VICTORY expected, low HP cost)? Or should it be genuinely threatening at this party level? If the latter, consider: (a) increasing SC HP from 33 to ~45; (b) adding a 4th shardcaller; (c) giving the barrage slot a bonus to prevent the R1 Fireball wipe (e.g., SC-B fires Barrage R1 before Fireball by winning initiative — see cycle 16 where SC-B had init 22 tied with Sabriel).

**FI-SCT-16-D (feel — Fireball dominant, 16th SCT cycle confirm of DD-18):**

Fireball is the single ability that defines this encounter's outcome — if it fires before any SC barrage, the encounter is trivially VICTORY. The `does Marwen get to cast Fireball before SCs barrage?` question determines 80% of the fight's feel. This has been consistent across all 16 SCT cycles. DD-18 pattern confirmed.
