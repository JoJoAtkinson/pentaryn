### Mechanical questions

**MQ-TP42-A: Tactical Drilling after Thunderwave push (NEW)**
Marwen's Thunderwave pushed DW2 10 ft away from the party on a failed save. DW2's subsequent Multiattack was modeled with Tactical Drilling (advantage) active, but if DW2 was pushed to ~25 ft from the gate and DW1 remained at ~15 ft from the party, DW2 may no longer be "within 5 feet of DW1" relative to the same target.
Per the Tactical Drilling clarification in deep-watch-derro.md: *"TD only activates when BOTH this derro AND the allied derro are each within 5 ft of the SAME target."* A 10-ft push that separates DW2 from the formation cluster would invalidate TD for DW2's next attack.
**Recommendation:** Add a note to deep-watch-derro.md Tactics section: *"If a push/knockback effect separates DW2 from DW1 by more than 5 ft relative to the shared target, TD is lost for DW2 until both DWs re-establish formation (move adjacent to target together). Cunning Action Dash may be used to close the gap if warranted."*
This is a DM-tracked condition, not a DB spec issue — no DB change required.
