### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-EV7-35-A (NEW): Maw auto-crit fires on already-downed grappled PC — rules interaction undefined.**
When Sabriel was grappled by Tentacle 1 and then downed by Tentacle 2 (same multiattack), the Maw auto-crit rider still applied (target remains grappled regardless of HP). The Maw fired on a 0-HP grappled target. RAW result: 8d8+4=30 damage → 2 auto-fail death saves. The .md says "if target is grappled, this attack is a critical hit instead" — no exception for already-downed. This is mechanically valid but feels odd: the grapple auto-crit is designed to punish a standing target, not to pile on a downed one. At a real table, a DM would probably redirect Maw to a still-standing target. **Recommendation:** Add a note to the Maw entry: "If the grappled target is already downed (0 HP), redirect Maw to another target in reach if available, or skip Maw (the crit-rider purpose is already fulfilled by the downed state)."

**FI-EV7-35-B (carry, 35th-EV7-confirm): LoH yoyo structural.** Sabriel used all 25 LoH points on Bazgar R1, giving him 25 HP. Void Scream R2 (24 psychic, area) immediately re-downed him. The LoH investment returned exactly 0 turns of Bazgar action (he was grappled and missed twice in R2 anyway). LoH extends the fight marginally but does not change outcome. 35th consecutive confirm. No spec fix — LoH yoyo is inherent to VS AoE + beholder burst profile; DM should note this at table as "you can try to revive fallen allies but VS will likely re-down them."

**FI-EV7-35-C (carry, 35th-EV7-confirm): LA-empty turn when all PCs downed.** Beholder had 3 LA remaining at end of R3 with all PCs at 0 HP. Per FIX-EV7-30-A (DR wasted on downed targets) and per VS/DR/Tentacle-LA all being wasted against 0-HP targets, the beholder literally had nothing to do with its 3 LA. This is the 7th+ consecutive EV7 cycle where the beholder ends the fight with unspent LA. The "distinctive abilities" (VS, DR, DD) rarely all fire in a 3-round fight. **DESIGN DECISION carry:** Should lair/legendary options include a "consolidation" action when all PCs are downed — e.g., begin Clay-Shaping, retreat to lower shaft, do something narratively interesting instead of standing idle?

**FI-EV7-35-D (carry, 35th-EV7-confirm): Void Ray output "each creature in single target" phrasing.**  
The dispatched Void Ray output reads: "[ASKING PLAYER: each creature in single target within 120 ft rolls DC 16 Dex save]" — "each creature in single target" is grammatically awkward. Minor. The action is correct (single target save), but the template phrasing inherited "each creature" from area-template. Log for human cleanup of the DB spec's `area.target_description` or equivalent field.

**FI-EV7-35-E (carry): Negotiation dead end for Compass Edge specifically.** Beholder's negotiation conditions (offer something from the god, don't be here to restore shrine, agree to leave lower shaft) are impossible for the Compass Edge: Sabriel IS what the beholder wants, and it detects deception via truesight + telepathy. The de-escalation hooks in the .md imply negotiation is viable but all options require either lying (auto-detected) or oath-breaking (Sabriel cannot). For a party without any divine character, negotiation would be more viable. **DESIGN QUESTION:** Is the negotiation path intentionally a trap for divine parties? If so, add a DM note: "Against the Compass Edge specifically, negotiation will fail unless Sabriel is willing to fall from grace or the party brings an actual divine artifact." If negotiation is meant to be a real option, add a hook the paladin can use (e.g., "pledge to never speak of the sealed god's name" — something costly but not oath-breaking).
