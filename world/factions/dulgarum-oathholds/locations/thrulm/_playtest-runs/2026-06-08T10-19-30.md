### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-SCT6-44-A: CW charge waste — 6/9 charges unspent in 3-SC formation.** SC-A (middle-init, holds CW per FI-SCT-34-A) died in R2 with 3 unused charges. SC-B (lowest-init, fires Barrage R1 then retreats) had no valid CW target once SC-A was dead (can't self-CW; SC-C was within range but already had 0 charges and 0 remaining CW; SC-B had 3 charges but both remaining allies were CW'd from SC-C already). Total used: 3/9 (SC-C depleted all hers). The stagger protocol leaves the lowest-init SC structurally unable to spend CW charges in short fights. DESIGN QUESTION: Is this intentional (CW is an insurance-policy mechanic)? If not, consider: if only 1 ally alive and your CW charges > 0 and the ally is >20HP, spend CW even if the ally just acted (MQ-12 hold is already overridden by "last SC standing" logic).

- **FI-SCT6-44-B: PTV-expressed R4 — confirms FI-SCT-43-B.** SC-C hit Marwen twice in R3, granting PTV disadvantage. SC-C's Barrage in R4 applied that disadvantage correctly → Marwen rolled 12 vs DC 13 → FAILED → 14 piercing → DOWN. PTV was the decisive kill mechanic. This is the second confirmation across distinct seeds (43rd + 44th cycles) that PTV delivers when SCs focus-fire the same target before a Barrage. PTV works and matters in SC-only formations when SCs and the Barrage-firer share focus target. Contradicts earlier FI-SCT6-C ("PTV useless SC-only formation"). The key is that SC multiattacks must precede the Barrage in the same initiative window; here SC-C's multiattacks in R3 primed R4's Barrage.

- **FI-SCT6-44-C: VICTORY R5 — longest SC-only fight yet (vs R2/R3 in prior cycles).** SC-C survived to R5 because the party focused SC-A and SC-B first (correct tactic: attack lowest HP). SC-C was at full 33 HP until Sabriel targeted it in R4. The extended fight was not due to any SC ability expression but rather focus-fire priority. At R5, SC-C at 9 HP landed 1 hit (10 piercing) before dying to a Divine Smite. Feel: anticlimactic solo finale. Design note: if SC-C had Barrage available in R5, it would have gotten one more area shot — but it spent Barrage in R4 (correct by stagger solo protocol). No fix needed; feel is expected for a CR-2 creature going solo.

- **FI-SCT6-44-D: Dual-dice gap ongoing.** Barrage dispatch outputs (11 in R1, 11 in R4) differ from hand-rolled per-target values (10, 14). Carry MQ-TW37-B: sim should accept dispatch output as authoritative narration and resolve saves against that value, not re-roll.

- **MQ-SCT6-44-A: SC-B's CW charges structurally unspendable.** In a 3-SC formation where SC-B is lowest-init (fires Barrage) and SC-A dies early, SC-B is left with 3 CW charges and no eligible ally within range to spend them on (SC-C is at 40+ ft, outside the 30-ft range, and SC-B is retreating each turn). The .md's CW range note (DD-41) says CW is out of range once the Rager charges to melee (~50 ft gap); same geometry applies to SC-B-to-SC-C in the endgame. Potential note: in shardcaller-only formations, the lowest-init SC should reposition toward SC-C after Barrage fires (R2+), closing the gap to enable CW use in later rounds if SC-A falls.
