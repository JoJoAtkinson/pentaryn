### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-SR5-45-A Taunt mechanically inert SR5 45th-cycle confirm (carry FI-SR5-44-D)**
- Taunt fired 5 times across the fight. The effect "disadvantage on attack rolls vs
  non-rager targets" is structurally void in a solo-rager formation — ALL targets
  are ragers. Each Taunt check costs a bonus action and a DC 12 Cha save, producing
  zero in-combat impact.
- This is the 5th+ consecutive SR5 cycle with this finding.
- Recommendation: add a DM-facing note to derro-rager.md Tactics: "In formations
  where only ragers are present (solo-rager-rush), Taunt disadv is moot; DM may
  narrate as intimidation flavor without tracking the save." Alternatively, add a
  secondary Taunt clause for all-melee scenarios — but that is a DESIGN DECISION
  for the human reviewer, not an auto-fix.

**FI-SR5-45-B Triple Berserk R1 narration repetition — new**
- All 3 ragers fired Berserk on round 1 (Berserk starts recharged; all 3 had 3 PCs
  in reach after the initiative order placed PCs first). The DB narration text
  "Plants its feet and spins — the greataxe carves a horizontal arc through everything
  hostile in range." printed verbatim three times in a row.
- Minor feel issue: repetitive narration dulls the dramatic impact of the third spin.
- Recommendation: add 2 alternate narration strings to the Berserk spec (e.g.,

- This is a log-for-human item, not an auto-fix.

### Mechanical questions

**MQ-SR5-45-A Incoming Damage Aggro: "next attack" — one roll or all rolls in multiattack?**
- The derro-rager.md reads: "The next attack the rager makes against that enemy
  has advantage. It remembers who hit it."
- In this run, Rager-Gamma's multiattack targeted Marwen (its aggro target) with
  advantage on BOTH attack rolls (the sim applied ADV to each roll vs Marwen).
- Strict reading of "the next attack" implies only the FIRST attack roll should get
  advantage. If the first roll misses, the second roll returns to normal.
- At the table this could matter: should a Fighter-player expect that a rager's
  advantage on their aggro target applies to only one swing?
- Recommendation: Clarify in derro-rager.md as: "The **first** attack roll the
  rager makes against that enemy on its next turn has advantage." This is unambiguous
  and matches 5e precedent for single-use advantage (e.g., Pack Tactics).
- This is an auto-fixable .md clarification. Flagging as MQ here rather than
  auto-fixing because it could affect balance (reducing aggro ADV from 2 rolls → 1
  roll in multiattack is a small nerf; human should decide).
