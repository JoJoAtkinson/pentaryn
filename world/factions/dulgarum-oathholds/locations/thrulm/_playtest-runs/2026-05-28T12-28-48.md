- **FI-NEW-EV6-A — RECORD EV DAMAGE (~70 HP sim / ~80-90 HP corrected):** This is the most damage the party has dealt to the beholder in any EV cycle across 6 runs. The smite-deployment problem is primarily initiative-dependent, not slot-stranding. FI-184 remains partially open — in 5 of 6 cycles Sabriel dies slot-full.

The auto-fix addresses this, but worth confirming at table: when only 1 PC is upright and attacking, DD on a downed target wastes the only LA-based damage window.
