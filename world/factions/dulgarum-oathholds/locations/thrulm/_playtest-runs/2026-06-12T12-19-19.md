### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-FC52-A (POSITIVE): Maw auto-crit finally fires — FC slice**
The Maw auto-crit (8d8+3 = 44 pierce) fired correctly in R2 against grappled Sabriel. This is the first confirmed Maw auto-crit expression in the FC slice after multiple consecutive BEL3 "Maw auto-crit never fired" flags. The pattern: Maw requires a grappled STANDING target. In FC slice R1, the beholder's first multiattack usually targets Marwen (lowest AC); Marwen goes down before Maw resolves, leaving no grappled standing target. Maw fires correctly in R2 once Sabriel becomes the grapple target.
- **Recommendation:** The Maw mechanic is working as designed. The R1 miss pattern is structural (Marwen dies fast), not a bug.

**FI-FC52-B: Marwen-structural-preemption — 52nd FC carry**
Marwen (init 5, lowest) was downed before her first action for the 52nd consecutive FC-cycle appearance of this pattern. Beholder init 11 > Marwen init 5. Marwen's DEX +2 makes her bottom of init most rolls. This means Marwen never casts Fireball in the FC slice — the party's best AoE tool never fires against the full roster.
- **Consequence:** The 4 thralls + 2 shrine-touched starting at full HP are never hit by Fireball. The encounter difficulty is meaningfully higher when Marwen can't act in R1.
- **Recommendation (DESIGN DECISION):** Consider whether the encounter intent is that the party has a chance to thin the thralls before the beholder acts. If yes, the current encounter as designed structurally denies that chance when Marwen loses init. Options: (a) give Marwen Alert feat (+5 init) [big mechanical change]; (b) have the party enter in a Surprise round against the distracted beholder/derro; (c) accept the pattern as correct — this encounter is meant to be overwhelming and Marwen dying before acting contributes to that.

**FI-FC52-C: DD double-miss R1+R2 — variance confirm**
Drain Divinity failed to strip slots in both R1 (Sabriel CHA 16 = exact tie = pass) and R2 (CHA 17 = pass). Sabriel's CHA modifier is +3 — she succeeds DD saves on 13+ (65% success rate). The 3 LA cost feels expensive when the ability has only 35% reliability. The R1-hold protocol (reserve all 3 LA for DD) burns the entire legendary action budget for a coin-flip-weighted ability.
- **Note:** DD is anti-divine design — it targets Sabriel specifically to drain smite potential. It works when it fires (strips L2 smite = 3d8 = ~13 radiant prevented). Two consecutive whiffs in FC slice.
- **Recommendation (DESIGN DECISION):** Reduce DD CHA save DC from 16 to 14 (50% → 65% success for Sabriel, but still a live threat vs other PCs)? Or lower the LA cost from 3 to 2 (allowing it to coexist with other LA spend)? Current 35% fire rate feels like a trap for the beholder's action economy.

**FI-FC52-D: STD-A AR as primary kill vector**
The shrine-touched Ancient Resonance was the killing blow for BOTH Bazgar (R2, cone while prone) and nearly Marwen (R1, partial). The STD-A AR is consistently the most impactful non-beholder ability in the FC encounter. The pattern: STD-A fires AR R1 (stagger correct), recharges on d6=6 (lucky), fires again R2. The shrine-touched are doing the design work intended.

### Mechanical questions

**MQ-FC52-A: Prone + AR DEX save interaction**
Bazgar was PRONE when STD-A fired Ancient Resonance in R2. Prone imposes disadvantage on attack rolls made by the prone creature and on attack rolls against it from >5 ft — it does NOT impose disadvantage on saving throws (PHB p. 292). Applied correctly: Bazgar rolled DEX save normally (got 2, low roll). If the sim had accidentally applied disadvantage to Bazgar's save, the result would be the same (2 is low either way), but the rule should be confirmed explicitly.
- **Ruling:** Prone has no effect on DEX saves against AR. Confirmed per PHB.

**MQ-FC52-B: DD LA cost vs CHA save probability (carry from FC51)**
At +3 CHA (Sabriel L5 paladin), DD saves on 13+ = 65% of the time. DD cost 3 LA = the beholder's full R1 legendary action budget. Expected value of DD per R1: 35% × (highest slot stripped) vs 3 LA of Tentacle+VoidRay doing guaranteed damage. Against Sabriel's AC 19, Tentacle at 2 LA = ~35% hit rate × 3d6+3 avg 13.5 = 4.7 expected damage. DD at 3 LA = 35% × slot stripping (design value varies). The math is close. But the feel issue is: when DD misses (65% of the time), the beholder wasted its entire LA budget for R1.
- **Recommendation:** Log for human review. Options: lower DC to 14, lower cost to 2 LA, or change DD to scale on proficiency so it becomes more reliable at higher BH HP.

**MQ-FC52-C: Disintegration Ray recharged R2 but beholder had no standing targets (TPK imminent)**
DR recharged (d6=5) at the start of R2. However, by the time the beholder acted in R2 (init 11), STD-A had already downed Bazgar with AR, leaving only Sabriel standing. The beholder used Multiattack + Maw-autocrit to down Sabriel before DR could fire. DR is now "recharged but unused." This is mechanically correct — Multiattack + auto-crit was the higher-value play. No action needed.
