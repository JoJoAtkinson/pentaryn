### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-TW2-47-A (carry/new-mechanism): Rager lowest-init dies R1 before Taunt fires (47th TW2 consecutive non-expression)**
  This run's CW lockout differs from the canonical range-lockout: the Rager rolled init 6 (lowest of all combatants), meaning the entire party acted before the Rager's first turn. Combined with Fireball (26 fire) + Sabriel longsword (8) + Bazgar Trip (18), the Rager was dead before Taunt or any Rager action ever resolved. The slice's stated test purpose — "taunt + call_weakness pairing" — has now failed to express across 47 consecutive TW2 cycles. Root causes cycle between (a) SC Fireball one-shot R1 removing the SC before Rager acts, (b) CW range-lockout after Rager charges, and (c) this run's Rager-lowest-init wipeout. **Recommendation (carry from FI-TW2-46-B):** bump SC HP from 33 to 45–50 to survive a DC 14 Fireball half (currently 16 fire on save, vs 33 HP = guaranteed one-shot on fail). Alternatively, place NPCs in hidden/ambush start so they act before the party.

- **FI-TW2-47-B: SC zombie at 1 HP — 5 rounds of gamey attrition**
  The Shardcaller survived at exactly 1 HP from R3 through R7, landing 3 hits on Marwen (4+7+0 = 11 piercing across 5 rounds) while Sabriel wasted all 6 attack attempts on the dead Rager (sim bug) and Bazgar similarly. In a real table run, the SC at 1 HP would be trivially finished in one melee hit — but because Sabriel/Bazgar weren't redirected, the "zombie phase" lasted 5 rounds. **Sim artifact** amplifies the feel issue, but the underlying problem is real: the SC has no "last stand" dramatic action; it just whittles with throws until someone bothers to kill it. No action needed on the DB — this is an encounter pacing note.

- **DD-TW2-47-A: CW lockout mechanism shift — now ally-death not range**
  Previous TW2 cycles CW locked out because the Rager charged to ~50ft (past 30ft CW range). This cycle CW locked because the Rager was dead before SC's first turn. Either way, CW reached end of fight with all 3 charges unused (47th consecutive TW2 zero-CW outcome). The design decision remains open: CW needs an eligible ally alive and within 30ft when SC acts. In a 2-NPC slice where 1 NPC is a melee charger, CW requires SC to win initiative over the Rager to use it in R1 (before Rager closes range). Here SC init 8 > Rager init 6, but Rager was already dead. **Structural: CW cannot express in TW2 as currently designed unless Rager survives to R2 and SC repositions into range — which contradicts SC's "stay 40–60ft back" tactics.**
