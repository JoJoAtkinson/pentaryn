### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-84 (DD-41 3rd tank-wall confirm): CW 3/3 stranded — init-dependent structural gap.**
SC init 10 < Rager init 13. Rager charges before SC acts. SC-to-Rager gap grows to ~50 ft — beyond 30 ft CW range. CW cannot legally fire on any ally this turn (no other melee ally in range). 3/3 CW uses wasted. In both today's tank-wall cycles (07:00 seed 494359, 17:18 seed 494369), CW fired 0 times. Out of all tank-wall cycles across all fires, CW fired at most 1× (3rd-cycle: advantage on Rager R1 that produced a miss). The Taunt+CW pairing the slice is designed to showcase has never demonstrated together. Structural, not auto-fixable.

**FI-85 (DD-26 2nd consecutive confirm): Berserk 0 fires in 2nd tank-wall cycle.**
Rager moved R1 → Berserk ineligible R1. Rager received 30 fire Fireball + pending Sabriel smite → died in R2 before acting. Berserk window requires Rager to survive to R2+ with 2+ PCs in reach and no movement taken. DD-26 recommendation (raise Rager HP 52→~70) unimplemented. This cycle confirms DD-26 pattern holds even with the "1st-cycle" 07:00 run (R9) also producing 0 Berserk fires (0/4 recharge chances but none attempted before Rager died R5).

**FI-86 (NEW): Extreme fight-length variance — R2 vs R9 — purely from initiative.**
07:00 cycle (seed 494359): SC init 21, Bazgar 19, Marwen 16, Rager 7, Sabriel 6 → SC acts first, CW fires on Rager, Rager acts low, fight lasts R9. 17:18 cycle (seed 494369): Marwen 20, Sabriel 18, Bazgar 14, Rager 13, SC 10 → all PCs act before any NPC R2, Fireball + smite erases both NPCs. 7-round delta driven entirely by initiative. Tank-wall's design goals (taunt+CW pairing, Berserk burst feel) require the Rager to act early; in PCs-first initiatives they're structurally unachievable in any round.

**FI-87 (DD-24 2nd confirm): Taunted Marwen casts Fireball — Taunt fully bypassed.**
Marwen failed DC 12 Cha save → taunted. Immediately cast Fireball (DC 14 Dex save, AoE). Taunt's "disadvantage on attack rolls vs non-Rager" has zero effect on a saving-throw-based spell. Rager spent its bonus action resource to zero effect. This matches the DD-24 note already in derro-rager.md. No additional fix — it's a known structural limitation of the preemptive Taunt design vs save-casters. The Rager correctly Taunted the most dangerous PC; the PC correctly circumvented it by using a non-attack-roll ability.
