### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-TW-18-A (FEEL — repeat): Rager durability failure.** Rager dropped from 52→2 HP in Round 1 from a Sabriel smite (25) + partial Scorching Ray (8) + partial Bazgar (17) = ~50 damage in a single burst round. The "tank wall" concept requires the Rager to survive long enough for Taunt to redirect at least one follow-up attack. The Rager never survived past R2 (finished off R2 turn 1). This is the **18th tank-wall cycle** — every run shows the same pattern: focused level-5 burst collapses the Rager before Taunt can create meaningful pressure.

**Note on prior cycles**: `_playtest-log.md` confirms this pattern in cycles 9 (TW-9th) and 12 (TW-12th). The durability gap is a recurring finding, not an outlier.

**FI-TW-18-B (FEEL): Taunt fires but never pays off.** Marwen failed the Cha DC 12 save and had disadvantage on non-Rager attacks. However: (a) the Rager died R2 before Marwen could take a second turn, so the disadvantage window was only ~1 action; (b) Marwen's highest-value action (Scorching Ray) was already aimed at the Rager anyway — Taunt was behaviorally irrelevant even when mechanically active. Taunt works correctly but the Rager's low durability makes it a 1-turn effect at best.

**FI-TW-18-C (FEEL — repeat): CW lockout structural.** SC used 1 of 3 CW charges productively (Bazgar R1, advantage → contributed to 2 hits). Once Rager died, 2 remaining charges had no valid target. This is now the **18th consecutive TW cycle** confirming the structural lockout pattern. Prior logged as FI-TW-1 (cycle 9), FI-NEW-TW12-A (cycle 12). SC position and Rager charge speed reliably push them apart before SC's R1 initiative.

**FI-TW-18-D (FEEL): Tactical Retreat never triggered.** SC at 70ft, Bazgar closed to 40ft by end of R2 — never within 15ft until Sabriel killed SC R3 first. TR is mechanically sound but structurally irrelevant in this slice's narrow-corridor geometry.

**DD-TW-18-1 (DESIGN DECISION — carries forward from prior cycles):** Is the Rager intended to be durable against a smite-capable level-5 party? If yes, consider: HP increase (~70-80), or half-resistance to one damage type (radiant is the killer here), or a "rage soak" mechanic (temp HP on taking damage that keeps it alive past R1). Current 52 HP is insufficient for a solo-tank role. This has been flagged in 3+ prior cycles without resolution.

**DD-TW-18-2 (DESIGN DECISION — repeat):** CW range 30ft is structurally unworkable in the tank-wall formation. Rager closes 30ft on initiative; SC stays back — gap grows to ~40-50ft. CW cannot reach the Rager after R1 unless SC repositions into danger. Either raise CW range to 60ft or add a terrain note to start the SC closer (25ft from party, 25ft from Rager). This has been flagged as FI-TW-1, FI-NEW-TW12-A, and again here — 18 cycles, 0 CW charges used on Rager in any TW run.
