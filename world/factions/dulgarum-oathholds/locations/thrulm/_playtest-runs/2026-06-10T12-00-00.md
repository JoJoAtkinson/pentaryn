### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-SR5-48-A: Berserk fires R1 (both R-A and R-C) — combined output 6 damage from 3 Berserk attacks.** R-C hit 1/2 with dispatch to-hit 18 (6 slashing, Bazgar); R-A whiffed 0/2 (dispatch to-hits 5 and 5 — both natural 1s equivalent even with ME+1). Meanwhile the two Fireballs dealt ~130 damage total. Berserk is mechanically correct but emotionally hollow when Fireball eliminates any tension before ragers can establish themselves. The "flagship" Berserk ability's most dramatic moment is dealing 6 damage to one PC. **Carry FI-SR5-47-B.**

- **FI-SR5-48-B: VICTORY R2 — most lopsided SR5 result yet.** PCs at 43/32/44 HP (88%/100%/100%). Only 6 total damage dealt to the party by 3 CR 2 creatures. Marwen and Sabriel took zero damage. 8th consecutive SR5 VICTORY confirms the structural issue documented in FI-SR5-47-A: this slice has never produced a meaningful fight. **Recommend retiring solo-rager-rush and replacing with Rager+Shardcaller or Rager+Shardcaller+Thrall.**

- **FI-SR5-48-C: Fireball double-fire decisive, 8th consecutive SR5.** Initiative: Marwen 22 (highest again). Both L3 slots spent R1+R2. R-B and R-C (Dex saves 13 and 2 vs DC 15): fail. R-A saves 20 R1 (pass), then fails save 5 on R2 Fireball. Structural floor: Dex+0 ragers will fail DC 15 ~75% of the time; in 8 cycles Fireball has been decisive every time. No rager mechanic (Berserk, Taunt, ME, Aggro) has materialized as meaningful because the fight ends before they compound.

- **FI-SR5-48-D: Taunt skipped 8th consecutive SR5.** Same-type formation, no non-rager targets. The .md note (FI-SR5-47-A fix applied last cycle) correctly documents this. Mechanics are clean; the slice's composition is the problem.

- **FI-SR5-48-E (POSITIVE): Aggro (Incoming Damage Aggro) expressed correctly.** R-C acquired aggro mark on Bazgar after Bazgar's Surge attacks. On Berserk, R-C applied advantage on target 1 → dispatch to-hit 18, HIT AC 18 exactly. The mechanic works correctly. In a longer fight where ragers survive, this creates meaningful retaliatory pressure. The slice ends too fast for the mechanic to compound.

- **FI-SR5-48-F: R-A Berserk total whiff despite ME+1.** Dispatch rolled 5 and 5 (natural 1 equivalent ×2) even with ME+1 applied (would be 6/6 on to-hit, still misses). At the table, watching the "flagship" ability of a dying rager deal zero damage is anticlimactic. This is variance, not a bug, but the pattern (FI-SR5-47-B, FI-SR5-48-F) confirms: Berserk on a badly-wounded rager at the end of its life is rarely impactful. Consider whether the intent is for Berserk to be a mid-fight ability (when rager is fresh) rather than a death-swing.
