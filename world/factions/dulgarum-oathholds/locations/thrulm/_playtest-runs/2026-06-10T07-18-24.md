### Sim gaps (not spec bugs; logged for harness improvement)
- **SIM-TP37-A CW advantage not applied to DW-B attack rolls:** The sim used straight `d20()` for DW-B's attack rolls in R1 and R2 despite CW being active. This is a harness gap — the advantage should have been `d20adv()`. DW-B's R1 miss (roll 8 on first attack) might have been a hit with adv; R2 crossbow miss (16 vs AC 18) likely would have remained a miss. Net outcome probably unchanged but masks true CW value. Carry forward.
- **SIM-TP37-B Tactical Drilling applied incorrectly R1:** DW-A attacked Marwen with advantage (Tactical Drilling), but DW-B was engaged with Bazgar — a different target. TD only activates when both DW and ally are adjacent to the *same* target. Sim granted adv incorrectly. DW-A's first attack was a crit (nat 20 = crit regardless); second attack missed — so outcome is unchanged, but the advantage should not have been active.
