### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-TW2-46-A — CW-46th-consecutive-TW2-lockout (carry)**
Call Weakness fired 0/3 uses. SC died R1 before its turn (init 9 < Marwen init 15). This is the 46th consecutive TW2 run where CW does not fire. Pattern: if Marwen wins initiative over SC, she Fireballs and SC is dead — CW never fires. If SC loses init, CW is permanently locked out that run. The central mechanical pairing this slice is meant to test (CW + Taunt) has never demonstrated together.

**FI-TW2-46-B — SC-Fireball-3rd-consecutive-R1-one-shot**
8d6 = 33 (rolled max) vs SC HP 33. This is the 3rd consecutive TW2 with Fireball killing SC R1 (seeds 494777, 494785 both exact-max). SC DEX save: d20+2 = 6 (very low roll), fail. Even a mean roll of ~8d6=28 one-shots SC (HP 33 with Fireball avg 28). The SC is a 33 HP squish that a level-5 Fireball deletes regardless of seed. Previous fix attempts: none yet (flagged 45th and earlier, no action taken). **Recommendation**: bump SC to 44 HP (8d6+8) — survives a typical Fireball (avg 28), forces the party to spend a second action or better positioning.

**FI-TW2-46-C — Berserk-R1-miss-both-AC18/19-wall**
Rager Berserk with Madness Endurance (+1 to hit) and Incoming Damage Aggro (adv vs Bazgar) still missed both targets: 14 vs AC 18, 12 vs AC 19. With +5 to hit (+4 base +1 Madness) and advantage, miss rate vs AC 18 is still ~40%. Rager feels impotent during its signature attack — the "pain fuels it" loop doesn't produce hits. Not a bug; this is correct variance. Logged as feel note.

**DD-TW2-46-A (carry) — Taunt-vs-save-spells-bypassed-4th-confirm**
Taunt specifies disadvantage on *attack rolls* vs non-Rager targets. Fireball (R1) and any future Hold Person, Thunderwave, etc. completely bypass Taunt — they use saves, not attack rolls. Taunt is effectively zero vs a Wizard who uses save-based spells as primary offense. This has been confirmed in TW2-44, TW2-45, and now TW2-46. Three options for human review: (1) accept it — Taunt is for melee/cantrip context only; (2) expand Taunt to include save disadvantage (significant buff); (3) replace Taunt with an ability that actually threatens back-line casters. No auto-fix; balance decision.

### Mechanical questions

**MQ-TW2-46-A — Berserk prerequisite +1 Madness Endurance baked vs dynamic**
The Berserk DB spec shows `to_hit_bonus: 4` (base). The Madness Endurance +1 and Incoming Damage Aggro advantage are described in the prerequisite text as DM-applied modifiers. The DB output rolls at call-time do not know the rager's damage-taken state. The DM must mentally add +1 and grant advantage manually after reading the dispatch output. In a live table session with the CLI runner, this is easy to miss. Consider adding a `rider_on_hit` or `pre_state` text block to the Berserk spec that more prominently surfaces the "+1 to-hit if damaged since last turn" as a reminder in the output block. Not a spec bug; a usability note.
