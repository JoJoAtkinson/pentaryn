SC-B enters R1 at 6 HP and dies to AS before it can act. Barrage fires 0 rounds. This is the structural Barrage-denial pattern: lowest-init = Barrage, but lowest-init is most likely to be dead before acting when party front-loads damage (Fireball R1 + AS). Previous cycles (35th: SC-C fires Barrage ✓ when SC-C was not lowest; 34th: SC-A fires ✓). Init lottery determines whether Barrage fires at all. **Recommendation (carry-forward FI-SCT-34-E, DD-SCT-6-D):** Tactics should note that the lowest-init SC should fire Barrage *even if it means firing before all PCs are in line* — a half-caught Barrage on 2 PCs is better than a 0% chance from a dead SC.
