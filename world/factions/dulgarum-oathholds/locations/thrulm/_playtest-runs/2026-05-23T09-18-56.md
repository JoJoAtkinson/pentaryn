### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-1: Phase B test harness simulates Tactical Drilling as flat +2 instead of advantage (NEW DD-22)**

The test script applies `d20+2` for DW attacks with Tactical Drilling active, instead of rolling `max(d20,d20)+2`. In this run, DW1 attacked Marwen (AC 15) twice in R1 with the harness at 40% hit rate each. Actual hit rate with advantage (need ≥13) is ~64% per attack. DW2 attacked Bazgar (AC 18) with advantage from Call Weakness; applied at 35% instead of ~56%. The DW threat is meaningfully understated in every sim that runs them in melee. Over 2 cycles, this has made the patrol feel more skewed toward party victory than it truly is.

**Fix needed (test harness, not DB):** The simulation loop should roll `max(d20a, d20b)+bonus` when advantage applies. Out of cron blast radius for DB edits; flagged for human fix.

**FI-2: Marwen burned all 3 spell slots in a sub-Medium patrol fight (2nd cycle observation)**

Same finding as first cycle (2026-05-23T01-41-59.md). Marwen used Thunderwave (2nd level) + 2× Magic Missile against a patrol worth ~500 XP. For 3 L5 PCs, Medium threshold is ~750 XP, so this is an Easy encounter, yet it consumed the entire arcane slot budget. This reinforces the concern that the patrol composition creates disproportionate spell-slot drain. If players chain directly from the patrol into the shrine-wedge or tank-wall, Marwen may arrive without slots.

Design note: this may be intentional (resource attrition), but if so, the encounter notes should say so. Currently nothing calls out expected slot drain.

**FI-3: DD-1 (Barrage double-fire) — data point from 2nd cycle**

In the first cycle, Barrage fired twice in 3 rounds (concern: too reliable). In this cycle, Barrage fired once (R1) and recharged in R3 but SC1 was at 1 HP and the fight ended before the second fire. Outcome: DD-1 is seed-dependent; on average (33%/round) the expected fires in a 4-round fight is ~1.3. Two fires in one run is high-variance, not structural. Recommendation remains to test more seeds before acting on DD-1.
