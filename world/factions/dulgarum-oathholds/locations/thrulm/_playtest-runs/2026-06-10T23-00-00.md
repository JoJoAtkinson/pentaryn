### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-TP39-A: VICTORY-R2 — 39th-consecutive threshold-patrol victory (Fireball structural problem)**
DW-2 eliminated before acting (31 fire > 27 HP). DW-1 survived on 12 HP but couldn't hold against two melee PCs. The patrol collapses in 2 rounds every time Marwen has a Fireball available. This is now confirmed across 39 consecutive cycles: a L5 Wizard with Fireball simply deletes this encounter. The DW HP of 27 is the core fragility; CR 1/8 was always going to be Fireball-fodder. This is a pre-beholder warm-up encounter, but it never shows any DW tactics (Tactical Drilling, Cunning Action, crossbow kiting). Carry FI-TP38-A.

**FI-TP39-B: Shard-Barrage R1 fired in corridor — FIRST R1 Barrage in threshold-patrol cycle history**
Prior: Barrage zero in 38th cycle (SC isolated post-DW-death, no line), R3 final-stand in 37th cycle. This is the first time Barrage fired R1 (as designed per tactics: corridor approach, 2+ PCs in line). The 10-pierce on 3 targets was meaningful — Bazgar took 10 (failed save; Dex −1) and both spellcasters took 5 each. This is the correct tactical behavior and felt good. CW was structurally blocked (DW-1 high init, low HP post-Fireball → MQ-12 hold), so TR resolved correctly as solo bonus-action.

**FI-TP39-C: CW 39th-consecutive zero in threshold-patrol**
SC always fires at init 9 (Dex +2 average), while DW-1 tends to roll high (init 20 this cycle). After Fireball, DW-1 is at ≤20 HP (MQ-12 guard activates). The CW window requires DW-1 to either: (a) act AFTER SC in initiative (requires DW-1 to roll ≤7 total, ~35% baseline), or (b) survive Fireball above 20 HP (requires DW-1 to save, cutting damage to 15-16 — DW-1 would be at 11-12 HP after save, still ≤20). Structural conclusion: CW cannot productively fire in TP unless Fireball is not available or DW-1 rolls extremely low initiative. This is an under-expressed ability in this slice.

**FI-TP39-D: DW-2 never acts — 39th-consecutive zero contribution**
Every cycle, DW-2 is eliminated by Fireball before its initiative comes around (low-init DW gets hit first; high-damage Fireball exceeds 27 HP). The second DW is functionally decorative against a Wizard-led party. This is a known carry but worth noting: adding a 3rd DW or giving them Evasion/+more HP would force Marwen to use two spell slots instead of one.

### Mechanical questions

**MQ-TP39-A: Barrage damage discrepancy — dispatch vs re-roll (SIM-TP39-A)**
The dispatched Shard-Barrage output reported "10 piercing" (3d6 = 5+3+2). The simulation re-rolled separately and got 13. Both values appear in the transcript. Dispatcher is authoritative; the re-rolled "13" should not have been used. Actual damage applied was dispatch-10 for purposes of official log (saves shown above use dispatch value). This is a recurring simulation methodology gap — the dispatcher rolls dice internally; the harness should extract the damage number from the output string rather than re-rolling.

**MQ-TP39-B: CW productive window in TP requires DW-1 low initiative**
CW's design intent (mark high-initiative ally, they get advantage before party acts) only works in TP when DW-1 rolls initiative below SC's roll (~35% chance both acting at init 9 or below). Consider whether this structural CW blockage is acceptable given TP is a warm-up encounter, or whether the SC should appear later in initiative order (lower Dex saves) so it can CW before the DWs act.
