### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-62 (NEW): R1 killed before acting — 7th-cycle solo-rager-rush last-init elimination.** R1 rolled init 2 (last). Bazgar dealt 19 damage (R1 HP 33), Sabriel smite'd 30 (R1 HP 3), Marwen Thunderwave'd 11 (R1 HP −8) — all before R1's turn. The rager with the lowest initiative was eliminated without ever swinging. This is the 7th solo-rager-rush cycle; the last-init elimination pattern is now well-confirmed (see also FI-19, prior cycles). With 3 PCs focused and a nova-capable party, a rager rolling init 2 will not survive to act. Not a bug — encounter design note.

- **FI-63 (NEW): Taunt splitting correctly applied (FI-31) but 0 effective outcomes — high-Cha casters plus taunter-targeting negates Taunt.** R2 taunted Marwen 4 times (rounds 1–4). Results: Pass (23), Pass (18), Pass (12 = exactly DC 12), FAIL (4). On the one fail (R4), Marwen's Scorching Ray targeted R2 (the taunter) → no disadvantage applies. Taunt produced zero mechanical impact across the entire encounter. Root causes: (1) Marwen's Cha +3 means she beats DC 12 ~65% of the time; (2) when she does fail, she rationally targets the taunter with her attack-roll spell. The 7th-cycle pattern confirms DD-14's structural finding: Taunt is mechanically inert in solo-rager-rush at the current DC 12 against a Cha +3 Wizard. FI-31 corollary (split taunt) is authored correctly — the issue is DC and target choice, not the ruling.

- **FI-64 (NEW): Berserk "all-in-R1" opening — both R2 and R3 Berserk on their first action.** All ragers start with Berserk recharged. With 3 PCs clustered in melee, 2 of 3 ragers fire Berserk on their very first turns (R2 in init position 2, R3 in init position 5). This means round 1 opens with two spinning AoE swings in rapid succession — dramatic, but it means the Berserk *recharge tension* (DD-14's "will it fire in time?") never materializes in round 1. The ability reads as a permanent R1 ability rather than a situational recharge. In rounds 2+ both ragers failed recharge (rolls 4, 1) before R2 recharged on d6=5 in R3. Compare: recharge fires were R1 (2×), R3 (1×) = 3 total across 5 rounds. The per-round recharge math (33%) feels correctly tuned for rounds 2+; the issue is the "free" R1 fire that makes the encounter open in a spinning-axes frenzy rather than building to it.

### Mechanical questions

- **MQ-35 (NEW): Thunderwave push voids Berserk eligibility.** Marwen's Thunderwave (Round 1) pushed all ragers 10 ft. R3 fired Berserk immediately after — but R3 was just pushed 10 ft away from the PCs. Berserk's prerequisite is "rager cannot move on this turn" and targets must be within reach (5 ft). A rager displaced 10 ft by a push effect would need to move to re-enter melee reach, which the "cannot move" Berserk constraint prevents. **Recommendation:** Add to `derro-rager.md` Berserk tactics bullet: *"Cannot Berserk on the same turn the rager was pushed or pulled away from its targets — it would need to move to re-enter reach, violating the no-movement prerequisite. In that case, move normally and Multiattack instead."* Authoring-only, low-risk.
