### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-TP0-A — Patrol too fragile vs L5 Fireball (primary concern):** Even at average rolls (8d6≈28), this patrol dies R1 if Marwen has init advantage and all enemies fail the DC 14 save. At 35 fire with triple fails, it's a wipe before DW-A fires its second swing. The patrol has no natural Fireball defense: no high Dex, no resistances, no spreading positions (corridor geometry pushes them into the AoE). Consider: (a) adding 2× thrall-derro to inflate HP pool and force Fireball coverage trade-offs, or (b) terrain spread — one DW positioned behind the shaft entrance corner so a 20-ft sphere can't catch all three. A patrol that dies without showcasing CW, Barrage follow-up, or Drilling feels like a warm-up that doesn't warm anything up.

- **FI-TP0-B — CW mechanic entirely invisible:** SC held 3/3 CW charges at fight end. Initiative luck (SC rolled 5, last in order) meant Sabriel+Marwen resolved before SC ever got its bonus action. The most mechanically rich part of this NPC — the "CW hold vs fire" matrix with all its guards (MQ-12, DD-39, init-priority) — contributed zero interesting decisions this run. At least 2-3 rounds are needed to showcase it. The 1-round Fireball wipe makes this slice a poor validator for SC behavior.

- **FI-TP0-C — SC at retreat threshold exactly after Sabriel smite:** After Sabriel's two attacks (21 total damage), SC was at exactly 12 HP — the threshold for "below 12 HP, retreat." The boundary is inclusive-exclusive ambiguous in the .md: "Below 12 HP" means ≤11, so 12 HP = no retreat. SC correctly fires Barrage at 12 HP. But a single additional 1-HP hit before SC's turn would trigger retreat and cancel the Barrage. This boundary is consistently reachable from a R1 Sabriel smite vs SC; worth adding a DM note that "12 HP = still fires" is the intended ruling.

- **FI-TP0-D — Tactical Drilling advantage contributed 0 meaningful hits:** DW-A hit once (adv on d20=17) for 4 damage out of 4 attempts across both DWs. The Drilling mechanic was technically active but damage output was negligible. This is variance (DW-B rolled 8 and 5 on both dice pairs). No fix needed, but the corridor slice could use a 2-round fight to show Drilling in a survival context.

### Mechanical questions

- **MQ-TP0-A — "Below 12 HP" SC retreat wording:** .md says "Below 12 HP: retreat behind a Rager or pillar." In threshold-patrol formations there is no Rager. SC correctly uses "pillar" fallback per the second clause ("if no cover exists, accept the exposed position"). However the text was written before the Rager-absent clarification was added. In this corridor slice the only cover is the pillar at 40 ft (which SC starts behind). Post-Sabriel-smite SC at exactly 12 HP should: stay at pillar, fire Barrage from cover, NOT retreat further. This is currently correct behavior but the .md phrasing doesn't make the 12 HP = still-fires ruling explicit. Recommend: add "(≤11 triggers retreat)" parenthetical to the threshold line.

- **MQ-TP0-B — Fireball AoE vs patrol geometry:** Threshold patrol briefing says "party arrives mid-conversation" implying the DWs are close together. In a standard patrol formation, two guards and their support caster 40 ft back would NOT all be in a 20-ft Fireball radius. The simulation put all three in the same Fireball because the corridor geometry was described as "25 ft wide" — but 40 ft of depth between DWs and SC should place SC outside a 20-ft radius Fireball centered on the DWs. If SC is 40 ft back, Fireball (origin on DWs) has a 20 ft radius and would reach 40 ft — barely catching SC at the edge. Strictly correct per geometry, but worth noting for DM adjudication. SC at 40 ft = just inside a 40-ft-radius Fireball (20 ft blast centered 20 ft from caster). Actually Fireball is 20-ft radius sphere, centered at any point within 150 ft. If Marwen is 10 ft from DWs and places center at DW position, SC at 40 ft behind is 30 ft from center — just outside the 20-ft radius. **This simulation may have incorrectly included SC in the Fireball.** This is a non-trivial geometry question that depends on where Marwen stands and where she aims the Fireball. Recommend clarifying in the patrol overview: "SC starts 40 ft behind DW-A/B — a Fireball centered on the DWs does NOT catch SC unless Marwen uses a more distant aim point."
