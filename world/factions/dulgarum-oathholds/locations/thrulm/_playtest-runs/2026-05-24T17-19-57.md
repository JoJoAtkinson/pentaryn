## Findings

### Bugs auto-fixed

- **FI-31 — Rager-only triple-Taunt MQ-5 degenerate state (LOW RISK authoring clarification):** All three ragers simultaneously Taunted Marwen in R1. Per MQ-5 ruling (already in .md), disadvantage only applies on attacks vs creatures that are NOT the taunting ragers. In a rager-only formation, ALL valid attack targets ARE ragers, so the triple-Taunt produces zero disadvantage on any possible PC action. Added "Rager-only formation corollary (FI-31)" to the Multi-Taunt ruling paragraph in `derro-rager.md`: split Taunt across multiple PCs in rager-only encounters rather than stacking on the same caster. Authoring-only, low-risk. This does not change mechanics; it informs the DM of the correct (and currently wasted) bonus-action usage pattern.

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **DD-14 confirmed 6th consecutive solo-rager-rush cycle:** Fireball (or equivalent AoE) decides the fight in R1, reducing all three ragers from 52 HP to ~20 HP before any other meaningful action. Victory in 2 rounds. Berserk fires in R2 but is mechanically "dead" (ragers at 20/52 HP, party converges and kills in same round as Berserk). Taunt fires every round but is mechanically inert (DD-36: immune to save-based spells; FI-31: inert against attack-roll spells due to MQ-5 triple-Taunt). Madness Endurance: Rager-C correctly applied +1 in R2 (Fireball damaged it R1), but the bonus missed by a wide enough margin on all swings that it had zero hit-rate impact. **None of the three signature slice mechanics produced a meaningful outcome across 6 cycles.** DD-14 (bumping rager HP or adding Fireball terrain constraint) remains the unresolved root fix.

- **FI-31 (new, auto-fix applied above):** Triple-Taunt on same PC in rager-only formation creates a MQ-5 degenerate state where Taunt is literally impossible to trigger — see auto-fixed section above. Design note: in the intended full-encounter context (ragers + beholder + other derro), Taunt correctly redirects PC attacks away from squishier allies. In the isolated solo-rager-rush slice, no such ally exists, making every Taunt structurally wasted.

- **Berserk sequencing feel (confirmed R2):** Berserk fires at the correct moment (R2, all 3 PCs in reach), but ragers are at 20/52 HP entering R2. Rager-B's Berserk landed 1 hit (9 slashing on Marwen), Rager-C's Berserk landed 1 hit (4 slashing on Marwen, with Madness Endurance +1 barely mattering). Berserk feels like a "too late, already dying" moment rather than the "grinding AOE escalation" it's designed to be. When Fireball fires R1, ragers enter Berserk range already at death's door. Without the DD-14 HP fix (or terrain constraint on Fireball), this sequencing problem persists structurally.

- **Phase A infra note:** External RNG (quantumnumbers + random.org) blocked in Anthropic sandbox. Local cache pre-seeded with pseudo-random numbers for 9th consecutive cycle. DD-10 (local fallback in dnd_roller.py) remains unimplemented. Not a Phase B blocker but should be addressed by human.
