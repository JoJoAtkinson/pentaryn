## Outcome summary

**Party victory in 4 rounds.** Sabriel fell in Round 3; Bazgar and Marwen survived. One PC down out of three = partial loss. The shrine-wedge slice is lethal enough to drop a PC but not cause a TPK — appropriate for a 2-NPC fight vs 3 PCs at level 5.

Key moments:
- Round 1: both derro fired Ancient Resonance immediately (party clustered) — extremely punishing opener
- Altar zone: Marwen's Fireball (would have done 44 fire to each, doubled for vulnerability) instead did 11 — the altar zone saved both NPCs from a Round 1 death
- Sabriel was targeted 3 consecutive rounds by Rune-B with Unstable Form advantage — the 10+ damage threshold for Unstable Form was met easily (12 thunder in R2, 7 in R3 but advantage persisted due to threshold from R2)
- Oath-Breaking Retaliation fired 4 times total — consistently punished the party for attacking; Rune-B's final retaliation triggered its own death (self-psychic damage sealed it)

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

#### DD-4: Altar zone vulnerability suppression must be explicitly stated (AUTO-FIX PARTIAL)
- **Context:** Slice #1 briefing says "resistant zone" near altar. The shrine-touched NPC has fire/radiant *vulnerability* normally (highly exploitable at level 5 — Fireball, Divine Smite). Without the altar zone rule, Marwen's Fireball would have killed both derro in Round 1 (44 fire each, doubled). With it, both survived to Round 4.
- **Auto-fix applied:** Added "Altar Zone" section to `shrine-touched-derro.md` explaining the suppression rule.
- **Remaining work:** The _overview.md doesn't mention this as a terrain feature. Recommend adding to the chamber Geography section. Also needs to clarify: does this apply to ALL fire/radiant damage from ALL sources (including e.g. a torch) or only spell damage? The .md now covers spells but edge cases exist.

#### DD-5: Unstable Form feedback loop — 10+ damage threshold triggers too easily
- **Context:** In this run, Rune-B had Unstable Form advantage for 3 consecutive rounds (R2, R3, R4 start before dying). Once the party is dealing full damage, the 10+ threshold is trivially met every round. This means Unstable Form is effectively permanent in most fights, not a "dangerous moment" mechanic.
- **Recommendation:** Consider raising threshold to 15+ damage, or limit to advantage on first attack only (not all attacks), or change to "if dropped to half HP last round" (HP milestone instead of damage threshold). The current version turns "took damage" into permanent advantage, undermining the tactical feel of managing the shrine-touched carefully.
- **Priority:** Medium — the mechanic creates a snowball effect where a more aggressive party gets punished harder.

#### DD-6: Both shrine-touched burn Ancient Resonance on Round 1 — starves later rounds
- **Context:** With 3 PCs naturally clustering in the approach path, both derro immediately qualify for the "2+ enemies in a 15-ft cone" Resonance trigger. Result: Round 1 delivers 2× Ancient Resonance (very punishing), then rounds 2+ are entirely Resonance-free since recharge 5-6 yields ~33% per round (0-1 fires expected in a 3-round fight).
- **Mechanical note:** This isn't a bug — it's correct per tactics. But it means the "dynamic" of Resonance recharging mid-fight almost never happens in a 2-shrine-touched slice. The fight is Round 1 nuke → melee slog.
- **Recommendation:** Either stagger Resonance usage in tactics (one derro holds it until round 2), OR reduce the cone trigger condition, OR increase recharge probability to 4-6. Flagging for human judgment.

### Mechanical questions

#### MQ-1: Multiattack output labels combined damage under primary type
- **Context:** Multiattack output shows `Shrine-Axe 1 to-hit 14 / dmg 7 slashing (incl +1 necrotic extra_damage)`. The "7 slashing" label is incorrect — it combines 6 slashing and 1 necrotic under the slashing type label. A DM with a slashing-resistant creature would halve the necrotic too.
- **Root cause:** dnd_roller.py multiattack renderer lumps total damage under the primary type label with a parenthetical for extra damage. This is a renderer issue, not a DB spec issue.
- **Recommendation:** Update multiattack summary to format as "6 slashing + 1 necrotic = 7 total" rather than "7 slashing (incl +1 necrotic)". Out of cron blast radius — human to implement.
- **Confirmed from DD-2 previous run:** save.notes renders correctly in narration (the bracket reminder fires). That part works.
