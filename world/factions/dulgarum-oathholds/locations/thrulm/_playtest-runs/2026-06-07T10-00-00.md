### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-TW37-A — CW range lockout 4th TW-cycle confirm (carry FI-TW-1, FI-TW2-A)**
SC initiative 3, Rager initiative 11. Rager charges 30ft to party before SC acts. By SC's turn,
SC-to-Rager gap is ~45ft — beyond CW's 30ft range. CW was geometrically impossible this round
regardless of tactics text. This is the 4th consecutive tank-wall cycle where CW fires 0/3.
Recommend: (a) SC start position ≤25ft from party (not 55ft) so Rager stays in CW range even
after charge, OR (b) extend CW range to 60ft to span the charge-width. The pairing is
tactically sound on paper but structurally defeated by geometry ~60% of the time when Rager
wins initiative over SC (which it does whenever Rager rolls >1, given SC's DEX+2 vs Rager DEX+0
— about equal odds, so ~50% of R1 CW-before-charge windows don't exist even with optimal init).
*Human decision required — don't auto-fix.*

**FI-TW37-B — Taunt DC 12 structural failure vs Wizard CHA**
Taunt fired twice (R1 and R2), Marwen resisted both (CHA saves 16 and 22 vs DC 12). Marwen's
CHA is presumably +2 to +3 as a Wizard; DC 12 gives only a 45-50% fail rate for her. Across 4
tank-wall cycles, Taunt has landed 0/4 times on Marwen. On Bazgar (CHA ~-1 to 0) it would land
more (~55-60%), but the tactics correctly identify the back-line caster as the higher-value
target. The Taunt mechanic is designed to redirect the front-line threat; vs the caster it
mostly fails. Low-priority design note; DC 13-14 would improve the feel without a major
rebalance. *Human decision — log only, no fix.*

**FI-TW37-C — Fight too quick for tank-wall pairing to be tested**
Rager died in round 2 (focus-fire from all 3 PCs). The SC then spent rounds 2-4 as a lone
ranged attacker with CW unused and Barrage recharged-but-never-needed. The slice's stated goal
(test taunt+CW pairing) wasn't achievable in 4 rounds — the Rager falls before the pairing
can develop. Consistent with FI-TW-2 (Rager HP 52 insufficient for tank role vs level-5 party).
This is the 4th confirm. Recommend 2×Ragers or Rager HP 75-80.
*Human decision — log only, no fix.*

### Mechanical questions

**MQ-TW37-A — Berserk DB output shows 3 attack lines, only 2 targets in reach**
The DB dispatched 3 Greataxe attack lines (matching the "3" in the spec). Only Bazgar and
Sabriel were in reach. The tactics note says "cap at actual enemies in reach; skip excess lines
for dead or absent targets" — this guidance is correct but the DM must apply it manually
every time. The excess line is confusing at-table. Consider adding a NOTE in the berserk spec
or .md that "this will always print N attack lines where N is the spec value; use only the
lines for creatures actually in reach." Low priority — doc note only, no DB change needed.

**MQ-TW37-B — Simulation dual-dice-gap: Barrage damage rolled twice**
Same structural sim bug as MQ-TW-SIM-A in prior cycles. The dispatch call returned DB value
of 12 (3d6=12), then the simulation re-rolled 3d6=4 manually. The sim used 4 for the actual
math (resulting in much lower damage to PCs than the DB intended). DB value of 12 is
authoritative; the sim should parse and use the DB damage output rather than re-rolling.
No spec fix needed — pure simulation harness improvement for future fires.
