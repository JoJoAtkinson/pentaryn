### Bugs auto-fixed

Note explains that VS's most durable effect is the fear condition, not the psychic damage, and quantifies: ~27% hit rate per Bazgar attack at disadv vs ~47% without, suppressing Fighter output ~50% for 2-3 rounds.

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-148 (4th EV cycle confirm):** Negotiation dead-end structural — 4th consecutive cycle confirms the shaft condition (party must leave lower shaft unexplored) is incompatible with party mission. This cycle adds the outright-fail Persuasion variant (d20+4=6; no window offered). Cycle sample: cycle 1 = VS wipe no negotiation, cycle 2 = partial window relic-unavailable, cycle 3 = Persuasion 18 succeeds but shaft blocks deal, cycle 4 = Persuasion fails outright. **All four failure modes confirmed. Human must decide before encounter goes to table: does the party need a planted Khôr-Bhog-Khôr relic in a prior session, or does option (b) (Sabriel's holy symbol as substitute, conditional on shaft concession) get added to Interaction Notes?**

- **FI-152 (NEW POSITIVE):** First EV cycle where beholder drops below 50% HP (44/110 by end of R3). Party dealt ~60 HP total — nearly double the prior record (39 HP in cycle 3). Root cause: DR double-miss R2-R3 (16% compound probability for +6 vs AC 15) allowed Marwen to survive and cast SR through R3, and Bazgar landed 2/2 hits in R3. **This cycle establishes a scenario where party output is close enough to threaten beholder survival if VS doesn't recharge. Path to victory requires: DR miss 3+ consecutive times AND VS not recharge AND party sustains. Very low probability, but not zero.** Log for feel: there IS a non-trivial outcome space.

- **FI-153 (NEW — feel/sim artifact interaction):** Under authoritative dispatch values, Marwen is disintegrated R1 every cycle (MQ-65: dispatch rolled 48 force → 48 > 32 HP). Under sim values, she survives R1 at 2 HP and contributes meaningfully through R3. The "interesting" 5-round cycle is entirely a sim artifact. At table, the fight is likely over in 2-3 rounds (Marwen disintegrated R1, Bazgar/Sabriel attrited by VS R2-3). **This cycle's high beholder damage (60 HP) would not occur at table with authoritative DR rolls.** Flag for human: the sim systematically understates DR lethality (MQ-65 is a chronic pattern: MQ-25, MQ-34, MQ-49, MQ-59, MQ-62, now MQ-65 — 6th occurrence). The double-roll problem should be fixed infrastructure-side.

- **FI-149 (NEW — DM note, auto-fixed):** VS fear rider: Bazgar 0/2 attacks at disadv R4. VS cost Bazgar ~18 expected damage output in R4 alone (0.47×∞ → 0.27× per attack × avg ~11 dmg). The fear condition is VS's real ongoing power — not the one-round psychic. Auto-fix: DM note added to beholder-thrulm.md. No balance change.

- **FI-154 (NEW — feel):** DD stripped Marwen's slots (L3 in R2, L1 in R4 — only L1 left after R3 DR miss). By R4, Marwen had 0 L3 and 0 L2; Fire Bolt was her only remaining option. DD's slot-erosion design is working but the visual at table is stark: by R4, the wizard is cantrip-locked. **Add DM note to tactics: narrate DD as "you feel the fire leave you — the slot is gone" to make the attrition visible to the player.** Low-risk editorial addition; optional for human.

### Mechanical questions

- **MQ-64 (NEW — auto-fixed):** When beholder descends from altitude to fire VS, standard fly movement triggers OA from a readied melee attack (Bazgar at +5, ~47% hit chance, ~11 avg damage). `shrine_drift` is the correct approach — it passes through obstacles and does not trigger OA. DM note added to beholder-thrulm.md.

- **MQ-65 (NEW — sim double-roll, 6th occurrence of pattern):** Disintegration Ray dispatch outputs authoritative roll results (to-hit 7/24/25, damage 48/53/38 across R1-R3) but the Phase B sim rolled independently (26/14/10 to-hit, 30 force applied). Authoritative dispatch would disintegrate Marwen R1; sim gives her 30 damage. This is the same chronic pattern as MQ-25/MQ-34/MQ-49/MQ-59/MQ-62. At-table reality: Marwen dies R1 every time +6 to-hit beats AC 15 (60% per round). **The sim systematically generates more interesting multi-round fights than occur at table.** Infrastructure fix required (parse dispatch output for HP deltas). Outside cron blast radius — human sign-off needed.

- **MQ-66 (NEW — design question):** DR recharged d6=5 in R2 and d6=6 in R3 (back-to-back recharges — 11% joint event for recharge-5 ability). After missing both R2 and R3, DR was not recharged in R4 (d6=1). The recharge math creates high fight-shape variance: cycles where DR recharges fast AND hits determine outcome in 2 rounds; cycles where it misses extend fights meaningfully. **Is there value in a DR recharge floor — e.g. "DR always recharged by the beholder's 3rd turn if not already recharged"?** This would reduce variance while keeping the 1-round recharge miss as a possible outcome. Design option only — do not auto-fix.
