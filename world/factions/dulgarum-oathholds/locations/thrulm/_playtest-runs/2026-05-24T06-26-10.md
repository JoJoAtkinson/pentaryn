### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**MQ-4 FIX VALIDATED (positive confirmation):** Void Scream fired R1 as intended. 42 psychic, all 3 PCs failed Wis save (roll 8/8/22 vs DC 16 — two hard fails). Bazgar immediately at 35% HP, Marwen at 37% HP after one opener. This is the most impactful single-round opening seen in any playtest run. The R1 timing of Void Scream transforms the encounter from "beholder hovering at range while party approaches" to "devastating ambush the moment PCs enter the vault." Feel: EXCELLENT. The beholder's alien menace is front-loaded exactly where it should be.

**DD-28 FIX PARTIALLY VALIDATED:** Drain Divinity fired ALL 3 rounds (R1: Sabriel fail ✓, R2: Marwen save, R3: Marwen save). First confirmed R1 success across all runs! Sabriel lost her L2 smite slot — she could not use it in R2 when she landed two hits. The stripped slot mattered. However, Marwen saved TWICE at exactly 17 vs DC 16 — a 1-point margin both times. Statistically improbable (two consecutive saves at the same narrow margin), but it means the beholder burned 6 legendary actions (R2+R3) for zero result on Marwen.

**DD-42 (NEW — log only, do NOT auto-fix):** Drain Divinity consuming the entire legendary budget (3 actions = full budget) means NO Void Ray or Tentacle legendary use in R1–R3. In R2 and R3, Marwen was at 12 HP — a Void Ray (avg 22, half = 11 on save) would have killed or near-killed her, permanently removing the L3 Fireball threat. Instead, both DD budget rounds produced zero effect (Marwen saved). 
- **Recommendation:** Add HP threshold to DD-28 priority rule: *"Drain Divinity FIRST if target is at ≥ 20 HP AND slot level ≥ 2. If the highest-slot target is at ≤ 15 HP, skip Drain Divinity and use Void Ray (2 actions) + Tentacle (1 action) instead — guaranteed kill > uncertain slot drain."* This gives the beholder tactical flexibility when targets are low on HP. Requires human sign-off (balance impact: this makes the beholder more lethal against low-HP targets).

**FI-3 CONFIRMED THIRD TIME — R3:** Beholder below 60 HP, Void Scream spent (d6=3), no disintegration. Tactics say "prefer ranged, hold 60+ ft up" but zero ranged main actions exist. Multiattack used by default. The guidance gap is confirmed across 3 independent runs. FI-3 was already marked "PENDING HUMAN REVIEW" in the log. Strongly recommend applying the prepared fix text from the log entry: *"Main action when VS + Disintegration both unavailable below 60 HP: Multiattack if any PC is within 10 ft; otherwise hold/move only. **FI-9 (NEW — Void Scream non-recharge pattern):** d6 recharge rolls for VS in R2, R3: 5, 3. Three consecutive non-6 rolls across R1-R3 (R1: available, R2: 5, R3: 3).
