### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-TP17-A — SC Barrage spam at ≤12 HP (feel issue, not a spec change)**

SC fired Barrage 4 times across 10 rounds, including 3 consecutive recharges in R5-R7 (rolls 6, 6, 5). This streak probability: 0.33³ ≈ 3.7%. SC was at 11 HP from end of R3 onward — classified as "below 12 HP" per tactics. Tactics say "retreat behind a Rager or pillar; only fire if no melee can reach." SC was using TR correctly to maintain range, but the consecutive Barrage recharges enabled a 11-HP creature to deal decisive damage (killing Bazgar R5, chipping Sabriel through R7 for 3+8+11=22 damage after Bazgar fell). The fight extended from expected ~4 rounds to 10 rounds because of this streak.

Feel note: a 11-HP creature that recharges a 3d6 area effect three consecutive turns feels less like a tactical retreat and more like a mechanical zombie. The tactics text doesn't restrict Barrage when at low HP — it only restricts melee. Barrage kiting at ≤12 HP is RAW-correct but might need a DM note: "A severely wounded SC should not just kite with Barrage — if cover is available it should try to break contact; if no cover exists, accept one final Barrage before fleeing the encounter." This is a **table coaching note**, not a stat change. 17th-cycle first extended fight due to low-HP Barrage streak.

**FI-TP17-B — CW fired on already-acted DWs R1+R2; TR blocked further uses R3-R10**

Both CW uses went to already-acted DWs (R1: DW1 at 27 HP; R2: DW2 at 27 HP). Both were legal per MQ-12 (HP > 20 guard passed). R3+: TR fired every turn as melee threat persisted, blocking CW. SC ended the fight with 1/3 charges remaining (at SC death, R10). 17th-cycle CW structural pattern: CW fires early on delayed targets, then TR monopolizes the bonus action. The 1 remaining CW charge was held but never spent. Feel: CW seems to exist only to fire in the opening 1-2 rounds, then disappears. This is a repeat of prior cycle findings (FI-TP11-C) — no new action needed.

**FI-TP17-C (MQ) — Sim coded CW as precluding R1 Barrage (simulation logic error, spec correct)**

In R1, the simulation fired CW (bonus action) and then multiattacked instead of Barraging. CW is a bonus action; Barrage is the main action — there is no conflict. The sim's logic `if not sc_barrage_expended and not cw_used: barrage` incorrectly blocked Barrage when CW was used. At the table, the correct SC play R1 in a corridor with PCs in line: fire Barrage as main action + CW as bonus (per MQ-41/FI-75 corridor rule). This run's R1 SC turn was therefore weaker than intended — Barrage should have landed on Bazgar+Sabriel R1, not R3. If R1 Barrage had landed at typical 9-15 damage, the fight might have ended sooner. **Not a spec bug; simulation harness error. Spec is correct.**

### Mechanical questions

**MQ-TP17-A — Tactical Drilling advantage applied before qualifying ally attacked**

DW2 (init 22) attacked Bazgar in R1 with advantage (sim coded `[ADV: 7,20]`). But Tactical Drilling requires an ally within 5 ft of the target to have already attacked — DW1 had not yet acted in R1 (init 20 < 22). Advantage was incorrectly granted R1 DW2. Result: DW2 hit with [20]+4=24 on axe1 — would have hit anyway, so outcome unchanged. But the accuracy benefit was wrong. For future sim runs: DW2 R1 axe1 should be flat; DW1 R1 axe1 gains advantage (DW2 adjacent to Bazgar when DW1 acts). **Not a spec bug — simulation harness init-order TD check error.**

**MQ-TP17-B — PTV notification fired on ally-alive check, not ally-hit check**

Pack Tactics Voice notifications were printed every throw regardless of whether allies hit this round. PTV should trigger only when an ally *hits*. In R1, DW2+DW1 did hit Bazgar, so PTV was active — but the sim printed it unconditionally. Downstream consequence: Marwen's Dex save was labeled as having disadvantage from PTV in rounds where allies missed (R5 DW2 missed R5 axe1 before axe2 hit). **Not a spec bug — simulation labeling error only.**
