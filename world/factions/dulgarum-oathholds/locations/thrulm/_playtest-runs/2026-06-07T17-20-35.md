### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-SW34-A (carry-forward):** 7th consecutive VICTORY on shrine-wedge, now VICTORY R2. Both STDs eliminated in 2 rounds by 2× L3 Fireball (33 and 34 fire damage) despite altar-zone suppression. At 45 HP, a STD cannot survive even one unhalved Fireball (28 average), let alone two in a row. The suppression zone's design intent is sound — it removes the doubling — but base AoE at level 5 still one-shots or two-shots these NPCs. Prior recommendation: add +1 thrall-derro buffer OR raise STD HP to 55–60. **Carries FI-SW33-A / FI-SW32-A / FI-SW31-A / FI-SW30-A. Human decision required.**

- **FI-SW34-B:** Stagger mechanic fired correctly (STD-B fires R1, STD-A holds), but the hold was irrelevant because STD-A died R1 to Fireball before it could use its held AR in R2. The stagger rule is only meaningful when both STDs survive to Round 2 — which has not happened in 7 consecutive cycles against an AoE-capable party. The mechanic is correctly authored; it just has no playtest expression at current STD HP. Tied to FI-SW34-A.

- **FI-SW34-C:** Unstable Form never expressed meaningfully. STD-B had UF active in R2 (took 16 fire damage in R1 ≥ 10 threshold), but UF advantage on Multiattack attacks still missed Bazgar (17, 12 vs AC 18). With UF correctly applied, the best-of-two rolls were still below AC 18. Without a longer fight (R3+), UF can only trigger once and at low STD HP. Tied to FI-SW34-A — longer fights = UF payoff.

- **FI-SW34-D:** Driven Escape never triggered — neither STD reached below 20 HP before dying outright. This ability appears unreachable in practice against any AoE party. The below-20-HP survival window is too narrow when a Fireball can take 45 HP → 12 HP in one roll (or more). Tied to FI-SW34-A.

- **FI-SW34-E (NEW):** Altar-zone suppression is working correctly but creates a decision trap for the Wizard player who may not know the zone is active. Marwen committed 2× L3 Fireball even with suppression — because 8d6 even without doubling is sufficient. The zone's real impact (halving potential output from 66+ to 33) is invisible to players unless the DM calls it out explicitly. **Recommend: add a COMBAT-CHEAT-SHEET callout for the altar zone that the DM can announce at the start of combat.** (FI-SW31-D / FI-SW30-B carry-forward.)
