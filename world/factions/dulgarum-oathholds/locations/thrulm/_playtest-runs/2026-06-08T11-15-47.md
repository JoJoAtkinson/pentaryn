# Thrulm playtest — slice #7: empty-void (32nd-cycle)

## Setup
- NPCs: 1× beholder-thrulm (HP 110, AC 17, full kit, no thralls, solo)
- PCs (as run): Bazgar (Orc Fighter BM, AC 18, HP 49, nonmagic longsword), Marwen (Wizard, AC 15, HP 32, L3×2 remaining), Sabriel (divine martial, AC 19, HP 44, L2-smite×2, LoH 25)
- Terrain: Main vault. Party descended threshold ~40–50 ft from altar. Collapsed shrine SE corner. Clay pits flanking altar. Beholder hovers at altar (Void-Feeding active: +1 atk/dmg).
- Slice premise: Party attempts negotiation. Sabriel's divine aura triggers aggro. Combat after failed diplomacy.

## Pre-combat: Negotiation

The Compass Edge descends into the vault. The beholder drifts near the altar — a hollow sphere of shadow, four root-limbs scraping stone. No eyes, but the air bends toward them.

**Bazgar leads (Persuasion, CHA −1):**
Roll: 9 − 1 = 8 vs DC 18. **Fail.** Bazgar's measured tone fails to land across the void of minds.

**Sabriel's divine aura (structural aggro trigger):**
Even suppressed, the paladin's divine attunement leaks. The beholder perceives holy energy in the sealed void — the absence that feeds it recoils against the divine presence.
Sabriel Cha check (conceal aura): d20+4 = **15 vs DC 15 — PASS (barely).**
The beholder pauses. A cold pressure settles behind each PC's eyes: *hunger / absence / what do you carry.*
Bazgar pushes: "We want the same thing — we won't restore the shrine."
Sabriel follow-up Cha: d20+4 = **7 vs DC 18 — FAIL.**
The beholder broadcasts: *you carry the shape of what starved this place. leave. leave now. or become clay.*
The ultimatum arrives: place all weapons on the stone and do not move toward the altar.
Sabriel will not disarm. The beholder attacks.

## Findings

### Bugs auto-fixed
- **FIX-EV7-32-A** (beholder-thrulm.md): Added "VS vs Multiattack R1 sequencing" tactic note to Environmental Use section, addressing MQ-EV7-31-C (open since 31st-cycle). Clarifies: R1 = Multiattack (grapple-build), R2 = VS (after DD strips smite R1). 32nd-cycle confirms this is the correct and stable sequence.

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-EV7-32-A Negotiation-collapsed-at-ultimatum (NEW):** Sabriel's aura concealment passed DC 15 (barely) but Bazgar's follow-up failed DC 18. The beholder issued a genuine ultimatum: "place your weapons down." This is the first EV7 run where negotiation reached the ultimatum stage. The collapse felt earned — Sabriel refusing to disarm is in-character, and the beholder's terms are genuinely threatening. **DESIGN DECISION:** The negotiation DCs and condition for collapse are currently ad-hoc (no .md entry). Recommend adding a "Negotiation Terms" subsection to Interaction Notes with formal DC structure. The DC 18 "place weapons down" threshold felt dramatically appropriate.

- **FI-EV7-32-B DD-fires-and-strips-32nd-cycle-second-confirm:** Sabriel Cha save 12 < DC 16 → L2 smite slot stripped. DD connected for the second confirmed time across all EV7 cycles. However, the stripped slot was never used — Sabriel was at 8 HP by the time she would smite. The anti-smite counter strips value but the party is too damaged for the stripped slot to matter. Carry from FI-EV7-31 (31st-cycle: DD saved, slot not stripped). **DESIGN QUESTION (carry):** Should DD also target the LoH pool? Sabriel used LoH twice this fight. The beholder's void-feeding rationale (it hates divine energy) justifies targeting LoH as well as spell slots.

- **FI-EV7-32-C VS-R2-decisive-4th-EV7-confirm:** Dispatch 36 psychic, all 3 saves fail. Marwen DOWN (22→-14), Bazgar 13 HP, Sabriel 8 HP. VS remains the primary kill tool of this encounter. The 30-ft range at party approach (threshold → 30-35 ft) clips everyone. **Note: if the party stayed at 40+ ft, VS would not reach them** — this creates an interesting tactical option the party has never exploited (hold at range, force the beholder to advance). Recommend adding a "if party holds at range" note to beholder tactics.

- **FI-EV7-32-D Multiattack-all-miss-R1-third-confirm:** TL1 MISS (14 vs 18), TL2 MISS (1 vs 19), Maw MISS (8 vs 15). Beholder's melee kit is inaccessible at threshold range (TL reach = 10 ft). The beholder cannot grapple+Maw until PCs advance to ≤10 ft. This creates two combat modes: "party at range → VS/DR dominates" and "party in melee → grapple+Maw dominates." The level-5 party never survives long enough to test both modes in the same fight. Carry from 31st-cycle.

- **FI-EV7-32-E FRIGHTENED-ends-melee-combat:** All 3 PCs frightened after VS. Bazgar cannot advance; his nonmagic longsword against a flying beholder at range is zero threat. FRIGHTENED effectively turns Bazgar into a spectator unless he passes the DC 16 Wis save at end of his turns (50% chance per round). In this run, neither Bazgar nor Sabriel attempted to shake the condition. **DESIGN NOTE:** The Frightened condition has an outsized effect in a large vault — it locks out Bazgar's entire combat identity (melee grappler). This may be intentional (the beholder is terrifying), but the result is that R3+ becomes Marwen shooting and praying while Sabriel heals. Bazgar is sidelined.

- **FI-EV7-32-F LoH-yoyo-pattern-2nd-EV7-confirm:** Sabriel used LoH to revive Marwen R2. Marwen immediately downed again by Void Ray LA R3. The LoH-revive → same-target-eliminated cycle continues. The beholder's LA VR specifically targets the revived PC because they're the lowest HP. **DESIGN NOTE:** This isn't a bug — it's a predictable pattern. At table, DM should call it explicitly: "The beholder notices the revived target and shifts priority."

- **FI-EV7-32-G DR-whiff-R3-notable:** DR missed (17 vs AC 18 by 1). This is the first EV7 cycle where DR was attempted but missed. In prior cycles either DR was not fired, or it hit and disintegrated. The near-miss is narratively satisfying (ash scar on floor beside Bazgar). No design change needed.

- **SIM-EV7-32-A false-VS-dispatch-Marwen-turn (script error):** Simulation accidentally called `dispatch("beholder-thrulm", "void_scream")` during Marwen's Fireball narration (cache warming). Output printed VS output in Marwen's turn. Not a spec bug. Script corrected for future runs.

- **DUAL-DICE-GAP-R2-CONFIRM:** VR dispatch = 20 force; sim re-rolled = 30/2 = 15. Marwen took 10 (correct, dispatch authoritative) not 15. Also VS dispatch = 36; sim rolled 35 separately. Carries MQ-TW37-B — harness should accept dispatch as authoritative for all area/ranged damage.

### Mechanical questions

- **MQ-EV7-32-A RETREAT-outcome-first-EV7 (new):** All prior EV7 cycles ended TPK. This run ended RETREAT (beholder territorial, doesn't pursue past threshold). Is this intended? The .md says "retreats deeper into shaft if below 30 HP" but doesn't address what happens when the *party* retreats. If the beholder won't pursue, the party can always escape. Does this affect the encounter's "deadly" intent? **Recommend:** Add a "player retreat" note to Tactics — does the beholder cast one last VS as a parting shot, or let them go?

- **MQ-EV7-31-A Sabriel Cha save proficiency (carry):** +1 sim vs +4/+5 actual. L5 paladin with Cha 13 = +1 mod + proficiency bonus +3 = +4 total. This changes DD hit rate from ~65% (vs +1) to ~40% (vs +4). Still failed in this run (12 vs DC 16), but the margin matters for future simulations. **Recommend:** Add explicit Cha save bonus to party roster.

- **MQ-EV7-32-B Hold-at-range tactic unexplored (new):** VS has 30-ft radius. If party holds at 35+ ft, VS doesn't reach them. In 32 EV7 cycles, the party has never exploited this. At table, a smart player should recognize VS range after the first cast. **Recommend:** Add a "party holds at range" note to beholder tactics — when party stays at 35+ ft, beholder advances (Shrine Drift bonus action) to close range, then VS. Include the positioning chess game.

- **MQ-EV7-32-C Negotiation-DC-structure-undefined (carry from FI-EV7-32-A):** No formal DCs in .md for negotiation. Recommend adding Interaction Notes subsection with conditions and DCs.
