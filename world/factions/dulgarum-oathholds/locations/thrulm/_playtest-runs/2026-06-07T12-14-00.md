## Findings

### Bugs auto-fixed
- **FIX-FC39-A:** Added "Maw timing note" to `beholder-thrulm.md` Tactics/Against Melee section. Maw MUST target a grappled PC to trigger auto-crit (8d8+3 vs 4d8+3). 10+ cycles of Maw targeting a third ungrappled PC — mechanic was never expressed. Note added with explicit instruction: both Tentacle Lashes fire first; if either hits, Maw targets the grappled PC.
- **FIX-FC39-B:** Added "Unstable Ground priority note" to `beholder-thrulm.md` Lair Actions section. Melee fighters (Bazgar, Sabriel) are higher value UG targets than back-line casters (Marwen). Prone on a melee fighter disrupts attack rolls and movement; prone on a caster costs a bonus action to stand with minimal combat impact. Note also recommends rotating to Void Eruption R3+ when melee fighters cluster within 20 ft of shrine.

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-FC39-A (carry FI-FC35-A):** Void Scream bimodal TPK pattern, 39th-cycle confirm. When VS fires, fight ends within the same round (avg 33 psychic = instant-kill for Marwen at 32 HP, crippling for Bazgar/Sabriel). When VS doesn't fire (recharge 6 = 17% per round), party has meaningful combat window. 2/3 FC-slice cycles show VS as the TPK vector; 1/3 show TPK via sustained multiattack + lair pressure without VS. Recommendation from prior cycles: lower recharge to 5–6 (33% per round) to increase expression rate and reduce RNG variance. **HUMAN DECISION: recharge 6→5-6 or accept bimodal?**

- **FI-FC39-B (new):** `void_scream` DB spec includes Frightened condition in `save.notes` ("On a FAILED save, target is also FRIGHTENED of the beholder for 1 minute") that is NOT in `beholder-thrulm.md` stat block. The stat block only describes psychic damage + shrine-proximity disadvantage. If the Frightened condition is intentional, the stat block must be updated. If it was copy-pasted from a standard beholder/psychic-scream template and wasn't intended, the DB spec should be corrected. Frightened would add "disadvantage on attack rolls vs beholder and can't willingly move toward it" — significant combat impact. **HUMAN DECISION: remove Frightened from DB spec or add to stat block?**

- **FI-FC39-C (carry MQ-EVD-7-A, MQ-BEL3-38-B):** Grapple mechanic discrepancy between stat block and DB. `beholder-thrulm.md` stat block: "the target is grappled (escape DC 16)" — implies automatic grapple on hit; DC 16 is the escape check (Str Athletics or Dex Acrobatics). DB spec: `apply_condition_on_hit: {condition: grappled, save_dc: 16, save_ability: str}` — renders as "[ASK PLAYER: DC 16 STR save to resist grappled]". At table, this gives PCs a ~30-35% chance to resist the grapple (save +0/+1/+2 Str mod for L5 PCs). RAW for this stat block: no resistance save, grapple is automatic. Same pattern seen in `glacier-stalker`. Keeping save-based is a house-rule that weakens the beholder. **HUMAN DECISION: keep save-based (house rule, PCs have agency) or remove save (RAW, auto-grapple on hit)?**

- **FI-FC39-D (new):** DD targeting unconscious PCs. Drain Divinity can legally target unconscious creatures (they have spell slots; they just can't act). Sabriel at 0 HP lost her last L3 slot to DD while unconscious. This feels exploitative — the beholder drains a helpless PC who can't even contest the save (auto-fail). The tactic note says "Use DD on Sabriel early." There is no note about stopping DD when Sabriel is unconscious. **Log for human:** recommend tactic note: "Once Sabriel is unconscious, DD has no further combat value (she can't use the slots anyway); redirect LA to Tentacle or Void Ray on remaining combatants."

- **FI-FC39-E (carry FI-FC35-E, FI-BEL3-37-D):** Thrall hit-rate 0/8 vs AC 18/19. *(1/8 actual: THRALL-2 hit Bazgar for 4 dmg in R2 only.)* At +1 to-hit vs AC 18, thralls hit on 17+ (20% chance). Manifest Thralls lair action was not tested this cycle (Unstable Ground used both R1/R2). The FIX-BEL3-38-A note covers the Manifest Thralls decision point. Thrall DPS contribution remains effectively zero; their role is HP-sponge and Compel-Thrall bait. 7th-consecutive FC-slice-confirm. **Carry: recommend HP 28-30 (more resilient sponge) or remove thralls from final-confrontation (replace with shrine-touched for more melee threat).**
