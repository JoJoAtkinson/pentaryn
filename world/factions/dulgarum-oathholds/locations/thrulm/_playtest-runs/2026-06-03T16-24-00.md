## Findings

### Bugs auto-fixed

- **FIX-EV7-22-A**: `void_eruption` dice in beholder-thrulm.md line 26 read `3d8` — corrected to `2d10` to match DB spec (`damage.dice = "2d10"`). Save DC and half-on-save were already correct.

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-EV7-22-A (NEW)**: **DR double-disintegration** — DR recharged on d6=6 (R2, 17% probability), hitting Sabriel (23 vs AC19) for 57 force. Two PCs disintegrated in R0+R2; Bazgar soloed from R3. Sabriel never contributed: died with full resource pool (L3:2, L2:3, L1:4, LoH 25, smites × full). The pattern is RNG-driven (DR recharge probability ~33% per round at recharge 5), but this cycle confirms the high-lethality burst ceiling. DESIGN DECISION: Is the current 10d8 DR magnitude + 5-6 recharge appropriate for a 3-PC L5 party? Even a single DR hit on AC15-19 routinely disintegrates a PC in one shot. Human review recommended.

- **FI-EV7-22-B**: **DD LA budget never executes** (22nd-cycle confirm) — DD resisted once (d20=? Cha+3 save) then Sabriel died before second attempt. 3 LA burned for 0 output. With Sabriel eliminated R2, the beholder's remaining LA budget was spent on VR/TL (neither fired — targets died to lair VE first). The LA economy collapses asymmetrically when DR is effective: party resources worth ~50 HP of healing/denial are made irrelevant by two force-damage rolls. DESIGN DECISION: if the encounter intent is to express the beholder's *legend* (DR, VS, DD, VR), the DR should be balanced so it doesn't terminate the fight before other abilities can express.

- **FI-EV7-22-C**: **Sabriel full-resources elimination** — divine martial died with all slots, full LoH, and smite pool intact. Only one attack action total (2 attacks: Antireality blocked one, the other hit for 6). Her Cha+3 DD-resist rate (~40% per save) is working as designed, but she was eliminated before DD could be used more than once. DESIGN NOTE: the divine martial's kit (LoH, smites, heals) is entirely anti-lair-damage and anti-attrition. Against a beholder that can bypass attrition with disintegration, the kit's value is near-zero.

- **FI-EV7-19-A (22nd confirm)**: `manifest_thralls` LOCKED in solo-beholder configuration. 22 cycles — consider replacing this lair action with a beholder-only alternative for the solo encounter variant.

- **FI-EV7-19-E (22nd confirm)**: `shrine_drift` narration monotony — same text 4× across R1-R4. High-repetition ability needs 2-3 alternate narration strings.

- **FI-EV7-22-D**: **Antireality one-trigger** — triggered on Sabriel's Atk1 R1 (hit=17 exactly at threshold AC+2=19) and converted to MISS. Then her Atk2 hit 24 (above threshold — correct no-trigger). Not fully dead-letter this cycle, but the FI-EV7-21-C concern about Bazgar +7 is confirmed: all Bazgar hits were frightened-disadv and either missed or hit at 24 (above threshold). Antireality only fires in a narrow band vs the fighter.
