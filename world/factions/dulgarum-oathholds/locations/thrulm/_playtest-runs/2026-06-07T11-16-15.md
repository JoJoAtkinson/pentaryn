### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-BEL3-38-A VS-DOUBLE-FIRE-38th-cycle** (CARRY from FI-BEL3-33-B/FI-BEL3-37-F):
- VS fired R1 (Marwen DOWN before acting) and R3 (all 3 PCs DOWN). VS is the exclusive TPK vector this cycle — without it, beholder took only 36 damage over 3 rounds.
- Bimodal confirmed: VS absent → fight runs 5+ rounds with player agency; VS present → 1-shot casters, fight over by R3.
- Prior cycles: 0/3 VS fires (37th), 1/5 (31st), 1/3 (33rd). This cycle: 2/3. RNG variance but structural.
- Recommend: recharge-6-only-once-per-encounter cap. Or reduce to 5d10 base (avg 27.5 → still kills Marwen, nearly kills Bazgar, but Sabriel survives at ~16HP and can LoH). Human decision required.

**FI-BEL3-38-B MARWEN-NEVER-ACTS-38th-cycle** (CARRY from FI-BEL3-37-D):
- Marwen was DOWN before her turn in both rounds she was alive (R1 VS, R3 VS at 1HP from LoH).
- She cast 0 spells across the entire 3-round fight.
- Root cause: WIS+0 save vs DC16 = 25% success rate. VS targets all 3 PCs; Marwen reliably fails.
- The LoH revival loop (Sabriel spends action to revive → VS fires again immediately → Marwen down before acting) is thematically tense but mechanically futile for Marwen.
- Recommend: DM coaching note — LoH-revive Marwen only if she can immediately act (i.e., she goes before beholder next round). In this cycle, Marwen's init 17 means she acts AFTER lair but before beholder — so R2 LoH revive was correct (beholder acted first in R2 and didn't fire VS; Marwen should have acted next). The problem is VS recharged and fired R3 before Marwen's init 17.

**FI-BEL3-38-C THRALLS-SURVIVE-ALL-3-ROUNDS-FIRST-BEL3-CYCLE**:
- Prior 7 BEL3 cycles: thralls always wiped by Fireball R1-R2. This cycle: Marwen DOWN R1, never casts Fireball. Thralls alive at fight end.
- Thrall damage contribution: 0 hits landed in R1 or R2 (all miss vs AC18 Bazgar, AC19 Sabriel). 1 reaction hit in R3 on already-downed Bazgar (irrelevant).
- Thrall impact: forced 3× Manifest Thralls lair (R1-R3). This blocked Unstable Ground or Void Eruption which might have added ground-control pressure.
- Encounter design implication: if party composition removes Fireball source (no wizard, or wizard downed first), thralls become permanent AC-18+ pinata misses + lair-action waste. Recommend: add stagger-start for thralls (one starts 10ft closer than party) OR split thrall HP to 28-30 (sturdier even if Fireball fires, forces LoH+smite attrition).

**FI-BEL3-38-D LA-SPLIT-PARTIAL-POSITIVE** (new this cycle):
- R1: DD(3) → Sabriel PASS (21). R2: Tentacle(1)+VR(2) → both miss/half-save. R3: DD(3) → Sabriel PASS (24 — nat 20+4).
- Sabriel's CHA+4 makes DD a 55% fail-rate for her (needs 12+ on d20). This cycle: 3/3 saves. Pure variance but structurally: DD-on-Sabriel is a coin flip per round.
- LA split in R2 (Tentacle+VR) added 9 force damage to Sabriel without consuming 3 full LA. Better value.
- Recommend: Add to tactics — "if Sabriel saves DD twice, switch to VR+Tentacle permanently; slot attrition via DD not viable against high-CHA divine martial."

**FI-BEL3-38-E MAW-AUTO-CRIT-NEVER-EXPRESSED-38th-confirm** (CARRY MQ-BEL3-37-A/DD-BEL3-32-A):
