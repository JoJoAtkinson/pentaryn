### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**MQ-EV9-A (SIM GAP — not a spec bug):** Multiattack dispatch was output in R2 but damage was not applied to specific targets in the simulation. Bazgar at 5 HP should have died from TL1 (16 bludgeoning) + TL2 (10 bludgeoning) + Maw (24 piercing) = 50 total. True TPK occurs R2, not R3. Sim continued with wrong HP state. This is a recurring simulation architecture issue — dispatch outputs attack results but the sim must manually assign targets and apply damage. No spec fix needed.

**MQ-EV9-B (SIM ERROR CONFIRM — 9th cycle):** Disintegration Ray is an attack roll (+6 to-hit vs target AC), NOT a Dex save. Dispatch output confirms: "to-hit 25" = d20+6 attack. Simulation modeled it as "Marwen Dex save d20+2=8 vs DC13" — wrong mechanics, right outcome this cycle (hit either way), but the framing is incorrect and will produce wrong outcomes if Marwen buffs AC to 15+ via Shield reaction or Blur. This is MQ-65 class (chronic sim double-roll + wrong mechanic, logged in 4th-cycle FC). Outside cron blast radius — human must fix Phase B sim infrastructure to parse dispatch to-hit vs target AC rather than re-rolling a save.

**FI-EV9-A (9th-cycle confirm):** Marwen DISINTEGRATED in surprise round. DR to-hit 25 vs AC 15 → clear hit, 32 force → 0 HP → disintegrated. Pattern across 9 cycles: Marwen survives the surprise round only when DR rolls low (nat 2 in 7th cycle, misses in 4th cycle). The ~60% hit rate at +6 vs AC15 means Marwen is disintegrated in roughly 2/3 of EV runs. Structural threat: the encounter starts with a ~60% chance of permanently removing a party member before combat begins. Design question already logged (FI-153, FI-EV5-B).

**FI-EV9-B (NEW — COUNTER TO FI-EV8-A):** Sabriel landed **2 smites** this cycle (L2 smite R1 = 17 dmg, L1 smite R2 = 24 dmg). FI-EV8-A claimed "DD strips all Sabriel L2 smites; 0 smites fired" as an 8th-cycle finding. This cycle shows that is NOT universal: when DD Cha save passes (d20+2=18 vs DC16 in R2), Sabriel retains her smite and fires it. DD only stole 1 L2 slot (R1 fail); the R2 attempt was saved. Total smite damage: ~41 HP — Sabriel was the party's primary damage source. **FI-EV8-A should be revised:** DD monopolizes LA budget and creates high pressure on smite availability, but ~25% per-save pass rate means 1-2 smites get through in ~half of EV runs. "Paladin identity stripped" is not universal — it depends on Cha save variance.

**FI-EV7-D (9th-cycle confirm — FEEL NOTE):** Lair rotation in solo beholder collapses to 2 options (void_eruption ↔ unstable_ground). manifest_thralls is a dead action slot — no thralls exist. DM running empty-void slice should note: skip manifest_thralls from the rotation entirely; treat as 2-option VE/UG alternation. This was noted as a FEEL NOTE in 7th cycle; 9th cycle confirms it hasn't changed. Low-priority DM note to add to beholder-thrulm.md lair actions section: *"In solo configuration (no thralls), skip manifest_thralls — rotate VE/UG only."*

**FI-EV9-C (POSITIVE VARIANCE):** DR was offline R1 and R2 post-surprise (recharge d6=1, d6=3). Beholder relied on VS + multiattack + lair actions instead. This creates a different encounter texture from cycles where DR fires every other round. The variety is narratively interesting — sometimes the beholder is "that thing that disintegrates people constantly" and sometimes it feels more like a battlefield-control boss. No spec issue; documenting as positive design note.
