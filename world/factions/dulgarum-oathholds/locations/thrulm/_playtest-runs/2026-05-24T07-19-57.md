### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**DD-44 (NEW): Void Scream / Disintegration Ray R1 co-availability conflict — no tiebreaker**

In R1, BOTH conditions were simultaneously met:
- Disintegration condition: "R1, all party visible → Disint on highest caster" (Marwen in range)
- Void Scream condition: "2+ PCs within 30 ft" (all 3 PCs clustered at fight start)

The sim chose Disint (per explicit R1 priority text), which killed Marwen outright (disintegration — strongest possible outcome). VS fires R2 and kills the remaining two PCs.

However: if VS had fired R1 instead, all 3 PCs would have taken 5d8 psychic (~22 avg). With Marwen at 23 HP after lair damage, she'd likely survive R1, Disint would fire R2 at a healthy target, fight runs 3–4 rounds.

**Recommendation:** Add explicit tiebreaker to beholder tactics: *"If both Disintegration Ray and Void Scream are available R1, and a disintegratable caster is at ≤40 HP from prior damage: fire Disint first (single-target kill > AoE wounding). If no caster is below 40 HP: Void Scream first (AoE softens multiple targets for follow-up). R1 Disint is almost always correct when party has entered a damage sequence."* Authoring-only. Low risk.
