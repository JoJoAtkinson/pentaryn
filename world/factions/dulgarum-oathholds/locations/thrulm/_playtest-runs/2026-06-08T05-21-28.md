### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-SW35-A — VICTORY R2 35th-cycle confirm (carry FI-SW34-A):** 8+ cycles of shrine-wedge yield consistent VICTORY R2-R3. 2× STD-only is not threatening at L5, even with altar zone suppression. The altar zone suppresses fire vuln but not Fireball *output* (28 avg fire from 8d6 still craters 45 HP). Recommend: HP 55-60 on STDs, or +1 thrall-derro as buffer. This is a DESIGN DECISION — encounter intent is "sub-encounter, not TPK threat," but current feel is one-sided.

- **FI-SW35-B — UF-never-expressed 8th consecutive confirm:** Unstable Form (STD-B R2: UF active, has advantage on attacks) was never used. Below-20-HP Driven Escape priority means the STD uses a bonus-action retreat and reckless AR on its main action — MA is not chosen. UF's advantage on attack rolls requires a MA turn which the STD is structurally prevented from taking once it drops below 20 HP. *Only way UF expresses:* STD takes 10+ in a round AND stays above 20 HP entering its next turn. Against L5 party with Fireball that window is extremely narrow. This is a core mechanic that is rarely expressed. DESIGN DECISION: either raise below-HP threshold to 10 (allowing more UF swings before Driven Escape kicks in) or accept UF as flavor that rarely matters.

- **FI-SW35-C — OBR misses 35th-cycle (carry MQ-SW34-A):** OBR fired twice, both misses (+4 vs AC 18 = 35%, +4 vs AC 19 = 30%). Self-damage applied both times (2 + 1 psychic, contributing to STD-B's early demise). The OBR mechanic's value is the 1d4 self-damage chipping the STD toward death, not the counter-attack. This is carry from prior cycles.

- **FI-SW35-D — Altar zone suppression remains the primary encounter lever:** Outside the zone, STD-B's Fireball would have dealt 60 fire (30 doubled). That's a guaranteed death. The suppression is the *only reason* this fight lasts 2 rounds instead of 1. DM must announce the altar zone when the party enters — if players don't know the vulnerability is suppressed, they won't have a reason to try to pull the STDs away. Confirmed carry from FI-SW34-E / FI-SW31-D: COMBAT-CHEAT-SHEET announcement recommended.

- **FI-SW35-E — Stagger rule fired and held correctly:** STD-B correctly held AR (lower init, both had AR). This did not matter because STD-B went into Driven Escape mode R2 and fired AR recklessly anyway. Stagger is working but moot in fast fights where one STD dies before R2 AR window.
