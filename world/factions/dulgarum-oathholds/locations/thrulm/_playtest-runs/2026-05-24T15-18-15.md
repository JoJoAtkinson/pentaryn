## Findings

### Bugs auto-fixed

1. **FI-20 — Compel Thrall targeting priority added** (`beholder-thrulm.md`): Thralls were directed at Sabriel (AC 19, +4 vs 19 = 30% hit rate) and missed all 12 combined attacks across 4 rounds. The beholder should compel thralls toward the lowest-AC PC (Marwen, AC 15 → 45% hit rate). Added explicit guidance to tactics: direct `compel_thrall` to lowest-AC PC; do not waste the command directing thralls at the divine martial. Low-risk authoring-only.

2. **FI-21 — Void Scream frightened rider DM reminder added** (`beholder-thrulm.md`): After VS hits, PCs who fail the save are frightened of the beholder (disadvantage on attacks when beholder visible, cannot voluntarily approach). The existing tactics line didn't remind the DM of this condition. Added a note to call it out each subsequent round — particularly meaningful for Bazgar (fighter who wants to close) and Marwen (whose Scorching Ray was at disadvantage R2 due to frightened). Low-risk authoring clarification.

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

1. **DD-42 (4th cycle confirmation) — Drain Divinity over-priority, HP threshold still unimplemented:** Drain Divinity fired 3 consecutive legendary-budget refreshes:
   - End of Marwen R1: Sabriel 44 HP → correct per current tactics (≥20 HP + L3 slot → fire DD).
   - End of Marwen R2: Sabriel 18 HP → **SHOULD** have triggered DD-42 HP threshold (< 20 HP → prefer Void Ray). Instead fired DD again.
   - End of Sabriel R3: Sabriel 18 HP → same issue. Fired DD instead of Void Ray (2) + Tentacle (1).
   All three DDs succeeded (Sabriel failed all three DC 16 Cha saves). The beholder gained 18 temp HP total. Despite the excessive DD spam, the outcome was correct (TPK). But if DD-42 HP threshold were applied in R2+R3, the beholder would have used Void Ray + Tentacle instead, likely killing Sabriel 1 round earlier. **The DD-42 fix is still needed and confirmed necessary by this run. Human sign-off required before authoring.**

2. **DD-11 (persists) — Maw grapple-crit same-multiattack one-action kill confirmed:** R2 multiattack vs Bazgar: Tentacle 1 → 20 bludgeoning + grapple, Tentacle 2 → 19 bludgeoning, Maw crit (grappled) → 30 piercing. Total = **69 damage in one action** vs 14 HP Bazgar. Instant death (total damage 104 > 2×49 HP). The timing question: grapple from Tentacle 1 takes effect immediately within the same multiattack action, enabling the Maw to crit in the same action. If DM rules grapple only activates at END of action (next-attack model), Maw never crits on the same multiattack. This is a RAW ambiguity — the grapple-crit pairing is intentional (DD-11 logged) but the one-action execution is brutal. **Human sign-off needed on whether grapple-crit requires a prior turn's grapple or can fire same-action.**

3. **FI-22 (new) — Thrall compel vs high-AC divine martial: near-zero contribution across 4 runs:** Across all 4 beholder-escorts-limited cycles, the thralls directed at Sabriel (AC 19) have produced ~1-3 successful hits total. Redirecting them to Marwen (AC 15) per the new FI-20 fix would significantly increase thrall threat. However, thematic question: does the beholder prefer to harass the healer (strategic, even if inefficient) or the softest target (efficient, less dramatic)? If the intent is "thralls create damage pressure while beholder drains divinity," targeting Marwen is correct. If the intent is "thralls slow Sabriel's healing," no target change is needed. **Human preference needed.** FI-20 fix assumes efficiency over theme; revert if theme is intended.

### Mechanical questions

1. **MQ-17 — Frightened from VS: does disadvantage persist when beholder is invisible/out-of-sight?** The beholder uses `shrine_drift` to pass through objects. If it ends its turn inside a pillar between rounds, is it "visible" to frightened PCs? Rules-as-written: frightened disadvantage requires the source to be visible. Shrine drift could technically break line-of-sight to enable penalty-free attacks. At table, rule the beholder is almost always visible (it's a 6-ft sphere in a shrine chamber). Recommend: add a note "treat the beholder as visible in the shrine chamber at all times unless explicitly hidden behind a wall of stone or equivalent effect — shrine_drift passes through but doesn't provide cover while hovering."

2. **MQ-18 — Instant death threshold for Bazgar's multiattack death:** Bazgar took 104 total damage (69 in the multiattack + prior lair actions). 2×max HP = 98. 104 > 98 → instant death (no death saves). At table: confirm this exceeds the 2×max HP threshold with the player before making the call — the overkill is dramatic and real, but DM transparency helps. Particularly important since the maw crit can spike high. No authoring change; just a table-feel note.
