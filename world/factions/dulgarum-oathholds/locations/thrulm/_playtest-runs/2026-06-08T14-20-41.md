## Findings

### Bugs auto-fixed

1. **OBR trigger timing contradiction** — Checklist (line 20) says OBR fires "when the shrine-touched takes damage" (after all damage, including smite riders). Tactics section (line 40) said "auto-fires when hit" — the word "hit" implies before smite is declared, contradicting the checklist. Fixed: changed "auto-fires when hit by a visible attacker" → "auto-fires after taking damage from a visible attacker … after ALL damage from that attack is applied (including Divine Smite or other added damage)." `.md` only; no DB change needed.

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**DESIGN-SW-A (log for human): Altar zone nukes both of Marwen's damage levers simultaneously**
Both STDs start in altar zone → fire/radiant suppressed → Marwen's Scorching Ray loses fire doubling; Sabriel's smites lose radiant doubling. In this run Marwen hit 2/3 Scorching Rays for 17 fire (normal) instead of ~34 (doubled). Sabriel's smites similarly dealt half-expected burst. The zone creates good tension, but having BOTH vulnerability types suppressed in the default slice setup may over-penalize the party's two strongest damage outputs simultaneously. Recommend: consider starting 1-of-2 STDs at 70+ ft from altar (outside zone) by default in this slice, giving the party one valid radiant/fire target. Alternatively, make the zone boundary more visually telegraphed so the party can choose to drag STDs out early.

**DESIGN-SW-B (log for human): AR fires once per STD in short fights; recharge math irrelevant**
In a 3-round fight with 2 STDs (one dropped R1), AR effectively fires once: STD-A holds (stagger R1), drops before it can use; STD-B fires once in R2, doesn't recharge. The 5-6 recharge is never stress-tested. For 2-STD encounters, the stagger rule is the dominant AR-shaping mechanic, not the recharge die. Not a bug, but the encounter designer should know the recharge die only matters in fights with ≥1 STD surviving into R3+.

**DESIGN-SW-C (log for human): OBR self-damage accelerates STD death**
STD-A took 3 OBR self-damage packets (1d4 each) in R1 alone — combined with attack damage, this shaved ~7 HP off its pool. OBR fires correctly (once per attacker turn, resets at STD's checklist), but the self-damage means every melee-heavy party accelerates the STD's death slightly. Possibly intentional (shrine punishing the STD), but worth watching across more cycles to see if it makes STDs feel too fragile vs melee parties.
