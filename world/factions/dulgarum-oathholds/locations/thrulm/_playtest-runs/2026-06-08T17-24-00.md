### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-FC42-A STD-B-multiattack-crushes-Marwen-R1 (carry):** STD-B fired two shrine-axe hits on Marwen (10+13=23 total), bringing her from 32→1 HP before the beholder landed a single meaningful attack. The CR2 shrine-touched did more first-round work than the CR13 boss. Table feel risk: players look at the beholder as the threat but get destroyed by a minor NPC. The beholder's TL1+TL2 both missed R1; Maw also missed Sabriel. When both STDs roll high initiative (tied at 20 here), they create a front-loaded pressure spike that overshadows the boss's R1 contribution. Consider: note in encounter setup that STDs act before lair at init 20 (lair loses ties) for high-drama openings, but this also means the boss's R1 grapple/maw pattern may not be the table's first impression.

- **FI-FC42-B VS-all-3-fail-R2-3rd-FC-confirm:** VS fired 6d10=33 psychic; every PC failed (Bazgar and Sabriel each rolled 5+1=6 and 3+3=6 on disadv; Marwen 8 vs DC16). This is the third consecutive FC cycle where VS ends the fight in a single action (R2 or R3). VS is THE kill vector. Disadv for Bazgar/Sabriel near the shrine amplifies this dramatically — a 33-psychic round feels earned as a boss moment. No fix recommended. However, note that the disadv source (shrine proximity) should be called out at the table; players walking into shrine range without awareness is a setup failure, not a mechanical one.

- **FI-FC42-C DD-fires-novel-init-config-POSITIVE:** First cycle where beholder had HIGHER initiative than Sabriel (init 19 vs Sabriel 15). FIX-FC40-A protocol still worked — beholder held 3 LA through its own turn and fired DD post-Sabriel at init 15. Sabriel FAIL (CHA 8 < DC16) stripped L2. DD fired correctly without the "acts-after-Sabriel" assumption. The protocol is initiative-order agnostic. **FIX-FC40-A VALIDATED** in this harder config.

- **FI-FC42-D VS-Frightened-rider-DB-vs-.md-4th-cycle-carry (DESIGN DECISION):** The VS DB dispatch output again shows: *"On a FAILED save, target is also FRIGHTENED of the beholder for 1 minute (DC 16 Wis save ends at end of each of the target's turns)."* The beholder's .md Void Scream stat block has NO Frightened condition — only psychic damage. This is a DB spec addition not reflected in the stat block. This appeared in cycles 39, 40, 41, 42. **Human decision required:** (a) Add Frightened to the .md Void Scream entry (balance note: Frightened + DC16 WIS can't-approach is punishing against melee fighters; recommend DM note about beholder-immunity making this moot for the beholder's own movement), or (b) remove Frightened from the DB spec. Carry from FI-FC39-B.

- **FI-FC42-E LoH-revive-flicker-then-VS-positive:** Sabriel revived Marwen via LoH (1 HP) then VS immediately killed Marwen again. This creates a brief dramatic beat — hope flickers and dies. The LoH yoyo is a recurring pattern (cycles 38, 40, 41, 42) but in this case it didn't prolong the fight; VS hit 1 second later. The flicker-of-hope-snuffed narrative beat is actually positive table feel. No fix.

- **FI-FC42-F Marwen-death-VE-not-VS:** Marwen's final kill was the lair action Void Eruption (hitting her at 1 HP), not VS. VS then finished Sabriel and Bazgar. The lair AoE doing cleanup on an already-wounded PC is mechanically valid but slightly anticlimactic — the dramatic "Void Scream wipes the party" beat is more effective when it alone does all three deaths. In this run: VE did Marwen, VS did Sabriel+Bazgar. Acceptable table experience but DMs should narrate VE and VS as part of one continuous void-pressure sequence, not separate events.

- **FI-FC42-G Thralls-0-hits-4-rounds-9th-FC-confirm (carry):** All four thralls missed every attack across both rounds (7 attack attempts vs AC18/19). Thralls at +3 to hit vs AC18/19 have ~25%/20% hit rate. This is the 9th consecutive FC cycle with near-zero thrall DPS. Thralls serve as initiative presence / action-economy pressure / lair-action targets, not actual damage dealers. This is established and accepted. No fix.

### Mechanical questions

- **MQ-FC42-A Lair-STD-tie-initiative:** When STDs roll 20 tied with the lair initiative count, the RAW rule "lair loses ties" means STDs fire before the lair action in that turn. In this run, the sim fired the lair first (bug), but since Bazgar saved UG the outcome was identical. At-table: DMs must track this and let STD-A and STD-B act before the lair action when all three share init 20. This creates brutal openings (STD-A AR + STD-B multiattack before lair even acts). Consider adding a note to `_overview.md` or the combat cheat sheet: "Lair loses init ties — any NPC at init 20 acts BEFORE the lair action."

- **MQ-FC42-B VS-Frightened-rider-source:** The Frightened condition in the VS dispatch has appeared in 4 consecutive FC cycles. It's not in the .md stat block. Carry from MQ-FC39-B / FI-FC39-B. Human review needed.

- **MQ-FC42-C Sabriel-CHA-save-modifier:** Sabriel rolled 5+3=8 for DD save. If correct save modifier is +5 (proficient paladin), that's 10, still a FAIL (DC16). Outcome identical but the accuracy of DD's 40% vs 25% hit rate matters for long-term balance analysis. Carry from MQ-EV7-31-A.
