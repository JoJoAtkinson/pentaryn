### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-TW20-A: CW range lockout — structural gap in tank-wall formation (DESIGN DECISION)**
- CW has 30 ft range. In standard tank-wall deployment, SC starts 45 ft from PCs, Rager starts 15 ft from PCs → SC-to-Rager = 30 ft (barely in range pre-charge).
- Once Rager charges to melee (5 ft from PCs), SC-to-Rager gap expands to ~40-45 ft → **CW is permanently unavailable for the rest of the fight.**
- This run: CW fired R1 (gave Rager natural-20 advantage → decisive hit, 4 slashing). Then 2 charges sat unspent for Rounds 2–6 (5 rounds). 2/3 of the CW budget was wasted.
- The slice's core test ("Taunt + CW pairing") works ONLY in the narrow window before the Rager charges — essentially R1 only if SC acts first in initiative. If Rager acts before SC, even R1 CW may miss the window.
- Options to discuss with DM: (a) increase CW range to 60 ft to match standard formation depth; (b) accept as intentional — CW is a "pre-charge buff" ability, tactically interesting because SC must choose between CW (buff Rager pre-charge) and Barrage (area pressure); (c) add CW targeting note: "in tank-wall, CW fires R1 only — SC uses remaining charges on PCs if Rager falls and party closes within 30 ft."
- **Recommend: Flag for DM. This is the primary balance lever for the slice.**

**FI-TW20-B: PTV (Pack Tactics Voice) entirely inactive in tank-wall formation (DESIGN DECISION)**
- PTV fires when "an ally within 30 ft hits." In standard tank-wall (Rager in melee ~40 ft from SC), PTV never triggers: Rager is out of range, and SC's own hits arguably don't count as "an ally."
- PTV is listed as a key passive on the shardcaller but it requires tight clustering inconsistent with SC's tactical role (40-60 ft back-line).
- PTV only reliably fires in: (a) multi-SC formations where SCs are within 30 ft of each other; (b) if SC interprets "ally" to include itself (ambiguous — see MQ-TW20-B below).
- In this run, PTV fired zero times. A passive mechanic that never fires in the standard formation is either misconfigured or underdocumented.
- **Recommend: Clarify PTV activation conditions in .md. Add note: "PTV is inactive in standard tank-wall if Rager has engaged melee and SC is at back-line range. PTV primarily functions in multi-SC formations or when SC has advanced into mid-range."**

**FI-TW20-C: SC solo kiting phase too long — 3 rounds of non-combat chase**
- After Rager died R3, SC solo-kited for Rounds 4–6 using TR to perpetually retreat. An open-vault room lets SC stay just out of melee indefinitely.
- SC dealt 6+8+8+7(barrage R5)+3(barrage save partial) = ~32 damage in 6 rounds solo. This is below the expected 3-round Multiattack of 2×(avg 7.5) = 15 dmg/round.
- The 3-round chase is tedious at the table. SC alone can't threaten the party (Marwen 13 HP → at risk, but SC's hit rate vs AC 19 Sabriel is very low).
- The room lacking pillars or cover means TR is an infinite "run away" ability when SC is solo.
- **Recommend: SC tactics note: "If Rager falls and no cover/pillar exists, assess surrender at ≤50% HP rather than indefinite kiting — the SC's threat level drops sharply without Rager support."**

**FI-TW20-D: Taunt on Marwen R1 was mechanically irrelevant (feel note)**
- Marwen failed Taunt save (5 vs DC 12) → disadvantage on non-Rager attacks. But Marwen then attacked the Rager (the Rager was in melee reach, highest threat). The Taunt restriction didn't affect her at all.
- Taunt works as designed when: (a) the target would otherwise attack the SC, another NPC, or another PC rather than the Rager; (b) the target is a save-based caster who can ignore the Rager and blast the SC with Fireball.
- Here, Marwen was taunted onto the Rager that she was already attacking. The Taunt generated no action-economy win.
- **Recommend (tactics note): "If a taunted PC was already focused on the Rager, Taunt this round was wasted. Next round redirect Taunt to whoever is trying to reach the SC (Sabriel advancing) or the PC who fired the Fireball last turn."**

### Mechanical questions

**MQ-TW20-A: Is CW range (30 ft) intentionally limited to R1 in tank-wall? (DESIGN DECISION)**
- The standard formation gives CW exactly one usable round (R1, if SC acts before Rager). Post-charge, 2/3 of CW budget is dead weight.
- If this is intentional: add tactical note making the constraint explicit. The SC player should know R1 CW is a "use it or lose it" window.
- If unintentional: increase CW range to 45-60 ft, or rewrite deployment to have SC mid-range (30 ft from PCs, 25 ft from Rager), which would allow R2 CW at cost of greater vulnerability.

**MQ-TW20-B: PTV "ally" — does SC's own hit count? (DESIGN DECISION)**
- PTV text: "when an ally within 30 ft hits, the target has disadvantage on its next saving throw."
- Does "ally" mean only OTHER creatures, or does the SC count as its own ally?
- If SC's own hits count: PTV fires on every SC multiattack hit → target has disadv on next save (relevant for Barrage DC 13 on following turn). This would make PTV consistently useful even in solo-SC formation.
- If "ally" requires another creature: PTV only fires in multi-NPC formations. In tank-wall, Rager must be within 30 ft of SC at time of hit → effectively only works if SC advances to mid-range.
- **Recommend: Clarify in .md. Suggest: "ally" means another allied creature (not the SC itself), and add note: "PTV is a multi-NPC passive — it functions in encounters with 2+ NPCs within 30 ft of each other."**
