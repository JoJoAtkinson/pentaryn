**FI-FC50-A — Beholder 0-damage (50th-FC-cycle carry)**
- Beholder took 0 damage again. Sabriel's only hit (15dmg) targeted STD-A, not the beholder. Bazgar missed both greataxe attacks (MISS 12 and 14 vs AC 16). No PC targeted the beholder directly.
- Structural cause: Fireball forces attention to thrall cluster; STDs are closer than beholder; beholder acts at init 18 and eliminates caster (Marwen) before Marwen's second action. The party never rotates priority to beholder before going down.
- This is the 50th-FC-cycle with confirmed 0-or-near-0 beholder damage in ~30+ recent runs. The fight reads as "NPCs delete PCs with no counterplay on the boss." For a feel issue (not balance), consider whether an encounter note should guide the DM to **bring the beholder in closer on R1** so Bazgar has a valid melee target without routing through STDs first. Not a spec change — a terrain/tactics note.

**FI-FC50-B — STD low-HP AR override fires correctly (POSITIVE confirmation)**
- STD-A at 10HP (below 20) fired AR despite stagger hold — dealt 12+13 to Bazgar and Sabriel respectively. This was decisive in setting up R2 TPK.
- The stagger exemption for low-HP STD produces meaningful pressure in the final round. Confirmed working as intended.

**FI-FC50-C — DD whiff on Sabriel CHA save (18% rate, third recent miss)**
- Sabriel saved DD (rolled nat-20 + CHA+3 = 23). 3 LA spent for zero effect.
- DC 16 vs CHA +3: failure rate 65% (~13/20 values). Success rate 35% (~7/20). This run hit the high end.
- After DD save success: beholder had 0 LA for remainder of R1, which limited reactive options but VS fired R2 anyway and ended the fight. The DD-whiff cost the beholder 3 LA but did not change the outcome.
- Feel: the variance is acceptable. DD is high-risk/high-reward. When it lands it strips smites; when it misses, the beholder loses an entire LA budget. Both outcomes feel real.

**FI-FC50-D — Marwen-first initiative order → Fireball → immediate Maw death**
