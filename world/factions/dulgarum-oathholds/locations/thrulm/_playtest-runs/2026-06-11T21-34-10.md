**FI-SCT6-53-A (carry confirm): Barrage-carrier-lowest-init dies before acting — 13th consecutive SCT6.**
SC-B was assigned Barrage per stagger (lowest init = 7), but Marwen (init 17) Fireballed SC-B dead before it could act. This is the 13th consecutive SCT6 cycle where Barrage was blocked. The stagger assigns Barrage to lowest-init, which is most likely to go after the wizard. MQ-SCT6-52-A recommended reassigning Barrage to highest-init SC (most likely to beat Marwen in initiative). This is a design decision — log as DESIGN DECISION with recommendation.

**FI-SCT6-53-B (new): CW-wasted-13th-consec — consider retiring SCT6 or rotating in a mixed formation.**
Call Weakness was used (SC-C → SC-A) but the target (SC-A) was Fireballed dead before it could benefit. CW has now been wasted in 13 consecutive shardcaller-only formations. The mechanic only gets to demonstrate value when: (a) at least two SCs survive into R2, and (b) the CW-receiving SC acts before dying. In shardcaller-only formations, Fireball routinely clears 2 of 3 SCs in R1 before CW delivers. CW should be tested in a mixed formation (SC + Rager). Recommend replacing this slice with a SC+Rager formation, or gating it to only fire when Marwen's init is ≤ the lowest-init SC's.

**FI-SCT6-53-C (new): Shard-Throw to-hit 15 exactly hits AC 15 — DM clarification note.**
In Round 2, SC-C rolled 15 on its second Throw vs Marwen's AC 15. 5e rule is "roll ≥ AC = hit." The sim initially marked this as MISS; correcting: it's a HIT. This is a recurring sim-fidelity error. DM note: roll equals AC is a hit. Not a spec bug; a sim-tracking reminder.

**FI-SCT6-53-D (structural-confirm): Fireball-decisive-13th-consec — pattern unchanged from #52.**
Every SCT6 run ends within 2-3 rounds with Marwen's Fireball as the decisive action. 8d6=40 this cycle (above average); even at 28 average, SC-B fails the DC 14 save >50% of the time (DEX +2 only, no proficiency) and takes full 28 to their 33 HP — surviving at 5. The SC's low DEX save (+2 raw) combined with Marwen's high Fireball damage makes the encounter consistently lopsided. This is a structural finding — not a bug, but worth noting to the DM: shardcallers die too easily to AOE for a 3-encounter formation.

**FI-SCT6-53-E (carry from #52 — MQ-SCT6-52-A): Barrage should be reassigned to highest-init SC.**
Current stagger: "lowest-init SC fires Barrage." This consistently means Barrage fires after Marwen's Fireball clears the formation. Recommended fix: assign Barrage to the HIGHEST-init SC to maximize the chance of Barrage landing before Marwen acts. DM decision required — this changes formation doctrine.
