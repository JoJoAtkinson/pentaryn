### Mechanical questions

- **MQ-80 (NEW):** When the Rager is Slowed and chooses its action (1 attack), it also loses its bonus action. But the Taunt text says "bonus action is available each turn." Under 5e Slow, the creature retains both action and bonus action — it just must choose one to use (per the spell: "the creature must spend its action or bonus action on such a[n action]" — actually, Slow says the creature can use only ONE action or ONE bonus action per turn, forfeiting the rest). So Taunt IS technically unavailable when the Rager chose its action. Checklist item 4 fix (FI-192 auto-applied) addresses this. No further ruling needed, but confirm with user that the 5e Slow rule is being applied correctly (it restricts to 1 action-or-bonus-action total, not just 1 action).

- **MQ-81 (NEW):** The Slow spell affects a 40-ft cube. The Rager (30 ft from party) and SC (85 ft from party) cannot both be caught in the same Slow AoE without centering the 40-ft cube somewhere between them (midpoint ~57 ft from party). At 57 ft, the edge reaches 37 ft from party (catches Rager at 30 ft) and 77 ft from party (does NOT reach SC at 85 ft). This cycle's Slow should only have targeted the Rager — the SC was outside the 20-ft radius. The SC passing the save was moot, but the simulation incorrectly offered the SC a save. No mechanical impact this cycle, but future cycles should not target SC with Slow unless the SC has repositioned within 20 ft of the Slow center.
