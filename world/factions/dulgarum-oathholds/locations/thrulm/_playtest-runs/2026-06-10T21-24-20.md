### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-SCT6-50-A: PTV EXPRESSED this cycle (first productive tracking in 50 SCT cycles)**
- Marwen's Barrage save applied PTV disadv (d20 dis = 5 vs would-have-been 6, save total 9 vs 10 — same FAIL outcome either way). PTV was mechanically correct.
- Finding: PTV is reliably triggering (SCs accumulate hits before Barrage fires) but rarely changes outcomes because the DC 13 save sits at a comfortable margin for low-Dex casters. PTV would be decisive vs a +0 Dex target at DC 13 — the party lacks one in current composition.
- Overhead cost: DM must track which PCs have disadv on their next save after every SC hit. In a 3-SC team this is 2–4 triggers per round.
- **RECOMMENDATION:** PTV is working correctly. The marginal feel impact is a party-composition artifact, not a design flaw. No change needed.

**FI-SCT6-50-B: PYRRHIC VICTORY R6 — first non-R2 SCT outcome in 10 consecutive cycles (POSITIVE)**
- Removing Fireball (L3 expended pre-encounter) caused a 6-round fight with 2 PCs downed.
- Without instant-win AoE, the SCs' sustained Shard-Throw spam and Barrage recharge pressure are genuinely threatening.
- Marwen was essentially eliminated by Turn 1 focus-fire + Barrage (8 HP after R1). SC-C finished her R2.
- Sabriel survived to R4 under combined fire but fell to accumulated Shard-Throw hits (no healing this fight).
- **CONFIRMED: shardcaller formation is well-calibrated against AoE-restricted parties. The VICTORY R2 / Fireball pattern in prior cycles is a Marwen-has-L3-slots artifact, not an encounter design flaw.**

**FI-SCT6-50-C: SIM ERROR — TR does not block Barrage (must fix in future sims)**
- In R3+, both SCs used TR (bonus) + Multiattack (main). Per the .md (and FIX-SCT6-45-A), TR is a bonus action and Barrage is the main action — these are NOT mutually exclusive.
- SC-C had Barrage available from R1 onward and was using TR every round; the correct play is TR (bonus) + Barrage (main) in a party corridor formation.
- **If SC-C had correctly fired Barrage in R3 and R4, estimated additional damage: 2× ~10–14 piercing = ~20–28 extra. This would likely have killed Bazgar before R6.**
- **Corrected outcome estimate: TPK or near-TPK, not Pyrrhic Victory.**
- This is a cron sim error, not an encounter design bug. The .md correctly describes TR+Barrage compatibility. Future cron sims must fix the bonus-action vs main-action logic.

**FI-SCT6-50-D: CW advantage granted SC-C in R1 — never consumed (10th-SCT6 CW-wasted carry)**
- SC-A correctly CW'd SC-C in R1 per stagger assignment.
- SC-C HAD advantage for its R1 Multiattack but the sim failed to apply it (bug: `cw_adv_for` indexed by recipient was not checked for NPC's own rolls).
- Separately: SC-A died before its R2 turn. The CW effect "expires end of the shardcaller's next turn" left an unresolved state. Fixed in .md (MQ-SCT6-50-A note added).
- Underlying pattern: CW advantage on a high-initiative SC that is immediately killed is wasted, because: (a) the recipient SC is melee-threatened from R3+ and uses TR bonus every turn, blocking CW bonus, AND (b) the "already acted" guard (MQ-12) prevents productive CW spending later.
- **No fix needed.** CW on SC-C in R1 is the correct play per FI-SCT-34-A; it's just frequently defeated by fight dynamics (SC-A dies R1, leaving SC-C's CW adv in an indeterminate state).
