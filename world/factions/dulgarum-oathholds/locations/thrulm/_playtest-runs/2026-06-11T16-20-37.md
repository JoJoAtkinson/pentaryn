### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-SW1-42-A — AR output variance (near-floor this run)**
Both PCs saved against STD-A's Ancient Resonance in R1 (Bazgar 15/DC14, Marwen 14/DC14 — both barely passed). The 2d10 roll was 4, giving 2 necrotic each after halving. This is the near-minimum possible AR output. Variance, not a design issue.

**FI-SW1-42-B [carry from FI-SW1-40-A, FI-SW1-41-A] — UF never expressed, 42nd consecutive SW1**
Unstable Form correctly triggered on STD-B (took 28 fire damage last round ≥ 10 threshold). STD-B went into R2 with UF active. However, STD-B died to Sabriel's smite before STD-B's turn in R2. UF advantage on attacks was never expressed. This is the 42nd consecutive shrine-wedge cycle in which Unstable Form either (a) does not trigger, (b) triggers but the STD dies before acting, or (c) triggers but below-20HP forces AR recklessly which wastes the advantage. **UF has never been observed executing in any SW1 playtest run.**

**FI-SW1-42-C — Stagger mechanic confirmed correct (positive)**
Stagger held STD-B correctly in R1 (both had AR at round start; STD-B lower initiative held). This prevented a potential double-AR opener. STD-B's AR was preserved going into R2 but became irrelevant as STD-B died to Sabriel's smite before acting. Stagger logic functioning as designed.

**FI-SW1-42-D — Altar zone suppression confirmed 42nd SW1 cycle (positive)**
Fire vulnerability suppression worked correctly. Fireball dealt 28 normal (not 56 doubled) to each STD. STD-A, already softened to 14 HP, died to Fireball regardless — suppression had no effect on the outcome for STD-A. For STD-B at full 45 HP: suppression allowed it to survive Fireball at 17 HP rather than dying at -11 HP, extending the fight by one round and costing Sabriel a smite. The mechanic is creating its intended tactical texture.

**DD-SW1-42-A [DESIGN DECISION — carry from FI-SW1-40-A, FI-SW1-41-A] — UF structural non-expression in 2×STD slice**
42 runs of shrine-wedge, zero observed UF attack-advantage expressions. The pattern is structural: with a level-5 party, STDs don't survive long enough above 20 HP to both (a) take 10+ damage in one round and (b) still be above 20 HP when their next turn comes. Fireball alone ensures any STD it hits will be at ≤17 HP on the first roll (since 28/2 = 14, and full HP is 45 → Fireball leaving 17 HP < 20 HP threshold immediately). Recommend: slice configuration should add a third STD, or impose a Fireball restriction variant (Marwen is out of 3rd level slots) to stress-test UF execution in a meaningful context.
