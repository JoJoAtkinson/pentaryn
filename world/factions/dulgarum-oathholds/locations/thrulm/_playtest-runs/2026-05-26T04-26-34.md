### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-92 (NEW — SIM BUG, not encounter spec):** First run attempt of this cycle had Beholder missing `"alive": True` flag in NPC dict. Beholder was skipped every turn; produced false "VICTORY R2" output (all escorts dead, beholder 110/110 HP never acted). Fixed in corrected run. This is a harness issue only — no encounter spec, .md, or DB change needed. Logged for awareness.

**FI-93 (NEW — FEEL/DESIGN):** Void Scream recharged on d6=6 in Round 2 (16.7% probability). VS fired twice: R1 (standard opening) and R2 (lucky recharge). Result: Sabriel dropped from 18 HP by VE, then killed by R2 VS. Party took 0 actions beyond Sabriel's R1 attack. **This is the first cycle where VS fires twice in R1-2.** Expected frequency: ~1 in 6 final-confrontation fights. When it fires twice in R1-2, the encounter is over by Round 2 with zero party counterplay — not even the "earned death" feel the design intends. **Recommendation for DM:** If VS recharges in R2 and the fight is already decided, consider NOT firing it — save VS as a narrative weapon for a desperate moment in R3+. This is a DM preference note, not a spec change. Log for human review.

**FI-94 (NEW — pattern):** Beholder took 0 HP damage across 2 rounds — eighth consecutive final-confrontation cycle where the beholder takes no meaningful damage from the party. In cycles where Marwen fires Fireball (FI-60, FI-91), the beholder takes 34 HP. In cycles where Marwen is downed before her turn (this cycle: initiative order placed her at init 6, beholder at init 13), the party deals nothing to the beholder. The encounter's feel varies wildly based on whether Marwen acts before or after the beholder. When she acts first: ~34 HP damage and some tension. When she acts after: complete shutout. **Pattern for human:** FC's feel is determined more by Marwen's initiative roll than by any PC decision-making.

**DD-33 (8th FC cycle confirm — unresolved):** STB init 20 + STA init 19 → both shrine-touched acted before entire party (Bazgar init 11, Marwen init 6, Sabriel init 2 this cycle). Lair also fired before all PCs. Pre-PC phase dealt: lair+AR to Bazgar (23 dmg), AR to Marwen (10 dmg), AR to Sabriel (10 dmg) — before a single PC action. Combined with VS (33 psychic), party was in "down or near-down" state before any PC turn. Eight cycles and the initiative-dominance issue remains the highest-priority unresolved design question. **Human intervention required.**

**MQ-46 (NEW — sim harness, low priority):** Sim sort key places Lair action before STB at init 20 (both tied). Per RAW, lair actions fire at init 20 LOSING TIES — NPCs with init 20 should act before lair. Low impact (same total damage, different event order). Fix: in sort key, give Lair a tie-break value that puts it after any NPC combatant with the same initiative. Harness-only; no spec change.
