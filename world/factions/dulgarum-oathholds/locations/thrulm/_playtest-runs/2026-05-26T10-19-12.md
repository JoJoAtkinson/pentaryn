### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-120 (NEW — POSITIVE): First CW-on-Rager R1 fires in any tank-wall cycle (3rd cycle).**  
SC init 14 > Rager init 9 → SC acts before Rager charges to melee, CW is within 30 ft range. **Partial DD-41 resolution confirmed: CW is viable R1 in ~50% of tank-wall runs (when SC wins the SC-vs-Rager initiative sub-contest, probability ≈ 50%).** CW advantage was applied to Rager's first attack — Rager hit Bazgar for 6 (to-hit 19, just cleared AC 18 regardless of advantage). The CW advantage didn't change the outcome this cycle (hit either way), but the mechanic is now reachable. **This is the first evidence that the CW + Taunt combo can coexist in a single round at this slice.** DD-41 is not fixed — the R1 CW window is initiative-gated — but the mechanic is no longer structurally 0/N.

**FI-121 (NEW — MILESTONE): Berserk fires R2 — first Berserk in 3 tank-wall cycles.**  
Rager held position after R1 charge, 3 PCs closed in. Berserk eligible and fired. Damage output: 1/3 hits (Marwen AC15, 5 slashing). Bazgar (AC18) and Sabriel (AC19) both avoided. **Finding: Berserk CAN trigger in the tank-wall slice when fight extends past R1 — but output is low vs high-AC party. The Rager's "I hit everything in range" design intent fires correctly; however, with AC18/19 on 2 of 3 PCs, expected Berserk damage ≈ (40% × 8) + (55% × 8) + (30% × 8) ≈ 10 slashing per Berserk use. Low but nonzero.** DD-26 (raise Rager HP) is still needed to make Berserk a recurring mechanic rather than a once-in-3-cycles event.

**FI-122 (NEW — sim harness): CW on Rager R2 at 3/52 HP violates ≤20 HP guard.**  
SC used Call Weakness on Rager in R2 despite Rager having 3/52 HP — clearly below the ≤20 HP exclusion guard in the SC tactics ("Never CW an ally at ≤20 HP"). CW was wasted (Rager died before acting again after Berserk). **Sim harness bug — the HP guard is not checked before dispatching CW.** See MQ-57.

**FI-123 (3rd tank-wall confirm — FI-75 pattern): Sabriel untouched 44/44 HP.**  
Third consecutive tank-wall cycle with Sabriel at full HP. She is structurally not targeted: Rager focuses Bazgar (closest melee); SC focuses Marwen (caster). Berserk R2 attempted all 3 PCs but missed Sabriel (AC19, 6 vs 19). This is structural, not lucky — Sabriel's 19 AC combined with not being primary target keeps her clean. **If this slice is meant to stress the divine martial, the Rager needs a targeting note: "If Sabriel is the closest melee or has the highest HP among PCs engaged with the Rager, prioritize her for multiattack — the Rager adapts to perceived threat."**

**FI-124 (NEW — POSITIVE): SC Barrage R1 threatens Marwen for first time in tank-wall slice.**  
In prior 2 cycles, Marwen was never hit in R1 in the tank-wall slice (Barrage either missed or didn't fire). This cycle: SC init 14, Barrage fires before Marwen acts — Marwen takes 12 piercing, enters R2 at 20/32 HP. Combined with Berserk hit R2 (5 slashing), Marwen ends at 15/32 — the lowest she has ever been in a tank-wall encounter. **The SC corridor-geometry Barrage R1 is working as intended when SC wins initiative. This is the correct feel for a back-line disruptor: soften the caster before she can Fireball.**

**DD-24 (3rd tank-wall confirm): Taunt + Fireball bypass — Marwen taunted, casts Fireball anyway.**  
Same as prior two cycles. Taunt's "disadv on attack rolls vs non-Rager" does not apply to save-based spells. Marwen's Fireball hit both NPCs for 12+25 fire regardless of taunted status. Rager.md already documents this limitation. No change needed — confirmed structural.

**DD-26 (continued): Fight ends R2 even with Berserk firing and SC acting first.**  
Bazgar's R1 burst (Precision Attack 22 + smite 7 = 29 on swing 1, 15 on swing 2 = 37 total in R1!) reduces Rager to 3/52 HP. Rager barely survives to fire one Berserk then dies. **The core issue is Bazgar's burst output (Precision Attack + smite = ~37 R1) vs Rager HP (52). With 70 HP (DD-26 recommendation), Rager enters R2 at 33 HP — survives Bazgar's followup swing, fires Berserk, requires R3 to kill. Still pending human sign-off.**

**DD-41 (4th tank-wall confirm — NUANCED): CW range barrier is initiative-conditional, not absolute.**  
When SC wins initiative vs Rager (~50% probability), CW fires R1 before Rager charges. When SC loses (~50%), CW is stranded all fight. The "CW never fires" across prior cycles was bad luck (SC lost init every time). This cycle validates the design: CW is a real mechanic when initiative favors SC. **Recommendation: add a note to SC tactics — "CW on Rager in tank-wall formation is ONLY viable R1 when SC acts before Rager in initiative. Check init order at the top of R1; if SC acted before Rager, CW is already spent correctly. Do not retry R2."**

### Mechanical questions

**MQ-57 (NEW — sim harness): CW ≤20 HP guard not enforced.**  
SC dispatched CW on Rager at 3/52 HP, violating the ≤20 HP guard in tactics. Harness fix: add `if npcs[ally]["hp"] > 20` check before CW dispatch. Low impact this cycle (fight outcome unchanged), but will produce wasted-CW artifacts in future high-HP-loss fights.

**MQ-58 (NEW — sim harness): CW advantage not applied to Rager's attack roll.**  
SC called CW on Rager R1. `cw_advantage["Rager"] = True` set, but the Rager's multiattack block used `d20(4)` without reading `cw_advantage`. CW advantage should manifest as `max(d20(), d20()) + 4` on the first attack. In this cycle, the hit was 19 vs AC18 (would have hit either way), so zero mechanical impact. Harness fix: check and consume `cw_advantage` in NPC attack block.
