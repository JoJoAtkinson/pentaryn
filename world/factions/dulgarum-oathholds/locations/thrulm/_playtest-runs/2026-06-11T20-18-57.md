## Findings

### Bugs auto-fixed
- **FIX-SR5-51-A (beholder-thrulm/maw rider wording):** DB `rider_on_hit` read "If the target is grappled by the beholder, this attack is a critical hit instead." — ambiguous; could be read as auto-hit + crit, bypassing the to-hit roll entirely. Rewrote to: "If the target is grappled by the beholder AND this attack hits, it scores a critical hit (damage dice doubled)." No mechanical change if DM reads correctly; prevents LLM-DM from skipping the attack roll.

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-SR5-51-A (Taunt-moot, 11th consecutive SR5 cycle):** Taunt correctly skipped 16× per existing FI-SR5-46-A tactics note. The note is working. However, this is now the **11th consecutive SR5 cycle** — the FI-SR5-50-A CRITICAL flag to retire this slice from the rotation carries forward with maximum urgency. No further mechanical value is being extracted from additional SR5 runs; Taunt-moot is fully confirmed and documented. **Recommend the human retire slice #5 from the rotation and replace with a mixed formation (Rager + Shardcaller, or Rager + Beholder) that exercises Taunt meaningfully.**

- **FI-SR5-51-B (Berserk working, but all-miss R1):** All 3 ragers used Berserk in R1 — and ALL MISSED every target. Combined 9 attacks, 0 hits. This is within variance (Berserk to-hit roll of +4 vs AC 18/15/19 means ~30–47% hit chance per swing) but feels anti-climactic on the first round. Marwen's Fireball had already done meaningful damage, but the rager counterattack whiffed entirely. Feel note: R1-Berserk-all-miss is a ~5% probability event for a 3×3 salvo and may simply be an unlucky seed. Not a balance issue.

- **FI-SR5-51-C (Aggro mechanic working, 2× observed):** Rager-A pivoted to Marwen in R2 (aggro from Marwen's Fire Bolt), Rager-B pivoted to Bazgar in R5 (aggro from Bazgar's Action Surge hits). Both redirected correctly with advantage on first attack. Mechanic is functioning and adding tactical texture.

- **FI-SR5-51-D (Berserk 3-line cap — 1-PC scenario, R6):** In R6, only Sabriel remained alive. Rager-C had Berserk recharged but correctly fell back to Multiattack (only 1 PC in reach). DB output still shows 3 attack lines for Berserk output — the DM/sim must cap at actual targets. This has been noted before (MQ-SR5 recurring). Not a bug; documented as accepted design.

- **FI-SR5-51-E (Death order feel — POSITIVE):** Marwen (lowest HP, AC 15) died R3, Bazgar (fighter) died R5, Sabriel (highest AC, divine healer) lasted to R7. Death order felt earned — the low-AC caster fell to concentrated rager pressure, the frontliner was ground down by multiple ragers once Marwen was gone. Sabriel's superior AC (19 vs rager to-hit +4 = 25% hit rate) bought significant survival time. Healing Word on Marwen (R3) prolonged the fight by 1 round but Rager-A's recharged Berserk overwhelmed the top-up.
