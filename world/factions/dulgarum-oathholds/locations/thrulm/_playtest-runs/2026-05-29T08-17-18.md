## Findings

### Bugs auto-fixed

- **OBR melee-range clarification (shrine-touched-derro.md):** The Oath-Breaking Retaliation reaction text said "auto-fires when hit; one Shrine-Axe counter-swing at the attacker" without specifying range. During the playtest, Fireball (ranged AoE) triggered OBR but the derro obviously cannot reach a distant caster with a melee swing. Added explicit text: "OBR does NOT fire against ranged attackers, ranged-spell attackers, or area-effect damage (Fireball, etc.) — only fires against attackers within melee reach (5 ft)." This is a clarification only; no change to OBR on-hit mechanic vs. melee attackers.
  - Diff: Reaction priority bullet in `shrine-touched-derro.md` extended with melee-range restriction and AoE exclusion.

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **[FI-SW-8-A] VICTORY in 2 rounds — 8th SW cycle:** Party wins again in 2 rounds with near-full HP (Bazgar 19, Sabriel 28; only Marwen genuinely threatened at 9 HP). This is the 8th shrine-wedge cycle: the party has won 5 of 8, with TPK cycles traceable to STD-A winning initiative and AR firing R1 before the party acts (FI-159). In this cycle the party won initiative decisively (16/11/7 vs 2/1) and front-loaded Bazgar Action Surge + Fireball + Sabriel smite. **The encounter outcome is near-deterministically linked to initiative.** Recommendation: this is a confirmed pattern across 8 cycles. Log for human review — no auto-fix.

- **[FI-SW-8-B] Unstable Form never fired in meaningful way (8th SW cycle confirm):** Both STDs had UF active entering R2 (STD-A 18 dmg R1 > threshold; STD-B 12 dmg R1 > threshold). However, STD-A was killed by Bazgar before its R2 turn, and STD-B was killed by Sabriel before STD-B's R2 turn. UF has been active on STDs entering R2 in 5 of 8 cycles and has fired productively 0 times in this slice (UF-generated attacks always pre-empted by party kill order). Consider: UF is a strong mechanic on paper but structurally unreachable in this 2-NPC slice because the party correctly focuses one STD at a time. More NPCs or higher STD HP might let UF matter.

- **[FI-SW-8-C] Fireball vulnerability suppression working as designed — fifth positive confirm:** Marwen's Fireball dealt 24 fire; both STDs saved (Dex +5), taking only 12 each instead of 48+ doubled. The altar zone suppression correctly reduces Fireball's effectiveness and creates the intended tactical decision (pull STDs out vs. commit to altar zone). Party note: Marwen should consider non-fire AoE while STDs are in altar zone. This is a POSITIVE design note — confirm per FI-189 pattern.

- **[FI-SW-8-D] Concentration check borderline R1:** Marwen failed AR save, taking 15+1 = 16 necrotic+psychic. Con check DC = max(10, half of 16 = 8) = 10. Marwen passed on exactly 10 (Con +2, rolled 8). This was one point from losing any active concentration spell. The AR → concentration-loss pressure is real and correctly threatening for a wizard. Positive design note.
