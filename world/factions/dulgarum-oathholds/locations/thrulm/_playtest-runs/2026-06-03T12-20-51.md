## Findings

### Bugs auto-fixed
- **SIM-BEL3-A (sim harness, not DB)**: `void_eruption` was called twice per lair-action trigger in the sim script — once in the generic `roll_npc(lair_choice)` block, then again inside the `elif lair_choice == "void_eruption"` branch. Second call's damage roll was used for saves; first was discarded. No DB fix needed; harness note only.

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-BEL3-A (design note)**: Beholder Dex+8 vs Fireball DC15 — beholder saved both times (rolls 28, 26 vs DC15). Both L3 Fireballs dealt only half damage: 13+17=30 total on beholder. Fireball is correct as a thrall-clearance spell (all 3 thralls dead R1-R2) but the beholder is effectively Fireball-resistant. No change recommended — this is the CR mismatch working correctly. Flag for DM: Marwen should not burn L3 slots on beholder; target thralls exclusively.

- **FI-BEL3-B (feel)**: Void Scream R2 delivered 31+32+27=90 total psychic in one action (3 failed saves). Bazgar and Marwen dropped simultaneously; Sabriel left at 17 HP. The fear rider never manifested in play — both targets were unconscious before their next turns. VS's most durable mechanic (frightened = disadvantage for 2-3 rounds) was bypassed entirely because the kills were immediate. The fight resolved fast (6 rounds) but the fear rider was invisible. Consider: DM should delay VS until after at least 1-2 rounds of melee exchange so the frightened condition has time to be expressed.

- **FI-BEL3-C (design clarification needed)**: DD vs FI-125 (LoH pressure) conflict confirmed live. Beholder spent all 3 LA on DD every turn after Sabriel's, leaving zero for Tentacle pressure. FI-125 says: if Sabriel < 30 HP and just used LoH, spend 1 LA Tentacle to pressure her. But DD costs 3 LA (whole budget). In this run Sabriel was at 17 HP from R3 onward and cycling LoH — exactly the FI-125 scenario — but DD consumed every LA and Tentacle pressure never fired. RECOMMEND: add explicit tiebreak — if Sabriel is the sole surviving PC AND < 30 HP AND LoH pool is not yet exhausted, drop DD and use Void Ray (2 LA) + Tentacle (1 LA) instead. DD on the only living target has lower marginal value than ensuring that target stays under death threat.

- **FI-BEL3-D (feel)**: Thrall hand axe +1 never hit in the 2 turns T2/T3 survived (rolled 10, 9 — needed 15+ for AC15). Thralls contributed zero damage. They are intended as pacing/distraction fodder and the Fireball kill was correct, but the two rounds they were alive they were pure tableau. Whether their +1 to-hit is intentionally sub-par (dominated, incomplete action) or accidentally too weak is a design question. Current spec narration says "puppeted — the body remembers how to hold an axe but not why" which supports the weak-to-hit intent. No change; log for awareness.

- **FI-BEL3-E (sim logic, not spec)**: Lair rotation violated FI-129 — void_eruption fired 5 consecutive rounds (R2-R6). Sim code used a simple modular condition rather than a rotation-capped counter. No DB fix; DM reminder: rotate lair actions per FI-129 (max 2 consecutive of any one type). `unstable_ground` was completely unused after R1 despite Sabriel being the only standing target in R3+; `manifest_thralls` was never triggered.

### Mechanical questions

- **MQ-BEL3-A**: Bazgar was grappled at 0 HP (R3 TL1 hit first, grapple attached, then knocked to 0 within the same action). Per MQ-FC14-A in beholder.md: unconscious ends grappled immediately. Sim tracked "grappled" condition on Bazgar at 0 HP — this is incorrect and is a harness tracking bug (not a spec bug). At table: declare grapple end immediately on knock-to-zero, before Maw fires, so Maw must retarget. This came up every time Sabriel revived Bazgar into a grappled state — he woke up grappled, which is also wrong (grapple ends at 0 HP → if revived at >0, grapple has already ended). DM should clear grappled condition when target drops.

- **MQ-BEL3-B**: Antireality (reaction) fired correctly in R3 — declared after TL2 roll (12 vs AC18), raised AC to 20, turned a hit into a miss. Trigger check: was 12 within AC+2=20? Yes (12 ≤ 20), but DD-43 says "only trigger if total ≤ AC+2" — 12 ≤ 20, correct. Result: miss. Antireality working correctly as specified.
