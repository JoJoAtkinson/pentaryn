### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-SR9-A** (NEW): Sabriel AC19 functionally invulnerable to ragers. R3 landed 1 of 8 attacks vs AC19 Sabriel over 4 dedicated rounds (R6–R8 + R7 partial). The rager's +4 attack bonus against AC19 is a 30% hit rate unmodified, ~35% with ME. Once Marwen/Bazgar were down, R3 was stuck in a slow-death loop against the armored divine martial. The 1v1 rager-vs-paladin lasted 3 rounds before a smite ended it. DESIGN NOTE: no coaching note in .md guides the rager to abandon the fight or shift strategy against very-high-AC targets. Add note: "Against AC19+ targets, Berserk + ME (+1) give ~57% double-miss rate per pair — do not abandon the attack, but note that Taunt is the rager's highest-value action once high-AC targets are the only ones standing (to compound disadvantage)."

- **FI-SR9-B** (MQ-SR8-C 9th-cycle confirm): R3 never met the Berserk 2+ PCs condition for the entire 8-round fight. Column geometry placed R3 adjacent to Bazgar only; Berserk was never eligible. Berserk was also only used once by R2 in R2 and it missed both targets (AC18, AC19). The .md should add: "In split-approach column formations, expect 1 of 3 ragers to have only 1 PC in reach — Berserk unavailable for that rager. Multiattack instead."

- **FI-SR9-C** (FI-SRR18-E 9th confirm): Fireball 1/3 fail (R2+R3 saved half → 39 HP entering R2). Fight went 8 rounds vs typical 3-4 when Fireball hits all 3. The solo-rager-rush fight length is structurally high-variance based on Fireball penetration. When R1 dies and two ragers survive at half HP, the party needs 4-5 more rounds of attrition to finish. This is appropriate tension.

- **FI-SR9-D** (MQ-SR8-B 9th confirm): Madness Endurance (+1 to-hit) was active on R2 and R3 in R2–R4, meaningfully combined with Aggro Mark advantage in several attack windows. Easy-to-forget tracking burden at-table. 9th-cycle confirm; consider adding DM shorthand to .md checklist.

- **FI-SR9-E** (NEW): Sabriel LoH pool unused (25 pts). Marwen died at -4 HP — LoH would have revived her to 1 HP if used before she died. But Sabriel's initiative (13) was after Marwen in the round Marwen died (R3 killed Marwen on Marwen's init). Sabriel could not revive Marwen until after R3 acted. In this run, Sabriel chose to continue attacking rather than use LoH. The LoH decision point is unclear in the .md (no guidance on revival priority in rager-only fights). Mild feel flag: 25 pts of healing wasted even in a 8-round fight.
