## Findings

### Bugs auto-fixed

These are substantially different (5d8 avg=22.5 vs 6d10 avg=33). Updated `.md` checklist to "6d10 psychic" to match the DB. Low-risk authoring alignment — no DB or mechanical change.

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-35 (NEW — POSITIVE): VS R1 wipe confirmed 4th consecutive empty-void cycle.** Beholder at 30 ft altitude fires VS immediately on its first turn (PCs at altar base = within 30 ft). 35 psychic (sim value; dispatch-authoritative was 46) killed Marwen outright, dropped Bazgar to 28%, Sabriel to 20%. All three frightened. Fight was decided by one action on the beholder's first turn, every cycle. This is narratively powerful (the void-creature's signature move feels overwhelming) but there is zero counterplay window. **Log only — this is expected for a level-5 party vs CR 13.**

- **FI-36 (NEW): Main action dead zone R2-R3 confirmed again (FI-3 pattern, 3rd empty-void cycle occurrence).** After VS fires, beholder held main action for R2 and R3. Root cause in this cycle: primary DR target (Marwen) was killed by VS before DR could retarget. At-table DM should retarget to Sabriel per `.md` retarget note ("next highest caster or divine martial"). If DR had fired R2 at Sabriel (9 HP, +6 to-hit vs AC 19 = ~40% hit), a hit would have been 10d8 = ~45 force — certain death and the disintegration rider. **FI-3 is still unfixed (requires human sign-off on intended below-60-HP behavior). Recommend at minimum adding explicit retarget logic to the "main action after VS fires" case: "If VS fires R1 and kills the primary DR target, immediately retarget DR to next living creature R2 rather than holding."** This is authoring-only and low-risk.

- **FI-37 (NEW): Legendary action stranded at altitude — 1 LA unused each round.** Void Ray costs 2 LA, leaving 1 LA. At 30 ft altitude: Tentacle (1 LA) requires 10 ft melee reach = beholder must descend to ground level. Legendary Move (1 LA) = descend to 0 ft, exposing to melee. Both options unfavorable. Across R2 and R3, 2× 1 LA were stranded per round. **Design question:** Should there be a 1-LA legendary option at range? Options: (a) add "Tentacle Lash (1 action) — upgrade range to 60 ft at legendary tier" (thematic reach extension); (b) add "Void Whisper (1 action) — psychic cantrip, target makes DC 16 Int save or has disadvantage on next check until end of its turn"; (c) accept stranded LA as correct (beholder is conservative with actions at altitude). **Log for human review.**

- **Empty-void negotiation feel (POSITIVE):** The pre-combat negotiation is the most distinct narrative moment in the entire thrulm encounter. The beholder's responses (cold, amused, alien patience) fit the `.md` description well. The trigger (Sabriel touching her holy symbol) is plausible and gives the party one clear "moment they could have avoided this." Consider adding one explicit DM prompt to `.md` Interaction Notes: "Beholder will engage in negotiation for up to 1 round (30 seconds) before moving to combat position. Use this time for the party to learn their options — they cannot actually satisfy it without offering something specific to the sealed god." **No .md change needed; log for human awareness.**

### Mechanical questions

- **MQ-25 (NEW — sim harness): Dispatch double-roll in AOE actions.** In this run, `void_scream` dispatch printed "46 psychic" (its own internal dice), but the sim then re-rolled 6d10=35. Applied 35 (not 46) to all PCs. At authoritative 46 damage, Bazgar would have been at 3/49 HP after R1 (instead of 14/49), making the Void Ray kill R2 certain even on a save. The correct approach (per MQ-21 recommendation) is: call dispatch for narration/output, take the damage printed by dispatch as the authoritative value. Fix sim harness: parse printed damage from dispatch output rather than re-rolling. Out of blast radius.

- **MQ-26 (NEW): VS range boundary in negotiation positioning.** Beholder is 30 ft above altar; altar is 40 ft from threshold. PCs at threshold are sqrt(40²+30²)=50 ft from beholder — OUTSIDE VS range. PCs who advance to the altar base for negotiation are exactly 30 ft (vertical only) from the beholder — on the boundary. The sim treated all PCs as within range (they advanced for negotiation), which is plausible but not modeled explicitly. At table: DM must confirm PC positions before firing VS. If PCs hang back at threshold, VS cannot reach without beholder descending. **Recommendation:** Add to `.md` VS tactics: "In the negotiation entry, PCs who advance to the altar base are at the VS boundary (30 ft vertical). If party hangs at threshold (40+ ft horizontal), beholder must descend to ≤30 ft horizontal or wait for PCs to advance." This links to DD-48 altitude gate. **Authoring-only, low-risk.**
