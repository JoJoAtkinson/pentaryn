### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-TW28-A (CW structural init-block, 28th-cycle confirm):** SC rolled init 6, Rager init 10 → Rager acts before SC every turn. CW rule requires target hasn't acted yet. CW never fires. The sim correctly printed "Hold CW (Rager already acted this round)" in R1 and TR preempted any R2 attempt. Prior logs show CW fired only 1 of 27 prior tank-wall cycles (FI-TW27-A, MQ-TW26-A). This is structural: when SC Dex+2 ties or trails Rager Dex−1 in initiative, CW is blocked every round. DESIGN DECISION: CW on the Rager remains locked unless SC wins initiative AND is within 30 ft. Recommend: (a) cap SC-to-Rager formation gap at ≤25 ft (reduces range-block in later rounds when Rager charges out) or (b) increase CW range to 60 ft so it can fire when Rager acts first next round (CW could work as advance-grant for R2 if SC acts first).
