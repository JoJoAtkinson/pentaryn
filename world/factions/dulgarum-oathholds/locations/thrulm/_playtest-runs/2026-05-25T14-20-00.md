**FI-73 (NEW, POSITIVE): MQ-27 CW fix validated — first mechanical outcome in 6 threshold-patrol cycles.**

- This is the first time the CW mechanic produced a measurable outcome in the threshold-patrol slice across 6 cycles. The MQ-27 fix (prefer highest-init DW) is working as intended.
- Caveat: 4 damage is low impact. The CW advantage is most valuable when the DW is in a strong attack position (multiple hits possible). In this fight, DW-B only had 1 attack with CW before its partner died. Root cause of low impact: only 1 DW alive at time of CW use.
- **No fix needed — positive signal. Track whether CW converts more consistently in cycles where both DWs survive to their turns.**
