### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-SRR12-A (harness bug):** Taunt "until end of [taunted creature's] next turn" — the sim's `taunt_log` resets at the **start of each round** rather than persisting to the taunted PC's next action. Concretely: R2 Taunted Marwen at the end of Round 1 (init 0); in Round 2, Marwen acts first (init 20) with the taunt still live — she should have disadvantage on Scorching Ray vs R1 (not R2). Harness cleared the log before Marwen acted. Possible fight-extension: without disadvantage, Marwen's roll 14+5=19 hit vs AC 16 finished R1 R2; with disadvantage she might have missed, leaving R1 alive and Bazgar (10hp) in serious danger. Not a spec bug — the taunt spec says "until end of next turn" correctly. DM coaching note: enforce the cross-round window at the table.

- **FI-SRR12-B (confirms FI-SRR11-C):** Berserk fired once this cycle, at **near-death (6hp)**, not proactively. The recharge 5-6 (33%) and the Fireball-first init pattern mean Berserk is effectively a death-thrash rather than a mid-fight escalation. Both R1 (2hp) and R3 (6hp) were crippled by Fireball before they could Berserk proactively. If Berserk is intended as a meaningful mid-fight tool, consider recharge 4+ (50%) or removing the "round 2" harness gate entirely. Flagged for human — balance decision.

- **FI-SRR12-C (POSITIVE):** R3's Berserk at 6hp — even though reactive — was the most vivid moment in the fight. Sweeping Marwen (12) and Sabriel (9) as a last act before dying gave the rager a distinct identity. The "death-spin" feel is compelling. If Berserk only fires reactively, the narration holds up. The question is whether the DM can rely on it as a mechanical contribution or it's purely flavor at this party level.

- **FI-SRR12-D (confirms FI-SRR10-D, 12th cycle):** Taunt DC 12 Cha vs a level-5 fighter (Bazgar, presumed Cha +1): 3/3 saves this cycle (rolls 20, 18, 14). DC 12 Cha structurally fails vs PCs with modest Cha. Taunt split (FI-31) worked perfectly — one rager per PC, no redundancy — but the save DC prevents mechanical effect on the toughest PC. Sabriel (divine martial, likely Cha +3 or higher) would be even more resistant. Recommend DC 13 minimum; consider Wisdom save (more thematic for a terrifying screaming derro, and Wis is typically lower for fighter/martial archetypes).

- **FI-SRR12-E (confirms FI-SRR11-A):** Fireball-before-ragers-act R1 pattern confirms 12th time (Marwen init 20 vs best rager init 9). Three CR-2 ragers never act in their prime state. This is not a spec bug but a fundamental encounter texture gap — the rager-only formation is fully neutered by a L3 Fireball before any rager swings. DM coaching: consider having one rager start adjacent to a PC (charge distance 0) so at least one Berserk or multiattack fires before Fireball, OR have the ragers not bunch.

### Mechanical questions
- **MQ-SRR12-A (new):** The Berserk output prints "Prereq: Rager cannot move on this turn. One Greataxe attack vs EACH enemy creature in reach" as visible inline text in the dispatch output. At the table this is useful DM coaching, but the prerequisite text appears in the middle of the result string where the player-facing narration is. Should the prerequisite be a comment or metadata field rather than inline narration? Low-priority formatting question.
- **MQ-SRR12-B (see FI-SRR12-A):** Taunt persistence across round boundaries — the harness clears it at round start; auth rule keeps it to "end of next turn." For a fast cron harness this is hard to fix cleanly (would need per-PC "taunt expires on actor X's next action" tracking). Logging for human awareness.
