### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-EV13-A — Hard-trigger DR opener unreliable (13th empty-void cycle confirm; MQ-EV10-A ongoing)**
- Roller authoritative: surprise-round DR to-hit 14 vs AC 15 = MISS. This is the third consecutive empty-void cycle where the surprise DR fails to land on Marwen (cycle 12: nat-1+6=7; cycle 11: rolled miss; cycle 13: 8+6=14). Base hit chance vs AC 15 = 60%. With 40% miss rate, the "negotiation-gone-wrong" opener is unreliable: miss → Marwen alive → completely different fight shape. Miss → hit is a binary outcome that determines whether the fight is meaningful or instantly decided.
- Previous recommendation (FI-EV12-A, logged cycle 12): consider VR warning-shot instead of DR as hard-trigger response — less binary, still threatening. DR would still be available for R1.
- **Log for human review; do not auto-fix.**

**FI-EV13-B — VS recharge-6 single-round kill shot (7th empty-void confirm; FI-EV12-C ongoing)**
- VS recharged on d6=6 in R2, fired, dealt 38 psychic — dropping Sabriel from 44 to 6 HP in one hit (fail). Lair void_eruption finished her (16 force, Dex fail). The sequence "VS + lair combo" killed Sabriel in one round without the beholder using any spell slots, LR charges, or even spending more than 1 LA.
- The VS rider (frightened 1 min) would have effectively eliminated Sabriel's offensive output for 2-3 turns even if she survived. The dual payload (damage + fear) makes VS the beholder's most decisive single action.
- Recharge-6-only means ~16% chance per round. In a sub-4-round fight, VS fires once or never. In cycles where it fires, it often ends the fight; in cycles where it doesn't, the beholder wins via attrition. This high-variance feels appropriate for a boss action but the DM coaching note (beholder is *patient* with VS, holds it for clustering) should reinforce that holding is intentional.
- **Log for human review; do not auto-fix.**

**FI-EV13-C — Healing spell backfire not tested (no slot-based heals attempted)**
- Sabriel was at 6 HP briefly before the lair eruption killed her. She never attempted a slot-based heal (no action available). The chamber backfire mechanic (healing spells deal necrotic instead) did not come up. The mechanic is most likely to surface if Sabriel survives VS and tries to Cure Wounds herself next turn — a situation worth noting in the scenario setup.
- **No action; log for awareness.**

**FI-EV13-D — Negotiation pacing confirmed correct (13th-cycle)**
- FI-NEW-EV5-B: 2 exchange minimum honoured. Probe (lower shaft question) correctly dismissed rather than attacked. Hard trigger (explicit shaft-investigation statement) correctly escalated to surprise DR. Beholder did not trigger on the vague inquiry — only the direct "we are going down that shaft regardless."
