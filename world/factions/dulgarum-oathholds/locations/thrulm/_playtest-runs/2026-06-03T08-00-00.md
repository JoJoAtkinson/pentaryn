## Findings

### Bugs auto-fixed
- **FIX-EV7-21-A**: Added disintegration + LoH ruling to Start-of-Turn checklist item #1 as a prominent inline note. Previously the rule was buried in the tactics section (FI-NEW-FC4-LOH / "Engaged by melee" sub-note). At-table DMs scan the checklist first; the rule needs to be there. Diff: appended "DISINTEGRATION RULING — announce immediately on a 0-HP hit: A creature reduced to 0 HP by DR is reduced to dust; LoH requires touch and cannot revive…" to checklist item 1.
- **FIX-EV7-21-B**: Added DD consecutive-fail escape hatch to Legendary priority section. After two consecutive rounds where DD was resisted (yielding zero LA output), the beholder should switch to VR(2) + TL(1). This cycle: 4 consecutive DD resists, 0 LA output total across 5 rounds. Rule already implied by "Below 30 HP: prefer Void Ray over Drain Divinity" but a higher-threshold trigger is needed before the beholder reaches 30 HP.

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-EV7-21-A VICTORY (anomalous) — 21st empty-void cycle**: First party victory in the empty-void slice. Root cause: DR missed 2/3 attempts (d20 = 6 and 2); DD resisted 4/4 times (Sabriel Cha +3 vs DC 16 succeeds on 13+ = 40%, probability of 4 in a row = 2.6%). Combined bad luck, not a spec problem. However: the party's burst damage output (Fireball half + Scorching Ray × 2 + two Divine Smites + Bazgar) regularly delivers ~70–80 damage in 3–4 rounds, which is enough to defeat the beholder when its action economy underperforms. **DESIGN DECISION for human review: the beholder's 110 HP pool may be slightly low for a fight where the primary win condition is party-attrition-via-LA-drain. Consider 120–130 HP or adding 1 level of Legendary Resistance (4/day) to improve consistency. But this is a balance change — DO NOT auto-fix.**

- **FI-EV7-21-B DD all-or-nothing economy (21st-cycle confirm)**: 12 legendary actions spent across 5 rounds; zero output (all 4 DD attempts resisted; sub-30 HP rounds should have switched to VR+TL). The escape hatch is now in the .md (FIX-EV7-21-B). This is the structural risk of a 3-LA-all-or-nothing spend: a single round of success (Sabriel loses a L2 slot) is +4 temp HP + disrupted healing — worthwhile. Four consecutive rounds of failure is catastrophic for the beholder's pressure window.

- **FI-EV7-21-C Antireality dead-letter (21st-cycle confirm)**: The +2 AC reaction never triggered this fight. Bazgar's hit rolls were 24, 26, 22 — all above the AC+2=19 threshold. DD-43 says "only trigger if total ≤ AC+2." Against a Fighter with +7 to-hit, the reaction is mechanically blocked except on very low roll-but-still-hit totals (18, 19 only). Over 21 cycles, Antireality fires perhaps 1 in 5 Bazgar hits. **DESIGN DECISION for human: consider relaxing DD-43 to "if total ≤ AC+4" (i.e., total ≤ 21) to make the reaction meaningful against +7-to-hit fighters, without making it fire on clean crits. Not auto-fixing — changes combat feel significantly.**

- **FI-EV7-21-D Simulation error allowed LoH revival of disintegrated Marwen**: The LoH revival in R4 (simulation) is incorrect per the .md. In authentic play, Marwen stays dust, Sabriel should attack the beholder instead. The R4 Ray of Frost crit (10 cold) that reduced the beholder to 2 HP was a simulation artefact — without it, beholder HP at start of R5 would be ~12, not 2. Authentic projection: Bazgar at disadvantage deals ~6–12 damage per round; Sabriel deals 10–25 per round. With beholder at 12 HP at R5 start, fight likely ends R5 (Sabriel one-shots) even without Marwen. Outcome remains VICTORY but margin is thinner. FIX-EV7-21-A (checklist note) is the remedy.
