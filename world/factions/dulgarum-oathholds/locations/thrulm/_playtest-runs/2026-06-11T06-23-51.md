## Findings

### Bugs auto-fixed
- **FIX-EV7-39-A**: Added LA timing clarification to `beholder-thrulm.md` under Legendary Actions: LA can only fire at end of another creature's turn — not during beholder's own action block. This prevents the recurring SIM-EV7-39-C error at the table. (LA timing was documented at the top of the block header "only at the end of another creature's turn" but lacked an explicit DM note about the consequence — e.g., that DD post-Sabriel exhausts all 3 LA, leaving zero for Bazgar/Marwen turns in R1.)

### Simulation divergences (harness issues, not spec changes)
- **SIM-EV7-39-A**: VR damage dispatch=24 force, sim re-rolled=32 force on Marwen. Dispatch authoritative (24). Marwen survives R1 with 8 HP, fires Fireball.
- **SIM-EV7-39-B**: Sabriel smite only checked on attack i=1. Hit occurred on i=2 (roll 27). L2 Smite ~13 radiant not applied. Beholder enters R2 with ~89 HP not 79 HP (sim underestimated beholder damage by ~10%).
- **SIM-EV7-39-C**: LA (VR on Marwen, Tentacle on Sabriel) fired within beholder's own R1 action block. Preempted Marwen's Fireball entirely. With correct timing, all 3 LA spent on DD post-Sabriel → 0 LA remain during beholder R1 turn → no VR possible until after beholder's R2 turn refresh.
- **SIM-EV7-39-D**: DR dispatch hit (18+6=24 vs AC18) vs sim miss (9+6=15). Dispatch authoritative: Bazgar DISINTEGRATED R3.

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-EV7-39-A (NEW)**: **Antireality protection window too narrow vs high-attack parties.** Against Sabriel and Bazgar (both +7 to hit vs AC 17), AR fires (+2 AC → 19) but only blocks rolls of exactly 10-11 (10% of hits, or ~5.5% of all attacks). In both R1 and R2, Sabriel rolled well above the threshold (27, 23). The reaction is consumed every hit opportunity but rarely changes the outcome. **Recommendation:** Consider increasing AR to +3 (AC 20) or +4 (AC 21) to widen the save window to 5-6 roll values (~25-30% of hits blocked). Or change AR to force a reroll at disadvantage. At current +2 vs a level-5 party with +7 attack, AR is functionally cosmetic.

- **FI-EV7-39-B (NEW)**: **Bazgar DISINTEGRATED (dispatch-auth) — dramatic and earned.** After LoH yoyo from Sabriel (R3: revived to 10 HP), beholder immediately DR'd Bazgar (dispatch HIT 18+6=24 vs AC18, 36 force → 0 HP = disintegrated). The sequence — revive → instant disintegrate — is the most dramatic kill available in the encounter and reads as intentional punishment for the LoH yoyo (FI-EV7-51-C pattern). This is **POSITIVE** feel: the beholder sees the revival and punishes it decisively. The body is gone — no follow-up revivify possible. This is the encounter fulfilling its design intent.

- **FI-EV7-39-C (CARRY)**: **Void-Feeding +1 AC/attack/damage absent from DB spec** (39th consecutive cycle applying manually). The +1 bonus from Void-Feeding is never in any dispatch output. Every cycle the DM manually adds +1 to-hit and +1 damage for every beholder attack while at the altar. This creates a permanent gap between dispatch output and real table behavior. **Recommendation:** bake the +1 into each action spec DB row, or add a permanent "while at altar" modifier note to the Tactics section as a hard reminder.

- **FI-EV7-39-D (CARRY)**: **LoH yoyo + instant disintegration = correct but needs table reminder.** Sabriel should be warned (in the DM's read-aloud or a pre-game note) that reviving a downed ally within DR range invites an immediate DR response. The encounter .md already documents this (DR target note FIX-EV7-30-A: conscious targets only). Positive find: the loop closed correctly this cycle.

- **FI-EV7-39-E (CARRY)**: **Sabriel's Fireball (via Marwen) blocked by SIM error 39th EV7** — see SIM-EV7-39-C. In the 38th cycle Marwen survived to R3 and was disintegrated. In this cycle she was incorrectly downed R1. With correct LA timing, Marwen fires Fireball R1 (potentially 14-28 damage after save) before the beholder can retaliate. This makes the fight materially harder for the beholder if Marwen acts freely.
