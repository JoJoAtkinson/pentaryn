**FI-TW38-D — SC solo endgame anticlimactic R3-R4 (confirm FI-TW37-D)**

The Barrage recharge-4-6 recommendation from prior cycles would improve this — recharge 4-6 gives ~33% vs current ~17%, meaning the SC is more likely to fire Barrage once in a 2-round endgame.
