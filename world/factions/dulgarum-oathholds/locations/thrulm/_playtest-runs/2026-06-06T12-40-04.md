### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-FC33-A DR-nat-1-opener-deadspot (feel, log for human)**  
Disintegration Ray opened with a natural 1 (cache roll d20=1, +6=7 vs AC15 = miss). The beholder's marquee ability whiffed on turn 1 before any PC had a chance to respond. The fight lost its terror opener. Marwen died via Void Scream R2 instead, which is less thematic. Recommend: consider if DR should have a minimum roll floor (house rule) or simply accept that the nat-1 whiff is a valid "near escape" dramatic beat. Does not need spec change.

**FI-FC33-B STD-AR-double-R1-stagger-33rd-confirm (feel, log for human)**  
Both STD-A and STD-B fired Ancient Resonance in R1 (stagger rule violated in simulation). With correct stagger, STD-A fires R1, STD-B holds. The double-R1 AR spike dealt 18+9=27 necrotic to Bazgar (FAIL both), dropping him to 12/49 before the beholder LA finished him. With proper stagger, only 18 necrotic lands R1 — Bazgar survives to R2. The stagger rule is important but hard to self-enforce in fast simulation. 33rd-cycle confirm.

**FI-FC33-C VS-38-kills-Marwen-before-her-turn-33rd-confirm (feel, POSITIVE)**  
Void Scream fired R2 at beholder init 20, before Marwen's turn (init 10). 38 psychic on a FAIL destroyed Marwen from 22/32. The VS as "anti-caster priority fire" before the caster gets a slot works as designed. Fireball never fired. Outcome: party has no area clear of thralls — all 4 thralls survive to R2, adding sustained chip damage that finishes Sabriel with thrall T4 + STD-A AR.

**FI-FC33-D AR-recharged-both-STDs-R2-d6-6-each (feel, note)**  
Both STD-A and STD-B rolled d6=6 on SOT R2 recharge checks — maximum roll, guaranteed recovery. This is a 1/36 probability (≈3%). Double-6 recharge after both were spent R1 contributed heavily to Sabriel's R2 death. In most cycles, at least one STD will not recharge on 5+ (recharge=5; P(not recharge) = 4/6 ≈ 67%). This run was unusually harsh on recharge luck.

**FI-FC33-E beholder-78HP-at-party-collapse (ongoing)**  
Beholder took only 32 damage (Bazgar R1 Action Surge all 4 hits). Sabriel missed, Marwen died before acting. Beholder at 71% HP when fight ended. The asymmetry (party fully dead, beholder barely scratched) remains structurally unchanged from prior cycles. Bazgar's Action Surge remains the party's only meaningful damage window.
