### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-NEW-SC7-A (feel, 7th SCT cycle confirm of FI-NEW-C — CW double-buff gap):** SC-C (init 13) and SC-B (init 11) both used Call Weakness on SC-A, violating the shardcaller-only tactics rule "stagger targets — don't double-buff the same ally." The "mutual CW" pattern — SC-C CW→SC-B, SC-B CW→SC-C — is stated in the tactics doc but was not executed. At-table a DM tracking carefully would avoid this; in a solo-DM multi-SC context it's easy to miss. Second charge from each SC was wasted on already-CW'd SC-A. No spec change needed (doc already correct); flagging for awareness. 7th consecutive SCT cycle with CW 0-1/9 effective.

- **FI-NEW-SC7-B (feel, positive): TR + Barrage same-turn kite confirmed again (2nd SCT cycle confirmation).** SC-A faced Bazgar at 15 ft melee threat in R1, used TR (bonus) to back off 30 ft, then fired Shard Barrage (main action) as part of the same turn. This is the intended "retreat-and-fire" kite pattern and it worked cleanly. The combo creates distinctive tactical feel: SC skitters away while still delivering area damage. SC-A put 14 piercing on Bazgar in the same turn it escaped melee. No fix needed — positive confirmation.

- **FI-NEW-SC7-C (feel, positive): Pack Tactics Voice (PTV) contributed a meaningful forced failure.** SC-A hit Marwen in R2 (Shard-Throw 1, CW advantage = 22 to-hit), applying PTV disadv on her next save. When SC-C fired Shard Barrage R2, Marwen rolled disadv Dex save: 6 (lowest of two rolls) vs DC 13 → **fail**, taking 10 piercing instead of 5. The 5-damage delta dropped Marwen to 3 HP instead of 8 HP — a narrow survival margin (if SC-B's multiattack had hit in the same round, Marwen would have died before Fireball). PTV is subtle (passive, no action cost) but meaningfully shifted the outcome. Confirms PTV is working as designed.

- **DD-18 8th SCT cycle confirm (DESIGN DECISION — open):** VICTORY R4, Bazgar 10/49, Marwen 11/32. This is the second-tightest SCT fight (after 5th cycle Slow-R1, R5). The fight was nearly flipped: Marwen cast Fireball at 3 HP with PTV disadv on her own Dex saves (if she had failed any save this round she would have been at 0 HP before Fireball). SC-A-Barrage R1 + SC-C-Barrage R2 back-to-back delivered 8+8+11+3 = 30 total piercing to Bazgar alone. The "SCs clustering for Fireball" issue (FI-145) remains open — if the party didn't have a Fireball-class AOE, this fight would have lasted 6+ rounds and likely ended in TPK. DD-18 design decision still open per prior cycles.
