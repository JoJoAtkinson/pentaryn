**FI-BEL27-A: DD at full beholder HP — temp HP entirely wasted**
Beholder opened at 110/110. DD on Sabriel succeeded (Cha save 10 vs DC 16), draining her L3 slot and granting +6 temp HP. But beholder was at max HP — the temp HP is lost immediately. DD STILL drained a high-value slot (preventing a L3 smite for the rest of the fight), but the DM gets nothing from the temp-HP half of the ability. Tactics note gap: add "If beholder is at or near max HP, DD temp HP is wasted; consider whether the slot drain alone is worth 3 LA vs 2 LA Void Ray." This is a judgment call, not a hard fix.
→ Recommend: add a coaching note in beholder.md Tactics under "Drain Divinity" that reads: "If beholder is at full HP, DD still drains the slot but grants no survivability benefit. Against a party likely to kill the beholder in 2-3 hits, slot drain is still worth it; against a party doing negligible damage, VR (2 LA) may be more immediately impactful."

### Mechanical questions

**MQ-BEL27-B: TA's initiative tie at 20 reveals undocumented creature-vs-lair priority**
TA rolled 20. The beholder .md says "lair action fires on init count 20, LOSING TIES." Does this mean TA (init exactly 20) acts before the lair? Yes — the lair LOSES ties. The rule is that the lair fires at 20 but yields to any creature that also rolled 20 on their initiative. The beholder's own turn works the same way (beholder rolled 14, so no conflict here). Recommend: clarify the beholder.md lair note with an example: "Example: if any creature rolls init 20, that creature acts before the lair fires."
