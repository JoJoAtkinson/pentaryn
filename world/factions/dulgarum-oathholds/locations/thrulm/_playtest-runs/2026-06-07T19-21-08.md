### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-BEL3-39-A — Marwen-downed-by-VR-R1, 39th-cycle-confirm** (carry FI-BEL3-38-B, FI-BEL3-37-D)
Marwen went down to the Void Ray legendary action in R1 before she could act — this time from save 5 vs DC 16. Pattern: Void Ray (avg 22 force) vs Marwen AC 15 / 32 HP. With failed save (50% chance for Marwen DEX +2 vs DC 16), she takes 22-44 force (4d10). She's at risk every round. Sabriel's LoH revives her but she gets VR'd again R2. This is the "Marwen never meaningfully acts" pattern confirmed 7+ cycles. She can't cast Fireball before going down. DESIGN DECISION: Marwen surviving long enough to act depends on the beholder not targeting her with LA in R1. If the beholder instead uses Tentacle (1 LA) + DD (3 LA — impossible in same round) or just Tentacle×2 + Move, Marwen acts R1 and Fireball wipes the thralls. Recommend tactic note: if Sabriel has high smite pool, use DD after Sabriel's turn (costs all 3 LA); don't waste VR on Marwen before Sabriel acts.

**FI-BEL3-39-B — DD-never-fired-39th-cycle-confirm** (carry FI-BEL3-38-D)
Drain Divinity (costs 3 LA) never fired. LA budget was consumed: VR (2 LA) after Bazgar's turn, then Move (1 LA) after Marwen's. The DD timing note (FIX-EV7-30-B) says to use DD after Sabriel's turn R1, but by then 0 LA remain. Trade-off: using VR on Marwen deals immediate damage and may kill her, but costs the DD window entirely. If beholder skips VR and saves 3 LA for DD (after Sabriel), it denies her smite slots before VS fires — likely more impactful. MQ-BEL3-39-A: recommend tactic note: "In R1, choose EITHER [VR on Marwen after Bazgar] OR [DD on Sabriel after Sabriel]. They compete for the same 2-3 LA budget. DD is higher value if Sabriel has L2+ smites; VR is higher value if Marwen is already low."

**FI-BEL3-39-E — Thralls-0-DPS-39th-confirm** (carry FI-BEL3-38-C, FI-FC35-E, FI-FC39-E)
3× thrall-derro across 5 rounds fired ~15 dagger attacks. Hit 2 (Sabriel R2 for 3, Sabriel R3 for 4). Total thrall damage: 7 HP. Against AC 18-19, +3 to hit = 25%/20% per swing. This is 7+ cycles of near-zero thrall DPS. The thralls add action-economy chaos (beholder uses Manifest Thralls lair, Compel Thrall bonus action) but deal no meaningful damage to the party. **SIM-BEL3-39-A — VS-fires-after-multiattack (simulation error, not spec bug)**
In the sim, Void Scream fired at "end of round 2" after the beholder had already taken its full multiattack action. This is mechanically wrong — VS is an Action (the beholder's main action), not a bonus or free action. In a real fight, the beholder chooses EITHER Multiattack OR Void Scream, not both. The fight outcome would differ: if VS fired in R2 instead of Multiattack, the beholder loses TL+TL+Maw (including the autocrit that downed Bazgar) but gains 48 psychic AoE (could down Marwen AND Sabriel). This is a simulation logic flaw, not a DB spec issue. Flagged for harness improvement. At-table DM should choose VS vs Multiattack based on target HP totals — don't combine them.

### Mechanical questions

**MQ-BEL3-39-A — VR-vs-DD LA budget competition** (new, derive from FI-BEL3-39-B)
Void Ray (2 LA) and Drain Divinity (3 LA) both compete for the 3-LA pool. Using VR burns 2/3, leaving 1 LA which cannot afford DD. The beholder can't both VR a caster AND DD the paladin in the same round. The tactics notes address each separately but don't call out the mutual exclusion. Recommend adding to tactics: "VR and DD cannot share a round (VR=2, DD=3, total=5 > budget of 3). Choose based on current threat: Sabriel with smite slots → DD; Marwen alive and dangerous → VR. Don't default-VR-Marwen if DD window exists."
