### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-SW26-A (confirm FI-SW25-A):** 26th-cycle init-order-determines-difficulty. Bazgar (init 19) Action Surges STD-A to death R1 before STD-A can act at init 4. STD-A's held AR (stagger-correct) is completely wasted. This is now confirmed across multiple cycles: when Bazgar rolls high init, the encounter collapses in 1–2 rounds; when he rolls low, the STDs' AR back-to-back chains create genuine TPK risk. The variance band is extremely wide purely from initiative. DESIGN NOTE: whether this needs addressing depends on encounter intent, but 26 cycles of this pattern confirm it's structural.

- **FI-SW26-B (NEW):** Stagger-assigned-fires-on-last-breath pattern. STD-B was assigned to fire AR (higher init), but was reduced to 4 HP before its turn by Fireball+smite. It still fired AR successfully (all 3 PCs failed saves, 21 dmg each). This is dramatically correct (dying creature's final scream) but highlights the stagger rule creates an asymmetry: the "firing" STD may be the one that gets focus-fired, while the "holding" STD gets killed without ever firing. Consider whether the stagger rule should allow the dying STD to voluntarily cede the fire right to the surviving one if reduced below 20 HP mid-round. Currently not possible — stagger is evaluated at start of round only.

- **FI-SW26-C (confirm FI-SW25-B, 26th cycle):** Altar zone fire suppression remains invisible in practice. Fireball dealt full damage in both cycles where the DM correctly suppressed vulnerability. The mechanic is not meaningfully gated by anything a player would notice — Fireball simply deals base damage, identical to what it would deal in any room. Pull-away-to-re-expose puzzle has not manifested in 26 cycles. Coaching note to DM: explicitly call out "fire vulnerability suppressed" when Fireball fires, so players register the altar zone effect.

- **FI-SW26-D:** Second Fireball on 4-HP target is a slot waste. DM should use Firebolt (or any cantrip/basic attack) to finish near-dead targets, preserving L3 slots. Not a spec bug — DM coaching note for the harness DM script.
