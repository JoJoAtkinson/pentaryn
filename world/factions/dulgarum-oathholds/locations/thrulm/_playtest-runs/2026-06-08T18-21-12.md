### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-SR5-44-A VICTORY-R5 — 44th cycle, comfortable** (Bazgar 94%, Sabriel 91%, Marwen 66%; no PC dropped below 50%). The slice continues to be tractable for the party. Fireball's area coverage vs. clustered Berserking ragers remains the structural kill mechanism. *Carry FI-SR5-I/J/K/L from prior cycles.*

- **FI-SR5-44-B Berserk-zero-recharge-44th** — All 3 ragers fired Berserk R1 (RAW-correct) for a combined 7 damage (cold dice: Skullcrack +1ME = 22 hit Bazgar for 3, Eyeless +1ME = 24 hit Sabriel for 4). Recharge rolls in R2–R4: d6=3, 3, 3, 3, 3, 3 (all three ragers, all three rounds) — no recharges. Berserk was completely inert after R1. Recharge 5-6 (33% per roll) gives ~65% chance of at least one recharge in 3 rounds per rager, but with 3 ragers all failing simultaneously this run, the ability disappeared entirely after R1. The R1 Berserk generated only 7 damage before Fireball retaliated for 34+17+17=68 effective damage. *Carry FI-SR5-J: Berserk fires as death swing.*

- **FI-SR5-44-C Taunt-target-convergence-carry** — The tactics guidance "prefer the PC NOT already engaging you in melee" + "back-line caster is highest value" causes all three ragers to independently evaluate Marwen as the optimal target. In a 3-rager-vs-3-PC fight where all three ragers prefer Marwen, the taunt stacking rule means two bonus actions are guaranteed wasted. Real-table DMs may not catch this without an explicit round-robin note. *Recommend adding to tactics: "Rager 1 (highest init): Taunt Marwen; Rager 2: Taunt Bazgar; Rager 3: Taunt Sabriel — round-robin by initiative each round."* This maximizes Taunt coverage across the party and gives each rager a meaningful bonus action. *Carry MQ-SR5-B.*

- **FI-SR5-44-D Taunt-zero-influence-4th-SR5-confirm** — This run: Marwen Fireball (save-based, bypasses Taunt entirely R1); Marwen Scorching Ray vs Eyeless (her taunted rager, no disadvantage); Marwen Fire Bolt vs Bloodhands (her active taunter, no disadvantage). Taunt never constrained Marwen's targeting in any round. Bazgar was taunted (FAIL) R3 but targeted the taunted rager anyway. The DC 12 Cha save is too low for Sabriel (+2 = ~65% save rate) and bypassed entirely by save-based spells (Marwen's preferred arsenal). *Carry FI-SR5-K: recommend DC 13-14 OR adding a non-attack-roll effect (e.g., "on a fail, speed halved" to complement the attack-roll rider).*

- **FI-SR5-44-E Fireball-structural-counter-carry** — Marwen's Fireball (8d6, DC 13 DEX save vs Dex+0 ragers) is a structural hard-counter to all three ragers clustering for Berserk. In 4 of 4 consecutive SR5 cycles where Marwen fired Fireball R1, it reduced all ragers by 17-34 HP and set up R2 kills. The tactical note in the `.md` (*FI-SR5-L: added in prior cycle*) recommends DM should "delay Marwen's Fireball to let melee mechanics express," but this note hasn't been added yet — it remains in the design-decisions backlog. *Carry for human action: add note to slice scenario guidance (either in `_overview.md` or the rotation file) recommending DM delay Marwen's Fireball to R2+ to allow Berserk/Taunt mechanics to express for ≥1 full round.*

### Mechanical questions

- **MQ-SR5-44-A Berserk-cap-note-visibility** (carry MQ-SR5-C from prior cycle) — The Berserk dispatch always prints 3 attack lines even when fewer targets are in reach. The cap note is buried in the `_Prereq:_` line: "Cap output at enemies actually in reach — DB always shows 3 lines." A DM running at speed may miss this and apply all 3 attacks when only 1-2 targets are adjacent. *Recommend moving the cap instruction to the narration field or bolding it more prominently in the prereq.*

- **MQ-SR5-44-B Madness-Endurance-display** — The dispatch output's `_Prereq:_` line reads "if this rager took damage since its last turn, add +1 to each to-hit roll." The to-hit values in the dispatch block do NOT include the +1 (they show the raw roll). DM must manually add +1 when Madness Endurance is active. This is correct behavior (the .md state is not tracked in the dispatch), but at a busy table, the +1 is easy to forget. *Consider adding a DM reminder line in the dispatch or narration field when the to-hit prereq condition is active.*
