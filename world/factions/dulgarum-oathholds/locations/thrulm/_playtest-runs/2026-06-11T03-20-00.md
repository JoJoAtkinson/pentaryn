## Findings

### Bugs auto-fixed
- **(FIX-FC48-A) VS unconscious targeting rule** — added DM note to beholder-thrulm.md Void Scream section: unconscious creatures (unaware of surroundings per PHB 294) cannot hear VS and are excluded from its effect. Prevents accidental instant-kill of already-downed PCs via area damage. (Without this ruling: Marwen at 0/32 HP within 30 ft of VS = 35 damage ≥ 32 max HP = instant death — anticlimactic and likely unintended.)
- **(FIX-FC48-B) Shrine-Drift grapple release rule** — added DM note to beholder-thrulm.md Shrine-Drift section: Shrine-Drift is a bonus-action repositioning ability, not standard fly movement. It does NOT drag grappled creatures. If beholder Shrine-Drifts more than 10 ft from a grappled target, the grapple ends. Consequence: beholder must choose between grapple maintenance (use fly movement at half speed to drag) and repositioning (use Shrine-Drift, lose the grapple).

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-FC48-A — DD protocol clean, Sabriel SAVES (first time in FC cycle history)**
- Beholder correctly held 3 LA through Bazgar and Marwen turns (R1-hold protocol FIX-FC40-A clean).
- Post-Sabriel DD fired: d20=19+3=22 → SUCCESS vs DC 16. DD fails — no slot stripped.

- DD effectiveness: ~40% chance Sabriel makes the save (needs 13+ on d20 with Cha +3). Combined with ~30–40% chance the protocol actually fires (depends on beholder init vs Sabriel init), expected slot stripping is rare.
- **DESIGN DECISION**: Is the DD anti-divine countermeasure worth 3 LA when it only fires ~30% of the time and succeeds ~60% of those times (net ~18% effective rate)?
