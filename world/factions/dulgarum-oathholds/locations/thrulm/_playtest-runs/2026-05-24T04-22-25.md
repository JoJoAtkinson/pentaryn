## Findings

### Bugs auto-fixed

**FI-1 (DD-6 stagger — applied):** Both shrine-touched fired Ancient Resonance in R1 for the 3rd consecutive shrine-wedge cycle. The stagger tactic (only the first-initiative STD fires AR R1; second uses Multiattack) has been added to `shrine-touched-derro.md` tactics. Additionally, a note was added to prefer Multiattack over AR when Unstable Form is active (UF advantage is wasted on AR's save-based damage). Authoring-only change, low-risk.

**FI-2 (OBR direction confirmed — no fix needed):** OBR correctly fired on Sabriel's hit (PC attacking STD triggers reaction, not STD landing a hit). DD-23 fix confirmed holding. Missed vs AC 19. STD self-psychic (2) applied. No spec change.

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**DD-40 (NEW): Unstable Form advantage has never applied to an attack roll across all 3 shrine-wedge cycles**
- R2: STD-A had UF active but was killed by Fireball before acting. STD-B had UF active AND recharged AR — fired AR (save-based, not attack roll) instead of Multiattack, wasting UF advantage.
- R3: STD-B had UF active (from R2 Fireball) but was killed by Scorching Ray before acting.
- Root cause: UF activates when STD takes 10+ damage in one turn — which almost always happens from Marwen's Fireballs or Sabriel's smite. On the FOLLOWING turn, STD's AR condition (2+ in cone) is still met, so AR fires instead of Multiattack. UF advantage (attack rolls only) is structurally wasted whenever AR is the preferred action.
- **Recommendation (do NOT auto-fix):** (a) Modify UF to add +2 to AR save DC when active (not advantage, since AR is a DC check — requires DB spec change); OR (b) Raise AR condition to 3+ enemies in cone when UF is active (forcing Multiattack if only 2 PCs present — avoids wasting UF advantage); OR (c) Redesign UF to trigger on HP falling below threshold (half HP or below) rather than per-turn damage, making it a distinct "dying fury" phase. Requires human review before any DB change.

**DD-6 (confirmed 3rd cycle):** Double Ancient Resonance R1 confirmed again before this cycle's stagger fix. STD-B (init 18) fired R1 AR before Bazgar (init 17) could act. STD-A (init 13) also fired AR R1. STD-B then RECHARGED (d6=6) and fired a 3rd AR in R2. Total AR firings: 3 across 3 rounds. Combined AR damage to party: Bazgar −36 HP (73% of max HP from AoE necrotic+psychic), Marwen −15 HP. The stagger fix applied this cycle should prevent double R1 AR in future runs.

**FI-3 (observation): Bazgar 0/4 attack hits — frontliner never connected**
- Raw rolls: 5, 2, 1, 7 (all below 9, which is the threshold for +7 vs AC16). 4 consecutive misses is ~0.3% probability.
- 3/3 shrine-wedge cycles: Bazgar has contributed zero melee damage. This is extreme RNG variance, not a balance issue. However, it means the slice has never stress-tested "what happens when the frontliner connects and OBR retaliates mid-multiattack" — the intended push-pull dynamic.
- **Recommendation:** No spec change. Note for human: in a live table game, Battlemaster maneuvers (Precision Strike) would correct one of these misses into a hit. The simulation doesn't use maneuvers — consider adding Precision Strike use to the PC playbook when Bazgar rolls a near-miss (within 5 of AC).

**FI-4 (observation): Sabriel untouched 3rd consecutive shrine-wedge cycle**
- OBR missed vs AC 19 both times it fired. Shrine-Axe Multiattack never targeted Sabriel (STDs fired AR whenever Sabriel was in cone). 
- Not a balance problem — AC 19 is high for a level-5 party slot. However, Sabriel's divine smite has hit once across 3 cycles with zero pushback. The "reactive danger" of OBR is invisible if Sabriel is never threatened.
- **Recommendation:** Consider adding tactics note: "If both Bazgar and Sabriel are in range, target Sabriel with Multiattack (not AR) when only one target is in cone — the high-AC divine martial who shrugs off OBR misses represents a larger threat than the already-damaged fighter."
