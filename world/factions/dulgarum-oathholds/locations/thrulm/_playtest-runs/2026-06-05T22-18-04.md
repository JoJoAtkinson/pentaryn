### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-TP27-A** SC Barrage-R2-recharge-unexercised (27th cycle): SC recharged Barrage on R2 (d6=5) but sim chose multiattack+CW instead. The SC tactics say "default to Barrage R1" but don't explicitly state whether to re-fire on R2+ when recharged in a narrow corridor. In this run, re-firing Barrage in R2 on advancing Bazgar+Sabriel (both in corridor line) would have been tactically correct and more damaging. This is a tactics documentation gap rather than a spec bug — the R2+ Barrage decision in threshold-patrol has not been documented. *(Previously raised as FI-TP24-D in cycle 24 with no resolution.)*

- **FI-TP27-B** Shatter-DC13-exact-pass by SC (marginal slot value): SC Con save was d20=12+1=13, exactly meeting DC 13 = pass. Marwen's L2 Shatter did not kill SC; SC survived on 15 HP to deal 2 more rounds of pressure on Marwen. The spell-vs-multiattack choice is close in this encounter — Shatter can whiff on good saves and still cost a slot. Not a balance issue but a feel note for the wizard player.

- **FI-TP27-C** CW structural lockout 27th-cycle confirm: SC fired CW on DW-1 correctly (DW-1 had 27 HP, already acted R1, MQ-12 spend-correct). DW-1 attacked R2 with advantage but rolled 1 and 6 (3 and 8 total vs AC 18 = both miss). CW again provided zero HP conversion. Cumulative: CW 0 meaningful conversions in 27 threshold-patrol cycles (with one exception at cycle 23 where CW fired before DW-1 acted). **DESIGN DECISION: at 26+ cycles of structural CW waste, this formation is effectively a 3-unit encounter where CW is dead weight. Joe should consider patrol spacing changes (bring SC closer) or CW range/charges rework for this slice.**

- **FI-TP27-D** No-Fireball VICTORY: First time in recent cycles (26th cycle also had Fireball). Marwen chose Magic Missile (R1, L1) + Shatter (R2, L2) + Firebolt (R4). Party won in 4 rounds vs 3 rounds with Fireball. This confirms the patrol is tractable for L5 party via multiple strategies — the fight is not Fireball-dependent, unlike previous cycles.

- **FI-TP27-E** Sabriel untouched 27th consecutive cycle: No NPC targeted Sabriel (AC 19 too high for DW to bother; SC targeted Marwen). Pattern is structural — DWDs prefer lower-AC targets and Marwen (AC 15) is always the priority. Sabriel's 44 HP is a resource the party never needed. This is fine for slice 0 (gatekeeper fight) but worth noting.
