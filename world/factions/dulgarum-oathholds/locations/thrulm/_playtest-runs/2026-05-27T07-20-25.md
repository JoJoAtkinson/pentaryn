### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-175 (sim harness fidelity — log for human):** Berserk+Slow (MQ-63) not enforced in simulation. RG3 fired Berserk vs 2 targets in R2 while Slowed (should be 1 target per MQ-63). Both attacks missed anyway so outcome unchanged. Sim harness needs a `slowed` flag that caps attack count to 1 for both Multiattack and Berserk. Not an NPC spec bug.

**FI-176 (NEW — DESIGN DECISION): Solo-rager-rush is highly initiative-dependent.** This is the 10th SRR cycle. PCs rolled 20/14/11, all 3 ragers rolled 8/4/1. **Every PC acted before every rager.** Result: Bazgar's Action Surge + Sabriel's smite dropped RG1 to 1 HP; Marwen's Fireball finished it plus chipped the others. By the time any rager acted, the fight was 3v2 with both survivors under pressure. Marwen's Slow in R2 finished the job. **Party took 10 total damage.** This is the extreme end of the initiative distribution, but it confirms the structural finding: at level 5, front-loaded resources (Action Surge, Fireball, smite) can effectively skip the first rager turn, reducing the encounter to a mop-up. **Design options:**
- **(a) Accept as-is:** initiative variance is normal; when ragers go first the outcome is much more dangerous (they'd be at roughly 30-35% hit rate vs the party's AC, but a R1 Berserk-before-Slow could land 1d12+2 on multiple targets).
- **(b) Add a rager R1 environment advantage:** give each rager a free 10 ft charge move before initiative is rolled ("the derros are already running when you enter"). This doesn't change the action economy but gives them a positioning advantage.
- **(c) Reduce rager count to 2 and add 1 thrall-derro:** adds range pressure that isn't dependent on initiative, while keeping the melee feel.
**Do not auto-fix.**

**FI-177 (NEW — FEEL): Berserk contributed 0 damage for the 10th consecutive SRR cycle.** In this run: 1 Berserk use (R2), 1 target (Slow-corrected), d20+5=8 vs AC18 — miss. Root cause: +4 to-hit gives ~30% hit rate vs AC18 (Bazgar), ~35% vs AC15 (Marwen). Combined with recharge 5–6 (~33%), Berserk's expected DPR over a 4-round fight is < 2. Across 10 SRR cycles, Berserk has landed damage in zero runs (FI-53/FI-64/FI-85/FI-96/FI-121/FI-136/FI-141 prior confirms). **Mechanical options:**
- **(a) Raise to-hit bonus to +5:** marginal improvement (35%/40% hit rate vs AC18/15), doesn't fix the structural low-probability issue.
- **(b) Lower recharge to 4–6:** Berserk fires more often, but hit rate issue persists.
- **(c) Accept:** Berserk is a zone-control ability, not reliable damage; its threat-of-use forces PC positioning. The terror of Berserk potential is the mechanical effect, even if it rarely lands.
**Do not auto-fix.**

**FI-178 (POSITIVE CONFIRM): Multi-taunt split executed correctly, 10th consecutive cycle.** RG3→Marwen, RG2→Sabriel (split, not stacked). FI-31 rule correctly applied by NPC tactics. No violation. No action needed.

**FI-179 (NEW — FEEL): RG1 died without taking a single action (init 1 = last).** Pattern across SRR cycles: the rager with the lowest initiative roll almost always dies before its turn in R1 due to combined Action Surge / Fireball. In 10 SRR cycles, the last-initiative rager has acted in R1 exactly 0 times (FI-62, FI-96, FI-140 prior confirms). A combatant that is a pure HP sponge in R1 doesn't add to encounter feel. This is acceptable at CR2 vs L5 but informs the FI-176 design question: if the encounter is meant to feel like a dangerous rush, some rager R1 action is needed.

### Mechanical questions

**MQ-71 (NEW): Madness Endurance R1 ambiguity.** The derro-rager.md says: *"If rager took damage last turn: +1 to attack rolls this turn (Madness Endurance)."* In this run, Marwen's Fireball hit RG2 and RG3 during R1 (at init 11) before they had their own turns (init 8 and 4). Sim applied +1 ME bonus when those ragers acted later in R1 on the grounds that "they took damage before their action." However, **"last turn"** in 5e means the prior round's turn for that creature. Since R1 is the first round, ragers have no "last turn" — ME should NOT apply.

**Recommendation:** Clarify derro-rager.md Madness Endurance trigger: *"If the rager took damage since the start of its LAST turn (i.e., from the previous round forward) — not from the current round's earlier initiatives — the +1 applies. Round 1 is excluded since no prior turn exists."* This is a minor simulation correction; in most R1 cases the +1 is inconsequential but it's worth locking down for correctness. **Do not auto-fix — human should confirm the intent.**
