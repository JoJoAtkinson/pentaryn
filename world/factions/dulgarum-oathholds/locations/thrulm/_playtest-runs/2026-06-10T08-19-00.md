### Mechanical questions

**MQ-BEL3-4TH-A (NEW) — LoH vs overkill heal math (sim bug)**  
In the sim, Sabriel's LoH added 10 HP to Marwen who was at −39 HP (net −29), which `hp_bar` rendered as HP 0. Correct 5e ruling: Lay on Hands always brings a downed creature to at least 1 HP (PHB p.86: "a creature regains hit points equal to the amount spent from the pool"). If Marwen is at −39, LoH sets her to max(1, −39 + 10) = 1 HP, not 0. Sim should be updated to reflect this. No spec change needed.

**MQ-BEL3-4TH-B — Grapple persistence past 0 HP**  
In the sim, Marwen remained flagged as GRAPPLED for R2–R6 even after going down. Per 5e: the grapple requires the BEHOLDER to maintain it (the beholder is the grappLER); the beholder doesn't need to be conscious — the tentacle holds mechanically. Marwen (grappled AND unconscious) at 0 HP is a valid rules state — the beholder is still holding her. The sim flagging is correct for the beholder-as-grappLER case. No spec change. *DM coaching note: an unconscious PC held by the beholder's tentacle cannot escape until the tentacle is severed or the beholder is downed.*

**MQ-BEL3-4TH-C (carry) — VS recharge 5-6 vs 6 design decision**  
4th BEL3 cycle: VS recharged (rolled 6) but was not fired in sim. Prior 3 BEL3 cycles: VS never recharged at all (d6=2/4/4/5 failures). In a ~4-round BEL3 fight, Recharge 6 fires in ~52% of encounters; Recharge 5-6 fires in ~80%. VS is the beholder's most distinctive ability; at Recharge 6, it's absent half the fights.
