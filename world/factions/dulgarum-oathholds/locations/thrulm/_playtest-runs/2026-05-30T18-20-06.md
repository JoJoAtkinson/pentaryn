### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-TP18-A: CW structural lockout — new mechanism (Fireball kill window)**
- Cycles 11–18 all show 0–1 CW charges used. This cycle adds a new lockout vector:
  Marwen init 11 ties SC init 11; as PC, Marwen resolves first. Fireball R1 kills both DW-A and DW-B before SC acts → SC has zero eligible CW targets. 3/3 charges wasted.
- Prior cycles: lockout via HP guard (≤20 HP) or already-acted guard (MQ-12). This cycle: allies simply don't exist when SC's turn arrives.
- **Pattern conclusion (18 cycles):** CW is structurally non-functional in threshold-patrol. The 30-ft range and 3-charge pool are unworkable when DWs die to Fireball, half-HP crossbow-retreat, or act-first ordering. Recommend: (a) increase CW range to 60 ft, (b) give SC a pre-combat CW declaration phase, or (c) replace CW with a reactive buff that doesn't require turn-order setup. FLAGGED — human decision.

**FI-TP18-B: Fireball R1 single-handedly resolves the encounter**
- 31 fire (hot roll, ~85th percentile for 8d6) vs DC 15 Dex (DW/SC Dex +2): all three NPCs failed.
- DW-A and DW-B both have 27 HP — any Fireball ≥27 fire with failed save = instant kill.
- Expected Fireball damage (8d6 avg = 28) exceeds DW HP. At avg damage even with one save:
  the DWs almost always die to Fireball if all fail. The encounter has no surviving melee presence
  from round 1 onward when Marwen acts before SC.
- Feel note: The fight felt like "Marwen presses Fireball and packs up." DW-A was already crossbow-mode before Fireball; both DWs out of melee before party took meaningful pressure. SC's Barrage landed (~14 piercing spread) but the fight was effectively won on Marwen's action.
- **Baseline TP feel concern (18th-cycle confirm):** When Marwen acts at or before init 11, Fireball ends the encounter before NPCs can apply sustained pressure. The SC's Barrage zombie pattern (FI-TP-1, cycle 16+17) is the only notable NPC output, and it only fires when SC survives past R1. Recommend adding terrain/positioning rule that delays Marwen's Fireball range (e.g., SC starts behind a pillar 45 ft away, requiring Marwen to dash R1 for range, delaying Fireball to R2).

**FI-TP18-C: DW-A half-HP crossbow fallback fires R1 vs Bazgar burst**
- Bazgar dealt 21 damage to DW-A in R1 (two hits), putting DW-A at 6 HP — below the 13 HP threshold.
- DW-A immediately switched to crossbow (missed), losing all melee output in R1.
- Both DW-A's melee attacks against Bazgar were forfeited due to Bazgar acting first with high initiative.
- Feel note: The DW half-HP fallback is correct tactically but means the DW contributes very little when a high-init fighter opens on them. Consider a "don't disengage R1 unless in immediate death range" clause.
