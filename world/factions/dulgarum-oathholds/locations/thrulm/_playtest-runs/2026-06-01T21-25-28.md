### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-EV7-19-A (NEW): `manifest_thralls` lair action locked in solo beholder.**
In empty-void (solo beholder, no thralls), `manifest_thralls` is permanently unavailable — the effect requires "up to 3 derro charmed by the beholder within 60 ft." Beholder has only 2 usable lair actions (void_eruption, unstable_ground). Lair rotation cycles between exactly these two. Tactically sound (the .md correctly notes "manifest_thralls only if thralls are alive"), but the lair feels thin: alternating void_eruption/unstable_ground has limited surprise value. 19th-cycle first explicit documentation. 
**Recommendation for DM note:** In solo beholder encounters specifically, add a third lair option ("void feeding pulse" — DC 14 Con, 1d6 psychic, 30 ft radius self) to give the lair 3 real options. Or accept 2-option rotation as intended.
**Do NOT auto-fix** — this is a design/balance question.
