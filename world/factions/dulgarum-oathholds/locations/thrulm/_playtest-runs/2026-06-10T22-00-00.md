### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-EV7-51-A — Void-Feeding +1 not in DB spec (log for human)**
The beholder's Void-Feeding trait grants +1 to attack rolls and damage rolls while in the shrine chamber. This bonus is not present in any of the beholder's DB action specs (roller output shows +6 for Tentacle Lash, matching the base .md stat, not +7). Applied manually as `BEHOLDER_VOID_BONUS = 1` in this sim. This has been an open gap for all prior cycles and should be resolved in one of two ways: (a) add a `dm_note` field to each beholder attack action calling out the +1 reminder, so the at-table DM sees it in the rolled output; or (b) accept the current state (DM memorizes the bonus). Option (a) requires updating all 7 beholder attack-roll actions; (b) is lightweight but error-prone. **Do not auto-fix** — changes fundamental attack math; human decision.

**FI-EV7-51-B — Bazgar nonmagic-weapon irrelevance (log for human)**
Bazgar dealt 4+6=10 total damage across his R1 attacks (both hits resisted by the beholder's nonmagic B/P/S resistance), then went 0-for-2 in R2, then was downed by VS in R2 (6 HP left). His total contribution to beholder damage: ~10 HP out of the ~87 dealt before the encounter ended. With no magic or sanctified weapon, the front-line fighter is essentially a warm body absorbing grapple/VS damage. This has been a consistent finding across beholder-slice cycles. Worth noting that the combat roster (`combat-roster.yml`) doesn't specify magic weapon equipment — the sim always assumes mundane. If the table assumes Bazgar has a +1 weapon going in, the balance shifts measurably. **Recommend**: update the playtest harness config or combat-roster.yml with Bazgar's assumed weapon status, and run at least one cycle each way.

**FI-EV7-51-C — LoH revive extends fight but doesn't change outcome (observation)**
Sabriel revived Marwen (R2, 5 HP) and Bazgar (R3, 5 HP) via LoH. Both were re-downed within the same round by lair actions (Void Eruption). This adds 1 round of narrative texture but the outcome is unchanged. Consistent with prior LoH-in-beholder-slice findings. Not a bug; confirms LoH is a delay mechanism under this pressure level. Worth noting for the DM: announce lair action targets explicitly so Sabriel can make an informed LoH decision (reviving someone who is about to be hit by Void Eruption is often a wasted LoH charge).

**FI-EV7-51-D — 2 LA wasted R2 after VS downed Marwen (sim observation)**
After VS dropped Marwen in R2, the beholder had 3 LA remaining. Code path: (a) Void Ray check → `'marwen' in alive_pcs()` = False, skip; (b) Tentacle check → fires on Bazgar (MISS, 16 vs AC 18); (c) 2 LA remaining, no further code path → wasted. In real table play, the DM should redirect: after VS, if Marwen is down, use the remaining 2 LA for Void Ray (2 LA) on Sabriel instead, or save for after-Sabriel Drain Divinity next round. This is a sim simplification artifact, but the tactics section should clarify LA re-targeting when the primary Void Ray target is downed. Add note to beholder .md tactics under legendary actions.

### Mechanical questions

**MQ-EV7-51-A — VS effective range vs threshold positioning**
The sim assumed all PCs were within 30ft of the beholder (VS range) by R2. But the setup places the party at the threshold (~40ft from the beholder). For VS to hit all three PCs in R2, either: (a) the PCs closed to within 30ft of the beholder in R1 (likely if they're advancing to fight), or (b) the beholder drifted (Shrine-Drift bonus action) toward the party before VS. In the sim, Bazgar and Sabriel moved to melee in R1, but Marwen likely stayed at range (40ft). If Marwen didn't close, she's outside VS radius. The .md notes VS as "30-ft radius (self)" so the beholder's position matters. **Recommend**: in future sims, track PC positions explicitly and only apply VS to PCs within 30ft of the beholder's location (not the shrine). This could significantly reduce VS lethality in early rounds.
