When SC-A fired Barrage as a defensive action at init 11 (melee-threatened → TR → Barrage), SC-C recharged at the start of its own turn (init 7, d6=5) and also fired Barrage — double-Barrage same round. Added explicit DM note to `derro-shardcaller.md` checklist item 5: *"If an SC fires Barrage as a defensive action mid-round, that counts as the round's one Barrage — any SC recharged later that round must Multiattack instead."*

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS
- **FI-SCT6-45-A CW-waste-3/9-structural-3rd-consec (carry FI-SCT6-44-A):** SC-A dies R2 with 3/3 CW charges unspent (middle-init holds per FI-SCT-34-A then dies before spending); SC-C spends 1 charge on Barrage (wasted — stagger violation) and dies with 2 unused. 3/9 productive total is consistent across 43rd/44th/45th cycles. The stagger-hold protocol structurally prevents CW spending in 3-SC formation whenever any SC dies early. Recommend DM note: "When middle-init SC falls in R2, lowest-init SC should reposition toward SC-C to stay within 30ft CW range (carry FI-SCT6-44-A)." Human decision required — no mechanical fix without redesigning assignment protocol.

- **FI-SCT6-45-B VICTORY-R4-5th-consecutive-SCT (carry FI-SCT6-44-C):** SC-only formation has lost every cycle. 4+ consecutive victories for the party. Fight ends R3-R4 consistently. Either the formation needs reinforcement (thrall meatshield, or +1 SC at 3 HP each) or this slice is intentionally weak as a preamble encounter.

- **FI-SCT6-45-C SC-C-untouched-endgame-solo-R4 (carry FI-SCT6-44-C):** SC-C survived R1–R3 at full HP (33) because party focused SC-A (melee threat) and SC-B (high init, Scorching Ray). Solo endgame R3–R4: SC-C had no Barrage recharges (d6=4,4) and missed both Multiattack throws each round. Undramatic finish. Consider: when SC-C is last SC alive, drop the 12HP retreat threshold to 5HP — let it fight from its current position rather than retreating to empty space.

- **FI-SCT6-45-D PTV-expressed-once (SC-B hit Marwen R1 → PTV disadv on Barrage save):** Marwen rolled exactly DC13 (d20=13+2 with disadv [14,11]). PTV was set but didn't change the outcome. PTV has been decisive in other cycles (44th, 43rd) when multiple hits accumulate before Barrage. Mechanic works; this cycle's single hit was insufficient to push below threshold.

- **MQ-SCT6-45-A: Stagger + defensive-fire creates CW assignment ambiguity:** When SC-A fires Barrage defensively at init 11 (not the assigned lowest-init firer), SC-B's original CW assignment was to CW SC-A (non-barrage → non-barrage per FI-SCT-34-A). But SC-A ended up firing Barrage too, meaning SC-B CW'd a Barrage-firer. The round-of-fire isn't predictable when a SC fires defensively. Recommend: always CW the highest-init ally who has NOT yet fired Barrage this round, evaluated at the time of the bonus action (not pre-planned).
