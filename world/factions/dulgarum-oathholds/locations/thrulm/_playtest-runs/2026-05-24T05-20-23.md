- **Rager R1 miss streak (3/3 cycles):** Both Rager R1 greataxe swings missed Bazgar (AC 18) in every tank-wall run. This cycle: natural 1 and 2 (to-hit 5, 6 vs AC 18). Probability of 2× consecutive miss = 65%² ≈ 42% per cycle; three-cycle repeat = ~7.4%. Not a bug, but DMs should note that Rager vs AC 18 heavy-armor fighter has only a 35% hit rate per swing — pairing with CW advantage is correct (first swing take-higher), but R1 CW fires before Rager acts and misses anyway if rolls are extreme. Informational only.
- **CW range breaks once Rager closes (DD-41, NEW):** CW has 30ft range. Starting positions: party at 0ft, Rager at 40ft, SC at 55ft → SC-to-Rager distance = 15ft (in range ✓). After Rager charges to melee (Rager now at ~5ft from party): SC-to-Rager = ~50ft (OUT of CW 30ft range ✗). R1 CW (when SC acts before Rager charges) is the only window to CW the Rager without repositioning. R2+ CW on melee Rager requires SC to move within 30ft of the party — putting SC in melee danger. Authoring fix applied to `derro-shardcaller.md` (one-line range note added). Recommend: document at table "use CW on Rager R1 before it charges or hold for a fresh ally."

Marwen's R2 action was Fireball — she IMPOSED saves on NPCs, she did not roll a save herself. PTV's effect on Marwen had no valid target.
