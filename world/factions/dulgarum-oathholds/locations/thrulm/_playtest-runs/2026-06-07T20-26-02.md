## Findings

### Bugs auto-fixed

1. **FIX-FC40-A** — Added R1 LA reserve protocol to `beholder-thrulm.md` tactics (DD timing section): beholder must skip ALL reactive LAs on Bazgar and Marwen turns in R1 to preserve 3-LA budget for DD after Sabriel. Previous note (FIX-EV7-30-B) stated the priority but didn't address the LA budget conflict that causes it to fail in practice.

2. **FIX-FC40-B** — Added DM note to Disintegration Ray action text in `beholder-thrulm.md`: disintegrated PCs (ash) cannot be targeted by Lay on Hands, healing word, revivify, or other "restore HP to dying creature" effects. DM must announce disintegration explicitly at the table to prevent Sabriel wasting LoH on a non-existent body.

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-FC40-A DD-never-fires-40th-cycle-confirm**: Drain Divinity (3 LA, post-Sabriel) never fires. Root cause: LA budget (3/round) is exhausted by reactive tentacle (1 LA after Bazgar) + void ray (2 LA after Marwen) before Sabriel acts. FIX-FC40-A adds explicit protocol, but this is fundamentally a zero-sum LA problem — DD and reactive LAs are mutually exclusive in R1. DESIGN DECISION: should DD cost 2 LA instead of 3? Or should the beholder have 4 LA/round? Carry forward.

- **FI-FC40-B VS-double-fire-R2+R3-40th-cycle**: VS fired in R2 (standard use) then recharged on nat 20 at R3 start and fired again. Two VS blasts in consecutive rounds ended the fight instantly. Recharge 6 means 17%/round — but the crit-recharge in this run is statistically uncommon. The double-VS feel is very punishing but represents the RNG ceiling. Carry FI-FC35-A (recommend recharge 5–6 to give VS more presence across runs).

- **FI-FC40-C Fireball-never-fires-structural**: Marwen (init 12) goes AFTER the beholder (init 18) in R1. Beholder always fires DR at Marwen before she can Fireball. In this slice, Marwen is the highest-value R1 action (Fireball wipes thralls), but the init structure prevents it whenever beholder wins initiative over Marwen. DESIGN DECISION: this is intentional CR-gap tension, not a bug. But the absence of Fireball means thralls survive and the fight becomes a 1v3 vs surviving STDs + beholder. Note for table: Marwen should use Counterspell (reaction) if beholder fires DR in R1 before her turn — wastes her R1 action but saves her life.

- **FI-FC40-D Thralls-zero-DPS-8th-FC-confirm**: 4 thralls made 10 total attack attempts across 3 rounds: 0 hits. +3 to-hit vs AC 15-19 is ~15-30% hit rate per attack, but RNG in this run was poor. The thralls have never landed meaningful damage across 8 FC cycles. DESIGN DECISION for human: raise thrall to-hit to +5, or accept that thralls serve as LoH-forcing chaff rather than DPS pressure.

- **FI-FC40-E Maw-auto-crit-never-fires-FC-cycle**: Beholder never used Multiattack — VS and DR were always available and chosen. Maw auto-crit (8d8+3 avg ~39 vs grappled target) is theoretically the highest single-attack output but never expresses when the beholder has rechargeable artillery available. This is correct tactical behavior (VS/DR > Multiattack) but means the Maw exists only as a multiattack fallback. Consider whether the .md should note this hierarchy explicitly.

- **FI-FC40-F Disintegration+LoH sim-gap-clarified**: Sim incorrectly revived disintegrated Marwen with LoH (saving her from "2 death saves" at round start). At the real table: Marwen is ash, LoH is wasted, Sabriel needs to know this immediately. The fix (FIX-FC40-B) addresses the DM callout. If DM correctly blocks LoH, R1 effectively removes Marwen permanently, leaving only 2 PCs for R2+.

### Mechanical questions

- **MQ-FC40-A: What is Sabriel's optimal R1 play when Marwen is disintegrated before her turn?** With Marwen gone as ash (no revive), Sabriel should not waste LoH. Options: (a) attack beholder R1 and save LoH for Bazgar, (b) use Shield of Faith concentration to boost AC, (c) retreat to force beholder away from altar. This should be a table coaching note, not an NPC fix.

- **MQ-FC40-B: VS recharge at start of beholder's turn vs. start of round** — the sim checks recharge at the top of the round loop (before all combatants, including those with higher init). RAW: recharge is checked at the start of the creature's own turn. When Bazgar has init 19 and beholder has 18, the beholder's recharge should be checked AFTER Bazgar acts (at the start of beholder's turn), not at the top of the round. Outcome delta in this run: minimal (VS would still recharge before VS fires), but sim methodology is slightly wrong. Carry forward as a low-priority sim correctness note.

- **MQ-FC40-C: Can Marwen use Counterspell on Disintegration Ray?** DR is a ranged spell attack. Counterspell (reaction, L3) can interrupt it if Marwen succeeds on a DC 15 ability check (or upcasts to auto-succeed at L3 if DR is L3 equivalent — DR is not explicitly a spell level in the .md). This would save Marwen but burn her only L3 slot. Not modeled in the sim. DESIGN QUESTION: the .md should specify whether DR is treated as a spell for Counterspell purposes. Per SRD beholder rules, beholder abilities are not spells — but this is a custom beholder.
