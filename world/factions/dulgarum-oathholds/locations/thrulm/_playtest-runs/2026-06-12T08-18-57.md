### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-SW1-44-A (carry — 44th consecutive): Unstable Form structurally unreachable window**
- UF requires NPC takes 10+ damage in one turn, SURVIVES to next turn, then gets advantage.
- In 44 SW1 playtests, UF has never activated. Root cause: STDs take massive damage from Fireball + Bazgar multiattack in R1 (often 20-30+ in one turn), but then die R2 before acting with UF. The 10-damage threshold is trivially crossed but the survival window is not.
- RECOMMENDATION (carry from prior runs): Either (a) reduce UF threshold to 6+ damage (makes it fire after lighter hits like crossbow or single melee), or (b) remove UF and replace with something that triggers proactively (e.g., "if shrine-touched starts turn below half HP, deal bonus psychic damage on Multiattack"). The mechanic as written cannot express itself at this party level.

**FI-SW1-44-B (new): Hold Person (L2) trivially disables shrine-touched-derro**
- DC 14 Wis save. STD Wis save: no bonus listed (+0 implied by typical CR3 derro stats). Average roll ~10, need 14 → ~25% chance to resist. Paralysis shuts off OBR, AR, and all actions.
- Marwen spent L2 to functionally remove STD2 from combat instantly. The shrine power narrative implies these derro should have some protection against mind-affecting magic — their minds are *already broken* by the shrine.
- RECOMMENDATION: Give shrine-touched advantage on Wis saves (shrine's will overrides external domination attempts). Or add "immune: charmed, frightened" already exists — consider adding advantage on Wis saves vs enchantment spells. This would reduce Hold Person success to ~44% from ~75%.

**FI-SW1-44-C (44th confirm): Altar zone fire/radiant suppression working correctly**
- Marwen's Fireball hit normally (no fire-vuln doubling). Sabriel's smite hit normally (no radiant-vuln doubling).
- Both PCs are apparently gravitating toward fire/radiant spells without knowing the altar zone nullifies the bonus. Feels realistic (PCs discover through play). No change needed to spec.
- However: if Marwen had known, she'd have cast Shatter (thunder, not suppressed) or Hold Person instead of Fireball. This is a valid strategic interaction but Marwen has no way to know pre-combat.

### Mechanical questions

**MQ-SW1-44-A (new): Paralyzed + within-5ft autocrit interaction with Hold Person**
- RAW 5e: attacks against a paralyzed target auto-crit if attacker is within 5 ft. Simulation did not enforce this (SIM-SW1-44-A). In a real game, Sabriel's attack on paralyzed STD2 would be an autocrit, dealing 2× dice on the hit + smite dice.
- With autocrit: 2×(1d8+4) + 2×(2d8 smite) = ~10 slashing + ~18 radiant = ~28 avg. STD2 had 1 HP, so it dies either way, but the autocrit interaction with Hold Person is a powerful combo worth flagging.
- RECOMMENDATION: Add a note to shrine-touched-derro.md under Interaction Notes: "Paralyzed shrine-touched takes autocrit from melee attackers (within 5 ft) per 5e rules. Hold Person + melee combo effectively doubles damage."
