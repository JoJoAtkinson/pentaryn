### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-14 CONFIRMED 5th cycle — Sabriel untouched in threshold-patrol:** NPCs
  target Marwen (lowest AC 15) in all 5 cycles. Sabriel and Bazgar both untouched
  across most rounds. DW targeting logic has no fallback to highest-threat target.
  Pattern is expected at Enc-2 difficulty but could be improved by adding "if primary
  target < 30% HP, switch to highest-damage dealer" clause. Log-only — low priority
  for Enc-2.

- **DD-39/MQ-12 CONFIRMED 5th cycle — CW wasted on DW-A (init 5, killed before acting):**
  SC-X (init 16) spends CW R1 on DW-A (init 5). Bazgar (init 14) hits DW-A to 10 HP;
  Sabriel (init 6) kills DW-A. DW-A (init 5) never acts. CW wasted.
  The existing DD-39 fix says "don't CW if no ally alive" — insufficient.
  **MQ-27 (NEW):** The correct fix is "CW the highest-initiative ally" so that the CW
  target is most likely to survive to its turn. In this run, DW-B (init 15) would have
  been the correct CW target — DW-B reliably acts before Bazgar (14) and Sabriel (6).
  Authoring fix: in SC-X tactics, "use Call Weakness on the ally with the highest
  initiative count (most likely to survive to its turn)." Low-risk; recommend auto-fix.

- **MQ-23 ONGOING — CW advantage not modeled in sim harness:** In R2 and R3, SC-X
  grants CW to DW-B, but the sim doesn't apply advantage to DW-B's attack rolls.
  At-table, DW-B should have had advantage on its first attack each round (better
  Marwen hit rate). Not a spec bug — harness tracking gap only.
