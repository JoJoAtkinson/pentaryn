### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-SCT6-52-A** (R1 Barrage fires before Fireball — rare but POSITIVE): SC-C (init 9) beat Marwen (init 3; nat-1) and fired Barrage R1 before Fireball. Bazgar took 4 piercing (save), Marwen took 8 piercing (fail → 2 HP remaining). This is the structural window where Barrage fires meaningfully: the low-init SC must beat Marwen's roll. Marwen's init is d20+2; SC-C's is d20+2. Beat probability ≈ 50% on equal Dex. When SC-C wins, Barrage fires before Fireball and the damage is felt. When Marwen wins, Barrage carrier is likely dead (in Fireball blast). **The stagger rule assigns Barrage to lowest-init SC, which is also most likely to sit in Fireball's blast. Consider reassigning Barrage to the highest-init SC (or the first SC to act) to increase R1 Barrage fire rate.** DESIGN DECISION.

- **FI-SCT6-52-B** (PTV disadv on SC's own Fireball save — ironic but correct): SC-A and SC-B hits triggered PTV on Marwen. Marwen's Fireball: SC-B's Dex save had PTV disadv (ally hits → target disadv on next save). SC-B rolled nat-20 Dex → with disadv rolled 8 → FAIL → 34 fire (vs 17 save). In a formation without the beholder present, PTV creates a feedback loop that accelerates SC death. **Worth noting in the shardcaller .md that PTV inadvertently debuffs SCs' Fireball resistance in the shardcaller-only formation.** DESIGN DECISION.
