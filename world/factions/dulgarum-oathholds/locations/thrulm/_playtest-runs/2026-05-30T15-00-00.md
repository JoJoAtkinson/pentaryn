### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-SRR-16-A (new): Wizard-taunter dies before acting — Taunt split structural gap.** Grimjaw (init 6, last in order) was the rager assigned to taunt Marwen per FI-31 split-taunt formation. He died in R1 without ever taking a turn: Fireball brought him from 52 to 26 HP, Sabriel's smite took him to 7, Bazgar finished him last in initiative. Marwen cast two Fireballs (R1+R2) completely uncontested. The split-taunt strategy relies on each rager acting before its assigned target — when the wizard's taunter draws the lowest initiative, she operates freely for the entire fight. This is qualitatively different from the scenario in the .md because the DM usually has no control over which rager gets the low roll. **Recommendation:** Note in rager tactics that the rager nearest to the wizard should Taunt her on its FIRST action, regardless of the split-taunt rotation — even a half-turn Taunt before Fireball is better than none.

- **FI-SRR-16-B (update to FI-SRR-B): Berserk hit rate is AC-gated, not uniformly weak.** Previous cycles (5-15th) flagged "Berserk fizzle vs AC18-19." This run shows Berserk connects reliably against AC15 (Marwen): Bonesnap Berserk hit her R2 (19 vs AC15), and Bonesnap Multiattack hit her R3 (24). Against AC19 (Sabriel), Berserk still fizzled (7, 12, 12 — all miss). Against AC18 (Bazgar), Berserk hit all three attempts. The fizzle pattern from FI-SRR-B is valid but specifically AC19-targeting: the recharge-4-6 recommendation should note it mainly benefits Sabriel-targeting angles. At AC15 Berserk already lands ~50% without change.

- **FI-SRR-16-C: Taunt on Sabriel (DC12 Cha, Cha+4) fails 3 of 4 saves.** Sabriel failed 3/4 Taunt saves across R2-R4 (saves: 19, 8, 7, 8). Her Cha modifier is +4 but DC 12 = needs 8 raw — 65% fail rate at +4? Wait: Cha save 19 = d20+4 (nat 15), saves 8 = d20+4 (nat 4), saves 7 = d20+4 (nat 3). Three successive low rolls. Statistically unusual but not a spec issue. The taunts on Sabriel R2-R4 all expired as moot since Splitear was the only remaining target. Net: 0 of 3 taunts changed Sabriel's combat behavior (she was already targeting Splitear/Bonesnap). Confirms FI-SRR-B 16th: Taunt is navigationally inert in rager-only encounters when PCs naturally focus their assigned rager.

- **FI-SRR-16-D: Berserk attack-line-cap confirmed in R3.** Splitear Berserk R3 had 3 attack lines but only 2 living PCs in reach (Marwen unconscious). Line 3 was correctly skipped. This is spec-correct per .md ("DB output always shows 3 attack lines — DM caps at actual in-reach creature count"). No issue — positive confirmation.
