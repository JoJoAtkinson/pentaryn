## Findings

### Bugs auto-fixed

- **FIX-EV7-41-A: void_ray DM note added (single-target clarification)**
  - The `void_ray` action is `type: area` with `area: "120-ft range"`, which causes the roller to output "each creature in 120-ft range rolls DC 16 Dex save" — implying AoE. But Void Ray is a single-target LA (one creature, beholder's choice). A DM reading the output cold would think everyone in 120 ft must save.
  - **Fix applied:** Added `save.notes` to the void_ray DB spec: `"Single-target LA — the beholder chooses ONE creature within 120 ft to make this save; it does NOT affect all creatures in range. Despite the area-format output, treat this as a targeted attack on one creature."`
  - This note now appears in tool output as `_(DM: ...)_` immediately after the "each creature" line.
  - This has been flagged as `MQ-BEL3-6-A` (void_ray-each-creature-text-recurring) in multiple prior cycles. Fix applied this run.
  - DB validated: 0 invalid records after upsert.

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-EV7-41-A — Detect Magic negotiation trigger not in Interaction Notes (carry from FI-EV7-40-A)**
  The beholder's Interaction Notes say it can negotiate "if the party is clearly not here to restore the shrine." But there's no list of what constitutes a "restoration attempt" in the beholder's perception. Detect Magic near the altar, Holy Water in range, a Guidance or Bless cast, a cleric's Channel Divinity — all of these could plausibly trigger the beholder's attack. Without a list, the DM improvises, which is fine, but a short "restoration triggers" note under Interaction Notes would make the encounter more consistent across DMs.
  **Recommendation:** Add 3-5 bullet examples of "perceived restoration attempts" to the Interaction Notes. Detect Magic targeting the seal is the clearest example from this run.

- **FI-EV7-41-B — Manifest Thralls correctly skipped when no thralls present (POSITIVE)**
  The lair action rotated to Unstable Ground correctly. In the prior 40th-EV7-cycle the same happened. The .md DM note is sufficient. No spec change needed.

- **FI-EV7-41-C — R2 TPK from VS is the dominant solo-beholder kill pattern (structural, carry)**
  Across EV7 cycles: VS recharged R2 leads to instant TPK via psychic blast + Void Ray LA cleanup. When VS doesn't recharge R2, the fight extends to R3-R4 with grapple-Maw attrition. The empty-void slice always ends in ≤3 rounds regardless of recharge luck. The "feel" question for the human: is a 2-round TPK (when VS fires R2) satisfying as a "negotiation failed → overwhelming force" moment? Or does it rob the party of meaningful decisions after the trigger? Current consensus from 41 EV7 cycles: the pattern is consistent enough that it reads as earned.

- **FI-EV7-41-D — LA budget after DD is tight but correct (POSITIVE)**
  DD spends all 3 LA R1. From R2 onward, the beholder has 3 fresh LA every round. With VS consuming the action (not an LA), the beholder has full LA budget in R2: VR (2) + Tentacle (1) = 3. In this run, VS killed both remaining PCs on the action alone, so LAs weren't needed. If VS misses (both PCs save), VR + Tentacle provide a solid cleanup path. Economy is working correctly.

### Mechanical questions

- **MQ-EV7-41-A — VoidFeeding +1 attack/damage bonus not in DB (recurring carry)**
  The beholder's Combat Traits list VoidFeeding (+1 to attack rolls and damage rolls while in the shrine chamber). This modifier is referenced in prior MQ flags (MQ-BEL3-7-A, MQ-BEL3-9-A, MQ-BEL3-38-B, MQ-FC48-A, etc.) but has never been added to any DB action spec. The roller can't add a conditional bonus without knowing the encounter context. **Recommendation:** Document in the beholder.md Tactics section that the DM should manually add +1 to all attack rolls and damage rolls when the beholder is in the shrine chamber, or add a `rider_on_hit: "+1 to all attack/damage rolls while in shrine chamber (VoidFeeding)"` reminder to the multiattack action narration.
  - This +1 was NOT applied in this playtest (sim used raw +6 to-hit). The Tentacle Lash 2 hitting Sabriel (AC 19) on a 24 would have been a 25 with VoidFeeding — no difference on the hit. Maw crit was already firing on a hit. No outcome change this cycle, but prior cycles (BEL3-7, BEL3-9) showed VoidFeeding as decisive for AC 19 targets.
