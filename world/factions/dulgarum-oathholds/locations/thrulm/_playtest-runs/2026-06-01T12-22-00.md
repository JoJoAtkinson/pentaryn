### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

1. **DD-47 ~17th-cycle confirm (POSITIVE):** TR fired twice — SC-B TR R1 (Sabriel's high initiative (21) pushed party_pos forward before SC-B acted, bringing the front to 5 ft of SC-B at 52 ft), SC-C TR R2 (same dynamic one round later). This is the 2nd documented cycle with TR fires. Pattern from cycles with TR: Sabriel's init ≥18 is the consistent driver — she advances party_pos before any SC acts, triggering TR on the end-of-round SC (lowest init). With Sabriel init lower, SCs act before party closes and TR never fires. TR is initiative-order dependent, not structural. The FI-181/FI-182 authoring fixes are correct.

2. **FI-35 ~17th-cycle confirm:** PTV triggered 3× (SC-A hit Marwen ×2 via dispatch, SC-B hit Marwen ×1) but was never consumed. Marwen didn't need to make a saving throw before the combat ended — SCs were dead before any save opportunity arose. In SC-only formations, PTV accumulates on Marwen but is structurally difficult to consume: the only save-triggering SC ability is Barrage, which doesn't trigger PTV (Barrage = save, not a hit). PTV's disadv on saves is most impactful in cross-formation encounters (VS, AR, Drain Divinity). No change needed.

3. **CW coordination confirmed correct ✓:** SC-C (barrage firer) CW'd SC-A (multiattacking ally — stagger+CW rule per checklist item 5 HELD). SC-A CW'd SC-B (next-initiative multiattacking SC — DD-39/MQ-12 rule HELD). SC-B used TR over CW (TR priority per FI-181 fix HELD). All three CW interactions fired in their correct priority order.

4. **Stagger rule confirmed ✓:** Only SC-C fired Barrage in R1. SC-A and SC-B multiattacked. In R2 Barrage failed to recharge (d6=2). Zero stagger violations.

5. **MQ-23 ongoing:** CW advantage (granted to SC-A from SC-C, and to SC-B from SC-A) is not reflected in roll_combat_action's multiattack dispatch output. Dispatch shows flat to-hit rolls. At-table DM must manually apply advantage (roll two d20s keep higher) for the benefiting SC's throws. Out of cron blast radius — requires dnd_roller.py fix.
