### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**DD-SCT-6-A: Pack Tactics Voice (PTV) is dead weight in SC-only formations.**
PTV grants the *hit target* disadv on its next *saving throw*. In an SC-only fight, NPCs use attack rolls — when they hit a PC, that PC rarely needs to make a save during the same round. PTV is designed to chain with beholder ray saves or shrine-touched area saves. In SC-only slice #6, PTV fired zero times meaningfully (tracking confirmed: every ptv_disadv flag expired without a matching save). **Design question for Joe:** Should PTV's benefit extend to attack rolls, or is SC-only always a support formation where this gap is acceptable? No auto-fix — this is a balance/design call.

**DD-SCT-6-B: CW charges collapse under melee pressure — 6/9 wasted.**
With Bazgar and Sabriel closing to melee range by R2, every SC spends its bonus action on Tactical Retreat from R2 onward. Only R1 CW charges (1 per SC, 3 total) are spent. The remaining 6 charges are carried to death unused. The ability's tactical identity ("calls formations and weaknesses") evaporates the moment front-liners engage. This is mechanically correct per the checklist (TR > CW, always), but the *feel* result is that CW looks like a 1-use ability per combat, not 3-use. **Design question for Joe:** Should there be a SC-only formation tactic where 1 SC holds a defensive position (no TR) to continue CW duty while the others kite? Currently the .md doesn't address this.

**DD-SCT-6-C: ≥25 ft spread does not prevent 2-SC Fireball catch.**
The spread rule (≥25 ft apart per FI-SCT-20-B) prevents a single Fireball from hitting all three SCs. But two SCs 25 ft apart are each 12.5 ft from their midpoint — well within the 20-ft blast radius. Marwen correctly centered the Fireball and caught SC-B and SC-C for 14 fire each (after saves). To fully protect against a 20-ft-radius Fireball, SCs would need ≥41 ft separation. **Recommendation for Joe:** Update FI-SCT-20-B guidance in the .md to note "≥25 ft prevents a 3-SC catch; to prevent a 2-SC catch, use ≥41 ft spacing, but this sacrifices Pack Tactics Voice range entirely." Log as design tension, not a bug.

**DD-SCT-6-D: SC-C's Barrage never fires in R2+ (only R1 corridor-approach default).**
SC-C had Barrage available and recharged throughout the fight but the sim defaulted to Multiattack after R1 (only the first-acting SC fires in R1). SC-C's Barrage charged through R2–R6 unused. The real-table DM should fire SC-C's Barrage in R2 when 2 PCs are in the corridor retreat path — the sim's simplification of "only R1 Barrage" understates Barrage usage frequency. Note for log: the tactics section says "default to Barrage R1 even with only 2 PCs in a corridor" but doesn't explicitly say WHICH SC fires it. Checklist item 5 (stagger) should note that R2+ Barrages are legal for the SCs that didn't fire in R1.

### Mechanical questions

**MQ-SCT-6-A: Does PTV trigger on Shard-Barrage?**
PTV passive text: "when an ally within 30 ft hits, the target has disadvantage on the next saving throw." Shard-Barrage is a Dex save, not an attack roll. Does "hits" include save-based damage? Strict reading: no (a save is not a hit). Broad reading: any damage application. This is unresolved in the .md. **Recommendation:** Add explicit clarification: "PTV triggers on successful attack rolls only, not on save-based area effects (Shard-Barrage)." This prevents confusion at the table.

**MQ-SCT-6-B: SC-A's CW on SC-C (init 9 grants CW to init 18 — already acted).**
In R1, SC-A (init 9) CW'd SC-C (init 18) after SC-C already took its turn. The CW advantage carried to R2 SC-C's turn. The .md "already-acted ally guard (MQ-12)" says to hold the charge if the acted ally has ≤20 HP. SC-C was at 19 HP entering R2 — technically below the 20 HP guard threshold. Per strict MQ-12 rules, SC-A should have held the CW charge rather than granting it. However, this is a marginal case (19 vs 20 HP threshold), and SC-C ended up using the advantage to hit Marwen for 7 piercing, bringing her to 4 HP — a consequential hit. This is a borderline case that confirms the MQ-12 threshold is functioning correctly.
