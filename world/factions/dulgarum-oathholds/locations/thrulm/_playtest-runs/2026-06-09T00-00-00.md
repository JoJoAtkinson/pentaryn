### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-SW-104-A — Init-order-dependence (CARRY FI-SW-100+): Marwen init 22 → Fireball before STDs act.**
Party had extremely favorable initiative (Marwen highest in fight). If STD-B had won initiative (realistic: Dex +2 roll ≥16 wins), AR fires first doing 11+3 to Bazgar and chunking party, then STD-A multiattacks before Fireball fires. VICTORY R2 outcome is strongly initiative-dependent. The encounter's feel at the table will vary significantly based on who goes first. Carry for human.

**FI-SW-104-B — UF advantage wasted on AR (103rd-consecutive carry).**
STD-A took 13dmg R1 (≥10 threshold) → Unstable Form. R2: 3 PCs in cone → AR priority per tactics. Advantage applies to attack rolls; AR uses DC 14 save. UF advantage was completely unexpressed. The tactics correctly chose AR (3-target priority), but the UF buff never contributed to outcome. This is structurally unavoidable given UF fires on high incoming damage → STDs also tend to use AoE (more targets). Consider whether UF advantage should instead apply a -1 to AR DC (e.g., DC 13 if UF active) to make the rider meaningful.

**FI-SW-104-C — Comfortable victory (all PCs >50% HP) suggests Shrine Wedge undertuned.**
Party finished with 51%/75%/68% HP. Only 2 spell slots spent. The encounter is labeled "Hard (party levels +1)" in encounters.md. Against a level 5 party with Action Surge and Fireball, 2× STD alone reads as Medium. Prior cycle (103rd) was VICTORY R3 — also comfortable. Recommend adding 1× Thrall Derro or 1× Derro Shardcaller to Shrine Wedge formation, or note in encounters.md that the Hard label applies without full party spell resources.

**FI-SW-104-D — OBR 2/2 MISS: reaction feels underpowered in this seed.**
Both STD OBR triggers fired on melee attacks (Bazgar Attack 1, Sabriel Attack 1). Both missed. Only consequence: 1 psychic self-damage each (1HP). If OBR had landed (e.g., vs Bazgar AC 18 needs 14+, ~25% chance), it would have dealt 5-15 damage — meaningful in a 2-round fight. The reaction trigger fires reliably (melee attacker present every time) but the ~25% hit rate vs fighter/paladin AC feels underpowered. Consider whether OBR should deal guaranteed self-damage plus a save-based effect rather than an attack roll. Not blocking — feel flag only.

**FI-SW-104-E — Altar zone fire/radiant suppression DECISIVE again (carry DESIGN-SW-A).**
Without suppression, STD-B takes 52 fire from Fireball (26×2) → one-shot before acting. With suppression, STD-B survives to fire AR. The suppression mechanically extends the encounter from ~1 round to 2-3 rounds. This is working as intended, but the DM needs to remember to announce suppression BEFORE the wizard player decides to cast Fireball (otherwise the player feels cheated). Add a reminder to the combat cheat sheet.
