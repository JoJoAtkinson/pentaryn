### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-BEL3-3RD-A: OUTCOME INDETERMINATE (near-TPK) — 3rd-BEL3-cycle**
All 3 PCs downed by R3 end with 2 death-save failures each. Beholder at 9 HP. Without disintegration ray, the party nearly won but couldn't close. The decisive kill vector was: Maw auto-crit R1 (52 piercing, downs Bazgar), Maw auto-crit R2 (26 piercing, downs Sabriel after LoH revive), Scorching Ray near-wins by Marwen R3 but she was downed by beholder multiattack. Result feels tense and appropriate for the slice intent: "tests legendary economy, lair actions, action-economy pressure." The close result validates the slice.

**FI-BEL3-3RD-B: Maw auto-crit to-hit ambiguity — CARRY (NEW)**
Simulation skipped the Maw to-hit roll and applied crit directly when target was grappled. The DB spec says `rider_on_hit: "If the target is grappled by the beholder, this attack is a critical hit instead."` By RAW, "on a hit" implies a to-hit roll is still required (the crit applies when the attack hits, not unconditionally). If to-hit is required: R1 Maw vs grappled Bazgar (AC 18) requires roll ≥12 at +7. ~55% chance to hit. If Maw misses, the one-shot of Bazgar R1 doesn't happen. This could change the fight substantially. **Recommend adding clarifying note to `.md` Maw action: "Roll to hit normally (+6); on a hit, automatically score a critical hit if the target is grappled (8d8+3 avg 39 piercing instead of 4d8+3)."**

**FI-BEL3-3RD-C: VS never recharged — 3rd-BEL3-cycle confirm (carry FI-BEL3-38-B)**
Void Scream (recharge 6) failed to recharge across 3 rounds. 42% chance of never firing in a 3-round fight. This is the 3rd consecutive BEL3 cycle where VS did not fire. The prior 2 cycles it fired only from R1 multiattack (sim error) or not at all. VS not contributing is now a structural pattern in the beholder-only slice. **Carry recommendation: VS recharge 5-6 (33% per round; ~70% chance to fire in 3 rounds).**

**FI-BEL3-3RD-D: LoH yo-yo confirmed — 3rd-BEL3-cycle (carry FI-EV7-33-C)**
Bazgar was downed and revived at 1 HP twice (R1, R2). Sabriel's bonus actions were entirely consumed. The driver: Maw auto-crit (~40 avg) from grappled state drops Bazgar from any HP in 1-2 hits. Reviving to 1 HP gives him one action before the next down. Net effect: Bazgar contributed ~11 HP damage (2 halved attacks R2) at cost of 2 Sabriel bonus actions. Sabriel spending bonus actions on LoH rather than offensive action has a real opportunity cost.

**FI-BEL3-3RD-E: Thrall damage trivially low — 3rd-BEL3-cycle confirm (carry FI-BEL3-38-C)**
Total thrall damage across 3 rounds: ~9 HP. Thralls missed every Manifest Thralls reaction attack (R3 lair). At +1 hit vs AC 18-19, hit rate ~15%. Even 3 thralls attacking every round for 3 rounds (9 attacks) produced <10 HP damage. Manifest Thralls lair is essentially wasted whenever party AC ≥ 18. **Carry: DM should switch lair to Unstable Ground if thralls missed all attacks last round (AC ≥ 18 party). Already noted per FIX-BEL3-38-A.**

**FI-BEL3-3RD-F: Fireball sim gap — thrall splash not modeled**
Marwen targeted Fireball solely at the beholder. At a real table with thralls clustering near the altar, a well-placed fireball catches all 3 thralls (22 HP each, Dex save ~0 prof). Thralls likely wiped R1 by Fireball splash. This changes the lair economy: with no thralls, Manifest Thralls becomes void; beholder switches to Unstable Ground or Void Eruption R2+. The effective beholder-only phase starts R2. This simulation underestimated Marwen's true output and overstated thrall survivability.

**FI-BEL3-3RD-G: DD protocol worked — slot attrition meaningful but not decisive**
R1 LA reserve (FIX-FC40-A) held correctly: beholder waited for all 3 LA post-Sabriel R1, spent on DD. Sabriel failed Cha save (6 vs DC 16), lost L2 slot. She then smited R2 (L2) and R3 (L2) for 15+17=32 radiant total — decisive damage. DD stripped 1 of 3 L2 slots; 2 remained. **Note: at L5, Sabriel has 3 L2 slots. Stripping one via DD still leaves 2 smites available. DD becomes more impactful at L6+ when slots are fewer and slot levels are higher.**

**FI-BEL3-3RD-H: Bazgar nonmagic weapon resistance is a major constraint — carry**
Bazgar dealt ~11 damage total over 3 rounds (both rounds he acted had hits, all halved). Without the resistance, output would be ~22-24 HP — enough to close the fight R2 instead of R3 edge. **At-table this is a player communication point: melee without magical/sanctified weapon vs this beholder is 50% output. A single +1 weapon or magic weapon changes his threat tier from nuisance to meaningful.**
