### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-BEL3-38-A: Maw auto-crit = sole kill vector — 2nd BEL3 cycle confirm**
All 3 PCs killed by Tentacle→Maw auto-crit (Bazgar R1, Marwen R3, Sabriel R4). VS never fired; DD was resisted. Both BEL3 cycles (07:23 and 16:24) produced identical kill paths — only the round the party dies differs (R3 vs R4). The beholder feels mechanically one-dimensional without DR: it grapples and auto-crits repeatedly until everyone is dead. No other ability expressed in either run.
Recommendation: VS recharge 5-6 is required for it to fire in sub-5-round fights. Maw auto-crit dice are already flagged (8d8+3 avg ~39; FI-BEL3-S3-A from 1st cycle). These are both fundamental balance changes — DESIGN DECISION, do not auto-fix.

**FI-BEL3-38-B: Void Scream recharge 6 fires 0/2 BEL3 cycles (0/5 total recharge rolls)**
VS recharge failed on d6=4, d6=2, d6=4 across this cycle's 3 recharge windows. 1st BEL3 cycle also shows 0 VS fires in 3 rounds. Pattern: at Recharge 6 (17%/round), a 4-round fight has ≈ 57% chance VS never fires (5/6)^3. With this party dying in R3-R4, VS fires in fewer than half of BEL3 encounters. The beholder's only psychic AoE (and frightened condition application) is effectively absent from this slice entirely. DESIGN DECISION: Change VS to Recharge 5-6 (33%/round → ~70% in 3 rounds).

**FI-BEL3-38-C: Thralls negligible threat — 2nd BEL3 cycle confirm**
Manifest Thralls lair R1: 6 reaction dagger attacks (3 thralls × 2 initiative windows) — 0 hits vs AC 15/18/19. T1+T3 combined damage: 6+3=9 total. T2 wiped by Fireball R1 (failed save). Thralls are present as flavor/action-economy tokens but contribute no real threat. Prior cycle logged same (FI-BEL3-S3-C: 29 total dmg). Accept role-as-distraction design intent, but note: Manifest Thralls lair action is wasted R2 vs armored party (switch to UG). Already patched in .md with FIX-BEL3-38-A (prior cycle). Carry: no further fix needed.

**FI-BEL3-38-D: Bazgar played 0 turns — initiative and resistance compound**
Beholder init 23, Bazgar init 2. Beholder grapple+Maw crit R1 killed Bazgar (50 piercing) before his turn. Bazgar dealt 0 damage, made 0 attacks. With nonmagic weapon resistance (longsword deals half to beholder), even if Bazgar survived, he contributes ~4-6 damage/hit. The combination of lowest initiative + nonmagic resistance removes Bazgar from BEL3 fights almost entirely. DESIGN DECISION: DM should announce nonmagic resistance explicitly at table. Consider a "staggered entry" variant where Bazgar gets distance before melee engagement, or a brief "approach round" before beholder can grapple.

**FI-BEL3-38-E: Drain Divinity resisted (Sabriel Cha save 20) — DD fails 50% of time at DC16**
DD correctly held (3 LA post-Sabriel R1 per FIX-FC40-A), but Sabriel rolled 20 and resisted. 1st BEL3 cycle: DD fired (save 5 < DC16). 50% rate empirically confirmed across 2 cycles. When DD fires, the slot strip is meaningful; when resisted, 3 LA are spent for 0 effect. This is inherent beholder-LA gambling. No fix. Tactics protocol confirmed correct.
