### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-SCT-19-A (new) — CW double-grant: missing hold-rule when only one future actor exists**

SC-B (init 14) correctly CW'd SC-C (init 3, only future actor, HP 33 > 20). SC-A (init 11) then also CW'd SC-C — the only future actor available — wasting a CW charge since SC-C already had advantage. The checklist says "don't double-buff the same ally" but provides no fallback for the case where there is *no other eligible target*. The advantage is not stackable per checklist item 4 ("Toggle, not stackable"). This wasted 1 charge with no benefit.

Recommendation: Add to the CW checklist, after "don't double-buff the same ally": *"If the only eligible future actor already has CW advantage from another shardcaller this turn, hold the charge — do not re-grant. The advantage is already set; the charge is wasted. Use the bonus action for Tactical Retreat instead, or carry the charge forward."*

This is distinct from the DD-SCT7-A pattern (charges never deplete in 3-round fights). This is specifically about two SCs both wanting to CW the same single future actor in the same turn sequence. It occurred because initiative put SC-C last and SC-A + SC-B both acted before it.

**FI-SCT-19-B (DD-SCT7-A 19th-cycle confirm) — CW 3/day non-binding**

2/9 total CW charges were spent (both wasted — SC-C missed with advantage (adv 9,9), double-grant consumed two charges for zero impact). The 3/day limit has never bound in any shardcaller-team run. This is the 19th-cycle confirm of DD-SCT7-A. No code change; design-level question for human: reduce to 2/day or 1/encounter?

**FI-SCT-19-C (pattern confirm) — Sabriel 44/44 untouched; Fireball decisive**

SC-B's Barrage targeted Bazgar+Marwen (corridor line, correct per R1 tactics). SC-A targeted Marwen. SC-C targeted Marwen. Sabriel never took damage. Marwen's two Fireballs (25 and 22 fire) were the decisive damage events — SC-B died to Fireball+Sabriel smite chain, SC-C survived Fireball R1 (passed save) but was irrelevant in R2. The 3× shardcaller formation has no answer to AOE; the fight lasted 2 rounds primarily because SC-C survived R1's Fireball on a save.

### Mechanical questions

**MQ-SCT-19-A — PTV triggered but never spent**

SC-A crit-hit Marwen in R1 (nat-20), setting PTV → Marwen has disadv on her next save. No SC used a save-requiring ability against Marwen in subsequent turns (SC-C's Shard-Throws are attack rolls, not saves; Fireball is Marwen casting, not saving). PTV disadv was wasted. This is the structural PTV synergy gap already noted in FI-SCT7-B / FI-TW24-D: in shardcaller-only or SC-dominant formations, the PTV → Barrage disadv chain requires (a) a multiattack hit on a target, then (b) a Barrage targeting the same PC later in the same round. With the stagger rule, the barrage SC often acts before the multiattack SCs (since barrage-SC has high initiative or goes first), making the synergy timing nearly impossible to achieve. To get PTV → Barrage synergy: the multiattacking SCs must act *before* the barrage SC in initiative. This run: SC-B (barrage, init 14) acted before SC-A (multiattack, init 11), so PTV couldn't prime before Barrage fired. 19th-cycle pattern, no new insight.
