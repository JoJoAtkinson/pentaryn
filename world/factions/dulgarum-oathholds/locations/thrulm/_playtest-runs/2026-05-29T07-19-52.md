### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-TP11-A — PTV invisible at DW-B hit events (11th TP cycle, FI-35 class confirm):**
DW-B landed two hits on Bazgar in R1 (init 10, after SC at init 11). Pack Tactics Voice should have applied disadvantage on Bazgar's next save when DW-B hit at attack 1. In this run, Marwen's SR (R1, after DW-B) uses attack rolls not saves, so PTV had no mechanical effect. However in any round where a DW hits and a PC then rolls a save (especially Barrage or AR), this matters. The checklist lists PTV on the SC's turn card — but PTV activates on ALLY hit events during OTHER NPCs' turns. Result: consistently missed by DM-sim and will be missed at table.
- Recommendation: add a parenthetical reminder in the DW multiattack narration: `(Pack Tactics Voice: on hit, target has disadv on next save — SC calls this out)`. This is a DM aid, not a spec bug.
- Severity: LOW in this slice (PTV went untracked but outcome was the same). Medium concern for harder clusters.

**FI-TP11-B — DW-A "≤13" threshold edge case (minor, 1st notation):**
DW-A ended R1 at exactly 13 HP — the tactics threshold. The .md says "Below half HP (≤13): Cunning Action → Disengage." DM-sim initially misread this as "not below" since 13 is exactly half of 27 (13.5 rounded down). The spec is mechanically correct (≤13 = at or below half), but creates micro-confusion at the table when a creature is at exactly the named threshold. Had DW-A correctly disengaged and switched to crossbow, the fight would have extended ~1 round (DW-A contributing crossbow pressure while avoiding Sabriel's longsword). Not a design change candidate — just a note for the human.

**FI-TP11-C — CW structural lockout, 11th TP cycle (FI-TP9-A pattern, 11th confirm):**
SC called CW on DW-B R1 (correct). DW-B collected the advantage and landed both attacks. BUT by R2, DW-B was at 19 HP (≤20 guard blocks CW). SC correctly held R2 CW. DW-B died to Fireball R2. Result: CW fired once (R1) but was "productive" in that DW-B landed hits (10 dmg total to Bazgar). So this cycle breaks the CW-total-lockout pattern — CW fired once AND was consumed. However the CW charge structure (3/day) with only 2 DW allies that die in 2 rounds means CW will almost never be used more than once in threshold-patrol. The 3-charge pool is wasted; SC ends the fight with 2 unused charges. DESIGN DECISION noted (same as FI-TP9-A): recommend reducing CW to 1/day in TP slice, or extending DW HP to survive into R3+ to enable multiple CW fires.

### Mechanical questions

**MQ-TP11-A — CW advantage + Tactical Drilling advantage stacking:**
DW-B in R1 had BOTH CW advantage (from SC) AND Tactical Drilling advantage (DW-A alive and in Bazgar's reach). Per 5e RAW, multiple advantage sources don't stack — still just advantage. The sim applied this correctly. Confirming that the spec is silent on this interaction but 5e handles it cleanly. No spec change needed.

**MQ-TP11-B — SC "≤12 HP retreat" vs "≤20 HP CW hold" interaction:**
At 17 HP, SC is above 12 HP (fight mode) but any surviving DW ally with ≤20 HP blocks CW. These two thresholds interact: SC could be at 13 HP (above 12, fight), ally at 19 HP (≤20, no CW) — SC multiattacks with no bonus. This is correct behavior, just noting the interaction is implicit in the two separate conditions.
