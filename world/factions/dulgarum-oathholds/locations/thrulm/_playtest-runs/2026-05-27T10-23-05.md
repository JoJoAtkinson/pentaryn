## Scene Setup

The party descends the shaft into the threshold antechamber — a low-ceilinged passage before the great vault door. Two Deep Watch Derro stand post at the foot of the stairs. Behind them, 45 feet into the corridor, a Derro Shardcaller sits cross-legged on a flat stone, working a bone charm on a cord.

The DWDs raise their axes as the party arrives but hold. The Deep Watch maintains form even here; their eyes carry the thousand-yard gaze of soldiers who've heard too much from below.

**DWD-A (flat voice):** "Third Gate is sealed. Turn back. This place is closed by High King's decree."

A silence. The Shardcaller stops working the charm.

**DWD-A (jaw tight):** "Run."

Combat begins.

## Findings

### Bugs auto-fixed
- **MQ-12 already-acted CW guard added to `derro-shardcaller.md`**: Clarified that if the highest-init alive ally has already acted this round AND has ≤20 HP, hold the CW charge (the ally won't survive to collect next round). Extends the DD-8/DD-25 HP guard to the "already-acted" scenario. 9 consecutive TP cycles confirm this pattern. Diff: added one sentence to the CW guidance in the Tactics section.

### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS
- **FI-185 NEW (sim bug)**: Phase B script used both CW and TR as bonus actions in R2 (violates FI-181; only one bonus action per turn). Additional root cause: TR should not have fired at all — SC was ~45 ft from PCs, well outside the 15-ft trigger radius. The .md is correct ("TR fires at start of own turn when threat is within 15 ft"). Simulation code did not check SC-to-PC distance, triggering TR whenever any melee PC was alive. Not a spec fix; .md already correct.

- **FI-186 NEW (sim bug)**: Phase B script re-rolled Barrage damage (3d6 = 9) instead of using the value from `roll_combat_action` output (14 piercing). The "ASKING PLAYER" format in the dispatch output means the DM captures the printed damage and applies it to all saves. The sim should parse the dispatch output rather than re-rolling. Impact: party took 9 piercing instead of the correct 14 on failed saves — Bazgar was at 35/49 and Sabriel at 30/44 after R1, not 40/49 and 35/44.

- **FI-14 PARTIAL BREAK (9th cycle confirm)**: Sabriel hit by Barrage for 9 piercing (first Barrage hit on Sabriel in 9 TP cycles). However, DWDs never directly targeted Sabriel — both DWDs focused Marwen (lower HP). Core FI-14 pattern (DWDs never target Sabriel directly) still holds; Barrage is the only NPC attack that reaches her.

- **DD-39/MQ-12 CONFIRMED (9th consecutive TP cycle)**: SC CW'd DWD-B in R2. DWD-B had already acted in R2 (same init tick, DWD-B > SC). CW advantage sits in reserve for R3. DWD-B dies at R3 start (Bazgar double-hit). CW wasted again. With newly applied MQ-12 guard: DWD-B at 18 HP at time of CW → ≤20 HP guard should have blocked it. Auto-fix should address future cycles.

- **CW withheld correctly R1 (positive)**: SC correctly skipped CW when planning Barrage. 9th consecutive correct application. The tactics note ("CW is for Multiattack turns only, never Barrage turns") continues to fire correctly.

- **Pack Tactics Voice not tracked (ongoing)**: DWD-A and DWD-B both hit Marwen in R1 before SC acted. Per PTV passive, Marwen should have had disadvantage on the Barrage DC 13 Dex save. She rolled 18 — outcome unchanged, but the passive was not enforced in sim. Existing log note (FI-13/PTV) stands; DM reminder text is in the SC .md.

### Mechanical questions
- **MQ-77 NEW**: Phase B simulation convention — when dispatching an area-effect action (Barrage, Void Scream, etc.) that outputs "ASKING PLAYER: roll save, failers take X," the simulation script should parse X from the dispatch output rather than rolling fresh dice. The dispatch roll is the canonical result; re-rolling creates a dual-stream discrepancy. Recommend: add a note to the cron-prompt briefing (or the Phase B preamble in thrulm-rotation comments) specifying this convention.
