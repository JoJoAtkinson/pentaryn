### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-NEW-BEL6-A (6th cycle confirm FI-165):** VS R1 with beholder winning init (21) → 40 psychic → Bazgar 9/49 + frightened, Sabriel 4/44 + frightened before either PC acts. This pattern has now occurred in 5 of 6 BEL cycles where the beholder won initiative. Bazgar and Sabriel are functionally eliminated as offensive threats in R1: both miss 2/2 attacks at disadvantage. *DESIGN DECISION: The VS R1 opener when beholder wins init effectively turns a 3v1 into a 1v1 (Marwen vs beholder) from turn one. This is the intended difficulty, but there is no counterplay window for the melee PCs. The only mitigation is if the party has a readied action or Sabriel uses LoH to keep Bazgar alive long enough to act (which she can't at 4 HP). Consider whether a 20-ft or 25-ft VS radius (instead of 30 ft) would give the wizard at 30-40 ft enough distance to position out of range.*

**FI-NEW-BEL6-B (5th consecutive BEL cycle confirm FI-184):** Sabriel's smite slots entirely unused at death — L1×4, L2×3, L3×2 all intact. She was downed in R2 by a Maw hit (17 piercing from 4 HP after VS). The FI-125 LoH-countermeasure fix was never triggered because Sabriel was downed before using LoH. Five consecutive BEL cycles where Sabriel contributes zero smite damage. *DESIGN DECISION: Sabriel's combat identity (divine smiter) is structurally disabled by the VS opener in BEL slice. The only way she contributes is if the party closes before the beholder acts. Recommend: If running BEL at table, consider giving Sabriel a readied attack on the beholder's approach — this would at least guarantee 1-2 hits before VS.*

**FI-NEW-BEL6-C (new):** `compel_thrall` bonus action dead weight R2-R5 — beholder spent 4 consecutive bonus actions directing Thrall-A toward Marwen, but Thrall-A hit 0/3 attempts (to-hit rolls: 9, 12, 8 vs AC15 — all miss despite only needing 14+). Once Bazgar and Sabriel are downed, `shrine_drift` would be more useful for repositioning toward Marwen or retreating. The compel_thrall Cha save is DC16, meaning many thralls resist the command anyway. *Recommendation: Add a tactics note — "Once both melee PCs are down, switch bonus action from compel_thrall to shrine_drift (close on surviving ranged PC) or save it."*

**FI-NEW-BEL6-D (new, POSITIVE):** VS second fire at R5 (d6=6 on 4th recharge check after R1 fire) delivered the killing blow to last-standing Marwen at 5 HP with 34 psychic. She missed the DC16 Wis save by exactly 1 (rolled 15). The tension of "will VS recharge?" is genuine — it failed 3 consecutive checks before firing. *Positive finding: the recharge-6 mechanic created real suspense (would Marwen survive long enough?). The near-miss save adds drama. This is VS working as intended.*

**FI-NEW-BEL6-E (POSITIVE, FI-128 fix confirmed):** Beholder correctly switched from DD (3 LA) to VR (2 LA) in R4 when Marwen's highest remaining slot was L1. The FI-128 fix ("DD only worth 3 LA when target has L2+ slots") functioned correctly — VR for 22 force was more efficient than DD for +2 temp HP. First BEL cycle where the FI-128 fix was testable with this exact scenario.
