### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **MQ-TW-19-A (sim error):** Sim fired Berserk after the Rager moved 25 ft, violating the "Rager cannot move on this turn" prerequisite from both the DB spec and the .md Tactics section. Spec is correct. The sim has no movement-gate on Berserk. Corrected play: Rager would have Multiattacked R1. Outcome is narratively similar (2 swings vs 3-arc sweep, comparable damage), but this is a recurring sim gap for any harness running the Rager.

- **FI-TW-19-A (feel):** Berserk R1 hit 1/3 swings (10 dmg to Bazgar; Sabriel AC19 and Marwen AC15 both missed). AC 18–19 wall blocks 55–65% of Berserk swings. The Berserk sounds threatening but delivers ≈1 hit across the 3-target sweep in this run — less than a Multiattack against a single target would provide. Confirmed pattern across multiple cycles.

- **FI-TW-19-B (feel):** Taunt window too narrow — Taunt fired twice (R1 Marwen PASS, R2 Marwen FAIL), but the Rager was dead by R3 before Taunt meaningfully influenced combat. The R2 taunted-Marwen missed her Fire Bolt anyway (disadv Fire Bolt misses SC). Taunt's window is only as wide as the Rager's lifespan, which is 2–3 rounds.

- **FI-TW-19-C (feel):** SC solo at 5 HP R2–R3 is a formality, not a threat. Throws 2/3 shots hit Marwen for 11 total in R2, then dies R3 to a Scorching Ray. Barrage R1 spread (avg 7 dmg per PC) is cosmetic in context. SC's toolkit is designed for medium-range kiting, but a 5 HP glass cannon with no Barrage remaining is eliminated by any action Marwen takes.

### DESIGN DECISIONS (human review required)

- **DD-TW-19-1 — CW range lockout (19th consecutive cycle confirm):** Call Weakness (30 ft range) cannot reach the Rager in tank-wall formation. SC starts 40 ft behind Rager; as Rager charges forward, the gap widens to 50+ ft. CW 3/3 charges unused at fight end. This has been logged every tank-wall cycle since cycle 1. **Recommendation (unchanged):** Raise CW range to 60 ft, OR explicitly note in SC's Tactics: "In tank-wall formation, SC starts ≤25 ft behind the Rager to keep CW in range." At current range, CW is structurally dead in this formation.

- **DD-TW-19-2 — Rager durability (19th consecutive cycle confirm):** Rager absorbed 38 dmg in R1 (Bazgar AS 10 + Fireball 28), ending at 14/52 HP before Sabriel even acted. 52 HP cannot absorb a L5 Fireball (avg 28) plus incidental melee. In 19 cycles, Rager consistently dies R2–R3 before the SC's support abilities (CW, Barrage rotation) can compound. **Recommendation:** Raise Rager HP to 65–70 (add 2 HD), or give Rager a damage-reduction / resistance trigger (e.g., "first time reduced below half HP, gain resistance to one damage type that round" — flavor fits Madness Endurance). Neither should be auto-fixed; this requires balance author sign-off.
