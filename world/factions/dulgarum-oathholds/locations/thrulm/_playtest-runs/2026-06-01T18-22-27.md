### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS
- **FI-FC25-A (25th cycle confirm, FI-FC16-C / FI-FC24-A pattern):** Beholder init 28 >> Marwen init 4 — Marwen DISINTEGRATED before she acts every time beholder rolls high initiative. 25 consecutive final-confrontation runs: this is the structural constant of the slice. Not a bug. The fight design relies on Marwen dying early — confirm this is intended.
- **FI-FC25-B (NEW — auth only, not previously logged):** When Marwen is disintegrated, Sabriel has no valid LoH target. If the table misses the disintegration announcement, Sabriel wastes her action on an impossible revive, then VS fires and ends the fight. The .md disintegration note exists but is embedded in a long paragraph. Recommend: add a `**⚠️ DM PAUSE BEFORE FIGHT:** announce disintegration rules before combat begins — Marwen's glass AC and the beholder's R1 priority make this the expected R1 outcome` marker to the tactics section.
- **FI-FC25-C (NEW — auth):** All 4 thralls missed Bazgar R1 (auth dispatch to-hits: 17, 4, 9, 7 all vs AC18). Thralls are cosmetic damage dealers at AC18. They fill initiative slots and attract beholder compel_thrall attention, but deal 0 expected damage per round vs Bazgar. This is 3rd cross-run data point (SIM-FC25-C confirms the thrall miss pattern is more severe than previously modeled due to sim errors inflating their hit rate).
- **FI-FC25-D (VS-R2 TPK, 25th cycle confirm of FI-FC24-A):** DR fires R1 → kills caster → VS available R2 (never used R1) → wipes survivors. This 2-round kill pattern is extremely reliable when beholder wins init. No change recommended — it is the intended feel of the encounter.
- **MQ-FC25-A (NEW):** `compel_thrall` bonus action value collapses when caster is disintegrated R1. With Marwen gone, directing thralls at Bazgar (AC18) or Sabriel (AC19) has ~25–30% hit rate — marginal. .md says "directing thralls at the divine martial is a near-wasted command" but doesn't address the all-melee-PC case. Recommend: add note in Compel Thrall tactics: "If primary caster (Marwen) is down, skip compel_thrall — use shrine_drift instead to reposition the beholder out of melee."
