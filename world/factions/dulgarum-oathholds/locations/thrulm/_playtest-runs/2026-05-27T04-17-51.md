### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

**FI-163** — *Tank Wall 10th-cycle: victory R3, party at 83–92% HP.*
This is the 10th run of slice #2. Party wins comfortably every cycle (1× R5, 2× R3, 2× R3, 1× R3, 1× R4 [prior log summary]). The fight does not threaten the party at level 5. The slice is tagged as testing "taunt + call_weakness pairing" but the mechanics never land simultaneously: CW fires 0 times this cycle, Taunt fires 1 time but the Rager dies before it swings again. At this point, 10 cycles of Tank Wall confirm it is structurally insufficient as a standalone slice vs level 5.
**Recommendation (for human review):** Upgrade tank-wall to 2× Rager + 1× SC, or accept that this is an encounter 2 sub-encounter and redesign the slice to test a specific mechanic rather than a fight outcome.

**FI-164** — *Initiative collapse is the primary Tank Wall failure mode (pattern across cycles).*
In this cycle: Sabriel 22, Marwen 18, Bazgar 14 — all three PCs act before Rager (8) and SC (6). By the time Rager acts, it is at 8/52 HP, has missed two attacks, and is dead before the end of R2. This pattern appears in multiple Tank Wall cycles. The Rager has +1 Dex (low initiative), and against a well-statted party, losing initiative effectively removes the slice's primary mechanic test. The party dealt 44 damage to the Rager before it took its first action.
**Recommendation:** Either give the Rager a higher initiative bonus (+3–4 Dex-equivalent) or build Tank Wall around a surprised-party scenario where NPCs roll higher.

**MQ-68** — *Slow (3rd-level) vs dual low-WIS derro: both failed in R1.*
Marwen cast Slow in R1 and both Rager (WIS 9, -1 mod) and SC (WIS 4, +2 mod) failed DC 14 immediately. Slowed Rager lost 2 AC (14→12 effective with Slow's modifier), limited to 1 attack/action. Slowed SC limited to 1 action/turn. A single concentration spell from a level-5 Wizard essentially solved the entire encounter. The derro's WIS saves are expected to be low (CR 2 creatures), but the combination of Slow's action-economy suppression plus the party having full action advantage means there's no tension window.
**This is a feel observation, not a balance fix — Slow should work.** But if Tank Wall is intended as a Taunt/CW test, a Wizard with Slow trivially bypasses the intended interaction (Slow-ed Rager can still Taunt but can't demonstrate Berserk's multi-target sweep, and CW on a Slowed Rager wastes the charge since it gets 1 attack anyway).

**DD-52** — *Taunt-on-taunted-Scorching-Ray confirmed mechanical interaction: R2 disadv applies.*
Marwen was taunted by Rager (failed DC 12 Cha save). In R2, Marwen fired Scorching Ray at SC (Rager was dead, only valid target). Scorching Ray IS an attack roll (confirmed by existing rager.md note on MQ-36), so Taunt's disadvantage correctly applied. Marwen hit 2/3 rays at disadvantage. Mechanical note works as documented.
**This is not a bug.** The interaction is correctly documented in derro-rager.md. Logging as confirmation.
