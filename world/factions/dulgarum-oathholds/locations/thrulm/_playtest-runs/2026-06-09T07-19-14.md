### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

- **FI-TP35-A Barrage-0/3-recharge 35th-cycle** — SC rolled 1, 1, 3 on recharge across R2–R4. Shard-Barrage never re-fired. SC spent 3 straight rounds as a pure Shard-Throw multiattacker. The patrol's signature area threat expresses only once. At the recharge-5-6 rate (33% per round), there is a ~30% chance of 0 recharges in 3 attempts — this occurs often enough to feel like a one-shot opener rather than a rotational threat. Carry from prior cycles. Consider recharge 4–6 (50% per round) to let Barrage express ~once more per patrol-length fight.

- **FI-TP35-B CW-dispatch-gap ongoing** — SC correctly CW'd DW-A (init 10 > 7; DW-A acts after SC; HP 27 > 20 guard ✓). Advantage was granted but not expressed in the simulation's manual re-rolls. DW-A hit Marwen (roll 21) on a crossbow shot — would have hit with or without advantage. No outcome delta this cycle, but the gap persists. Carry MQ-TP35-A.

- **FI-TP35-C VICTORY-R4 35th consecutive threshold-patrol cycle** — Party ended at 57%/66%/80% HP. No deaths. 4-round fight vs 3 in 34th-cycle (split Web save in R1 let DW-A remain active one extra round). Encounter remains warm-up difficulty. Carry FI-TP34-A, FI-TP34-C. Recommend: raise SC HP to 45–50 (prevents smite one-shot and gives it a rotation window), or add 1 thrall.

- **FI-TP35-D Restrained-DW Tactical Drilling cancellation — sim gap carry** — DW-B Restrained from R1; attacks in R2 should be straight roll (Restrained disadv + Tactical Drilling adv cancel per 5e RAW). Sim applied advantage-only, inflating DW-B output by ~10–15%. Already documented in FIX-TP33-A (.md). No new spec fix needed; note for at-table DM: enforce straight roll for Restrained + Tactical Drilling.

- **FI-TP35-E Web-slot-economy** — Marwen cast Web 3 consecutive rounds, exhausting all L2 slots by R3. Pattern: Marwen's optimal play burns her entire L2 pool on a single patrol encounter. At a table with multiple encounters per day, this is a meaningful resource tax; in a single-session context it feels overpowered. Not a spec bug; DM note that this encounter occurs mid-dungeon, not as a day-opener.
