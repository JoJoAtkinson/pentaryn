### Feel issues / DESIGN DECISIONS

#### DD-38 (NEW): Altitude retreat conflicts with Drain Divinity range
- **Context:** R3. Beholder HP 40/110 (below 60 HP threshold). `shrine_drift` bonus moved beholder to 65 ft altitude per "prefer ranged, hold 60+ ft up" tactic. This placed the beholder >30 ft above all PCs, making Drain Divinity (range 30 ft) unreachable. The below-60-HP altitude preference and the Drain Divinity priority order (DD-28 fix: "Drain Divinity FIRST") are mutually exclusive when the beholder retreats high while PCs remain on the chamber floor.
- **Observed in:** 2nd empty-void cycle. Not observed in cycle 1 (beholder stayed lower during the 7-round fight).
- **Recommendation (log only):** Two options: (a) Add a precedence rule — "below 60 HP altitude preference is secondary to Drain Divinity range if a slot-holder is still alive with L2+ slots; stay within 30 ft of that target even if below 60 HP"; (b) Increase Drain Divinity range from 30 ft to 60 ft (matches Telepathy range conceptually). Option (b) is a DB spec change — requires human sign-off. Option (a) is authoring-only but changes encounter feel (beholder stays close to divine martial even when damaged, creating more dangerous melee exposure). Do NOT auto-fix.

#### FI-7 (NEW): Antireality 0-fire structural pattern in empty-void
- **Context:** 0/~4 firing rounds in 2nd cycle (matches 0/7 from cycle 1). Root cause: Antireality fires on attack rolls that hit and deal ≥10 damage. In empty-void, the beholder spends most of the fight at 40–65 ft altitude. Sabriel (the melee Divine Smite attacker Antireality is designed to counter) never reaches melee range during the fight because: (1) the beholder uses shrine_drift to maintain altitude; (2) the solo retreat path (DD-20 fix) puts the beholder near the shaft only below 30 HP — the beholder in this run never dropped below 40 HP. Bazgar's javelins are below the 10-damage threshold. Sacred Flame / Fireball / Scorching Ray / Void Ray are all saves, not attacks.
- **Structural observation:** Antireality is a melee-anti-smite reaction with no melee opponent in the empty-void slice. Its design value is only testable in slice #3 (beholder-escorts-limited) where party closes to melee, or in the final-confrontation extended endgame. The 0-fire pattern in empty-void is not a malfunction — it's correct behavior given the fight geometry.
- **Recommendation (log only):** Document expected Antireality scenarios: "In altitude-dominant fights (empty-void, beholder-escorts-limited R1–R3), Antireality rarely fires. Antireality demonstrates in R3+ of melee-close fights or after the solo retreat (shaft lip hover) puts the beholder within 5 ft of Sabriel." No DB change needed.

#### FI-3 (2nd cycle confirmation — STILL PENDING HUMAN REVIEW)
- **Context:** R3 confirmed. Beholder at 40 HP, Disintegration on cooldown, Void Scream on cooldown. Beholder drifted to 65 ft altitude and took no main action for the entire turn. This is the 2nd consecutive empty-void run confirming the same pattern (cycle 1: R4–R5 main action gap; cycle 2: R3 main action gap). The cooldown window for Disintegration (recharge 5–6, ~1/3 per round) combined with Void Scream (recharge 6, 1/6 per round) creates gaps of 1–3 rounds where no main-action ranged attack is available, yet the beholder's below-60-HP tactics demand altitude rather than melee.
- **No change this run.** Explicitly flagged as NOT auto-fix; pending human review of below-60-HP intended behavior. Recommendation remains: add text "Main action is still Void Ray [legendary] or Multiattack from altitude — do not skip" to the below-60-HP tactics clause. Also note that Void Ray is a LEGENDARY action (2 actions), not a main action — the tactics text "prefer ranged" implicitly assumes Disintegration is always available as the main action, which is false when on cooldown.
