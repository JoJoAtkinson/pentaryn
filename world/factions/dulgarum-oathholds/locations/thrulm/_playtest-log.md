Option (a) recommended.** Outside cron blast radius (scripts/dnd_roller.py). See MQ-54.

  2. **FI-115 (3rd structural confirm — FI-80): Unstable Form activated but never used.** 3rd consecutive shrine-wedge cycle. STD-Alpha took 19 damage in R1 (≥10 threshold triggered correctly), but was beaten to 1 HP in R2 before acting with UF advantage, then downed by SG aura. **Pattern is confirmed across 3 cycles: UF triggers but STD doesn't survive to use it.** DM should be aware UF is a "rare, spectacular moment" mechanic — not a reliable per-round feature. Without self-damage, STD-Alpha would have had 35 HP entering R2 instead of 29, potentially surviving 1 extra round. The .md is explicit ("regardless of whether the attack hits") — so this appears intentional. **If intentional: add a DM callout in the .md noting that OBR is a calculated risk vs high-AC PCs (Sabriel AC 19 → OBR hits ~25% of the time, self-damages 100% of the time; each miss costs the STD an expected ~2.5 HP). Resources cost: 1 L3 + 1 L2 + 1 L1 slot + Action Surge — all recoverable on a short rest. **DESIGN DECISION: If shrine-wedge is meant to be a meaningful challenge (not just a warm-up), options: (a) 3× STDs (cheapest, most raid-like); (b) add 1 thrall-derro as a distraction; (c) add altar lair action (2d6 necrotic, 30-ft burst, Dex DC 13 save, every other round — supported by existing shrine-bound lore); (d) accept 2-STD slice as "atmosphere encounter" — sets tone, doesn't tax resources, lets players feel the altar zone mechanics before the beholder.**
