## TACTICAL QUICK-SCAN

**When party enters combat, ask:**

1. **Are there ranged derro (Shardcaller)?** → They're 40+ feet back. Prioritize them or they buff allies.
2. **Is there a tank (Rager)?** → It will Taunt and mark attackers. Don't ignore it or it taunts your casters.
3. **Are there Shrine-Touched?** → They resist psychic/necrotic and are faster near altar. Reposition or use fire/radiant.
4. **Is the beholder here?** → It's hovering above, picking off spellcasters. Your casters need cover or interruption.
5. **How many Thralls?** → They move on beholder's turn, not their own. They're minions; eliminate to reduce beholder's action economy.

## ONE-LINER TACTICS PER TYPE

| Type | What They Do | How To Exploit It |
|------|---|---|
| **Deep Watch** | Stand in formation, focus-fire targets | Break formation, flank, separate from group |
| **Thrall** | Move where beholder commands | Kill beholder = thralls stand confused |
| **Shrine-Touched** | Stay near altar, resist magic, hit harder when hurt | Pull them away from altar or use non-magical damage |
| **Shardcaller** | Hang back 40+ ft, call weaknesses, weaken saves | Rush them or target from range, prevent Weakness calls |
| **Rager** | Charge in, tank damage, mark attackers | Let them hit your tank, go around them to reach others |
| **Beholder** | Hover, disintegrate, drain divinity, avoid melee | Get close to it, interrupt rituals, protect casters |

## ROUND-BY-ROUND CHECKLIST

**Start of Round:**
1. [CHECK] Which derro go this initiative count?
2. [CHECK] Is the Beholder using Clay-Shaping? (If yes, it can't move/attack; thralls shield it)
3. [CHECK] Shardcaller giving Weakness to allies? (-2 to enemy saves for one of them)
4. [CHECK] Rager Taunt active? (Check if anyone has disadvantage vs non-Rager targets)
5. [CHECK] Are Shrine-Touched within 60 ft of altar? (They move +10 ft and resist psychic/necrotic)

**When Beholder Acts:**
- Does it have a caster lined up? → Disintegration Ray
- Any clerics/paladins? → Drain Divinity (takes spell slots)
- Otherwise → Void Ray + reposition or Void Scream (full party damage)

**When Derro Acts:**
- Rager? → Charges nearest, uses Taunt if vulnerable target present
- Shardcaller? → Calls Weakness on whoever is winning, then attacks/retreats
- Shrine-Touched? → Ancient Resonance on groups, melee on solo targets
- Deep Watch? → Focus-fire with allies, Cunning Action to Disengage
- Thrall? → Moves toward beholder's target (predictable, use it)

## WHAT KILLS WHAT FASTEST

| Target Type | Best Attack Type | Best Spell | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Deep Watch** | Ranged, any | Magic Missile, Scorching Ray | Low HP, no resistances |
| **Thrall** | Force, fire, melee | Magic Missile, Scorching Ray | Low HP; has psychic resistance — avoid psychic |
| **Shrine-Touched** | Fire, radiant, nonmagic | Fireball, Guiding Bolt | Resistant to psychic/necrotic |
| **Shardcaller** | Melee, close-in | Misty Step + melee, Command (retreat) | Squishy if you get close |
| **Rager** | Magic (it has no resist except poison) | Magic Missile, Eldritch Blast | No saves needed, pure damage |
| **Beholder** | Sanctified weapons, Dispel Magic | True Strike + sanctified attack | Resistances and flight = annoying |

## IF YOUR PC ASKS "WHAT DO I DO?"

**Fighter?** → Go for the Rager or Shardcaller. Rager will Taunt and keep you there; good deal for you.

**Spellcaster?** → Hide or get cover. Beholder targets you. If no cover, Shardcaller probably just called a Weakness on you.

**Cleric?** → Beholder wants to Drain Divinity from you. Position defensively, heal from range.

**Rogue?** → Circle the Ragers, sneak-attack the Shardcaller in the back. Thralls are good Sneak Attack batteries.

**Paladin?** → Beholder HATES you (sanctified weapons). Engage it directly while others handle derro.
