### 2) Ardenhaven (AR-den-HAY-vun) ‚Äî The Haven Realm

- People: Humans (with small halfling/gnome/etc. minorities)
- Theme: Imperfect hope, post-war recovery, trying to do the right thing
- Government: Constitutional / Council Monarchy (monarch limited by lords, guilds, temples)
- Background: Starting region for heroes. Decent at heart but weighed down by war scars, edge corruption, and thin defenses. Treaties with the Empire remain tense. It‚Äôs a place where the party can genuinely make things better over time.

### 3) Calderon Imperium (CAL-der-on im-PEER-ee-um)

- Nickname: "The Ashen Empire" (enemy slang referencing soot-choked factories and scorched-earth campaigns)
- People: Humans (with subject peoples and slaves/serfs)
- Theme: Oppressive order, unity by fear, human corruption
- Government: Centralized Imperial Autocracy with vast bureaucracy and secret police
- Background: Determined to unify humanity, the Imperium waged brutal wars that forged the Haven Realm in opposition. A stalemate birthed uneasy trade and colder peace. Now the Imperium quietly rearms and prepares for the next existential threat. Most citizens stay not from fear, but prosperity: safe roads, stable grain prices, functioning courts outweigh Ardenhaven's chaotic freedoms. Compulsory military service creates a veteran class deeply invested in the ideology of "order before all, unity through law." The use of undead auxiliaries at the front lines is an unspoken truth‚Äîeveryone knows, no one discusses it openly. It's a grim calculus: better the risen dead hold the border than more living sons and daughters come home in boxes. The state frames it as pragmatic deterrence; most citizens quietly accept it as the price of security, though the moral weight sits heavy on the national conscience.
- Demonym: Calderonite (cal-DER-on-ite)
- Adjectival form: Calderonic (cal-der-ON-ik)
- Capital: Ironstead
- Doc: [calderon-imperium.md](calderon-imperium/_overview.md)

### 4) Merrowgate (MER-oh-gate) ‚Äî The City of Bargains

- Government: Merchant Oligarchy / Corrupt Republic (merchant princes, guildmasters, crime lords)
- Background: A neutral trade hub where everything is for sale. Prosperity flows from a mysterious Silent Patron‚Äîglimpsed as devil, dragon, god of wealth, or living city. Major contracts and policies subtly bend to the Patron's long game while the council pretends it leads. Open to all who can pay; allegiance is measured in gold, not blood.
- Demonym: Merrowgater (MER-oh-gay-ter)
- Adjectival form: Merrowgatean (MER-oh-GAY-tee-an)
- Capital: Merrowgate
- Doc: [merrowgate.md](merrowgate/_overview.md)

### 8) Garrok Confederation (GAR-rok) ‚Äî Western Orc Confederation

- People: Orcs (with some mixed-heritage and warrior allies)
- Theme: Harsh honor, earned respect, distant but negotiable

- Adjectival form: Garrokan (GAR-uh-kan)

- Capital: Rakthok
