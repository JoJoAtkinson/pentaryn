# Gnoll Warrior

**HP** 11 (2d8+2) **·** **AC** 14 (leather armor) **·** **Speed** 40 ft. **·** **Saves** — **·** **Darkvision 60 ft.** **·** **CR** 1/2 (100 XP each)

> Unnamed gnoll warriors of the Gar-Vally pack. Default count is 2 (the matron rarely rides without at least a pair). Pack Tactics apply when an ally is within 5 ft of the target.

> Action mechanics live in `combat-runner/actions.jsonl` (DB) — see the launcher-injected **Ready actions** reference for verbs and call signatures.

---

## Start-of-turn checklist (Haiku's responsibility, not in the DB)

1. Recount alive members.
2. Pack Tactics: advantage on attacks when an ally (gnoll or hyena) is within 5 ft of the target.
3. **Sun-Burdened**: disadvantage on attack rolls in direct sunlight — the DM tags this manually if relevant.

---

## Tactics — when the DM asks "what does it do?"

- **Default:** close to melee, multiattack the nearest target, exploit Pack Tactics.
- **Below half HP:** attempts to flee toward the matron or reinforcements.
- **Sunlight:** more cautious; prefers shadows.

---

## Description

A hyena-headed humanoid in scarred leather armor, smelling of blood and wild sage. Young and unproven — has not yet earned a victim-name.
