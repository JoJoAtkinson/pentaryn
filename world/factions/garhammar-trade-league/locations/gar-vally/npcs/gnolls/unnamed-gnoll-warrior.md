## Combat Traits

**Pack Tactics.** The gnoll has advantage on melee attack rolls against a creature if at least one other gnoll ally is within 5 feet of the target and the ally is not incapacitated.

**Sun-Burdened.** The gnoll has disadvantage on attack rolls while in direct sunlight.

---

## Actions

**Multiattack.** The gnoll makes two melee attacks: one with its bite and one with its claws.

**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.

**Claws.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 4 (1d4 + 2) slashing damage.

---

## Tactics

**Priority Targets:** Any lone enemy; casters if possible.

**Movement:** Aggressive; closes to melee quickly. Uses pack tactics to flank.

**Desperation:** Below half HP, attempts to flee to reinforcements.

**Environmental:** Avoids sunlit areas if possible; prefers shadows and moonlight.

---

## Interaction Notes

**Motivations:** Obeys the hunt-captain and matron. Young and unproven; seeks to earn a victim-name by making its first kill of a humanoid.

**Voice & Personality:** Communicates in guttural barks and growls. Does not speak Common.

**De-escalation Hooks:** Showing reverence for the dead, offering tribute, acknowledging the hunt as sacred.

---

## Description & Flavor

A hyena-headed humanoid, scarred and muscular. Its fur is matted, stinking of blood and wild sage. Claws rake the ground as it moves. It wears simple leather armor and bone-carved talismans. Its eyes are bright and feral—the eyes of a predator that has not yet made its first true kill.
