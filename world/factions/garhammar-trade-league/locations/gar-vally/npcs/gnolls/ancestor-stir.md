## Combat Traits

**Undead Nature.** The Ancestor-Stir has no need to breathe, eat, drink, or sleep.

**Ethereal Manifestation.** The Ancestor-Stir is partially incorporeal. It can move through creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain. It takes 5 (1d10) force damage if it ends its turn inside an object.

**Lunar Fury.** When a moon is visible (night, outdoors), the Ancestor-Stir's attack rolls gain a +2 bonus and its damage rolls gain 1d6 bonus damage.

**Ancient Sorrow.** When the Ancestor-Stir is first manifested, all living gnolls within 60 feet that can see it must succeed on a DC 14 Wisdom save or be frightened for 1 minute. A creature can repeat the save at the end of each of its turns.

---

## Actions

**Multiattack.** The Ancestor-Stir makes two claw attacks or uses Wail twice.

**Claws.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 10 (2d6 + 3) slashing damage plus 7 (2d6) psychic damage. The target must succeed on a DC 14 Wisdom save or be frightened of the Ancestor-Stir until the end of its next turn.

**Wail (Recharge 5-6).** The Ancestor-Stir emits a piercing wail. Each creature within 20 feet that can hear it must make a DC 14 Wisdom save or take 22 (4d10) psychic damage. A successful save reduces the damage by half.

---

## Reactions

**Incorporeal Escape.** When the Ancestor-Stir takes damage, it can use its reaction to move up to its speed without provoking opportunity attacks. It can move through creatures and objects but takes 5 (1d10) force damage if it ends its turn inside an object.

---

## Tactics

**Priority Targets:** The party. The Ancestor-Stir is not here to negotiate—it will attack with primal fury.

**Movement:** Aggressive. Uses incorporeal movement to bypass obstacles.

**Desperation:** Cannot retreat or flee. The Ancestor-Stir is determined and will only stop when destroyed.

**Environmental:** Stronger at night under moonlight. If combat moves to daylight, loses the bonus but continues fighting.

---

## Description & Flavor

The matron's corpse shudders. For a moment, nothing happens. Then—a terrible sound, like wind through a canyon filled with bones. A shape rises from the body, incorporeal and terrible. It is *not* the matron—it is something *older*, something *beneath* her. The echo of a previous matron, a ghost given form by interrupted succession.

The spirit is hyena-shaped but wrong: too large, too many eyes, fur made of shadow. Its howl carries voices—dozens of them, generations of matrons speaking at once, their voices distorted into a wail of grief and rage.

---

## Lore & Background

The Ancestor-Stir is what happens when a gnoll matron dies without a proper successor. The accumulated wisdom, memory, and power of all the eaten mothers has nowhere to go. Instead of passing into the next vessel, it is trapped, stranded between life and death, manifesting as a vengeful spirit.

It is not evil—it is *devastated*. Generations of inheritance, shattered. The lineage, broken. The mothers' sacrifice, wasted.

If the party sees this creature, they are witnessing the direct consequence of their actions.

---

## Treasure & Rewards

**Immediate Loot:** None. The Ancestor-Stir is incorporeal and carries nothing.

**Corpse Treasure:** When defeated, the Ancestor-Stir dissipates. There is no body to loot. Only shadow and silence remain.

**Intangibles:** If the party survives this encounter, they have earned the eternal enmity of the gnoll clan. The matron's lineage is gone. The gnolls will not forgive.
