## Combat Traits

**Pack Tactics.** Jorran has advantage on melee attack rolls against a creature if at least one other gnoll ally is within 5 feet of the target and the ally is not incapacitated.

**Sun-Burdened.** Jorran has disadvantage on attack rolls while in direct sunlight.

**Lunar Hunt.** When a moon is visible (night, outdoors), Jorran gains advantage on Stealth checks and adds 1d4 to the first attack roll each round.

---

## Actions

**Multiattack.** Jorran makes two attacks: one with its bite and one with its claws.

**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit (or +7 in moonlight), reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 7 (1d8 + 3) piercing damage.

**Claws.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit (or +7 in moonlight), reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 6 (1d6 + 3) slashing damage.

**Prospector's Pickaxe.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 8 (1d10 + 3) piercing damage.

---

## Bonus Actions

**Disengage.** Jorran can use its bonus action to disengage from combat.

---

## Reactions

**Eat the Fallen (Reaction, Once Per Turn).** When another gnoll within 5 feet of Jorran drops to 0 HP, Jorran can use its reaction to perform the eating-rite. Jorran gains 5 temporary HP and advantage on its next attack roll before the end of its next turn.

---

## Tactics

**Priority Targets:** Enemy leaders, casters, anyone who looks weak or wounded.

**Movement:** Aggressive. Uses Pack Tactics to flank. If Lunar Hunt is active, uses Stealth to position before attacking.

**Desperation:** Below half HP, attempts to retreat toward the matron or reinforcements.

**Environmental:** Prefers night and moonlight. In daylight, is more cautious.

---

## Interaction Notes

**Motivations:** A proven warrior, named after his first kill (the dwarf Jorran). Loyal to the matron and clan. Seeks to rise in status by leading hunts and protecting the valley.

**Voice & Personality:** Speaks in low growls and barks. Confident, commanding, respectful to the matron.

**Weaknesses (If Known):** Values honor in the hunt. If the party shows respect for the dead or the hunt itself, Jorran will pause and listen. Pride in his victim-name is a potential hook.

**De-escalation Hooks:** Acknowledging Jorran by his victim-name, showing respect for the hunt, offering tribute, or yielding with dignity.

---

## Description & Flavor

A massive gnoll, larger and more scarred than the others. It wears leather armor and a shield carved from bone. A dwarf-forged prospector's pickaxe hangs from a cord around its neck—the artifact of its first kill, worn as a trophy and talisman. When it speaks, its voice carries authority. Its eyes are bright with intelligence and pride.

---

## Lore & Background

Jorran earned his name by killing and eating the prospector Jorran many seasons ago. The prospector had wandered into the valley, seeking minerals. Jorran (the gnoll) tracked him, hunted him, and killed him in the shadow of a moonlit stone. By eating him, Jorran inherited the prospector's strength, cunning, and memories. Now, Jorran wears the prospector's name as his own identity, and carries the pickaxe as proof.

Jorran is a proven warrior and hunt-captain under the matron.

---

## Treasure & Rewards

**Immediate Loot:** Leather armor (50 gp), shield (50 gp), prospector's pickaxe (100 gp).

**Corpse Treasure:** If the party allows the gnolls to eat Jorran in the rite, his body is sacred. If they prevent the rite, items can be taken. Jorran's corpse is *very* important to the gnolls—desecrating it is a grave insult.

**Intangibles:** If Jorran is spared or yields, he becomes a liaison to the matron. If killed disrespectfully, the matron will seek vengeance.
