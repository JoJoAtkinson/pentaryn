## Combat Traits

**Pack Tactics.** The hyena has advantage on melee attack rolls against a creature if at least one other hyena or gnoll ally is within 5 feet of the target and the ally is not incapacitated.

---

## Actions

**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 4 (1d6 + 1) piercing damage.

---

## Tactics

**Priority Targets:** Wounded or fleeing enemies.

**Movement:** Pack animal; follows gnoll leaders and uses Pack Tactics relentlessly.

**Environmental:** Prefers open terrain for mobility.

---

## Description & Flavor

A wild hyena, larger than normal, bearing the scars of a hunting life. Its fur is matted and unkempt. Around its neck or hide, gnoll clan-marks are painted or tattooed, indicating it belongs to the matron's pack. Its eyes are bright with hunger and intelligence.

---

## Lore & Background

These hyenas are companions and hunting-partners to the gnolls of Gar-Vally. They are not owned in the way civilized people own animals—rather, they are kin-hunters, part of the clan's broader family. The gnolls respect them and include them in their hunts and feasts.

---

## Treasure & Rewards

**Immediate Loot:** Furs (worth 10 gp if carefully skinned).

**Corpse Treasure:** The hyena's body is sacred to the gnolls if allowed to perform the rite. Eating the slain hyena is an honor, not desecration.

**Intangibles:** If spared, the hyena may bond with the party.
