## Combat Traits

**Pack Tactics.** The lieutenant has advantage on melee attack rolls against a creature if at least one other gnoll ally is within 5 feet of the target and the ally is not incapacitated.

**Sun-Burdened.** The lieutenant has disadvantage on attack rolls while in direct sunlight.

**Lunar Hunt.** When a moon is visible (night, outdoors), the lieutenant gains advantage on Stealth checks and adds 1d4 to the first attack roll each round.

**Chosen Successor.** The lieutenant is the matron's chosen heir. If the matron is reduced to 0 HP while within 5 feet, the lieutenant can use her reaction to perform the **Rite of Succession**.

---

## Actions

**Multiattack.** The lieutenant makes two attacks: one with its bite and one with its claws.

**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit (or +6 in moonlight), reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 6 (1d8 + 2) piercing damage.

**Claws.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit (or +6 in moonlight), reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 5 (1d6 + 2) slashing damage.

---

## Reactions

**Eat the Fallen (Reaction, Once Per Turn).** When another gnoll within 5 feet of the lieutenant drops to 0 HP, the lieutenant can use its reaction to perform the eating-rite. The lieutenant gains 5 temporary HP and advantage on its next attack roll before the end of its next turn.

**Rite of Succession (Reaction, Once Per Combat).** When the matron is reduced to 0 HP while within 5 feet, the lieutenant can use her reaction to perform the rite:
- The matron dies immediately.
- The lieutenant **gains the matron's full stat block, spells, and abilities** for 1 minute.
- The lieutenant becomes the new matron and can continue fighting.
- If this rite is completed, **Ancestor-Stir does NOT manifest**.

---

## Tactics

**Priority Targets:** Enemies attacking the matron.

**Movement:** Defensive, near the matron. Stays within 5 feet so the rite can be performed if needed.

**Desperation:** If the matron falls and the rite is performed, immediately adopts the matron's tactics. If prevented, attempts to protect the matron's body.

**Environmental:** Prefers night and moonlight.

---

## Interaction Notes

**Motivations:** Loyal to the matron. Dreams of earning a victim-name and becoming a full warrior. Is preparing to be eaten by the matron and inherit her wisdom.

**Voice & Personality:** Speaks in soft growls and barks. Respectful, attentive, focused. When the rite is performed, her voice becomes layered and ancient.

**Weaknesses (If Known):** Young and untested. Can be intimidated if separated from the matron. Values loyalty and duty above all.

**De-escalation Hooks:** Showing respect for the matron, acknowledging the lieutenant's future role, avoiding attacks on the matron.

---

## Description & Flavor

A younger gnoll, leaner and less scarred than the warriors. She stands always near the matron, attentive and ready. Her fur is dark, her eyes sharp. She wears simple leather armor and carries no trophies or victim-names. She is waiting—waiting for her first true kill, for her chance to rise, for the day the matron passes her wisdom down.

---

## Lore & Background

The lieutenant is the matron's chosen successor. She has proven herself as a hunter and a follower, and the matron has selected her to be the next vessel of the mothers. When the matron dies, the lieutenant will perform the Rite of Succession, eating her predecessor and inheriting her power, her memories, and the names of all the mothers before.

Until that day, the lieutenant waits. She guards the matron, she hunts, she prepares. And she dreams of the names she will carry someday.

---

## Treasure & Rewards

**Immediate Loot:** Leather armor (50 gp).

**Corpse Treasure:** If killed, the lieutenant's body is mourned by the gnolls. Desecrating her body is an insult to the matron.

**Intangibles:** If the lieutenant performs the Rite of Succession and becomes the new matron, she inherits the matron's intentions toward the party.
