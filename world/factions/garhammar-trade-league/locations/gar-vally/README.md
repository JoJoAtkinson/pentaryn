# Gar-Vally — GM Quick Reference

**One-sentence:** A three-tier gnoll encounter with a lunar, matriarchal culture (not demon-worshippers). Social-first, combat-optional.

**Party Level:** 4 (3 players)  
**Encounter Type:** Escalating social/combat hybrid  
**Tiers:** Patrol → Hunt → Matron  
**Tone:** Anthropological horror; gnolls are people, not monsters

---

## Encounter Overview

| Tier | Location | Enemies | CR Budget | Type | Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **1A (default)** | Road, sunset | 2 warriors + 1 hyena | 337 XP (easy) | Social/optional combat | Escorted in OR fought |
| **1B (fallback)** | Road, night | 3 warriors + captain + 1 hyena | 825 XP (hard) | Combat-first | Ambush if party hostile |
| **2** | Valley clearing | Captain + 2 warriors + hyena | 675 XP (hard) | Social/combat | Parley or fight |
| **3** | Matron's hearth | Matron + lieutenant + 2 warriors | 2200 XP (deadly, fail-state) | Social, combat is death | Parley or retreat |

## Key Lore Points

- **They are not demon-bound.** The Yeenoghu tracts are lies.
- **They honor the dead by eating them.** This is sacred, not grotesque.
- **Gnolls take the names of their first victims.** Unnamed gnolls are young. Named gnolls wear stolen identities.
- **The matron is a vessel.** She carries eaten mothers within her. To kill her is to unmake a genealogy.
- **They worship the moon, fear the sun.** Environment matters.
