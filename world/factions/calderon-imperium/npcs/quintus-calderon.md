## Background

Quintus Calderon is a dynast — he did not seize the throne, he was born to it. The Calderon name built the Imperium's edict code and standing legions, and Quintus governs as the living extension of that institutional will. He is more interested in the machinery of empire holding together than in personal glory.

## Role in the Imperium

- Final authority over all edicts; the High Edict Council advises, but his word is law
- Commands ultimate disposition of the legion structure through the Provincial Legates
- The Censorate reports directly to him on matters of internal security and arcane R&D

## Using Quintus in Play

- Unlikely to appear in person outside Ironstead; his presence would signal something unprecedented
- Most contact with the Imperium filters through Legates, Prefects, or the High Censor
- When the players finally reach him, the scene should feel like an audience with an institution, not a person
- He is not a villain. He is the shape that power takes when it believes it is correct.
