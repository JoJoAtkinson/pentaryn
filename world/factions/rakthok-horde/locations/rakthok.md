# Rakthok

> War-shaman bastion on the blighted steppe.

- Culture: Orc (Rakthok Horde)
- Type: Capital stronghold
- Faction: [Rakthok-Horde](../_overview.md)

## Description
Rakthok’s bone-marked palisades and omen towers stand over scarred plains. Vision Councils convene to name Chosen Warlords when threats loom.

## Points of Interest
- Omen Tower — shamanic observatory of dreams and signs
- Vision Circle — council ground for naming a Warlord
- Ashstep Bastion — fortifications against cursed incursions

## Adventure Hooks
- A grim omen sparks a pre-emptive strike debate.
- A spirit guide warns of betrayal by “trusted neighbors.”
- The blight spreads beyond ritual containment lines.
