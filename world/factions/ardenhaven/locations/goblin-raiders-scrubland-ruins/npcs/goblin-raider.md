# Goblin Raider

**HP** 10 (3d6) **·** **AC** 15 (leather armor, shield) **·** **Speed** 30 ft. **·** **Save** Dex +2 **·** **Stealth** +6 **·** **Darkvision** 60 ft. **·** **Passive Perception** 9 **·** **CR** 1/4 (50 XP)

> Action mechanics live in `combat-runner/actions.jsonl` — see the launcher's **Ready actions** reference for verbs.

---

## Start-of-turn checklist

1. **Nimble Escape** is available every turn — a bonus-action Disengage or Hide. Use it.
2. If this raider is below 5 HP, or the boss has fallen, it wants out (Disengage + Dash for the cave or open ground).

---

## Tactics — when the DM asks "what does it do?"

- **Round 1:** loose a shortbow shot from behind boulder cover, then Nimble Escape to stay hidden.
- **Kite:** never stand and trade. Shoot, Hide/Disengage, reposition — make the party come to the rocks.
- **In melee:** scimitar only if cornered; it would rather Disengage away and go back to the bow.
- **Gang up:** if two goblins flank one PC, their attacks have Advantage (+1d4 damage on a hit) — call it out.
- **Cowardice:** below 5 HP, or once the boss drops or half the band is down, it breaks and runs.

## Description (one line)

A wiry, leather-clad goblin with a notched scimitar and a shortbow, quick to dart between the rubble.
