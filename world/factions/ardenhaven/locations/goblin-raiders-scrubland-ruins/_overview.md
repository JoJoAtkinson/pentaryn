# Goblin Raiders — Western Scrubland Ruins

> A second goblin warband crept into the cave the last band was driven out of. Bigger, better led, and a tenday settled in.

**Where:** the cave lair beneath the [Western Scrubland Ruins](../ruins-western-scrubland.md), ~3 miles SW of Ardenford.
**Tuned for:** 4–6 level-1 PCs. Default 4 raiders + 1 boss (~400 XP) — hard for four, solid for five. Scale raiders in the launcher's count spinner.

## Terrain

- **Above ground:** crumbled walls 2–3 ft high; scattered boulders give half cover; loose stone is difficult terrain in patches.
- **Cave mouth:** 3–4 ft high — a choke point; Medium creatures crouch through.
- **Inside:** 25×20 ft main chamber with a cold fire pit; a low passage leads to a damp back chamber (the old sheep pen).
- **Light:** dim at dusk outside, dark in the cave. Goblins have darkvision 60 ft — the party may not.

## How it opens

A lookout on the rubble spots the party unless they out-stealth its passive Perception. The band sets up: raiders to the boulders with shortbows, the boss hanging back barking orders. The boss will parley if the fight turns against it — and it lies.

## Tactics

Raiders kite — shoot, Nimble Escape behind cover, repeat. The boss directs from range and uses Redirect Attack to feed raiders to incoming blows. Once the boss drops or half the band is down, survivors break for the back chamber and scatter.

## NPCs

- **goblin-raider** ×4 — the warband (CR 1/4 each)
- **goblin-boss** ×1 — the scarred leader (CR 1)
