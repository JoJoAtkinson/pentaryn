# Giant Wolf Spider

**HP** 13 (3d6+3) **·** **AC** 13 (natural armor) **·** **Speed** 40 ft., climb 40 ft. **·** **Perception** +3 **·** **Stealth** +5 **·** **Darkvision** 60 ft. **·** **Blindsight** 10 ft. **·** **Passive Perception** 13 **·** **CR** 1/4 (50 XP)

> Action mechanics live in `combat-runner/actions.jsonl` — see the launcher's **Ready actions** reference for verbs.

---

## Start-of-turn checklist

1. **Spider Climb:** full-speed movement on trees and trunks, even upside down.
2. No web of its own — this is the fast ground hunter. Use the 40 ft. speed every turn.

---

## Tactics — when the DM asks "what does it do?"

- **Ambush:** opens from the leaf litter or a low trunk, darting in on whoever the giant spider hasn't pinned.
- **Hit and reposition:** fast enough to bite and still climb out of reach, or flank with the other wolf spider for Advantage.
- **Run down stragglers:** the 40 ft. speed catches fleeing or low-HP PCs — chase them.
- **Mindless:** no fear, no retreat — fights to the death.

## Description (one line)

A swift, low-slung hunting spider the size of a large hound, mottled brown for the forest floor.
