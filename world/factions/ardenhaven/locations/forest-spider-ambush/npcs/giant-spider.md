# Giant Spider

**HP** 26 (4d10+4) **·** **AC** 14 (natural armor) **·** **Speed** 30 ft., climb 30 ft. **·** **Stealth** +7 **·** **Darkvision** 60 ft. **·** **Blindsight** 10 ft. **·** **Passive Perception** 10 **·** **CR** 1 (200 XP)

> Action mechanics live in `combat-runner/actions.jsonl` — see the launcher's **Ready actions** reference for verbs.

---

## Start-of-turn checklist

1. **Web** — if USED, roll 1d6 (`roll_dice(1, 6)`); it recharges on a 5 or 6.
2. **Spider Climb:** the spider moves on the canopy web and tree trunks at full speed, even upside down — track its vertical position.
3. **Web Walker:** it ignores movement restrictions caused by webbing (its own or anyone's).

---

## Tactics — when the DM asks "what does it do?"

- **Opener:** from the canopy, Web the most dangerous PC (DC 13 Dex save or Restrained).
- **Follow up:** drop or climb adjacent to a Restrained target and Bite — attacks against a Restrained creature have Advantage.
- **Stay high:** between bites, climb back up out of melee reach; melee PCs must chase it up the web.
- **No retreat:** a hunting spider does not flee — it fights until killed.

## Description (one line)

A dog-sized hunting spider, bristled and grey, hanging in a sheet of web strung between the trees.
