# Trash Mobs (7) — The Gloomcinders

Run these as two squads (Lantern Skulkers + Hook Bruisers). Name them as needed from: [Derro Open Names](../derro-open-names.md).

## Derro Lantern Skulker (x4)

Small humanoid (derro), chaotic evil

**AC** 13 (leather) **HP** 7 (2d8 - 2) **Speed** 30 ft.  
**STR** 8 (-1) **DEX** 14 (+2) **CON** 8 (-1) **INT** 10 (+0) **WIS** 10 (+0) **CHA** 8 (-1)  
**Skills** Stealth +4 **Senses** darkvision 120 ft.  
**Sunlight Sensitivity.**

**Actions:**  
- **Dagger.** +4 to hit, 1d4+2 piercing.  
- **Light Crossbow.** +4 to hit, 1d8+2 piercing.

**Soot Bomb (1/Day).** The skulker throws a clay bomb at a point within 20 feet. A 10-foot-radius cloud is heavily obscured until the start of the skulker’s next turn.

**How to run:** Hide, shoot, and force the party to fight at the edge of vision. If cornered, drop Soot Bomb and flee.

## Derro Hook Bruiser (x3)

Small humanoid (derro), chaotic evil

**AC** 14 (studded leather) **HP** 9 (2d8) **Speed** 25 ft.  
**STR** 12 (+1) **DEX** 12 (+1) **CON** 10 (+0) **INT** 8 (-1) **WIS** 10 (+0) **CHA** 8 (-1)  
**Senses** darkvision 120 ft.  
**Sunlight Sensitivity.**

**Actions:**  
- **Club.** +3 to hit, 1d4+1 bludgeoning.  
- **Handaxe.** +3 to hit (melee or 20/60), 1d6+1 slashing.

**How to run:** Don’t chase; hold angles. If a PC goes prone or blind, swarm them.
