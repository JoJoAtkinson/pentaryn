# Derro Foe Party: The Shardclaws (10)

## Quick Premise

The Shardclaws are a scavenger-pack of derro raiders who treat the Deep Fall Ruins like a pantry. Their “wizard” is not learned—he is a **relic user** who burns scrolls, snaps wands, and gulps single-use charms like medicine.

**Vibe:** godless, cruel, practical. They don’t chant. They inventory.

## Party Roster (10)

### The 3 that matter

1) **Grumkhor-hammar** (GROOM-kor-HAM-mar) — *hammering*  
Role: “Wizard” leader (item caster). Highest level.

2) **Khardruk-dun** (KHAR-druck-dun) — *rusted*  
Role: skirmisher lieutenant (stab-and-fade).

3) **Brakthar-kar** (BRAK-thar-kar) — *chainbound*  
Role: bruiser lieutenant (net, shove, finish).

### The 7 mooks (run as two squads)

4–7) **Derro Skulkers (x4)** — optional names: Thar'brum, Khorbad-dum, Drakmor-dur, Thargrum-dun  
8–10) **Derro Bruisers (x3)** — optional names: Bardrog-dur, Drogbar-bor, (pick one from open list)
