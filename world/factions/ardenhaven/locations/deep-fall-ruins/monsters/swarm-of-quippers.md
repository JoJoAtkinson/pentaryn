# Swarm of Quippers (CR 1)

**Slug:** `swarm-of-quippers`

## DM Use

- Turns any fall into water into a panic moment.
- Best used as a **hazard** that forces repositioning while another threat pressures the party.

## Encounter Seeds

- Quippers in a flooded trench beneath an “easy” crossing.
- Quippers released from a broken derro holding pen as a distraction.
