## Derro Choir Skulker (x4)

Small humanoid (derro), chaotic evil

**AC** 13 (leather) **HP** 7 (2d8 - 2) **Speed** 30 ft.  
**STR** 8 (-1) **DEX** 14 (+2) **CON** 8 (-1) **INT** 10 (+0) **WIS** 10 (+0) **CHA** 8 (-1)  
**Skills** Stealth +4 **Senses** darkvision 120 ft.  
**Sunlight Sensitivity.**

**Actions:**  
- **Dagger.** +4 to hit, 1d4+2 piercing.  
- **Light Crossbow.** +4 to hit, 1d8+2 piercing.

**Chime-Shard (1/Day).** A skulker throws a jagged crystal at a point within 20 feet. Each creature within 5 feet of that point must succeed on a DC 11 Con save or be deafened until the end of its next turn.

**How to run:** Hold angles, focus-fire anyone concentrating, and use Chime-Shard to make the cave feel hostile.

## Derro Glasscutter (x3)

Small humanoid (derro), chaotic evil

**AC** 14 (studded leather) **HP** 9 (2d8) **Speed** 30 ft.  
**STR** 10 (+0) **DEX** 14 (+2) **CON** 10 (+0) **INT** 8 (-1) **WIS** 10 (+0) **CHA** 8 (-1)  
**Senses** darkvision 120 ft.  
**Sunlight Sensitivity.**

**Actions:**  
- **Glass Knife.** +4 to hit, 1d4+2 piercing.

**Cut & Scatter.** If the glasscutter hits a creature with Glass Knife, it can immediately move 10 feet without provoking opportunity attacks.

**How to run:** Run in pairs, stab once, scatter, and bait the party into the hook lane.
