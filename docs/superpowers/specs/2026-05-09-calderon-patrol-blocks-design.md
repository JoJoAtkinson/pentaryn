# Calderon Gar Valley Pass Patrol Stat Blocks

**Purpose:** Create 5 reusable stat blocks (soldier, veteran, necromancer, diviner, cleric) that can be mixed for random and scripted patrol encounters at the Gar Valley Pass checkpoint. Players can quickly assemble patrols without creating new stat blocks each time.

**Design principle:** All blocks are modular. A small patrol = 2 soldiers + 1 diviner. A tense checkpoint confrontation = 1 veteran + 1 necromancer. Large ambush = 3-4 soldiers + 2 casters. Mix freely.

**Calderon aesthetic:** Disciplined, bureaucratic, necromantic. Soldiers follow tactics. Casters use death magic not for cruelty but control (raise dead for labor, hold person for arrest, detect thoughts for census). The empire runs on death magic *as infrastructure*.

---

## 1. Calderon Soldier

**Role:** Infantry, patrol grunt, checkpoint guard.

**Stat Block:**
- **AC** 16 (mail + shield)
- **HP** 22 (4d8 + 4)
- **Speed** 30 ft
- **Abilities:** STR 15 (+2), DEX 10 (+0), CON 12 (+1), INT 10 (+0), WIS 11 (+0), CHA 10 (+0)
- **Skills:** Perception +2
- **Senses:** Passive Perception 12

**Actions:**
- **Halberd:** +4 to hit, 1d10+2 slashing (or 1d12+2 if two-handed)
- **Crossbow, Light:** +2 to hit, 1d6 piercing (range 80/320)
- **Multiattack:** Two halberd or crossbow attacks (one each), or two halberd attacks if no crossbow engaged

**Tactics:** Forms shield walls, uses cover, follows orders. Disciplined but no special abilities.

**Flavor:** "Calderon infantry. Competent, loyal, organized."

## 2. Calderon Veteran

**Role:** Squad sergeant, checkpoint commander, tactical leader.

**Stat Block:**
- **AC** 17 (splint armor)
- **HP** 38 (7d8 + 7)
- **Speed** 30 ft
- **Abilities:** STR 16 (+3), DEX 11 (+0), CON 12 (+1), INT 12 (+1), WIS 13 (+1), CHA 12 (+1)
- **Skills:** Athletics +5, Insight +3, Perception +3
- **Saving Throws:** STR +5
- **Senses:** Passive Perception 13

**Actions:**
- **Greatsword:** +5 to hit, 2d6+3 slashing damage
- **Crossbow, Heavy:** +3 to hit, 2d10+1 piercing (range 100/400)
- **Multiattack:** Two greatsword attacks or one greatsword + one crossbow

**Reactions:**
- **Parry:** +2 to AC against a melee attack within 5 ft (must wield melee weapon)

**Spellcasting (1st level, 2 slots):**
- **Spells:** Command (hold person for 1 round), Detect Magic (ritual)
- **Spell save DC** 12, **Spell attack** +3

**Tactics:** Gives orders. Uses parry to soak damage. Commands soldiers to Hold or stay back. Uses Detect Magic on suspicious travelers.

**Flavor:** "Disciplined. Speaks with authority. The voice of Calderon at the checkpoint."

## 3. Calderon Necromancer

**Role:** Caster, death-magic specialist, enforcer.

**Stat Block:**
- **AC** 13 (leather)
- **HP** 44 (8d8 + 8)
- **Speed** 30 ft
- **Abilities:** STR 10 (+0), DEX 12 (+1), CON 12 (+1), INT 16 (+3), WIS 14 (+2), CHA 11 (+0)
- **Skills:** Arcana +5, Insight +4, Perception +4
- **Saving Throws:** INT +5, WIS +4
- **Senses:** Passive Perception 14
- **Languages:** Infernal (and one other)

**Actions:**
- **Dagger:** +2 to hit, 1d4+1 piercing
- **Chill Touch (cantrip):** Melee spell attack +5, 2d10 necrotic damage, prevents HP regain until next turn

**Spellcasting (3rd level caster):**
- **Spell save DC** 15, **Spell attack** +5
- **Cantrips (at-will):** Chill Touch, Spare the Dying
- **1st level (4 slots):** Inflict Wounds (+5 to hit, 2d10 necrotic touch), False Life (self, 2d4+4 temp HP)
- **2nd level (3 slots):** Ray of Enfeeblement (ranged, target disadvantage on STR checks/damage), Animate Dead (minion summoning)
- **3rd level (2 slots):** Bestow Curse (touch save DC 15, +1d8 necrotic damage per hit, concentration), Vampiric Touch (melee spell attack +5, 3d6 necrotic, heal for half)

**Tactics:** Stays back, opens with Animate Dead or Bestow Curse. Uses Ray of Enfeeblement to disable warriors. Chill Touch from range. If engaged in melee, uses Inflict Wounds. Flees if HP drop below 15.

**Flavor:** "The Imperium's grip made flesh. Death magic as policy. Bureaucratic evil."

## 4. Calderon Diviner

**Role:** Information officer, interrogator, scout.

**Stat Block:**
- **AC** 14 (leather + protective ward)
- **HP** 33 (6d8 + 6)
- **Speed** 30 ft
- **Abilities:** STR 10 (+0), DEX 13 (+1), CON 12 (+1), INT 15 (+2), WIS 15 (+2), CHA 12 (+1)
- **Skills:** Insight +5, Investigation +4, Perception +5
- **Saving Throws:** INT +4, WIS +4
- **Senses:** Passive Perception 15
- **Languages:** Thieves' Cant, Infernal

**Actions:**
- **Quarterstaff:** +1 to hit, 1d6 bludgeoning
- **Mind Spike (2nd level spell):** Ranged save DC 14, 3d8 psychic damage, you can sense target's location while on same plane (concentration, 1 hour)

**Spellcasting (3rd level caster):**
- **Spell save DC** 14, **Spell attack** +4
- **Cantrips (at-will):** Mage Hand, Prestidigitation
- **1st level (4 slots):** Detect Thoughts (concentration, 30 ft, probe minds), Comprehend Languages (ritual, understand any language 1 hour), Identify (ritual)
- **2nd level (3 slots):** Mind Spike, Augury (ritual, Weal/Woe/Indifference omen for next 30 min)
- **3rd level (1 slot):** Clairvoyance (ritual, scry a location, 10 min casting/concentration)

**Tactics:** Uses Detect Thoughts to interrogate prisoners. Prepares Comprehend Languages for border checks. Uses Augury to sense danger. Mind Spike if combat starts. Wants information, not death.

**Flavor:** "The census-taker. The thought-reader. Calderon's reach into minds."

## 5. Calderon Cleric

**Role:** Support/defensive caster, healer, tactical enabler.

**Stat Block:**
- **AC** 15 (chain shirt + shield)
- **HP** 40 (8d8)
- **Speed** 30 ft
- **Abilities:** STR 13 (+1), DEX 10 (+0), CON 10 (+0), INT 12 (+1), WIS 15 (+2), CHA 13 (+1)
- **Skills:** Insight +5, Medicine +5, Perception +2
- **Saving Throws:** WIS +5
- **Senses:** Passive Perception 12
- **Languages:** Infernal

**Actions:**
- **Mace:** +3 to hit, 1d6+1 bludgeoning
- **Spiritual Weapon (conjure, bonus action):** Melee spell attack +4, 1d8+2 force damage, hovers, can attack at range (concentration, 1 minute)

**Spellcasting (3rd level caster):**
- **Spell save DC** 13, **Spell attack** +4
- **Cantrips (at-will):** Light, Sacred Flame (+4 to hit, 1d8 radiant, no save)
- **1st level (4 slots):** Command (hold/retreat/flee), Healing Word (bonus action, heal 1d4+2 at range 60 ft), Bless (concentration, +1d4 to attacks/saves for up to 3 creatures)
- **2nd level (3 slots):** Hold Person (concentration, paralyzed on failed save), Spiritual Weapon (see above), Blindness/Deafness (saves DC 13)
- **3rd level (1 slot):** Spirit Guardians (concentration, 15 ft aura, 3d8 radiant damage on save fail, spirits move with caster)

**Tactics:** Opens with Bless on allies. Uses Hold Person on threats. Summons Spiritual Weapon to control space. Healing Word to revive downed soldiers. If things escalate, casts Spirit Guardians. Defensive, not aggressive.

**Flavor:** "The state's chaplain. Hold them fast. Keep them loyal."

## Encounter Templates

### Small Patrol (500 XP budget)
- 2 Soldiers
- 1 Diviner

**Setup:** Border checkpoint. Asking questions, checking papers, scanning for magic. Turns combat if provoked.

### Medium Patrol (1000 XP budget)
- 1 Veteran (leader)
- 2 Soldiers
- 1 Necromancer

**Setup:** Roving patrol. Encounters players on road. Arrests or conscription mission. Combat likely if players resist.

### Large Patrol (1500 XP budget)
- 1 Veteran (commander)
- 3-4 Soldiers
- 1 Necromancer
- 1 Cleric (support)

**Setup:** Full ambush or major checkpoint confrontation. Player vs. organized military unit.

### Interrogation Scene (no combat, 0 XP)
- 1 Diviner (lead interrogator)
- 1 Veteran (intimidation/guard)
- 2 Soldiers (guards)

**Setup:** Players captured or detained. Diviner uses Detect Thoughts, Comprehend Languages. Veteran threatens. Soldiers block exits.

## Mix-and-Match Logic

- **For flavor:** Pair casters (any of the three) with soldiers to taste. Necromancer = enforcement. Diviner = intelligence. Cleric = internal loyalty.
- **For CR:** 1 Soldier ≈ CR 1/2. 1 Veteran ≈ CR 2. 1 Caster ≈ CR 2-3. Scale up or down by adding/removing soldiers.
- **For tactics:** Soldiers follow orders (Veteran commands). Casters stay back. Diviner wants prisoners. Necromancer wants fear. Cleric wants battlefield control.
